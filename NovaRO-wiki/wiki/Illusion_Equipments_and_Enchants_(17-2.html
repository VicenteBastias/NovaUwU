Automatic Equipment Enchants

The Automatic equipments can be enchanted by using Automatic Modules, given by Yecheng (ba_in01 87, 380)Copied!.

They are divided into 5 categories.

Normal: Defense, Magic Defense, Vit, Luk, Str, Agi, Int, Dex.
Rare: HP Recovery, SP Recovery, Spell, Attack Speed, Fatal, Expert Archer, Vital, Mental, Heal, Attack Power, Magic Power, Shooter, Fast, Caster, Critical.
Unique: Magical Force, Attacker Force, Range Force, Critical Force, Recovery Force, Mirror Counter, Delay after skill, Fixed Casting, Above All, Drain Life, Drain Soul, Magic Healing, Magic Soul, Power Force, Robust, Powerful, All Force, Reflection Reject.
Legendary: Unlimited Vital, Spell Buster, Firing Shot, Overpower, Fatal Flash, Lucky Strike.
Epic: Skill-specific ones.

The regular modules (Normal, Rare, Unique, Legendary) are obtained from two different methods:

  • You can exchange 90  Varmeal Tickets with Yecheng (ba_in01 87, 380)Copied!.
  • An  Automatic Module Box, which is a low chance reward of the Second Security Area instance.

The Epic Modules are obtained from two different methods:

  • You can exchange 300  Magical Soapstones with Spiera (ba_in01 87, 373)Copied!. The Magical Soapstones are obtained as a potential reward of the Second Security Area instance or by exchanging 350  Varmeal Tickets with Yecheng (ba_in01 87, 380)Copied!.
  • An  Epic Module Box, which is a low chance reward of the Second Security Area instance.

To apply the Automatic Modules, equip the Automatic armor and then speak with Liza (ba_in01 87, 370)Copied!. The enchanting process consumes the Automatic Module and never fails.

Every Automatic equipment can receive up to 3 modifications, but each Module type can only be applied a certain number of times (check Max Count on the table below for each enchant), depending of the equipment piece.

Be aware that:

  • You can have up to 3 Epic Enchants in the Automatic Armor, up to 2 of the same enchant. So it's either 3 different Epic Enchants, or 2 of the same and 1 different.
  • You can have up to 3 different/unique Legendary Enchants in the Automatic Leg. This means that none of them can be repeated.
