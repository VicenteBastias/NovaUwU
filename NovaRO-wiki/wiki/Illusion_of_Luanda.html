Main Quest

Requirements
Base Level: 160
Starting Point: beach_dun2 245/251
Rewards
Experience: None
  1. To enter the Illusion of Luanda dungeon, click on the light at (beach_dun2 245/251) and select Investigate. You can get there using the Warper > Illusion Dungeons > Illusion of Luanda.
  2. Speak with Defense Officer Luanda at (com_d02_i 241/255).
    You have now unlocked all the daily quests for Illusion of Luanda.


Daily Quests

All the daily quests are obtained from the Bulletin Board located at com_d02_i 238/266 above the Defense Officer Luanda.

Proof of Megalith Extermination

Requirements
Base Level: 160
Starting Point: com_d02_i 238/266
Rewards
Experience: 107,500,000 (Base) and 70,000,000 (Job)
Items: 3  Illusion Stones (custom)
  1. Click on the Bulletin Board and choose [Daily] Proof of Megalith to accept the request.
  2. You will need to gather 3  Tokens of Bigstone from the following mobs:

    • Ancient Stalactic Golem
    • Ancient Megalith
    • Ancient Tao Gunka

Story Quests

Requirements
Base Level: 160
Starting Point: Various
Rewards
Experience: 28,000,000 (Base) and 16,250,000 (Job)
Items: None

Every 3 hours, two black vortexes will appear on the map at random locations. To proceed with the story quests, you must speak with them in order. These quests are optional and only give experience. They are fragments of the past that allows you to learn more about the story of Luanda. The location of each Vortex is indicated on the right sided picture.

Vortex locations.

Story 1

  1. Go to any active vortex on the map and click on it. You've identified a strange phenomenon occuring all over the dungeon.
  2. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 2

  1. Go to Vortex 1.
  2. Speak with the Second Young Man. You meet with the three brothers: Mao, Karu and Luanda. They're about to start a hunt!
  3. Speak with Second Young Man again. He tells you to leave this place before a terrible event occurs.
  4. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 3

  1. Go to Vortex 2.
  2. Speak with Karu. He's on a date with Ahsemem and you interrupt it.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 4

  1. Go to Vortex 3.
  2. Speak with the Young Man. You learn more about why Luanda is painting on the cave walls. He had a dream where the village burned down after monsters invaded the area.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 5

  1. Go to Vortex 4.
  2. Speak with the Young Man. You meet with Mao practicing his mental abilities.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 6

  1. Go to Vortex 5.
  2. Speak with the Young Man. He grabbed something in the water.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 7

  1. Go to Vortex 1.
  2. Speak with the Village Chief. The village is under attack, but the brothers aren't there to defend it.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 8

  1. Go to Vortex 6.
  2. Speak with the Third Young Man. He and his brothers smell the blood coming from the village and decide to head back immediately.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 9

  1. Go to Vortex 2.
  2. Speak with Karu. Karu lost his wife after the village incident.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.

Story 10

  1. Go to Vortex 7.
  2. Speak with the Dark Shadow (right one). You learn about who's responsible behind these attacks and the mysterious power held within the village.
  3. Return to Defense Officer Luanda.


Daily Quest Reset

As an exclusive feature to NovaRO, you are able to reset all your daily quests cooldowns.

That means you are able to farm Illusion Stones as much as you want on a single character, without having to create additional characters to bypass the cooldown.

To do so, talk to Osmundi. He can be found inside one of the following locations:

  • Illusion of Moonlight dungeon (pay_d03_i 155, 45)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Vampire dungeon (gef_dun01 130, 221)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Frozen dungeon (ice_d03_i 159, 40)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Abyss dungeon (alberta 224, 30)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Teddy Bear dungeon (ein_dun01 185, 78)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Luanda dungeon (com_d02_i 246, 249)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Twins dungeon (anthell02 170, 190)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Labyrinth dungeon (prt_maze01 94, 24)Copied!.
  • Illusion of Underwater dungeon (iz_d04_i 139, 228)Copied!.


They are separated in 5 distinct difficulty groups. Each reset will award you with a set amount of:

  • Tier 1: Illusion of Moonlight and Illusion of Frozen.
    Reset Reward:  10 Illusion Stone and a choice between  Moonlight Refine Box and  Frozen Refine Box
  • Tier 2: Illusion of Vampire and Illusion of Abyss
    Reset Reward:  12 Illusion Stone and a choice between  Vampire Refine Box and  Abyss Refine Box
  • Tier 3: Illusion of Teddy Bear and Illusion of Underwater - 1
    Reset Reward:  14 Illusion Stone and a choice between  Teddy Bear Refine Box and  Underwater Refine Box
  • Tier 4: Illusion of Luanda and Illusion of Twins
    Reset Reward:  16 Illusion Stone and a choice between  Luanda Refine Box and  Twins Refine Box
  • Tier 5: Illusion of Labyrinth and Illusion of Underwater - 2
    Reset Reward:  22 Illusion Stone and a choice between  Labyrinth Refine Box and  Underwater Refine Box

The first reset of the day, for each group, will give a 12  Illusion Stone bonus.

Once you complete the dailies of one of the Tiers above, you can reset them. After a reset, these quests will not give any experience until their original 24 hours cooldown pass.

Each Tier can be completed and reset separately, but you cannot reset dailies from a single Illusion Dungeon without completing the others in their respective group.



Mob Spawn

The monsters in the area are split in two clans: the Wootan clan and the Megalith clan.
Specific to NovaRo, the dungeon automatically swaps mob factions every hour, alternating between them.
You can only fight against one clan at a time. So if the Megalith clan is active on the map, you will only see the following mobs:

  • Ancient Tri Joints
  • Ancient Stalactic Golems
  • Ancient Megaliths

On the other hand, if the Wootan clan is active, you will only see the following mobs:

  • Ancient Stone Shooters
  • Ancient Wootan Shooters
  • Ancient Wootan Fighters

After killing 1500 mobs of a clan, the MVP of the clan will appear: Ancient Tao Gunka or Ancient Wootan Defender.
During the last 10 minutes of each hour, kill counts for spawning the MVP will temporarily pause until the factions are swapped.
Mob kill amounts for spawning the MVP are kept in-between factions swaps.

Illusion Enchants

Illusion equipment can be enchanted. For details, see here.

Refine Box

Completing Daily Quest Reset rewards the player with a Refine Box that can upgrade (or sometimes downgrade) a +4 or higher illusion equipment from the matching dungeon to +7 ~ +10.

Defeating an illusion MVP rewards the MVP player (to be confirmed) by chance with a Special Refine Box that can upgrade (or sometimes downgrade) a +4 or higher illusion equipment from the matching dungeon to +9 ~ +12.

You can exchange 25 Refine Box of a single dungeon for 1 Special Refine Box of the same dungeon from Illusion Merchant NPC ((prontera 88, 113)Copied!).

Note that:

  • The chance of getting each refine level is not uniform. For example, there is a significantly lower chance of getting +10 than getting +7 when using a regular Refine Box.
  • An upgrade is not guaranteed. For example, using a Refine Box on a +9 illusion equipment may result in a downgrade to +7.
