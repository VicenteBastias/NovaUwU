Summer Concert

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Summer Concert is a brand new event this year!
Come down to Rock Island and listen to The Hunter Insides closing ceremony!
The Concert will take place in the last 15 minutes of every second Summer Festival!
You can check the times on the Summer Festival main page.

Each concert will consist of a set of 3 songs.
You will be asked to do various tasks throughout the songs.
The better you do, the more  Guitar Picks you will get!
You can trade in  Guitar Picks after the show for Band Merch!

Mosh Pit (Gameplay)

Throughout the concert you will have to follow many commands as the band plays thier set.


When Astrid says Bounce;
Spam the  Bounce command!

When Astrid says Headbang;
Spam the  Headbang command!

When Astrid says Charge;
Spam the  Charge command!

When Astrid says Spin;
Spam the  Spin command!

When Astrid says Go Wild;
Spam the any and all of the commands!

When Astrid says Sing Along;
Retype the single line Astrid says!

  • Capitalization does not matter.

When Astrid says Circle Pit;
Follow the portals and run in a circle!
