Low Rank Shadow Gear

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Low Rank Shadow Gear is crafted with materials from Low Rank Hunts in the Flooded Forest. These Shadow Sets tend to boost a Primary Stat (STR, AGI, etc.), a related Secondary Stat (ATK, ASPD, etc), and are worn over existing gear. Low Rank Shadow Gear cannot be refined for additional bonuses.

They can be crafted by Harek Ignison, located at The Wyvern (2009rwc_03 245, 194)Copied!, accessible by typing @go MH.
