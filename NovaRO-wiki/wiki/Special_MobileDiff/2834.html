Bios Island is part of the [[Instances#14.3_Decisive_Battle|episode 14.3]] and is the prerequisite of all the other instances found in the Flame Cave. If you've completed the [[Fire Basin Quests#Dimensional Travel|Dimensional Travel]] quest, you will be able to unlock the '''Bios Island''' instance menu from the Warper.
Morse Cave is part of the [[Instances#14.3_Decisive_Battle|episode 14.3]] and is the sequel instance to Bios Island. If you've completed the [[Bios Island#Bios Island|Bios Island]] quest, you will be able to access this instance. To enter this instance, warp to ''Bios Island'' under instance tab, talk to ''Senior Tracker'' (<code>/navi moro_cav 61/69</code>) to generate the instance. Talk to the ''Red Flower'' (<code>/navi moro_cav 57/65</code>) to enter the instance.  


The instance is divided in 3 parts. In parts 1 and 2, your objective is to kill ''every single mob'' on the map in order to get the warp, on the far right side, to open up. These mobs move fairly slowly so it is possible to kite them. Don't over mob or you might die as they do hit pretty hard!
The instance is divided into 5 phases.
# You fight 9 [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3141/ Morroc's Ghoul], kill them all to proceed.
# [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/2998 Weakened Morroc] will spawn. Kill him and move on.
# Your party will be randomly split into 2 groups and be rooted in place, kill everything that spawns to proceed.
# Both groups will be teleported back into the same room. Kill everything to continue.
# Your party will face [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/2999 Morroc Necromancer] who will morph into his [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3000 FINAL FORM] when you killed his first form. [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3000 Morroc Necromancer] will drop 2 [[Prize of Hero]] upon death.


In part 3, you will have to clear 3 waves of monsters before [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3029 Reaper Yanku] appears. You do not have to be within the MVP's range when it is killed to obtain your {{Item |id=6684|name=Token of Hero}}.
Talk to the teleportation device to exit the map and speak to ''Senior Tracker'' (<code>/navi moro_cav 61/69</code>) in order to receive your Token of Hero.
