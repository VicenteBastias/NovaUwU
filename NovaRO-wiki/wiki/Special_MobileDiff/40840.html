Alternatively, if you choose to skip the tutorial, you may opt to level up your Novice the ol' fashioned way. ''Stabbing something until it dies.'' Fortunately, the server is on a 25x XP multiplier so this won't take too long. Below is a table of recommended mobs and where to find them. Most of these mobs would require having ample HIT (which means you're gonna spend those points into DEX) and some like the Fabres would require you to bring pots lest you risk dying while exchanging blows. You can receive a free batch of Novice Potions from Mom if you visit her at Home after skipping (or finishing) the tutorial.<br>
