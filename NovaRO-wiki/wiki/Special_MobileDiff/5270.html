===Investing with zeny===
Talk to the [[Guild Dungeon Access#Cat Paw Merchants (investment)|Cat Paw Merchant]] of the respective region to invest with zenies.


Once you've found the Cat Paw Merchant, select '''I want to invest in this area''' > '''Invest with Zeny'''. You will then be given the option of 1, 10 or 50 units. Each unit represents 10,000 Zeny. There is another option to choose the amount of units, ranging from 1 to 200.

=== Investing with items ===
[[Clan System]]
Talk to [[Guild Dungeon Access#Namis (investment)|Namis]], at Malangdo (<code>/navi malangdo 218/123</code>).


Tell him you wish to invest and select the items you wish to invest. The conversion chart for items to tickets is below. The item received for this investment is '''Cats Invest Certificate'''. Each of these tickets can be given to the [[Guild Dungeon Access#Cat Paw Merchants (investment)|Cat Paw Merchant]] in the realm in exchange for '''10 Thanks Invest Ticket'''. This gives each Cats Invest Ticket a value of 100,000 Zeny for investing.

[[Monster Hunter]]

== Times ==
[[Troubleshooting]]
* You can invest starting on '''Saturdays at 7 PM''', one hour after the Horn WoE ends.
* Investment closes on''' Tuesdays at 7 AM'''
* Dungeons will open on '''Tuesdays at 8 AM''', one hour after investment closes, if their requirements have been fulfilled
* Dungeons will close on '''Saturdays at 4 PM''', one hour before the Horn WoE begins again.
All of this in '''PST, servertime. Use @time in-game to see what time it is. '''
