'''Conclusions:'''
 
Alright! Compared to the last sets of tests, these are much more consistent and compareable.
 
There are 2 take aways from this in my opnion:
 
 
First off, as long as you are not looking for specifically Single Target Damage, it doesn't quite matter which skill you use. They all are fairly close to each other, apart from Blue Dragon, that outright wins in the Single Target department. Essentially what we expected from the maths before. In that sense, it's more important to think about what you want to use and for what purpose (and be it that you simply like how the skill looks or feels) instead of the damage.
 
Second off, 193 ASPD doesn't seem to be crucial. The difference between 193 ASPD of Test Two and 191 ASPD of Test Three are marginal, around 2 million of DPS only. And this doesn't factor in the +20 Stats, either.
 
Depending on what you do, 190-191 and even much less seems to be perfectly reasonable to go with. I've been farming Jupe 3 with around 173 ASPD only recently. Since I don't need to go full spam there, it works quite well. 193 ASPD appears to be overkill. Nice to have via buffs and all of course if you get them in a party enviorment, but yea. Nothing one needs to strive for on their own.
 
 
Hope this helps you with your decision which skills to go with :D
