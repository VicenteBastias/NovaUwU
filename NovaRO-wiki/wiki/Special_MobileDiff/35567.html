Hiiii, I'm Lady Narnia! Welcome to my comprehensive '''Monster Hunter''' guide to High Rank healing for Dorams. =)<br>
To help you guys out, I'll be breaking down differences & similarities between Arch Bishop skills and Summoner skills.<br>
I will also be highlighting the changes Summoner's skills get when entering high rank MH (and there is a lot).
Summoner is very special, [https://www.novaragnarok.com/forum/topic/13154-monster-hunting-roles-blog-healer/ being the only class to have skills expanded to fit the healer archetype!]
*"Bread & butter" skill instantly heals a target and includes a regen packed into one.
*Special skill to instantly shield AND heal for a high amount of HP.
*Multitude of unique buffs to help your team (Flee+100, AGI+40, Blessing, Increase AGI, ATK/MATK+10%)
All of these skills, when combined with the MH healer miscellaneous skills, help make for a powerful healer that can heal any team!
