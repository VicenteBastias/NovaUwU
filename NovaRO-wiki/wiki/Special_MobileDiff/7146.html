= Overview =
Hello everyone! One of my favourite class in Pre-Renewal is the Scholar, after coming to Renewal I have tinkered with the 3rd class of them, Sorcerers, and have decided to share what I know of this class and hopefully help both new players and veterans alike.
Before we get to the guide, lets briefly outline what a Scholar/Sorcerer provides
*Access to a variety of utility skills
*Wide access to AoE (Area of Effect) magic
*Access to some of the the most powerful debuff skills such as Fiber Lock and Dispell
*Different summons give different playstyles like a homunculus for a Genetic
*Insignias (which can be confusing and hard to use) but when done right, can be very powerful
= Stats =
*'''STR''' - Not the most influential, some people will run only 1 str, if you are planning on add str, it would to increase carry weight. Its mainly personal preference how much you want to add, I would only recommend add in increments of 10, and having the base str less than 50 as there are more important stats to have
*'''AGI''' - Semi important for pvp, as this stat helps reduce chance to get Igno’ed (Masquarade of Ignorance). It is also a state useful in the Spellfist/Hindsight build.
*'''VIT''' - Helps with stun immunity and increases HP pool, pretty self-explanatory that this would be a must for either PVM or PVP
*'''INT''' -  Increases Max SP and your damage. The problem with increasing this compared to Dex, is that without dex you’ll have to deal with more considerable cast time at early levels (this can be kind of offset a bit with Memorize, but that means you’ll have to constantly recast Memorize after spell casts)
*'''DEX''' - Essential for any build for reducing cast times.
*'''LUK''' - Optional stat, this stat does however help in reducing Howl of Mandragora and White Imprison in PVP. While doesn’t add as much MATK as int, it does help add a little bit. Also adds Perfect Dodge.
Almost all people will opt to use stats in distributions of 10, the most useful ones at 120/110 (Dex and Int for Sorcerer), secondary useful at 90/80 (for us Agi and Vit), and dump the rest into the other stat pools.
= Cast Time Reduction =
Skills have mainly two portions which affect their casting, Variable and Fixed cast times .Some skills may just have Variable, some may have just Fixed and some may have both. If you are unsure what they mean, you should watch [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5qdTbw0_w54 Munbalanced's ROLab about Skill delays and Cast Time]. Basically you have Variable Cast time and Fixed Cast Time affecting how long it takes to cast a skill.
=== Variable Cast Time ===

<pre> DEX × 2 + INT = 530 </pre>

=== Fixed Cast Time ===
Some skills will also have a Fixed Cast Time portion, these are only reduced by buffs and gears. The difference is that two % bonuses from different sources do not stack, and only the biggest buff applies. Sorcerers are lucky because we have access to a special mechanic that allows us to achieve instant cast on skills with 1 second or less FCT (Fixed Cast Time).

= Insignia’s =
Here is list of what insignias can do for you:
*Insignias can only be created from the 4 basic elements, like your Spirit summon
*Only one of each elemental insignia can be placed, you can however place multiple different elemental insignias at once
*They are 3x3
*They require a catalyst purchased from Geffen Tower, the level of the insignia corresponds to the amount of catalyst used
*If you stack different same level insignias on top of each other, the most recent will take effect
*Insignias boost the counter elemental damage, regardless of level
*Insignias will heal 1% HP per 5s if the monster inside is the same element.
*Insignias will damage 1% HP per 5s if monsters inside are the opposite element
*Level 1 - insignia boosts the HP and SP regen of the same elemental summon
*Level 2 - changes the weapon of any target inside the insignia to that element
*Level 3 - increases the players elemental magic damage of that property when inside the insignia
There are a lot to know about insignias, which can scare some people. It also requires communication and knowledge of who is wearing what armor, what the mob’s elemental property is and if your party members want endows or not. If used right however, they can provide incredible tools when taking into account elemental properties

= Specialized Builds =
While it does say "specialized", you will definitely be able to pick up skills from the other categories like support/dps/summoner even if you want to play in a specific play style. Playing one style will almost never limit you in using skills from other playstyles or contain you in playing only a specific way
=== Offensive Caster ===
Using the AoE offensive skills with various elements, you deal moderately high AoE damage. Since all of these skills have cool downs, your best method is cycling through skills, mainly starting off with Diamond Dust to hopefully crystallize enemies in PVP or Psychic Wave to provide hit stun to halt enemy mobs movement in PVM
Its best to have multiple AoE skills as Sorcerers benefit little from Poem of Bragi/Magic Strings since each individual AoE skill has cool downs.

==== Strategy ====
= Gameplay/Tricks and Tips =
==== Fire Wall Placement ====
Learning how to place your Fire Wall is quite an important skill in itself. Most players will only place it in front of them and lure mobs through them like an actual wall.
While some wont realize that you can also place the wall on your feet instead of in front of you, this makes it so that if mob walks towards you, if you stand at the tip of the fire wall on the other end, the mob will walk through the entire Fire Wall and take all the hits
==== Stacking Defensive Skills====
With Energy coat giving a large % damage reduction and the ability to stack Safety Wall ontop of Blinding Mist/ Wall of Fog, this gives us Sorcerers an immense defensive capabilities. These 3 skills will help protect against mainly physical skills.
What you then can do is stack MDEF skills. This is mainly for PVP, because the WoE Robe set gives a large MDEF +50, we only need another 50 MDEF to reach 100 MDEF for the ability to be unfrozen, meaning you can swap out that Marc Card for something else like a Ghostring.
While a lot of people opt for Black Rosary, not many people use Large Hibiscus. Using enough EoE Vit 3 and Large Hibiscus can allow you to reach 100 MDEF and not worry about getting accessory stripped since FCP (Full Chemical Protection) will not protect accessories
==== Dispell vs Fiber Lock/Spider Web====
While you may think you are contributing just spamming on anyone you see, it may be more beneficial to Spider Web instead. Targets that don't require pre-casted skills or buffs to be effective and/or move at a high/fast pace may be better to actually be locked in place.
Classes like Rune Knight, Shadow Chasers, Gullotine Cross and Genetics etc
Other classes however may rely more on precasted skills or buffs. These are Warlocks, Kagerou/Oboro, Royal Guards etc
==== Summon Cycling====
In essence, this is mainly for Elemental Shield. You open up with an unfavourable Summon, let's say Tera, sacrifice him for Elemental shield, Summon Aqua for the extra crystallize chance and then sacrifice again for elemental shield and then Summon Ventus for the 1 FCT reduction.
==== Insignia Stacking====
You can combine insignias to have increased benefits for yourself and your party. For example Amdarais is a fire property monster, so you can use a level 2 Fire Insignia on him and wear Fire Armor to counter his damage while increasing water attacks on him.
You then put down a level 1 water insignia because the fire insignia would otherwise heal him. With the water insignia this cancels out the effect. You use level 1 instead of 2 because two same level insignias do not stack and only the most recent same level insignia takes effect
==== Hitlocking With DoT Skills====
Some people won't be running full Cast Time Reduction gears/stats/foods etc, you can take advantage of this by using Psychic Wave and/or Killing Cloud (preferred) on top of them which will constantly hit them out of any casting they do.
This also works if you happen to dispell people that require Arch Bishop buffs for insta cast. Fiber Locking them in place and placing one of your DoT (Damage over Time) abilities above them will render them ineffective if they have no insta  cast and have semi moderate cast times (if they have short cast times, fast enough that it can be cast in-between your ticks of damage then this is less effective)
= Leveling =
As a sorcerer, the only real piece of equipment that in my opinion is a must get, is some form of equipment that gives “uninterruptable” cast. Whether this be Orleans Gown or Phen Card or something else, this makes levelling a lot easier, since a hit will not interrupt your valuable skills.
Other than that, there are (at least for me) three methods of levelling. Both revolve around the same idea, one however sacrifices time to gain equipment while the other does not. Credit to “Muff” who suggested this to me to level in this way. Before you attempt any of these, I really recommend reading the Leveling guide on NovaRO Wiki (especially if you are new) and do the Eden Group equivalent level if you are unable to reach a level requirement in the Leveling Guide (e.g. you are at lvl 79 and not 85 to participate in gramps, you would see which eden quests are 70-79 and do that to level up to 85)
The below guide is assuming you have at least read and understood the [https://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Leveling_Guide levelling guide] and how [https://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Eden_Group eden quests work] and [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Eden_Group_Equipments_Quests eden equipment quests]

=== How to Level ===
Soul Strike for Zombies/ Skeletons in Payon Dungeon
Soul Strike/ Fire Wall/ Fire Bolt if you choose to go into Anthell (Fire Wall should be maxed if you want to use it for the fastest cast time)
Soul Strike single targets, Fire wall for mobs in Orc Dungeon
Soul Strike will be great levelling in Glast Heim St. Abbey and Fire Wall will keep any mobs off you
After 85 I tend to do gramps, and just wait for the Cooldown of 4 hours. If you want to grind I would recommend checking out [https://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Mayo's_Warlock_Guide#Leveling Mayo's Leveling Guide of this Warlock Section]
Once you get proper Sorcerer skills:
*Warmer if you need HP and you dont have any priests
*Soul Exchange if DPS needs SP
*Vacuum Mobs for easier AoE
*Precast Memorize on downtime moments
*AoE when you see fit
*Use Striking on party members that use Physical damaging skills
