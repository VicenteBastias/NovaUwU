=== ASPD (Vulcan Arm) Robo ===
=== Hybrid Axe Robo ===
----
----
You may want to try maximizing ASPD so that you can spam Knuckle Boost more, but as your ASPD increase, Vulcan Arm's dps might actually start to catch up. In that case, you can actually try to use Vulcan Arm as your main dps for its 3x3 AoE and novelty factor.
This build takes advantage of the fact that you can still use the Axe skills while mounted on Mado. The most viable use for this build would be in PvP/WoE, as '''Axe Boomerang''' is still your most powerful PvP dps with '''+6/+9 Vellum Guillotine'''. It also allows you to continue killing people after you use '''Self-Destruct'''.  
 
One of the biggest (and probably only) advantage of Vulcan Arm is that it's the only dps build that can utilize the '''Kick Step Card combo (no Fuel Madogear)''', since Lockstep Card (Arms Cannon) does not make sense since it requires Reinforced Parts - Plate that has no slot.
