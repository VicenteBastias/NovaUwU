'''Game Play'''
From most threatening to least
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3213/seyren-windsor Seyren Windsor]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE Earthquake]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Ignition_Break Ignition Break]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Dragon_Breath Dragon Breath]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_CRITICALWOUND Critical Wounds]</span> >  <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Clashing_Spiral Clashing Spiral]</span> > Max Pain
Back up, support from a <span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/NovaExt /circle2]</span> distance, Safety Wall on the player attacked, Praefatio, Pneuma if there is no Land Protector. The tricky thing with Seyren is staying on the Land Protector yet not too close to get hit with the Earthquake. Ignition Break only creates a splash effect of death if it comes into contact with the ground. If your Sorcerer does not nullify this stay back for all the skills or Pneuma the Dragon Breath. Seyren Windsor uses Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) when he remains at 10% HP too long you can notice the difference from the cast bar comparison of Dragon Breath 0.5 second versus Max Pain's 1 second. He normally does not get the chance to cast it, however.
*Kathryne Keyron Earth Strain > Wide Freeze > Frost Nova (Does not work on Land Protector even if the animation shows) > Wide Curse > 3x3 hole created inside the Land Protector > Jupitel Thunder > Fire Wall
Stand 2 cells inward of the Land Protector (edges don't offer protection), if your equipment is divested from Earth Strain re-logging will remove the debuff or Full Chemical Protection from a Genetic will prevent it prior to the attack. > Assumptio key members to reduce as much magical damage as you can > Lauda Agnus the Wide Freeze/Frost Nova. Clementia the Wide Curse, get on the Land Protector and don't stand in the 3x3 hole created via Gabnbantein. Don't run head first into the Fire Wall over and over again, use /m2e settings to see what cells are affected by Fire Wall. Sometimes the 3x3 space created from Ganbantein is perfect for a Pneuma to dodge a Max Pain.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3228/celia-alde Celia Alde]</span> Dispell > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESTONE Wide Stone]</span> > Psychic Wave > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESOULDRAIN Souless Defeat]</span>
Prepare to Sacrement your Sura and throw Assumptio on them to speed up <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Mental_Strength Steel Body]</span>'s 2.5 seconds fixed cast time and survive the following <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Psychic_Wave Psychic Wave]</span>. Monsters have "cast delays" found on Divine-Pride before using another skill, so if Celia Alde opens up with a Psychic Wave or Sight expect Dispell seconds later.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3231/alphoccio-basil Alphoccio Basil]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDEFREEZE Wide Freeze]</span> > Lord of Vermilion > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_SLOWCAST Slow Cast]</span>
Lauda Agnus the Wide Freeze, Assumptio people off the Land Protector and are about to say hello to Lord of Vermilion. Standing only one cell inside of a Land Protector grants no benefits against any ground targeted skills.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3209/magaleta-sorin Magaleta Sorin]</span> Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) > Pneuma
Max Pain can be dodged by Pneuma if you are four cells away or Safety Wall if you 3 cells in. Magaleta Sorin can cast Pneuma on herself however you can cast Safety Wall in the 3x3 zone around Magaleta Sorin to prevent this as they can't overlap. Helps your Sorcerer (Land Protector) or Genetic (Crazy Weed) having to deal with it. Pneuma outside of the Land Protector and predict your Sorcerer's game play style to stay out of the range of them cancelling your Pneuma, use Redemptio preemptively if you see Magaleta Sorin A) Moving and being attacked B) Not directly next to someone but rather hit locked 1 and a half cells off players C) Your party composition consistently triggers it. To recover from Redemptio use an Ancillia stored to recast Redemptio right away as an inexperienced party will still attack whilst Max Pain's duration is persisting. Or move into a fresh Pneuma as your 10 seconds might be ending soon (<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/NovaExt /m2e setting]</span> helps however it is labelled under AC_SHOWER instead of Pneuma within MiniMiniEffects of your NovaEXT settings).
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3208/eremes-guille Eremes Guille]</span> Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) can be dodged by Pneuma if you are four cells away or Safety Wall if you 3 cells in. > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Soul_Destroyer Soul Destroyer]</span> > Sonic Blow > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Poisonous_Smoke Poisonous Smoke]</span> wait for the Land Protector and leave the area of effect.
Aspersio the DPS you discussed to Aspersio in the planning stages, Pneuma outside of the Land Protector but still within supporting distance (usually /circle2) in case Max Pain triggers (30% HP remaining), Safety Wall allies from Sonic Blow.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3226/randel-lawrence Randel Lawrence]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Smite Smite]</span> > Grand Cross
Arch Bishops have access to <span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Mora_Equipment#Arch_Bishop Robe of Affection]</span> which grants Holy Armor to entirely nullify Grand Cross along with Safety Wall for <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Smite Smite]</span> which runs the risk that any of your teammates will be knocked into more monsters and might accidentally trigger Max Pain.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3227/flamel-emule Flamel Emule]</span> - <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESLEEP Sleep]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESTUN Stun]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Bomb Demonstration]</span>
Lauda Ramus for Sleep and Stun. Pneuma the Demonstration cell to prevent some weapons from breaking.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3211/shecil-damon Shecil Damon]</span> Wide Curse > Arrow Storm > Arrow Repel
Stand on the Land Protector or throw up that Pneuma for the potential Arrow Storm if your Pneuma does not interfere. Prepare that Clementia cast as she uses Wide Curse often enough.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3229/chen-liu Chen Liu]</span> Pneuma > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_CHANGEUNDEAD Change Undead]</span> > Decrease AGI > Cursed Circle > Asura Strike
You can Safety Wall around Chen or on your tank to prevent Chen Liu from casting Pneuma on his cell. If he uses Change Undead, you will notice the person's Clementia and Canto Candidus icons gone from their name, yet their Sacrament remains. They will have a green hue on them which you have to @refresh (bind that to an ALT+M key of choice and you can change those in ESC> Shortcuts> Macros as well! The ~ is a popular one to bind) in this state they can not be healed however if it's safe you can use Clearance to remove the debuff. Cursed Circle and Asura strike I found using Safety Wall good enough to protect folks as there is a small delay after Cursed Circle in which Chen Liu follows up with Asura Strike.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3212/harword-alt-eisen Howard Alt Eisen]</span> Break Helm > Break Armor > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE Earthquake]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Lava_Flow Magma Eruption]</span>
Unless you're the tank or have a nice Genetic try to avoid getting into melee range for him to break your armors, an earthquake is avoided with proper distancing or having [https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE enough party members] to split the damage. Magma Eruption is canceled out with Land Protector.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3230/gertie-wie Gertie Wie]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_PULSESTRIKE Pulse Strike]</span> > Full Divestment
Aspersio the discussed DPS at the creation phase of the party. Pulse Strike can be dodged by Pneuma if you are four cells away or Safety Wall if you 3 cells in. Remember to @refresh to check if character sprites are genuinely at the location you view them as (position lag), or they are just slightly out of your line of sight. Full Divestment will be removed if you relog or ask for a Full Chemical Protection to remove the ailment.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3232/trentini Trentini]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Meteor_Storm Meteor Storm]</span>
