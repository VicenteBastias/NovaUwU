=== '''Mechanic Level 175''' ===
CONGRATS!!! You did it!. You are now max level, now what?
'''Farming Zeny and Items'''
Always remember to bring:
* 1x Thanatos Axe
* 200+ Fly Wing, or Your Creamy Card.
* And if Needed Enchanting Scrolls to change the element of your Weapon.
Always remember to:
* Set your @autoloot
* Set your @alootid +theitemyouwant
* Remember you can make lists and save them, for example you could have an aloot list saved for farming at Geffenia, one for Farming Oridecon in Glast Heim, etc etc.
* Remember to be efficient, don't waste SP killing every single mob you can, kill single monsters. If you are surrounded Hit Magma Eruption + Axe Tornado. Or just fly wing away.
'''Hunting MvP'''
Remember to bring:
* Highest Damage Axe (Kingbird Doom Slayer)
* Emergency Pots/Berry
* And if Needed Enchanting scrolls to change the element of your Weapon.
Remember to:
* Set your @autoloot
* Clear the mvps minions with Magma Eruption + Axe Tornado
* Research what's the best element to hit the MvP with.
* BE QUICK!!! MvPs are free for all, and you are not the only one that wants them.
