'''What about Dorams?'''
'''What about Dorams?'''


The cat-like [[Doram]] race only has one job class, which is Summoner. They can reach 175/50, they do not have a prior job class, do not job change into anything else, do not have alternative job class, and do not transcend. This may be subject to change, as Doram race is a very recent addition to kRO, and is still in beta stage in NovaRO. You can help improve the Doram race by reporting bugs related to them and their skills [http://novaragnarok.com/forum/topic/1301-known-bugserrors-doram/ in this forum topic].
You can learn more about them on the [[Doram]] wiki page.


'''Why not customize Taekwon class so they can reach Lv 175 as well?'''
'''Why not customize Taekwon class so they can reach Lv 175 as well?'''


Because their 3rd class, namely Star Emperor and Soul Reaper, [http://ro.gnjoy.com/news/devnote/View.asp?category=1&seq=3376048 are still under development in kRO], and customizing the Taekwon class may result in negative consequences that could cause irreversible damage. Translations of their skills and discussions are [https://www.novaragnarok.com/forum/topic/2038-new-taekwon-branch-info/ available in the forums].
Because their 3rd class, namely Star Emperor and Soul Reaper, are already implemented! You can check their release notes by clicking [https://www.novaragnarok.com/forum/news/soul-reaper-star-emperor-r209/ here].
