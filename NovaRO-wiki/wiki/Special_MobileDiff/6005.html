====Battlestats====
[[File:Battlestats.png|thumb|right|Battlestats Message in Chat Log]]
NovaRO has a unique convenience [[Player Command]] called <code>@Battlestats</code>. You can use this command while in-game to get a summary of your character's substats, including Offensive and Defensive capability; Attack Speed calculation; Accuracy, Flee, and Critical Rates; Critical Damage; Cast Time and Cast Delay; as well as bonuses and penalties related to each of the aforementioned substats.
