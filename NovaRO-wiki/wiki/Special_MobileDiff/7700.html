== Overview ==
== Overview ==
Brief introduction about yourself, the class and guide goes here.  
The way a Rune Knight differs from the Pre-Renewal Lord Knight is more than just one, this meaning that you can actually go for three different build paths, all viable and efficient at certain areas. You enjoyed going full on attack speed and bashing on monsters or other players?, then AGI RK is for you. Do you think a Rune Knight and his Dragon pal sound more like a magical duo? Then Dragon Breath is the way to go! Do you want to surprise players with a heavy bash on their head which will most likely just kill everyone? Then Crush Strike is for you! 
 
My name is Tachi and I'd like to welcome you to the exhausting but marvelous world of playing with your dragon.
== Stats ==
== Stats ==
Summary of the section
First of all, the stats for every build are ridiculously different from each other and I'd like to start up with the more common, WoE and PvE Oriented one: The Dragon Breath or Magical Burping Fire or Ice Dragon.
*'''STR''' - Notes, what's this Stat used for, recommended amounts
As the name of the skill suggests you need to have your Dragon with you at all times, which means there is a certain class that is your worst enemy: the Shadow Chasers. Hence, you will need to bulk up in some Masquerade resistances.
*'''AGI''' - Notes
*'''STR''' - 40 - 60 - More weight limit! Meaning that you can free more weight up to have a better chance to resist Masquerades.
*'''VIT''' - Notes
*'''AGI''' - 60 - 80 - Masquerade Resistance and Attack Speed for less animation delay between casts.
*'''INT''' - Notes
*'''VIT''' - 100 - 120 - This can vary depending on the gears you have available and if you'd like to stack on HP or resistances.
*'''DEX''' - Notes
*'''INT''' - 100 - 120 - Same as above. My advice is go as close to 120 as you can as you get a good increase in SP and therefore more damage (explained later!)
*'''LUK''' - Notes
*'''DEX''' - 120 - For Temporal Boots of Dexterity Fixed Casting decrease and percentage damage.
*'''LUK''' - 1 - 10 - Nowhere else to put points.
