== Introduction to the Class ==
If you want to tank monsters while doing damage and making your allies stronger all at once, you will probably want to be a mighty Rune Knight! They are great for both PvP and PvM!
The Rune Knight or RK usually have high HP, low-medium SP pool and medium-high damage, depending on how you’ll build it. Also, his damage can be both magical (Dragon Breath) and physical (Spear/Sword Skills/auto attacks).
There are some common builds for Rune Knights, such as Dragon Breath (DB) Build and ASPD Frenzy build which I will explain in this PvM/4Fun Guide.
Other builds like Hundred Spear build and Ignition Break build are also viable.
== Stats ==
There are 6 stats and each of them are important to the RK, some more important than the others, depending on your build.
STR – Highly important to ASPD builds and Spear/Sword skills damage since for each 1 point spent here you will have +1 atk, also +30 weight capacity per point.
AGI – Important for increasing ASPD and lowering the animation delay (DB animation delay for instance). Also, some flee can be useful sometimes.
VIT – Important for every kind of RK as it will increase your defense and max HP, as well as increasing the HP recovered from potions, 100 points = Stun immunity. (Extremely important for DB builds).
INT – Increases SP pool as well as decreases Variable Cast Time. (Again, DB builds love it).
DEX – Increases HIT (if you are going to MvP you’ll need this) and decreases Variable Cast Time with double the efficiency of the INT status.
LUK – Luck decreases the effect of various negative status on the player, gives +1 atk for every 3 points, Perfect Dodge for every 10 points, some critical resistance, hit, flee rate and finally Critical Rate (0.3% per point). (So, yeah, all that stuff).
== Important Skills ==
=== Swordsman ===
As a swordsman, you’ll probably want some skills for leveling and others for End Game purposes.
==== Leveling Skills ====
[[File:Bash.png]] '''Bash''' - Great single target skill, probably one of the first skills you’ll level.
[[File:RK MAGN.png]] '''Magnum Break''' - Other great skill for leveling, maybe even better than bash because it’s AoE and do fire damage (especially awesome for orc dungeon leveling).
Since you’ll have much stronger skills in the highest levels both Magnum Break and Bash will be overtaken. So, don’t expect to be using those skills too much.
==== End Game Usable Skills and Passives ====
[[File:RG ENDU.png]] '''Endure''' - A little bit of magic defense and you’ll not be stopped by 7 monster attacks, pretty nice if you ask me, just learn how to use it effectively.
[[File:RG INCR.png]] '''Increase HP Recovery''' – Increases HP recovery and healing items effectiveness.
[[File:RK PROV.png]] '''Provoke''' - Great for mobbing non-aggro monsters, also reduces their defenses.
[[File:RK TSWORD.png]] '''Two-Handed Mastery''' – If going to ASPD Two-Handed Sword build.
