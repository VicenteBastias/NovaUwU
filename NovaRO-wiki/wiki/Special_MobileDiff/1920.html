=== Monster Hunting ===
The Monster Hunter instance (MH) is a custom feature available only in NovaRO. It is a PvM instance where players can gather into a party of up to 5 people, and choose a specific monster to hunt. All of the monsters are extremely strong, so that it requires strategy, coordination, and high DPS to be able to kill them. These monsters drop special loots, which can be forged into Shadow Equipment for a small boost of stats, or into Monster Coupons which can be exchanged for MH-exclusive goods.
The Monster Hunter instance (MH) is a custom feature available only in NovaRO. It is a PvM instance where players can gather into a party of up to 5 people, and choose a specific monster to hunt. All of the monsters are extremely strong, so that it requires strategy, coordination, and high DPS to be able to kill them. These monsters drop special loots, which can be forged into Shadow Equipment for a small boost of stats, or into Monster Coupons which can be exchanged for MH-exclusive goods.
== Equipment ==
<b>Option #0:</b> Always try to [http://novaragnarok.com/?module=voteforpoints Vote For Points]! By voting, you can obtain (among others) a [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/22014/enhanced-variant-shoes-1 Enchanted Variant Shoes(1)] or a [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/2576/heroic-backpack-1 Heroic Backpack(1)].


<b>Option #1:</b> Check the market. (Type: @go market). On higher levels you should start looking around there as players sell both lower and higher tier equipment.
<b>Option #2:</b> Mora sets. Doing [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Mora_Village Mora Quests] reward you with items with set bonuses. You can also put [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Mora_Enchants Mora Enchants] on it.
<b>Option #3:</b> [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Malangdo_Enchants Malangdo Island] offers a wide variety of enchantable weapons. To obtain Coins, do some [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Malangdo Malangdo Quests].
<b>Option #4:</b> By doing [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Eclage_Daily_Quests Eclage Daily Quests], you can obtain [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Eclage#Eclage_Glove Stat Gloves].
<b>Option #5:</b> Above level 150, we have a nice place called [http://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Monster_Hunter Monster Hunter], where you can get costume pieces with stats.
<b>Option #6:</b> One of our [http://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Custom_Headgear_Quests Headgears] can offer you a useful alternative of your usual ones.
<b>Option #7:</b> Take a look at our Cash Shop. It has a wide variety of headgears and a few other items you might find useful.
<b>Option #8:</b> Use databases and look up some items by yourself. We recommend you to use [http://www.divine-pride.net/ Divine Pride], as it is the most up-to-date renewal database right now.
