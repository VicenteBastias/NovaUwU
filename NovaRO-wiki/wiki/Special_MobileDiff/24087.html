The '''Arch Bishop''' (AB) is a 3rd job class [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Introduction_to_Renewal introduced in Renewal]. Typically built as support roles in parties and capable of branching out to tanking, applying debuffs, giving up skill points in support to place into Holy element magic damage on enemies. Their potential for filling multiple roles is reflected on the range of weapon types they can equip, each with their own benefits and drawbacks for different builds. Please refer to the Equipment section for more details. To summarize;


* '''Mace''': Adoramus builds can opt for a [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Mora_Equipment#Arch_Bishop Judgement set] or the [https://www.novaragnarok.com/forum/news/patch-notes-r222/ recent] mixture of a the [https://www.novaragnarok.com/?module=vending&action=item&id=4685 Margaretha Sorin Card] found inside [[Tomb of the Fallen]].
* '''Rods''': Can be used for Full Supports ([https://irowiki.org/wiki/Rod reliable being naturally unbreakable]), [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Endless_Tower Holy Stick (Floor 100)] / [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Gold_Coins#Master_of_Coin Amistr Beret GC] Adoramus/Magnus Exorcismus/Judex Builds. Most players prefer one-handed over two-handed Staffs because a Shield slot is helpful for staying alive through size/elemental resistances or giving [https://www.novaragnarok.com/?module=vending&action=item&id=2162 Odin's Power] to damage dealers.
* '''Books''': Magnus Exorcismus and Judex potential builds perhaps and Duple Light variations gain [https://irowiki.org/wiki/Arch_Bishop#ASPD ASPD] using a book class weapons. Can be shared with [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Seandh%27s_Sorcerer_Guide#Equipment Sorcerer] and [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Colours%27_Guide_To_Star_Emperor#Equipment Star Emperor] classes.
* '''Knuckle''': [https://irowiki.org/wiki/Size Poor size modifiers] and a [https://irowiki.org/wiki/Arch_Bishop#ASPD high ASPD] penalty on Arch Bishops. Note: Knuckles don't show sprites on Arch Bishops.

== Builds ==
=== Full Support ===
Healer and Full Support builds are the most popular Arch Bishop build in party play and organized PvP. They utilize their healing and support abilities to assist their teammates in battle by providing various buffs and removing harmful status alignments. While support Arch Bishops are unable to deal efficient damage due to a lack of skill points to place into the Full Support skills and Adoramus.
Once Full Supports have their healing situation figured out they can optimize for significant healing 20k - 40k healing values or opt for off-tank support which is capable of giving the tank a bit of breathing room to set up again if things go sour or an unexpected monster attacks your casters and damage line. Both are capable of eliminating as much fixed/variable cast time they can fit into their builds. Through [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Tomb_of_the_Fallen#Enchants here] and [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Temporal_Boots#Upgrades here]. Both builds should, however, using skills such as Safety Wall and Kyrie Eleison to bolster their future and therefore the future of others. More information on SP management for higher healing and skills which protect found below.
=== Exorcist ===
Exorcist Arch Bishops are the strongest Holy magic casters in the world of Ragnarok Online. They utilize skills such as {{Skill List |id=79 |skill=Magnus Exorcismus |simple=yes}},{{Skill List |id=2038 |skill=Judex |simple=yes}} and {{Skill List |id=2040 |skill=Adoramus |simple=yes}} to deal powerful Holy magic damage, which is highly effective against Undead and Shadow element monsters, and Demon race monsters. Exorcist Bishops are also capable of supporting their teammates, however, due to their focus on dealing damage, they become rather fragile in battle compared to their Healer counterparts. Thanks to [https://www.novaragnarok.com/forum/news/arch-bishop-skill-update-r217/ Patch 217] Adoramus is now an AoE skill and is very easy to farm basic monsters and do simply dailies such as [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Ghost_Palace Ghost Palace], [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Geffen_Magic_Tournament Geffen Magic Tournament] to an extent and [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Sara%27s_Memory Sara's Memories] with a simple [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Mora_Equipment#Arch_Bishop Judgement Set] especially when paired with Vituperatum, updated Expiatio and an excellent old-fashioned Oratio.
=== Battle Bishops ===
Battle Arch Bishops utilize their Maces and Books to deal melee physical and mixed damage to their enemies. Their skill {{Skill List |id=65 |skill=Mace Mastery |simple=yes}} and {{Skill List |id=2054 |skill=Duple Light |simple=yes}}, along with a wide variety of auto-cast equipment, allows them to deal both physical and magic damage to defeat their enemies. Battle Bishops, due to their focus on high [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Attack_Speed ASPD] and damage, tend to be rather squishy without Speed of Light, [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Prize_of_Hero Hero Judgement Shawl], Safety Wall and various other methods of sustain. This path does not excel in being the only Arch Bishop in the party; therefore this build is a "fun" build, suitable for more experienced players.

'''STR (Strength)''': Increases Weight Limit and slightly reduces the duration of Sorcerers’ Vacuum Extreme debuff. Healer/Full Support and Exorcist/Adoramus builds should consider using {{Market |id=7776 Gym Pass}} to increase Weight Limit. Important for Battle Bishop build as it increases Status ATK and Weapon ATK when equipping melee weapons (e.g. Mace, Book, Knuckle).
'''AGI (Agility)''': A defensive stat for Healer/Support build, AGI increases resistance against Sleep, Bleeding, and reduces the duration of Shadow Chasers’ Masquerade debuff. Essential for Battle Bishop build as AGI increases ASPD and Flee.
'''INT (Intelligence)''': The most important stat for Healer/Support and Exorcist/Adoramus builds, INT directly affects healing skills, MATK, Variable Cast Time (VCT), and the success rates of Turn Undead skill. It also increases resistance against some debuffs, and reduces the duration of some debuffs (e.g. Sleep, Silence, Oblivion Curse).
'''VIT (Vitality)''': Increases MaxHP, Soft DEF, and Soft MDEF. Also increases resistance against some debuffs (e.g. Stun, Poison, Burning).
'''DEX (Dexterity)''': Reduces VCT and allows you to cast skils faster, making this an important skill for Exorcist/Adoramus and Healer/Support builds. For Battle Bishops this stat is necessary to increase Hit.
'''LUK (Luck)''': Increases the instant-kill chance of Turn Undead skill. Also somewhat important for Healer/Support build as LUK slightly increases resistance against some debuffs and reduces the duration of debuffs (e.g. Frozen, Sleep, Stone Curse, Genetic’s Mandragora Howling). For Battle Bishops, this stat is necessary for increasing Critical Hit Rate and Perfect Dodge.

== Equipment ==
Arch Bishops have access to a wide variety of equipment which might make gearing them up confusing for newer players. The key is to select a build first then gear up for that particular build. Weapon is an important factor in the effectiveness of a build, and different weapons suit different builds. The following section will discuss the benefits and drawbacks of weapon types that ABs can equip.
'''Staffs, Rods, Wands'''
<br>
Staffs, whether one-handed or two-handed, are usually equipped by '''Full Support''' and '''Exorcist''' builds due to most Staffs having high weapon MATK bonus (such as {{Market |id=1680 Crimson Rod}}, {{Market |id=1671 Evil Slayer Vanquisher Staff}}), some INT and DEX bonus ({{Market |id=1600 Vicious Mind Rod}} from [[Sky Fortress]]), and many staffs having '''Heal effectiveness bonus''' ({{Market |id=1669 Thanatos Staff}} from [[Ghost Palace]]). Additionally, Staffs are <u>naturally unbreakable in battle</u>.
All of Exorcist AB's skills scale off MATK, making Staffs the best weapon for '''increasing Magic Damage'''. Staffs apply a high ASPD penalty on AB and they are not suitable for spamming skills with ASPD-based cooldown. For example, an AB wearing a staff will not be able to spam skills such as Holy Light or Lex Aeterna as fast and as frequently as an AB that wears a Book or a Mace.
On the other hand, Staffs are excellent for builds that emphasize minimizing or completely '''eliminating Variable Cast Time'''. For example, the 3-slotted {{Market |id=26107 Elder Staff}} can offer a nice balance between increasing Heal effectiveness and minimizing Variable Cast Time by putting {{Market |id=4922 Essences of Evil DEX 3}} in it (see [[Temple of the Demon God]]). It's also a relatively cheaper and less RNG alternative to Vicious Mind/VM Rod with INT/DEX enchants.
Some staffs purchasable with [[Valor Badges]] from [[Battlegrounds]] have unique properties. In terms of '''minimizing After Cast Delay/ACD''' (or global skill cooldown), {{Item List2 |id=1635 |item=Speedy Recovery Wand}} is one of the best options available, aside from VM Rod with ACD reduction enchant. High refine {{Item List2 |id=1641 |item=Glorious Cure Wand}} is also a great option in terms of '''Heal effectiveness''' and '''SP efficiency''' when compared to high refine {{Market |id=1659 Light of Recovery}}.
Two-handed Staffs usually have higher MATK bonus but the trade-off is giving up on Shield and using Shield Cards. Considering that Full Support builds generally need multiple Shields for defensive purposes, and Exorcist builds need access to offensive Shield such as {{Market |id=2189 Mad Bunny}} to use MDEF-piercing card combos (e.g. {{Market |id=4626 Big Ben Card}} + {{Market |id=4628 Neo Punk Card}}), few end-game builds would use two-handed Staff. A lot of Staff weapons that ABs can wear can also be equipped by Warlocks, Sorcerers, Soul Linkers (and possibly Soul Reapers).
'''Books'''
<br>
Books usually offer less MATK than Staffs but allow ABs to reach high ASPD much easier than any other weapon types they can equip, and they are <u>naturally unbreakable in battle</u>. These qualities make Book type weapon an excellent choice for spamming skills such as Lex Aeterna or Judex. High refine {{Market |id=28604 Crimson Bible}} and {{Market |id=28605 Vicious Mind Book}} in particular are suitable for builds emphasizing '''high ASPD and MATK''' to utilize soul-linked '''Holy Light''', '''Judex''', '''Duple Light''', and '''Autocast''' builds.
Some Book type weapons purchasable with [[Valor Badges]] from [[Battlegrounds]] are potentially good for tackling Demi Human enemies in instance dungeons such as [[Geffen Magic Tournament]]. Some of these Books include {{Item List2 |id=1575 |item=Valorous Battle Strategy Book,}} {{Item List2 |id=1576 |item=Glorious Tablet,}} and {{Item List2 |id=1577 |item=Glorious Apocalypse.}} High refine {{Market |id=1565 Ledger of Death}} with its additional ASPD-boosting effect and chance to inflict Coma on Demi Human enemies is also a good alternative to consider. There are no [4] slotted book weapons and the closest you can get is: [https://www.novaragnarok.com/?module=vending&action=item&id=1550 Book [3]]
The drawback of Book type weapon is that it does full damage on Small and Medium enemies but only 50% damage on Large enemies. Similar to Knuckle type weapon's poor size modifiers, Book type weapon require Blacksmith class buff {{Skill List |id=112 |skill=Weapon Perfection |simple=yes}} to help it realize its true potential. Most Books can also be equipped by Sorcerers and Star Gladiators (and possibly Star Emperors).
'''Maces'''
<br>
Mace type weapons are commonly used by '''Autocast and melee physical builds''' that focus on '''high ASPD, ATK, Critical rate, and/or Critical damage'''. Some examples include {{Market |id=16033 Robot's Mechanical Arm}} or {{Market |id=1548 Veteran Hammer}} for Critical build; {{Market |id=16040 Crimson Mace}} or {{Market |id=16041 Vicious Mind Mace}} for high ATK; {{Market |id=16029 Noble Cross}} from [[Horror Toy Factory]] for Autocast build; {{Item List2 |id=1546 |item=Glorious Morning Star}} against Demi Human enemies. It has favorable size modifiers, inflicting full damage on Medium and Large enemies, 75% damage on Small enemies, and it is <u>naturally unbreakable in battle</u>. These qualities make it suitable for fighting against problematic enemies capable of breaking gears.
Some Mace type weapons have unique properties that can be used for defensive or debuffing purposes. For example, {{Market |id=1525 Long Mace}} offers additional resistance against physical long range damage. High refine {{Market |id=1541 Nemesis}} has a chance to reduce the Hard Defense of all Undead property and Demon race enemies on screen, including Boss protocol enemies. Similarly, high refine {{Market |id=16017 Bloody Cross}} has a chance to apply Burning debuff that can quickly kill any non-Boss protocol enemies with high HP.
While some Maces like {{Market |id=16028 Thanatos Hammer}} and {{Market |id=16018 Empowered Mace of Judgement}} have inherent weapon MATK and can receive MATK boosting enchants, they usually have less MATK than Staffs and Books. Most Mace type weapons can be equipped by numerous other classes including Sura, Rune Knight, Royal Guard, Mechanic, and Genetic.
'''Knuckles'''
<br>
Although there are some Knuckle type weapons that ABs can equip (e.g. {{Market |id=1839 Crimson Knuckle}}, {{Market |id=1800 Vicious Mind Knuckle}}, {{Item List2 |id=1826 |item=Glorious Claw}}, {{Item List2 |id=1823 |item=Valorous Battle Fist}}), their size modifiers are unfavorable. Knuckles inflict full damage on Small enemies, 75% on Medium, 50% on Large, and ABs receive relatively high ASPD penalty from wearing Knuckles. These factors make Knuckle type weapon unpopular for both ABs and Suras, the only two classes that can wear them.

Do no harm, do know harm.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3213/seyren-windsor Seyren Windsor]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE Earthquake]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Ignition_Break Ignition Break]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Dragon_Breath Dragon Breath]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_CRITICALWOUND Critical Wounds]</span> >  <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Clashing_Spiral Clashing Spiral]</span> > Max Pain
Back up, support from a <span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/NovaExt /circle2]</span> distance, Safety Wall on the player attacked, Praefatio, Pneuma if there is no Land Protector. The tricky thing with Seyren is staying on the Land Protector yet not too close to get hit with the Earthquake. Ignition Break only creates a splash effect of death if it comes into contact with the ground. If your Sorcerer does not nullify this stay back for all the skills or Pneuma the Dragon Breath. Seyren Windsor uses Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) when he remains at 10% HP too long you can notice the difference from the cast bar comparison of Dragon Breath 0.5 second versus Max Pain's 1 second. He normally does not get the chance to cast it, however.
*Kathryne Keyron Earth Strain > Wide Freeze > Frost Nova (Does not work on Land Protector even if the animation shows) > Wide Curse > 3x3 hole created inside the Land Protector > Jupitel Thunder > Fire Wall
Stand 2 cells inward of the Land Protector (edges don't offer protection), if your equipment is divested from Earth Strain re-logging will remove the debuff or Full Chemical Protection from a Genetic will prevent it prior to the attack. > Assumptio key members to reduce as much magical damage as you can > Lauda Agnus the Wide Freeze/Frost Nova. Clementia the Wide Curse, get on the Land Protector and don't stand in the 3x3 hole created via Gabnbantein. Don't run head first into the Fire Wall over and over again, use /m2e settings to see what cells are affected by Fire Wall. Sometimes the 3x3 space created from Ganbantein is perfect for a Pneuma to dodge a Max Pain.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3228/celia-alde Celia Alde]</span> Dispell > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESTONE Wide Stone]</span> > Psychic Wave > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESOULDRAIN Souless Defeat]</span>
Prepare to Sacrement your Sura and throw Assumptio on them to speed up <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Mental_Strength Steel Body]</span>'s 2.5 seconds fixed cast time and survive the following <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Psychic_Wave Psychic Wave]</span>. Monsters have "cast delays" found on Divine-Pride before using another skill, so if Celia Alde opens up with a Psychic Wave or Sight expect Dispell seconds later.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3231/alphoccio-basil Alphoccio Basil]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDEFREEZE Wide Freeze]</span> > Lord of Vermilion > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_SLOWCAST Slow Cast]</span>
Lauda Agnus the Wide Freeze, Assumptio people off the Land Protector and are about to say hello to Lord of Vermilion. Standing only one cell inside of a Land Protector grants no benefits against any ground targeted skills.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3209/magaleta-sorin Magaleta Sorin]</span> Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) > Pneuma
Max Pain can be dodged by Pneuma if you are four cells away or Safety Wall if you 3 cells in. Magaleta Sorin can cast Pneuma on herself however you can cast Safety Wall in the 3x3 zone around Magaleta Sorin to prevent this as they can't overlap. Helps your Sorcerer (Land Protector) or Genetic (Crazy Weed) having to deal with it. Pneuma outside of the Land Protector and predict your Sorcerer's game play style to stay out of the range of them cancelling your Pneuma, use Redemptio preemptively if you see Magaleta Sorin A) Moving and being attacked B) Not directly next to someone but rather hit locked 1 and a half cells off players C) Your party composition consistently triggers it. To recover from Redemptio use an Ancillia stored to recast Redemptio right away as an inexperienced party will still attack whilst Max Pain's duration is persisting. Or move into a fresh Pneuma as your 10 seconds might be ending soon (<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/NovaExt /m2e setting]</span> helps however it is labelled under AC_SHOWER instead of Pneuma within MiniMiniEffects of your NovaEXT settings).
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3208/eremes-guille Eremes Guille]</span> Max Pain (Turns Dark Gold) can be dodged by Pneuma if you are four cells away or Safety Wall if you 3 cells in. > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Soul_Destroyer Soul Destroyer]</span> > Sonic Blow > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Poisonous_Smoke Poisonous Smoke]</span> wait for the Land Protector and leave the area of effect.
Aspersio the DPS you discussed to Aspersio in the planning stages, Pneuma outside of the Land Protector but still within supporting distance (usually /circle2) in case Max Pain triggers (30% HP remaining), Safety Wall allies from Sonic Blow.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3226/randel-lawrence Randel Lawrence]</span> <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Smite Smite]</span> > Grand Cross
Arch Bishops have access to <span class="plainlinks">[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Mora_Equipment#Arch_Bishop Robe of Affection]</span> which grants Holy Armor to entirely nullify Grand Cross along with Safety Wall for <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Smite Smite]</span> which runs the risk that any of your teammates will be knocked into more monsters and might accidentally trigger Max Pain.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3227/flamel-emule Flamel Emule]</span> - <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESLEEP Sleep]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_WIDESTUN Stun]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Bomb Demonstration]</span>
Lauda Ramus for Sleep and Stun. Pneuma the Demonstration cell to prevent some weapons from breaking.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3211/shecil-damon Shecil Damon]</span> Wide Curse > Arrow Storm > Arrow Repel
Stand on the Land Protector or throw up that Pneuma for the potential Arrow Storm if your Pneuma does not interfere. Prepare that Clementia cast as she uses Wide Curse often enough.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3229/chen-liu Chen Liu]</span> Pneuma > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_CHANGEUNDEAD Change Undead]</span> > Decrease AGI > Cursed Circle > Asura Strike
You can Safety Wall around Chen or on your tank to prevent Chen Liu from casting Pneuma on his cell. If he uses Change Undead, you will notice the person's Clementia and Canto Candidus icons gone from their name, yet their Sacrament remains. They will have a green hue on them which you have to @refresh (bind that to an ALT+M key of choice and you can change those in ESC> Shortcuts> Macros as well! The ~ is a popular one to bind) in this state they can not be healed however if it's safe you can use Clearance to remove the debuff. Cursed Circle and Asura strike I found using Safety Wall good enough to protect folks as there is a small delay after Cursed Circle in which Chen Liu follows up with Asura Strike.
*<span class="plainlinks">[https://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3212/harword-alt-eisen Howard Alt Eisen]</span> Break Helm > Break Armor > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE Earthquake]</span> > <span class="plainlinks">[https://irowiki.org/wiki/Lava_Flow Magma Eruption]</span>
Unless you're the tank or have a nice Genetic try to avoid getting into melee range for him to break your armors, an earthquake is avoided with proper distancing or having [https://irowiki.org/wiki/NPC_EARTHQUAKE enough party members] to split the damage. Magma Eruption is canceled out with Land Protector.
