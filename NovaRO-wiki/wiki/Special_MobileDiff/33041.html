== Rules ==
All server [[Rules]] apply to the War of Emperium.
''Main article: [[Siege Rules]]''
 
<br clear="both">
To create a more fun and engaging WoE environment, several rules are set in place. These can be changed by the administration as the situation demands for it.
*There is a maximum cap of 24 players per guild within a castle in WoE 1.
*Guild emblem's design must be at least 80% visible to other players.
*Do not steal another guild's name or guild emblem. Even parodies are not allowed.
*Alliances between guilds are not allowed.
**Guilds allied through the in-game system will be able to damage each other within the castles.
*Do not prevent players from entering a castle during WoE. This includes Bloody Branches at the entrance, Warp Portals, Ice Walls, etc.
*Your guild must have an unique emblem to participate in WoE.
*Win-Trading and rigging the outcome of the War of Emperium is not allowed.
*Do not purposefully target/harass only a specific guild. This does not apply to targeting only the defending guild or strategically targeting a guild to ensure an advantage to your guild's WoE performance.
 
=== Breaking WoE Rules ===
==== First offense ====
*Temporary hardware account ban for 2 weeks (2 WoEs).
==== Second offense ====
*Temporary hardware account ban for 1 month (4 WoEs).
==== Third offense ====
''This means the player know what he's doing and is doing so intentionally. This person does not care about punishment or the health of WoE on NovaRO at all.''
*'Cheater' ban.
**Current accounts permanently banned + hardware ban for 60 days. After 60 days pass, these players can appeal for another chance to play NovaRO on a fresh new account.
 
== Cheating ==
* Cheating is against the rules and will not be tolerated.
 
Due to a number of cases regarding players using cheats in WoE, we have decided to implement a more clearly defined set of consequences for cheaters
 
Player's caught cheating in Nova RO will have all of their related accounts permanently banned. In addition to this, they will also be '''hardware banned for 60 days'''.
That means, after 60 days pass, these players can attempt to play Nova RO once more by creating a new account.
 
* Players found trying to bypass the hardware ban before 60 days will have their punishment escalated.
 
* Players who return from their 60 day ban and are found once more to be cheating will be hit with a permanent hardware ban as well.
 
* Players may turn in their cheats to the GM team when they are banned, if they do so, the hardware ban will be reduced to 30 days instead of 60.
 
 
Note: these rules do not apply to cases involving botting, which will forever continue to be a permanent hardware ban.
