== Overview ==
*THIS GUIDE WILL NOT SHOWCASE STARTING GEARS
This guide is for those who like to try a PVE sura and enjoy how fun using the class is. I made this guide so that other classes will have the opportunity to do a 4 man party.
NOTE: To main sorcerer and sura pkayers, our time is now!
With this guide you will be able to play the DPS role in a OGH hard 4 man party
I will try to give you the cheapest and the best gear combinations as much as I can based from my experience.
You can choose between two builds which I am going to showcase with their PROS and CONS.
Also BIG THANKS to '''Munbalance''' for making the GOH build possible.

=== Stat Build ===
COMBO STATS
This build is the best I could come up with to maximize the damage of TIGER CANNON
STR = 120 (Get the bonus stats from TEMP STR boots), AGI = 100 (FASTER combo animation), VIT = 120 (Get as much vit stats as possible), INT = 0  (Get as low sp as possible), DEX = 90  (HIT CHANCE), LUK = 0
*DEAL TONS OF DAMAGE AGAINST NORMA and BOSS monsters.
*Heavily relies on heals from priest
GOH STATS
STR = 100 (more atk more damage), AGI = 0  (0 if you want to get instant cast without bragi), VIT = 100, INT = 99, DEX = 130 (130 for faster cast), LUK = 0
This build is good for duo OGH normal.
GOH STATS 2
STR = 100, AGI = 69, VIT = 100, INT = 99, DEX = 120 (bonus -0.5 cast time from Temp Dex Boots), LUK = 0
*TURNS YOU INTO A HUMAN DORAM
*Heavily relies on SP potions.

=== Builds ===
[[File:COMBO_build_skill.png]]
Doing the combo makes you deal a million damage just for tiger cannon. But doing the entire combo will give you 1.5 - 1.8 million total, numbers based on Amdarais Normal.
But how do you get this much damage? You are gonna have to chain your combos.
Here is my chain sequence. (MORE AGI and DEX the faster you cast the combos)
Rising Dragon > Dragon Combo > Fallen Empire > Tiger Cannon > Dragon Combo > Fallen Empire > GOH > Flash Combo > Danger soul collect > Repeat
'''Notice''': You will be spamming your sp pots mid combos.
[[File:GOH_build_skill.png]]
SECOND BUILD IS FOR GOH
This build allows you to access the DPS role for OGH HARD. With this build you will be able to 1 shot Raydric Archers in OGH hard, and deal 400k per GOH on Amdarais.
NOTE: 400k is without ORATIO or Insignia combo.
This build will depend on ur FCT of Dangerous Soul Collect, that is why the need for the Dex Boots.
You will deal 400k x 3 per 0.75 seconds on Amdarais.
ALSO, MOST IMPORTANTLY the pro sorcs will clear the normal monsters for you =). Hi to the pro sorcerers.
GOH > GOH > GOH > Dangerous Soul Collect > GOH GOH GOH > REPEAT
'''Notice''': FOR THIS BUILD YOU WILL ONLY NEED 1 MORA MANDARIN TO FILL YOUR SP POOL. 1 Mandarin per GOH.
