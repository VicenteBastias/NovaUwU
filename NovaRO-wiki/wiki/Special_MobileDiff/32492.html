This is a guide I've decided to write in order to give players an idea of what my opinion of an ideal WoE Shadow Chaser does. This guide will also go over ways to combat them. Shadow Chaser is an extremely powerful class, but equipped with the knowledge of how a Shadow Chaser functions and what their ultimate game plan is during WoE, you should be able to find ways to play around their tools.


== General Shadow Chaser Information ==
What a WoE Shadow Chaser is:
* A class that focuses on debuffing foes
* A class that focuses on supporting allies
* Immune to Dispell when linked by a Soul Linker
* Incredibly tanky and difficult to lock down
What a WoE Shadow Chaser is not:
* A damage dealing class
* A class that likes to stand still or in the back line
Merits of playing Shadow Chaser:
* You make your opponents angry
* You make your opponents sad
* It's incredibly satisfying to watch your foes walk around for 20 seconds after disabling all of their skills
Demerits of playing Shadow Chaser:
* Everybody hates you
* You will get targeted frequently
* Keeping track of who you've disabled and how long they have until they're back in action takes getting used to
* Everybody hates you

=== Notes on Gear Swapping ===
* Vellum Damascus could be a good option for additional support, but I don't recommend using it unless you have a very good connection and a precise ability to click key targets. Because it reduces DEF and MDEF to 1 for a few seconds, it could allow for a key tanky target to be taken down quickly. However, this requires a lot of coordination and is probably too small to make a big impact in the chaos of WoE.
* +12 Immune Shield is the only refine of Immune Shield that I would ever recommend using. Only swap to this when taking damage from a forced Neutral element damage, and only if you absolutely need to (i.e Curse Circle -> Asura Strike/Tiger Cannon).
* Hero Ring is the replacement for the old Zerom Orleans Glove. I almost never swap to this, but I keep it around for the +5 Dex in case I need to reach instant cast.
