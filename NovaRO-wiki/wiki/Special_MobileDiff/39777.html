That means you are able to farm Illusion Stones as much as you want on a single character, without having to create additional characters to bypass the cooldown.
 
To do so, talk to '''Osmundi'''. He can be found inside one of the following locations:
* Illusion of Moonlight dungeon {{NaviLink|map=pay_d03_i|x=155|y=45}}.
* Illusion of Vampire dungeon {{NaviLink|map=gef_dun01|x=130|y=221}}.
* Illusion of Frozen dungeon {{NaviLink|map=ice_d03_i|x=159|y=40}}.
* Illusion of Abyss dungeon {{NaviLink|map=alberta|x=224|y=30}}.
* Illusion of Teddy Bear dungeon {{NaviLink|map=ein_dun01|x=185|y=78}}.
* Illusion of Luanda dungeon {{NaviLink|map=com_d02_i|x=246|y=249}}.
* Illusion of Twins dungeon {{NaviLink|map=anthell02|x=170|y=190}}.
* Illusion of Labyrinth dungeon {{NaviLink|map=prt_maze01|x=94|y=24}}.
* Illusion of Underwater dungeon {{NaviLink|map=iz_d04_i|x=139|y=228}}.
 
 
They are separated in 5 distinct difficulty groups. Each reset will award you with a set amount of:
 
* Tier 1: [[Illusion of Moonlight]] and [[Illusion of Frozen]].
*: Reset Reward: {{Item List2|id=25271|item=Illusion Stone|num=10}} and a choice between {{Item List2|id=100391|item=Moonlight Refine Box}} and {{Item List2|id=9785|item=Frozen Refine Box}}
* Tier 2: [[Illusion of Vampire]] and [[Illusion of Abyss]]
*: Reset Reward: {{Item List2|id=25271|item=Illusion Stone|num=12}} and a choice between {{Item List2|id=100414|item=Vampire Refine Box}} and {{Item List2|id=100417|item=Abyss Refine Box}}
* Tier 3: [[Illusion of Teddy Bear]] and [[Illusion of Underwater|Illusion of Underwater - 1]]
*: Reset Reward: {{Item List2|id=25271|item=Illusion Stone|num=14}} and a choice between {{Item List2|id=100419|item=Teddy Bear Refine Box}} and {{Item List2|id=100425|item=Underwater Refine Box}}
* Tier 4: [[Illusion of Luanda]] and [[Illusion of Twins]]
*: Reset Reward: {{Item List2|id=25271|item=Illusion Stone|num=16}} and a choice between {{Item List2|id=100421|item=Luanda Refine Box}} and {{Item List2|id=100699|item=Twins Refine Box}}
* Tier 5: [[Illusion of Labyrinth]] and [[Illusion of Underwater|Illusion of Underwater - 2]]
*: Reset Reward: {{Item List2|id=25271|item=Illusion Stone|num=22}} and a choice between {{Item List2|id=100423|item=Labyrinth Refine Box}} and {{Item List2|id=100425|item=Underwater Refine Box}}

'''Once you complete the dailies of one of the Tiers above, you can reset them.''' After a reset, these quests will not give any experience until their original 24 hours cooldown pass.
 
'''Each Tier can be completed and reset separately''', but you cannot reset dailies from a single Illusion Dungeon without completing the others in their respective group.
