The '''Arch Bishop''' (AB) is a 3rd job class [https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Introduction_to_Renewal introduced in Renewal]. Typically built as support roles in parties and capable of branching out to tanking, applying debuffs, giving up skill points in support to place into Holy element magic damage on enemies. Their potential for filling multiple roles is reflected on the range of weapon types they can equip, each with their own benefits and drawbacks for different builds. Please refer to the Equipment section for more details. To summarize;
When Priest and High Priest heard of the Holy Grail's location only one returned. We hope to find the secrets of the Holy Grail to better humanity but we need a particular job to protect it while we run some experiments. We will call them Arch Bishops!
