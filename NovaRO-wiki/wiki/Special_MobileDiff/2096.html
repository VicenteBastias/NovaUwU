=== Monster Hunting ===
The Monster Hunter instance (MH) is a custom feature available only in NovaRO. It is a PvM instance where players can gather into a party of up to 5 people, and choose a specific monster to hunt. All of the monsters are extremely strong, so that it requires strategy, coordination, and high DPS to be able to kill them. These monsters drop special loots, which can be forged into Shadow Equipment for a small boost of stats, or into Monster Coupons which can be exchanged for MH-exclusive goods.
The [[Monster Hunter]] instance (MH) is a custom feature available only in NovaRO. It is a PvM instance where players can gather into a party of up to 5 people, and choose a specific monster to hunt.
