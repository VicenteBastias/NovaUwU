**Penetration shadow shield and accessories, Tempest shadow accessories.

**Mammoth shadow plate.

**Mutated White Knight and Mutated Khalitzburg Knight Cards.
**Void Mimic Card.
**Rekenber Senior Guard Card.

**[https://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Rudus_4 Rudus 4] Cards '''except MVP'''.
*Recently enabled costume stones:
**All Lapises.
**All Jades.

*{{Item List2|id=16021|item=Vellum Flail}} applies Critical Wounds instead of No Recovery at a lower chance.
*{{Item List2|id=28946|item=Purified Knight's Shield}}'s DEF and MDEF bypass enchants are disabled.
*[[Endeavor Tokens|Endeavor Token Shop]] consumables are disabled, aside from {{Item List2||id=34165|item=Gin Sacred Draught}}.
*All items '''not''' listed below have a 5-second cooldown:
**Mega White Potion, Mega Blue Potion, Violet Potion, WoE Blue Potion, BG Light Blue Potion, and their boxes, all Genetic Syrup Potions, Cursed Fragment, Butterfly Wing, Yggdrasil Seed, Ancilla, Pertz Runestone, Box of Gloom, Green Potion, Panacea, Aloevera and Warlock Spellbooks.

=== Cards ===
*{{Item List2|id=4171|item=Dark Priest}} effect only works with autoattackss.
*{{Item List2|id=4610|item=<nowiki>[MVP]</nowiki> Sarah Card}} will keep its old effect for Siege environments: If the user receives damage greater than their current maximum HP amount, this card will decrease the damage received by a large amount.
*{{Item List2|id=4522|item=Sropho Card}} effect only works with autoattacks.
*{{Item List2|id=4128|item=Golden Thief Bug Card}}’s magic damage immunity no longer grants status or dispel immunity.
* '''All [MVP] cards''' follow a tiering system. The card can be limited or allowed based on its Tier. All MVP cards have their respective Tier listed on their description.
**<u>Tier 0</u>
*::Cards in this tier are banned from any Siege game mode. This tier is generally reserved as a blacklist for cards that have not yet been obtained and/or implemented at the time they are classified as Tier 0.
**<u>Tier 1</u>
*::Cards in this tier are limited to '''3 per Guild''' in War of Emperium, and disabled in other Siege game modes.
**<u>Tier 2</u>
*::Cards in this tier are limited to '''5 per Guild''' in War of Emperium, and disabled in other Siege game modes.
**<u>Tier 3</u>
*::Cards in this tier are disabled in [[King of Emperium]] but have '''no use limitations''' in all other Siege game modes.
