<br>
Let me give you some PROS why I play as a DPS Sura in OGH.<br>
:*You only need an AB to finish the run under 30 minutes.<br>
:*You can counter all monsters in the instance including the maggots.<br>
:*You can use an alternate Minstrel on a different account to buff you during BOSS fights.<br>
:*You don’t get one shot.<br>
:*You can use the environment to your advantage when fighting mobs due to your high mobility.<br>
:*Insane damage.<br>
<br>
Some CONS.<br>
:*Very steep learning curve.<br>
:*Dependent on SP foods and Potions.<br>

=====How to Play=====
:::To be as efficient as possible when playing a class that uses its own HP as damage, here are some tips on how to do it.<br>
::::*Train yourself to not consume any SP potions when against normal monsters. We have a skill called {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb  |simple=yes}} which regenerates our SP by using our spheres.<br>
::How to:
:::{{Skill List |id=401 |skill=Dangerous Soul Collect  |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb  |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=401 |skill=Dangerous Soul Collect |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb |simple=yes}} <br>
::Also you can regenerate your spheres through getting hit or hitting monsters through the skill called {{Skill List |id=2346 |skill=Gentle Touch - Energy Gain|simple=yes}}<br>
::::*Learn to lure monsters into groups before using Flash Combo or Tiger Cannon Combo. That way the % chance of leeching HP and SP is really high.<br>
::::*Practice the combos. Go to the training dummies and practice your combos. When facing Root or Amdarais, try to relax and combo like you’re playing a piano. Set your rhythm and pacing.<br>
::::*Farm your HP potions. When in a situation where you find it hard to leech HP or you do not have an AB to heal you, use {{Item List |id=12027 |item=HP Giggling Box|simple=yes}}. These drop while farming with Wraith Cards equipped..<br>

=='''HOW TO PLAY SURA'''==
:::::For OGH Hard Duo or 4 Man: In this run you are going to have to use two builds which is the Combo and Goh. I have tested it and it would only cost you about 300-400 Grape Juice or less and 200 Mandarins. Your consumption will highly depend on your AB if he Lexes mobs.<br>
:::::::*For the first part, cleaning the mobs is going to be very very easy since you have combo Tiger Cannon and Flash Combo. Even the maggots are not a problem.<br>
:::::::*ROOT - Here you will have to position and your '''Minstrel'''  1 cell above and 1 cell to the left from the center NPC (Himmelmez) which will put him to the left cheek of Root. Use Body Relocate on the same cell as your Minstrel. Your AB and other party mates should position themselves beside the left pillar (West Side). If you run with a Sorcerer, let your Sorcerer cast LP on top of the support members so that the poison cloud won’t be a bother. Also, your Minstrel can cast Apple, Assassin and Frigg’s Song on you which gives you a huge damage boost..<br>
::::::GAMEPLAY:<br>
:::::::*For the AB, he should only cast heal, safety wall and refreshing Kyrie and nothing more. AB should not use Lex so as not to add more complications to his skill rotation may it be a skilled or newbie one. The Sura will not benefit from the Lex spam since we will be using combos. It is better to just spam Safety Wall and keep the Sura away from the maggots. Adding lex to the rotation will just end up killing the Sura.<br>
:::::::*For Sorcerer, if you happen to run 4 man let your sorcerer cast Fire Insignia on you and Land Protector on the party. Also, they should use Striking and spam Safety Wall on you.<br>
:::::::*For the Sura, you can pretty much combo him down and not worry about dying. When maggots spawn you can leech the HP you have lost, the maggots pretty much favors leeching gears. What I mean by this, since Root spawns 5 to 7 Maggots you can actually leech the HP you have lost if you time your Flash Combo and Tiger Cannon combo right. Bring at least 5 Ygg Berries to heal you when debuffed by Critical Wounds.<br>
:::::::*When fighting the Raydrics and Archers, snap in to lure the Raydrics and try to hide on another wall. With this strategy, the Archers will follow you thus merging with the melee Raydrics and you can one shot them with flash. My other strategy for the archers is to snap in and out to lure them so they bundle up and then I snap back in to Flash Combo them. Always prioritize the archers.<br>
::::::I highly recommend this setup of combo on Root.<br>

=='''MY SUPPORT'''==
A Sura cannot perform all these maneuvers and positioning without an Arch Bishop's support. I greatly rely on an Arch Bishop's ability to think fast and react faster when faced with certain circumstances.<br>
Generally for an easy run I go with these supports:
:>Arch Bishop
:>Minstrel
:>Sorcerer
But when my friend and I want to earn as much loot as we can, we run with fewer people.<br>
Either the AB or I bring an alternate Minstrel for the extra buffs.<br>
<br>
When I run OGH, I always check if my AB has the skills that I really need to complete the instance.<br>
The buffs that I will always want to be used are:<br>
::>Sacrament - This is top priority since as Suras we greatly rely on recasting our spheres which are Fixed Cast-Time.<br>
::>Safety Wall - I only want this skill to be spammed during Boss fights since it protects me from a lot of damage.<br>
::>Heal - I always need this skill since I always lure groups of monsters before killing them together. Also, Sura's main damage skill uses HP.<br>
