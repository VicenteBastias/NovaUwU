=== Equipments ===
 
==== Weapons ====
* Pistols: [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/13122/altair-ares Altea & Ares]
** Altea & Ares is arguably the best Pistol that you can get in the game as of now. With an additional 30% Ranged Attack bonus on it, there's really no other pistols that can match the damage this pistols provides.
* Gatling Gun: [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/13198/tempest Tempest]
** Some may defer to the use of [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/13197/minigun-2 Mini Mei] as their choice of Gatling, but the Tempest as a Level 4 Weapon will definitely out damage the Mini Mei at high refinement, even if the Mini Mei is carded well.
 
===== Cards =====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/4608/white-knightage-card White Knightage Card]: This card is great for weapons, although none of the recommended firearms that Rebels use has a card slot to it. Still, it doesn't hurt to keep a few of them around until [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/13127/crimson-revolver-2 Crimson Revolver] comes out!
 
==== Headgear ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/5359/airship-captains-hat-1 <nowiki>Ship Captain Hat [1]</nowiki>]
** An additional 7% Ranged Attack bonus is probably one of the best headgears a Rebel can equip.
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/5208/rideword-hat-1 <nowiki>Rideword Hat [1]</nowiki>]
** Hooray for HP and SP leeching!
 
===== Cards =====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/4910/darklord-essence-force3 Essence of Evil STR3]: This "card" is special as it can be slotted to anywhere as long as it has a slot. Providing a nice +12 ATK as well as +4 STR (-4 INT though!), it's one of the better cards to be increase your damage output no matter where you slot them to.
*
 
==== Middle Headgear ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/5641/robo-eye Robo Eyes]
** A good mid headgear that gives DEX as well as %ATK.
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/5592/sigruns-wings Sigrun's Wings]
** 2% Ranged Attack bonus is better than 2% ATK increase from Robo Eyes because of how formulas are calculated, but you lose out on the DEX. I would recommend Sigrun's Wings over Robo Eyes though!
* Slotted Middle Headgear
** Some people would like to use a slotted middle headgear together with another card of their preferences.
 
==== Lower Headgear ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/5574/well-chewed-pencil Well-chewed Pencil]
** This headgear gives 2 DEX, as well as +3% HIT.
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/6187/blank-card Blank Card]
** Extremely helpful for new players to get their hands on some Oridecon Boxes and even an Old Card Album through the [http://www.novaragnarok.com/wiki/Main_Office Blank Card Exchanger in the Main Office].
 
==== Armor ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/2319/glittering-jacket-1 <nowiki>Glittering Jacket [1]</nowiki>]
** The reason as to why this particular armor is chosen is because it has the possibility of being enchanted with stats. Getting a +3 DEX or +3 AGI is probably the best enchantment you want for this armor.
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/15024/army-padding Army Padding]
** This requires Mora Coins to do enchantments and comes with no slots!
 
==== Garment ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/2576/heroic-backpack-1 <nowiki>Heroic Backpack [1]</nowiki>]
** Heroic Backpack is arguably one of the best garments any class could use. There's a huge amount of effects you can get from a +7 (and even better effects at +9!) Backpack, so if you have the chance of getting one, get it!
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/20718/giant-faceworm-snake-skin-1 <nowiki>Giant Snake Skin [1]</nowiki>]
** Only obtainable through the Faceworm Instance. This particular garment comes with an assortment of enchantments, making it one of the more valuable garments to use. As a Rebel, you would generally want to aim for +7 DEX / +7 AGI Giant Snake Skins as part of their enchantments. Not only that, you actually get more HP/SP by using this with a pair of Temporal Boots!
 
==== Boots ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/22008/temporal-dex-boots-1 <nowiki>DEX Temporal Boots [1]</nowiki>]
** There's really no replacements for boots as Temporal Boots are arguably one of the best boots you can find in the game. One thing of note is that the Temporal Boots requires 120 base stats to be allocated in their respective stats in order for a more powerful effect to take place. [https://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Temporal_Boots (How to get Temporal Boots!)]
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/22011/temporal-luk-boots-1 <nowiki>LUK Temporal Boots [1]</nowiki>]
** If you're interested in going to the CRIT route, you might be interested in this particular pair of boots instead. One thing of note is that the Temporal Boots requires 120 base stats to be allocated in their respective stats in order for a more powerful effect to take place. [https://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Temporal_Boots (How to get Temporal Boots!)]
 
==== Accessories ====
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/2733/sheriff-badge Sheriff's Badge]
** Obtainable through Battlegrounds, this is a very good accessory for any beginning Rebel.
* [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/2992/pendant-of-maelstorm-1 <nowiki>Pendant of Maelstrom [1]</nowiki>]
** Only obtainable through the Jitterbug Instance, this particular accessory is probably the best accessory anyone can get.
* Sarah's Earrings ([http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/28310/sarahs-left-earring Left] or [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/item/28311/sarahs-right-earring Right])
** Another alternative to Sheriff's Badge and Pendant of Maelstrom is Sarah's Earrings from the Sarah & Fenris instance. These earrings can be enchanted with Expert Archer, essentially rivaling Pendant of Maelstrom and Sheriff's Badge, although it lacks a slot.
