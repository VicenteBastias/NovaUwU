Sura is the third class job from Monk (Acolyte -> Monk -> Rebirth -> Champion -> Sura). Sura is way better than Monk and Champion because Sura has a variety of Area of Effect abilities, the ability to have up to 15 maximum spheres instead of 5 thanks to [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Rising_Dragon Rising Dragon], new powerful buffs like [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Gentle_Touch-Revitalize Gentle-Touch-Revitalize], [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Gentle_Touch-Convert Gentle-Touch-Change] and deals lot of damage by using his HP with [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Tiger_Cannon Tiger Cannon] and [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Gates_of_Hell Gates of Hell]. = Stats =
= Stats =
Here's the list of stats that give benefit to Sura:
*'''STR''' - Increases your Status Attack (the left ATK number on your stats screen) by 1 per point  Increases your weight limit by 30 per point.  One of Sura's main stats.
*'''AGI''' - Increases your Attack Speed and Flee.  Having high AGI increases the damage of  [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Sky_Blow Sky Net Blow] and ASPD affects how fast you can use combo skills and how fast you can snap around.
*'''VIT''' - Increases your Maximum HP by 1% per point and increases the effectiveness of healing items. [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Tiger_Cannon Tiger Cannon] and [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Gates_of_Hell Gates of Hell]'s damage increases based on your Maximum HP and [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Status_Effects#Stun stun immunity] is granted at 100 VIT (total vit left+bonus) which makes this important.
*'''INT''' - Increases your Maximum SP by 1% per point and increases the effectiveness of SP healing items.  Reduces variable cast time.  SP affects [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Tiger_Cannon Tiger Cannon] and  [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Guillotine_Fist Asura Strike] and the cast time is very handy.  Good to invest points into.
*'''DEX''' - Increases Status Attack by 1 per 5 points, increases your HIT by 1 per point reduces variable cast time '''with twice the effectiveness as INT '''and increases attack speed.  Very valuable stat even in small numbers.
*'''LUK''' - Increases your total ATK by 1 and your HIT by 1 every 3 points.  Increases perfect dodge.  Useful in very small numbers when STR becomes expensive to put points into.
