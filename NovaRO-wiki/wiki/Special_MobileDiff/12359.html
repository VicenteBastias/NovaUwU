=Equipment=
==Equipment==
* From level 12: [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Eden_Group_Equipments_Quests Eden Equipment].
 
* From level 100: Basic Doram Equipment from the Doram Shop in Prontera. <code>/navi prontera 126/212</code>
Dorams have some unique sets of equipment. In the lower levels, they can use [[Eden Group#Equipment_Quests|Eden Group Equipment]] like any other class, but at higher levels, their options revolve mainly around the Doram-exclusive armour set and accessories. Additionally, they can only use Foxtail-type staves as weapons.
 
The most basic armour set (Doram Private set) can be bought at the Doram Shop in Prontera (/navi prontera 126/212). The more advanced sets as well as the weapons are all monster drops. On the other hand, most of the Doram's accessories can only be obtained through [[Doram Equipment Exchange]].
