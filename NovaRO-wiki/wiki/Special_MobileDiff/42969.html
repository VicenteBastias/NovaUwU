= Introduction =


Yahoo! The name is Tamugi, just a random person who loved Soul Linker and eventually wanted to try it's third class, Soul Reaper. Then it's 4th, Soul Ascetic, after hearing about it for the first time.
Yahoo!


This is a guide made for Soul Ascetic which has very little representation in that regard, despite being a fantastically fun and amazingly strong class.


Despite my short time with Soul Reaper and Ascetic, having started on renewal and Nova just last year, I believe I can help out people new to the class quite a bit due to my love for theorycrafting and learning.


I am writing this guide because people do come to me with their questions about Soul Ascetic ( and even Soul Reaper previously) in the first place, and as there is no Guide yet from what I know, I figured I might aswell.
I'm just another enjoyer of the Class, and someone who has stuck around it ever since Soul Linker existed (even through the dark ages of Soul Reaper). While I don't know everything, I hope this will be helpful to you.
