# Enter the Fire Cave by talking to the Warper > Instances > Bios Island.
 
# Talk to the '''Wandering Old Man''' (<code>/navi moro_cav 45/60</code>) to begin the Bios Island quest.
The instance is divided into 5 phases.  
#; [[File:bi_01.png]]
# You fight 9 [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3141/ Morroc's Ghoul], kill them all to proceed.
# Click on the '''Yellow Seed''' (50, 64) which is right to the Wandering Old Man. You can generate and enter the instance from this NPC.
# [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/2998 Weakened Morroc] will spawn. Kill him and move on.
#; [[File:bi_02.png]]
# Your party will be randomly split into 2 groups and be rooted in place, kill everything that spawns to proceed.
# Once you've entered, walk a bit on the east to get a dialogue scene between Vrid and Zeith.
# Both groups will be teleported back into the same room. Kill everything to continue.
#; [[File:bi_03.png]]
# Your party will face [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/2999 Morroc Necromancer] who will morph into his [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3000 FINAL FORM] when you killed his first form. [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3000 Morroc Necromancer] will drop 2 [[Prize of Hero]] upon death.
# You will be asked to eliminate all the monsters in the first and second floor to proceed. They do a lot of damage so be careful!
 
# Once you reach the final room, you will meet Vrid and Zeith again. After the dialogue, 3 series of mobs will spawn. Kill them all to proceed.
Talk to the teleportation device to exit the map and speak to ''Senior Tracker'' (<code>/navi moro_cav 61/69</code>) in order to receive your Token of Hero.
#; [[File:bi_04.png]]
# The [http://www.divine-pride.net/database/monster/3029 Reaper Yanku] will then spawn. He is relatively easy, just make sure to avoid the Comet attack (you can use spellbreak on this one).
#; [[File:bi_05.png]]
# The MVP will drop a [[Prize of Hero]] (regular drop) after dying. You can then talk to Vrid to exit the area and be rewarded with a {{Item |id=6684|name=Token of Hero}}.
#; [[File:bi_06.png]]
