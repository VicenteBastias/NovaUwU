== Leveling Tips ==
* '''Level 1-85:'''
** Use hp increase potion(large). Can be used even at level 1. Buy from players (@ws hp increase potion(large))
** Use Scaraba Summoning Scroll and Fly Wing trick. Read previous section. (@ws scaraba summoning scroll). Need zeny? Read the tips below how to get 1 m zeny in 1 hour daily.
* '''Level 85-145:'''
** Use guarana candy. Cheap and self sufficient Increase Agility so you don't get left behind because you are so slow. (@ws guarana candy)
** Buy Ygggdrasil Leaf for resurrecting people (double click the item, then click the dead person). Buy at Tool Dealer NPC near every warper.
** Use rejuvenation potion (regen huge amount of HP per few seconds for 10 minutes). Buy in main office Floor 2, southeast corner.
** Use energy potion (regen huge amount of sp per few seconds for 10 minutes). Buy in main office Floor 2, southeast corner.
** Buy white syrup in aldebaran NPC Alchemy Supplies (much better than white potion)
** Buy blue syrup in aldebaran NPC Alchemy Supplies (much better than blue potion)
** Buy concentrated blue potion (Buy from player using @ws concentrated blue potion) (much better than blue syrup)
* '''Level 145-175:'''
** Use Lv5 assumptio scroll. Some classes can be 1 shot by mobs' skill in gramp 145. This will prevent that. Can be made in main office Floor 2, southwest corner
* '''Level 175-230:'''
** Use shining defense scroll. Stacks with Lv5 assumptio scroll. Makes you much harder to kill by mobs in Gramp 175 and Thana12. Need to be bought using Endeavor Token in Main Office, NPC is on the north
** Use Alice Card for shield card. Gramp 175 and Thanatos Tower 12 mobs are Boss protocol. Buy card from player
* '''How to get 1 m zeny in 1 hour daily?:''' Login for 1 hour (don't logout). Claim daily login in (<code>@go main</code>, go south west). Talk to Daily Rewards NPC. After getting Gold Coin, check with command <code>@ws gold coin</code>, see the cheapest price. Talk in #trade, "S> gold coin for X amount of zeny". Set your price cheaper than the cheapest price from @ws or vending. If you have 10 gold coins and sell them for 100k ea, you should get 1m. Then you can use the money to buy <code>@ws Scaraba Summoning Scroll</code>. '''Disclaimer''': Only do this when you are really just starting in NovaRo. If you already can farm and have decent gears, judge by yourself whether to continue this practice or not.
* '''Where to change job?''' In Main Office (<code>@go main</code>, go south west). Talk to npc Jagen the Job Master.

* '''What is max level difference to share EXP in a party?''' 30 base levels. More than that means you can't share EXP. Usually you will watch level difference when leveling not in Gramp.
* '''How to search where X mob spawn at? How to know what mob drop X item? etc.'''
** <code>'''@whereis <monster name>'''</code>: Displays a list of maps where a particular monster spawns and the spawn amount.
** <code>'''@monsterinfo <monster name>'''</code>: Displays detailed info of a particular monster.
** <code>'''@whodrops (Item Name)'''</code>: Displays a list of monsters that drop the particular item and its drop rate.
* '''I want to know what items spawn in X map. How to do that?'''
** <code>'''CTRL + `'''</code> : Displays the map of all fields in Rune Midgard. Press '''Alt''' or '''Tab''' to toggle map names, and the name and level of monster with the highest spawn amount on the map.
