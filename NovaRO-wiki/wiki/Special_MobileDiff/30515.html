== Summer Festival Coins ==
[[File:6767.png|thumb|left| Summer Festival Coin|link=]]<br>
<br>
The inhabitants of Summer Festival Island trade in a special Summer Festival Coins currency that is only available on the island.<br>
These coins are widely obtainable and can be exchanged for items, upgrades and experiences.<br>
You can hold a maximum of 30,000 Summer Coins on your character.<br>
To help you get started, you will be rewarded with a stack of coins upon your first arrival.<br>
Use these coins to familiarize yourself with the available mini-games and have fun!<br>
<br clear="all">
== Obtaining Summer Festival Coins ==
You can obtain Summer Festival Coins from the following:
* First time arrivals will be rewarded with a stack of Coins
* Participating in mini games on the Summer Island
* Digging on the Summer Island and [[King's Grotto]]
* Foraging on the Summer Island and [[King's Grotto]]
* Participating in the [[Fishing Tournament]] on the Summer Island
* Killing Monsters in The [[King's Grotto]]
* Opening Chests in The [[King's Grotto]]
* Buried Treasure automated event
* Completing Summer Festival Daily quests on the [[Frying Dutchman]]
* Completing Summer Festival Achievements
* Exchanging Rainbow Gemstones with the Festival Painter 1 Gemstone to 20 Festival Coins
* Exchanging Deep Water Pearls with the Diving Enthusiast 1 Pearl to 75 Festival Coins
* Exchanging Crushed Cans with the Festival King 1 Can to 5 Festival Coins
* Purchasing in the Arcade for 85,000z per coin at the Summer Coin Merchant
== Spending Summer Festival Coins ==
You can spend Summer Festival Coins doing the following:
* Entering the [[King's Grotto]]
* Upgrading your Wetsuit
* Completing the [[Hooligan|Hooligans]] quest
* Some games have a coin cost to play
* Purchasing bait during the [[Fishing Tournament]]
* Upgrading your Fishing Rod
* Recoloring [[Festival Hat Recolors|Festival Hats]] at the Festival Painter
* Upgrading your Shovel
* Purchasing goods from The [[Summer Shops|Festival King]] and other Summer Island [[Summer Shops|Shops]]
== Pouring Keys ==
[[File:Kingskey.png|thumb|left| Summer Festival Key|link=]]<br>
Pouring Keys are an item that can be obtained from various games, and activities around the island, including the [[King's Grotto]]!<br>
These keys allow players to open locked chests that can be found scattered around the [[King's Grotto]]<br>
== Summer Raffle ==
The Summer Raffle is a new feature this year!<br>
There will be a Raffle every time the Summer Festival opens!<br>
Everyone who enters the island will automatically be entered into the raffle.<br>
And you can buy additional entries if you want to increase your chances of winning!<br>
And if you do win, you will recieve a rare hat!<br>
