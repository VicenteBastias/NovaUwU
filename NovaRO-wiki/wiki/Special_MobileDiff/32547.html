==Opening Act==
==Opening Act==
Just another version of Secret Intel focused on Guillotine Cross. I post most of my damage test here since I farm 2 star with Guillotine Cross.
Guillotine Cross is a sneaky-style class cannon DPS. That means they are super fragile to any kind of attacks and will most likely die super fast and this ''sneaky'' part means you have to play the mindset of a real assassin. Meaning that your positioning gonna be your hardest to earn skill compared to your gears. Cause your positioning means almost everything for Guillotine Cross. Keep this in mind '''If you are dead, you are useless to your teammate'''. That is what my Master in PvP used to tell me.
 
Guillotine Cross is the hardest hitting single-target DPS by now '''(December 2020)''' thanks to the skill {{Skill Info|class=Assassin Cross|id=379|skill=Soul Destroyer}} to kill people from 9 cells away from you and {{Skill Info|class=Guillotine Cross|id=2022|skill=Cross Impact}} to kill people who are currently having high ranged reduction build. Mainly our task as GX in PvP is Ground Control.
 
The idea of Ground Control itself is basically you are making sure your defense is clean from any enemies. This could be Shadow Chaser, Sura, Genetic, RK, or basically anything that try to stay inside your pack. The way how I imagine this is basically if you are playing Bio5 you are not the main DPS. But you are making sure 100% that there are no other mobs who is currently '''diving''' into your small group. Basically those '''Divers''' need to be killed by you. After making sure that your '''pack''' is clear from enemy divers, another task of Ground Control is making sure that the Emperium Room is clear when you are defending.
