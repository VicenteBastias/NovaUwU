= Soul Ascetic, Elements and You [OUTDATED]=
A big thing about Soul Ascetic is the Taekwon skill, Warm Wind. It allows us to use any Element in the game other than Poison and Undead. With (almost) any of our damage skills, too!
As already talked about in the skills section, Warm Wind has 7 levels and each of them cast instantly, while giving different elements on different levels. These are as follows:
#Earth
#Wind
#Water
#Fire
#Ghost
#Shadow
#Holy
Since they do cast instantly, each of these need a special Hotkey to be put on. In order to swap back to neutral, you need to take off your weapon. This may not work in some patches. ( Currently it doesn't seem to work)
All this is becoming more important now with the new Elemental Table, making Elemental useage less "punishing". For example, before the "base 4 elements", ala Earth, Wind, Water and Fire, got their damage decreased significantly against Holy 4, while Holy got its damage decreased against basic element 4.
It made Shadow a horrible element to play with. Now these elements interact less with each other, and more with their actual counters. Even Poison became less of a pain in the ass because basic Elements now counter it aswell.
Our Warm Wind Ability has become a lot stronger and more flexible, especially in content where they use a lot of the "higher elements", ala Holy, Shadow, Ghost and the likes.
Ancient Juperos is a great example here, where before your best choice was to go with Normal and take the reduced damage against the Ghost 2 mobs, because the basic elements would have their damage reduced against multiple of the enemies, as they have Shadow, Holy, Poison and Ghost elements. Annoying combo back then, manageable now. Can use any of the basic 4 elements and actually have an advantage against Poison mobs, while being 100% against all the others.
