=Summer Festival Activities=
There's only one thing more important than sunshine, Mini Games!<br>
The Summer Festival is home to a total of 18 games scattered through the island, including multiplayer modes!<br>
Apart from being fun, they are also the main source of Summer Coins.<br>
If you are have trouble finding any of the games just talk to one of the Festival Guides around the Island, they will surely show you the right directions!

=Festival Painter=
[[File:Painter.png|thumb|left]]<br>
The Festival Painter is an NPC that is able to recolor a selection of Summer Festival hats.<br>
In order to recolor a hat players must bring the painter 500 Summer Festival coins and 1 Rainbow Gemstone, which can be found in grotto treasure chests.<br>
Once you bring the required materials to the Painter, he will change a hat of your choice to a random color. There is a low chance that your hat will become a rare color.<br>
Each hat has recolor counter, when this counter reaches 0, the hat can no longer be recolored.<br>
Golden Pearls can be used to guarantee a hat will become a rare color. This will also reset the recolor counter. Golden Pearls can be found rarely in grotto treasure chests.

==Obtaining a Shovel==
Speak to the Excavation Expert located on the Summer Festival map <code>@navi summer02 222 250</code><br>
He will offer to sell you an Iron Shovel for 5 Festival Coins so he can play some Mini Games.<br>
If this is your first time visiting the Festival, you can ask the Expert about digging and he will reward you with a free Iron Shovel.<br>

==Obtaining A Fishing Rod, Reel, and Bait==
Speak to the Cat Fisherman located on the Summer Festival Fishing Pier <code>@navi summer02 92 240</code><br>
He will offer to sell you a Wooden Fishing Rod for 1 Festival Coin.<br>
He will also sell you a Fishing Reel for 1 Festival Coin.<br>
Lastly you can purchase a variety of Bait from the Cat Fisherman<br>
If this is your first time visiting the Festival, you can ask the Fisherman about Fishing and he will reward you with some free fishing supplies to get you started.<br>
Similar to digging you'll want to quickly use any extra materials you fish up to upgrade your Fishing Rod.<br>
==Upgrading Your Fishing Rod and Bait==
Once you've done a little bit of fishing you'll find that you've gathered some excess material.<br>
You can bring that material to the Cat Fisherman and he can upgrade your rod.<br>

=King's Grotto=
[[File:Grotto.PNG|thumb|left|600px|King's Grotto]]
'''Explore the massive underwater cave!'''
 
Take refuge from the intense summer sun and go for a swim in the cool waters.
Here a host of experiences await enthusiastic swimmers! Not only are there more 2nd and 3rd tier digging sites inside the Grotto, you can also collect more Summer Festival Coins by defeating various monsters.
 
There you may also come head to head with a long-time resident of the Grotto, namely the Pouring King, who will periodically seek revenge on behalf of exterminated Grotto Pourings. Join in on helping the server defeat the King and obtain some extra coin!
 
Once you have gathered some Pouring Keys by playing other mini-games, use them to open Treasure Chests that are scattered across the map. Perhaps you will find rare Golden Pearls!
 
But before you go exploring, don't forget to don your Wetsuit! It is a useful piece of equipment that will make your underwater journey more pleasant while you keep an eye out for Underwater Bubbles to keep yourself from drowning. To increase your resistance to all manner of underwater currents, talk with Diving Enthusiast to see what cool upgrades he has on offer!
 
'''Entering the cave'''<br>
You can enter the King's Grotto by entering the portal located at the bottom right hand side of the Summer Festival map, Note that you will need to have a Wetsuit equipped in order to be allowed an entry.<br>
You can also fall into the Grotto when digging on the island and selecting the "Dig deeper" option, Note if you do not have a wetsuit on when selecting "Dig deeper" you will run out of breath at a MUCH faster rate in the Grotto.
 
<br clear="all">
==Diving Enthusiast==
Players can speak with the Diving Enthusiast <code>@navi summer02 107 102</code> to obtain a Wetsuit for 5 Summer Festival coins.<br>
Equipping a Wetsuit will allow players to enter and explore the King's Grotto, the Diving Enthusiast will also be able to upgrade a players wetsuit<br>
with various items found inside of Grotto Chests.
 
==Upgrading Your Wetsuit==
The Diving Enthusiast is able to upgrade a players Wetsuit, each upgrade will give players added benefits while inside the King's Grotto.<br>
Each upgrade costs 3 Deepwater Pearls and 100 Summer Festival Coins, Deepwater pearls can be obtained from Grotto Treasure Chests, inside the Grotto.<br>
If you fully enchant your Wetsuit with all 4 enchants you will gain the added benefit of having increased ASPD inside the King's Grotto.<br><br>
Having a fully enchanted wetsuit will also result in the Diving Enthusiast now exchanging Deepwater pearls for Summer Festival Coins at a 1 Pearl to 75 Festival Coins ratio.

=The Frying Dutchman=
[[File:Dutchman.png|thumb|left|600px|The Frying Dutchman]]
[[File:4_DR_GAMBERI.gif|thumb|right|Captain Mack summer02 92 378]]
 
Frying Dutchman Sailing the frying waters since 2018!
 
A ship has dropped its anchor near the Summer Island and it is none other than the famous Frying Dutchman!
Come take this once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to drool at gourmet dishes and say hello to part-time cook and part-time pirate Captain Mack Roni and his quirky, whiskered crew. Being the busy puss he is, Captain Mack will offer you 9 different daily quests to complete.
 
Finishing four quests a day (over the time span of 6 festival openings) will net you a special Summer Ticket. When you have 10 such tickets, talk to Mack's underling, Sous Chef, who collects and trades them for account-bound headgears!
 
Completing every daily quest will earn you Summer Festival Coins.
Completing all 8 daily quests will not earn you additional rewards.
