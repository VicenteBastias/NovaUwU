The '''Warlock''' (WL) is a 3rd job class and the final upgrade to Wizards. Warlocks are powerful magic damage dealers, capable of dealing heavy AoE and single target magic damage using verious elements such as Fire, Water, Wind, Earth, or Ghost. Along with their damage dealing capabilities, Warlocks are also able to inflict new status alignments such as Freezing, Burning, and Stasis to weaken their enemies in battle. They also have access to a newly introduced Spellbook system that allows them to cast skills without a cast time.

*'''Strength (STR)'''
</div>
For Warlocks, mainly used for increasing Weight Limit, which helps with Masquerade resistance as well. If you can spare some points, it's nice to have 20-30 STR.
    <div style="display: inline-block; vertical-align: bottom;"></div>
*'''Agility (AGI)'''
</div>
Both an offensive and a defensive stat. Raises [http://novaragnarok.com/wiki/Attack_Speed Attack speed], and flee rate. Attack speed reduces ASPD delay, which makes a huge difference in your skill spam, flee gives you a little more protection against monsters. For most builds, 100 AGI is recommended. <br /> 100 AGI gives immunity against Bleeding and Sleep statuses, also reduces the duration of Masquerades. It is advised to have ''at least'' 175 ASPD. To learn more about ASPD delay, please refer to [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5qdTbw0_w54 Munbalanced's ROLab #2 video.]<br />
</td>

*'''Vitality (VIT)'''
</tr></table>
Every point of VIT raises your Max HP by +1% and the effectiveness of potions. Since we have the lowest HP modifier among 3rd classes, and tend to die ''pretty easily'' in both party and PvP situations, it is advised to run at least 100 total VIT.<br />
| style="text-align:center; width:40%; border-left:1px solid grey;" |
100 VIT gives immunity to Stun and Curse, and protects against Crystallization, Freezing and Mandragora Howling. 
[[Alternative Outfits | Sura's new outfits are now available!]]
*'''Intelligence (INT)'''
 
A Mage's bread and butter, directly raises your mATK by +1.5 per point, decreases your [[#Decreasing variable cast time with stat points| cast time]], raises your SP pool and regeneration. Also gives immunity to Silence, helps with Sleep, Deep Sleep and Oblivion Curse duration.<br />
[[Rock Ridge|Sheriff Wyatt Warp needs help in Rock Ridge!]]
Since it is one of our main stats to get damage out of, it is recommended to get 120 base INT, however I would not recommend getting more than 120 base INT - it becomes very expensive in terms of status points to raise it - you can get more mATK and utility out of some LUK and DEX.
 
*'''Dexterity (DEX)'''
[[Alternative Outfits | Sorcerer's new outfits are now available!]]
Primary stat to reduce [[#Decreasing variable cast time with stat points| cast time]], but it also raises your ASPD, and your mATK by 1/5 therefore it is ideal to have your DEX at multiples of 5. I would recommend getting around 105-120 base DEX, depending whether you want instant cast (or can get it elsewhere) or not. <br /> The difference between you and your target's DEX affects the chance of divest from [[File:Wl earthstrain.gif]] [http://irowiki.org/wiki/Earth_Strain '''Earth Strain'''], and it also helps with Freezing duration.
 
*'''Luck (LUK)'''
[[Banquet for Heroes|Episode 16.1 has arrived to NovaRO!]]
This stat has gotten a major update with the introduction of renewal. Now every 3 points of LUK gives an additional +1 ATK/mATK, which makes it a very viable option to gain some mATK at later levels. Raising your INT from 120 to 130 would require you to use 340 stat points and would net you +15 mATK, while raising LUK from 1 to 30 would cost you only 86 stat points, and raise your mATK by +10. Glass Cannon and damage oriented builds usually have 30 or 60 LUK, depending on the player's taste.
