This build is the best I could come up with to maximize the damage of Tiger Cannon<br>

'''AGI''' = 100 (FASTER combo animation)<br>
'''VIT''' = 115(Get as much vit stats as possible)<br>
'''INT''' = 40  (Get as low sp as possible)<br>
'''DEX''' = 90  (HIT CHANCE)<br>
'''LUK''' = 0

-Deal tons of damage on Normal and BOSS monsters.<br>
-You wont need as much SP pots as GOH build. (50 Mora Mandarins is enough for an OGH Hard run)<br>
-Maggots in OGH Hard/Norm wont be a problem.<br>
-You can solo kill OGH mini boss. (Agony and Grudge of Royal Knight if your fast enough.)<br>

-Heavily relies on heals from priests.<br>

-You need to master your timing when doing the combos.<br>

=====How to Play=====
:::To be as efficient as possible when playing a class that uses its own HP as damage, here are some tips on how to do it.<br>
::::*Train yourself to not consume any SP potions when against normal monsters. We have a skill called {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb Lv 1 |simple=yes}} which regenerates our SP by using our spheres.<br>
::How to:
:::{{Skill List |id=401 |skill=Dangerous Soul Collect Lv 1 |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb Lv 1 |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=401 |skill=Dangerous Soul Collect Lv 1 |simple=yes}} > {{Skill List |id=2340 |skill=Power Absorb Lv 1 |simple=yes}} <br>
::Also you can regenerate your spheres through getting hit or hitting monsters through the skill called {{Skill List |id=2346 |skill=Gentle Touch - Energy Gain|simple=yes}}<br>
::::*Learn to lure monsters into groups before using Flash Combo or Tiger Cannon Combo. That way the % chance of leeching HP and SP is really high.<br>
::::*Practice the combos. Go to the training dummies and practice your combos. When facing Root or Amda, try to chill and combo like your playing a piano. Set your rhythm and pacing.<br>
::::*Farm your HP potions. When in a situation where you find it hard to leech HP or you do not have an AB to heal you, use {{Item List |id=12027 |item=HP Giggling Box|simple=yes}}. These drop from Wraith Cards.<br>

=='''HOW TO PLAY SURA'''==
:::::For OGH Hard Duo or 4 Man: In this run you are going to have to use two builds which is the Combo and Goh. I have tested it and it would only cost you about 300-400 Grape Juice or less and 200 Mandarins. Your consumption will highly depend on your AB if he lexes mobs.<br>
:::::::*For the first part, cleaning the mobs is going to be very very easy since you have combo Tiger Cannon and Flash Combo. Even the maggots are not a problem.<br>
:::::::*ROOT - Here you will have to position yourself 1 cell above and 1 cell to the left from the NPC which will put you to the left cheeck of root. Your AB and other party mates should position themselves beside the left pillar. If you run with a sorcerer the better, let your sorcerer cast LP on the cells of the supports so that poison cloud wont be a bother and also your Minstrel can cast <s>apple and assassin</s> Friggs Song on you which gives you tons of damage boost.<br>
::::::GAMEPLAY:<br>
:::::::*For the AB, he should only cast heal, safety wall and refreshing kyrie and nothing more. AB should not use lex so as not to add more complications to his spam.<br>
:::::::*For Sorcerer, if you happen to run 4 man let your sorcerer cast Fire Insignia on you and Land Protector on the party. Let him spam safety wall instead and striking.<br>
:::::::*For the SURA, you can pretty much combo him down and not worrying about dying. When maggots spawn you can leech the HP you have lost, the maggots pretty much favors leeching. (Bring atleast 5 Ygg Berries to heal you when debuffed by critical wounds.)<br>
:::::::*For the Archers and Raydrics, snap in to lure the Raydrics and try to hide on another wall. With this strategy, the archers will follow you thus merging with the melee Raydrics and you can one shot them with flash. My other strategy for the archers is to snap in and out to lure them so they bundle up and then I snap back in to Flash Combo them. Always prioritize the archers.<br>
::::::I highly recommend this setup of combo on Root.<br>
:::::'''SAFE OPTION'''
::::'''Dragon Combo''' > '''Fallen Empire''' > '''Tiger Cannon''' > '''Flash Combo''' > wait for couple of milliseconds > '''Dragon Combo''' > '''Fallen Empire''' > '''Tiger Cannon''' > '''Flash Combo'''<br>
:::::'''FASTER OPTION'''
::::'''Dragon Combo''' > '''Fallen Empire''' > '''Tiger Cannon''' > '''Dragon Combo''' > '''Fallen Empire''' > '''GoH''' > '''Flash Combo''' > '''Dangerous Soul Collect''' > '''Repeat'''<br>
::::Use a +20 Agi Food for faster animation and cast.<br>
:::::::*When Root spawns maggots, time your cooldowns so that you can leech Hp from them.<br>
