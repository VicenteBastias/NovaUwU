OVERVIEW
This guide is for those who like to try a PVE sura and enjoy how fun using the class is.
I made this guide so that other classes will have the opportunity to do a 4 man party.
NOTE: To main sorc players and sura, our time is now.
With this guide you will be able to play the DPS role in a OGH hard 4 man party
I will try to give you the cheapest and the best gear combinations as much as I can and based on my experience.
I am a sura main player who does not pvp, so any questions regarding pvp stuff I am sorry I cannot help you with it.


You can choose between two builds which I am going to showcase with their PROS and CONS.
Also BIG THANKS to Munbalance for making the GOH build possible.
STATS
STR = 100 - 120 (Main STAT for increasing damage of tiger cannon and gates of hell and reaching temporal and card requirements)
AGI = 0 - 100 (a second priority stat when you want to combo)
VIT = 100 - 120 (VIT increases ur tiger cannon damage and your GOH damage similar to STR)
INT = 0 - 100 ( this stat is mainly for the GOH build)
DEX = 90 - 130 ( primary stat for GOH build)
LUK = 0 (as much as possible get it to 0 with negative stats to make you immune to curse)

BUILDS
FIRST BUILD IS FOR COMBO
Doing the combo makes you deal a million damage just for tiger cannon.
But doing the entire combo will give you 1.5 - 1.8 million total, numbers based on Amdarais Norm.
But how do you get this much damage? You are gonna have to chain your combos.
Here is my chain sequence. (MORE AGI and DEX the faster you cast the combos)
Rising Dragon > Dragon Combo > Fallen Empire > Tiger Cannon > Dragon Combo > Fallen Empire > GOH > Flash Combo > Danger soul collect > Repeat
SECOND BUILD IS FOR GOH
This build allows you to access the DPS role for OGH HARD.
With this build you will be able to 1 shot raydric archers in OGH hard, and deal 400k per GOH on Amdarais.
NOTE: 400k is without ORATIO or Insignia combo.
This build will depend on ur FCT of Dangerous Soul Collect, that is why the need for the Dex Boots.
You will deal 400k x 3 per 0.75 seconds on Amdarais.
ALSO, MOST IMPORTANTLY the pro sorcs will clear the normal monsters for you =). Hi to the pro sorcs.
