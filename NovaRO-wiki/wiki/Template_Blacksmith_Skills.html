Full Adrenaline Rush Support
  • Soul Link Skill
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 64
  • Required weapon type: Any except Bows and Guns
 You must have Blacksmith Spirit active to use this skill.

For 150 seconds, increase the ASPD of yourself by 30%, and all on screen party members ASPD by 25%, except those wielding Bows or Guns.


  • This skill does not stack with Adrenaline Rush, One-handed Quicken, Two-handed Quicken, Spear Quicken, Assassin Cross of Sunset, or Solar, Lunar, Stellar Shadow and Protection.
