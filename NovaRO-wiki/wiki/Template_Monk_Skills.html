Blade Stop Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Sphere Cost: 1
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
 Allows you to "catch" and enemy during their attack animation. This will stop you and the target from doing anything for 10 seconds. If you or the target is killed in this time, the other will be freed.


Depending on the skill level used, it will enable another skill for you to use while in this status;

  • Level 1 : No skill
  • Level 2 : Finger Offensive
  • Level 3 : Investigate
  • Level 4 : Chain Combo
  • Level 5 : Asura Strike


  • This skill will only stop Boss monsters for 2 seconds.

  • [Lv 5] : +20 Critical Rate, -50% SP Recovery, Duration 180 seconds.
Spirit Recovery Passive
  • Max Level: 5
 Increase your HP and SP recovery while sitting.

This restores HP/SP every 10 seconds, or every 20 seconds if your weight is above 70%.


  • The HP/SP restored is in 2 parts, a flat bonus, and a percentage based bonus.


  • [Lv 1] : (4 + 0.2%) HP Restored, (2 + 0.2%) SP Restored.
  • [Lv 2] : (8 + 0.4%) HP Restored, (4 + 0.4%) SP Restored.
  • [Lv 3] : (12 + 0.6%) HP Restored, (6 + 0.6%) SP Restored.
  • [Lv 4] : (16 + 0.8%) HP Restored, (8 + 0.8%) SP Restored.
  • [Lv 5] : (20 + 1.0%) HP Restored, (10 + 1.0%) SP Restored.
Asura Strike Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 1
  • Sphere Cost: 5
  • Var. Cast Time: 2.25 - (0.25 x Skill Level)
  • Fixed Cast Time: 2.25 - (0.25 x Skill Level)
  • Cast Delay: 3.5 - (0.5 x Skill Level)
  • Range: 2
  • Required status : Fury
 Consumes all of your remaining SP and Spirit Spheres to strike a target with a DEF piercing attack.

After casting this skill, Fury status is removed and SP will not be naturally recovered for 10 seconds.


  • This skills damage is forced to the neutral element.


There are four ways this skill can be used, in all of them Fury status is required:

  • 5 Spirit Spheres: can be used in the same manner as single-target skills; by selecting Asura Strike through a hotkey and clicking on a target.
  • 4 Spirit Spheres: can be used during the delay of Chain Combo. This will bypass the Cast Time.
  • 4 Spirit Spheres: can be used on the target while it has been Rooted with Level 5 Stop Blade.
  • 1 Spirit Sphere: can be used during the delay of Combo Finish. This will bypass the Cast Time.
