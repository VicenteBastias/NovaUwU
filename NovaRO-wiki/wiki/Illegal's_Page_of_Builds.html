Ahoy, ahoy! Welcome to my page of builds, a few notes before you follow these setups:

  • These are general purpose builds. This means that they are designed so you can use them wherever you want, and not rely on other players/characters to function.
  • There will be gear and cards you can change when inside parties to make yourself even stronger, but they are case by case situations. They are not listed here.
  • There will always be a "stronger gear" for specific situations. (Like +% damage against demons is going to make you stronger against demon enemies).
  • These builds are meant to be guidelines, you do not need to follow them exactly, but you can if you wish to do so.
  • The examples of damage given are just that, examples. Elements, DEF, MDEF, and more can influence your results so the damage can be different than displayed.
  • The examples of damage use 200 DEF and 50 MDEF, this is roughly a 30% reduction of damage for both, allowing DEF/MDEF Piercing effects to show a reasonable increase.

Vulcan Arm (Geffenia Farmer)

Vulcan Arm - (Last Updated: July 22, 2021)

Pros and Cons
Pros Cons
  • Cheaper than a genetic farmer.
  • Gear requires no refining (except to +4).
  • Slightly longer range than Cart Cannon.
  • Skill only requires ASPD to spam.
  • Too many extra windows open will lower your spam.
  • Default Madogear outfit looks bad to me.
Notable Skills
Skill Description
Vulcan Arm
  • Deals up to 420% ATK, is increased by Base Level and DEX.
  • Every 1 Dex = 1% ATK increase. At level 200 the skills ATK% is doubled (840% + 2% per DEX).
  • Has 0.1s After Cast Delay, 0.1s Cooldown (Both of these do not matter at all).
  • Has 0.2s Variable Cast Time (This is so low that our DEX stat is enough to fully spam).
  • Consumes 1 Vulcan Bullet every cast. (Can be purchased in the main office).
Acceleration
  • This gives us max movement speed, but costs 1 Magic Madogear Fuel. (Can be purchased in the main office).
Maximize Over Thrust
  • Increases your damage by quite a bit, but costs 5,000 zeny per cast. (Not that big a deal when farming).
Maximize Power
  • Removes your weapons base variance, allowing you to deal more consistent and better damage. (Does not affect refinement variance).
Weapon Perfection
  • Removes your weapons size penalty, allowing you to deal full damage to any size of enemy.
Adrenaline Rush
  • Increases your ASPD when using a mace or axe weapon.
Loud Exlamation
  • Free ATK and STR for damage.

Newbie Gear Progression
Strategy/Explanation Slots Equipment Alternative Equipment
  • Go join a clan for free stats. Clan System video
  • Start off by doing the Poring Village instance, this will get you some quick gears and a +5 Crimson Katar at the end.
  • Then work on doing Eden Board Quests or join a gramps quest to level to 99. You can also try killing Buffalo Bandits in the field on the right side of @go rock.
  • After reaching level 99, rebirth, kill some porings, then start doing eden board quests again, or turn in some gramps quests if you haven't already.
  • At this point start doing level 100+ board quests to get rewarded with  Paradise Coins. These are what you use to buy the Paradise equipments.
  • These gears will be enough to do basic leveling and some basic farming, but nothing too efficient yet.
  • You should farm a couple  Poison Bottles so you run things like the Sara's Memories instance and Ghost Palace a bit easier.

Early Gear Progression
Strategy/Explanation Slots Equipment Alternative Equipment
  • The first thing you should work towards is doing the Rideword Hat quest to craft your own.
  • The next thing you want get is the  Thanatos Katar [1], this will let you start dealing decent damage and give you leech for survival.
  • I recommend doing 1 Ghost Palace run for Gray Shards, then buy the rest of the Shards you need until you reach 200. That will let you buy the Thanatos Katar from the NPC outside the instance.
  • You can also buy another 75 Gray Shards to enchant your  Thanatos Katar [1]. Sometimes you can get some decent damage enchants, but don't sink too much zeny into this.
  • Now save up and buy an  Incubus Pet so you can feed it over time while you work on other farming. When it hits loyal it will give you more SP leech.
  • The  illusion Armor A-Type [1] +  illusion Engine Wing B-Type [1] combo will give 10% After Cast Delay. Then with  Magic Mushroom that gives another 10% After Cast Delay, you will reach 20% ACD. Finally when you eventually get the

 Delay After Skill Module on the  +7 illusion Armor A-Type [1] you will reach 30% After Cast Delay, letting you spin pretty fast.

  • Another quest you can do for more movement speed and ASPD is the Gaurana Candy Quest



  • At level 110 you can start doing the Illusion questline, this will give you a ton of EXP and allow you to start obtaining the illusion equipment.


Get the illusion equipment in this order

  •  +4 illusion Armor A-Type [1]
  •  +4 illusion Engine Wing B-Type [1]
  •  +4 illusion Leg A-Type [1]
  •  illusion Booster R [1]
  •  illusion Booster L [1]


  • Now I recommend starting the Sage's Legacy questline. (This is also known as 17.2).
  • Once you get inside of Varmundt's Mansion you can buy a  illusion Reinforcement Cube for 10m zeny. This will turn your +4 illusion equipment into +7 with no chance to fail. Only do this for the armor, the other illusion gear are not important to refine.


  • At this point you are now at a good spot to farm a lot of content, and you should now start looking into a stronger endgame build, such as Cross Ripper Slasher, Counter Slash, Cross Impact, or Soul Destroyer. It's highly recommended for newer players to move on to Cross Impact.

Pros and Cons
Pros Cons
  • Strong area of effect damage
  • High survivability/tankiness
  • Very good at soloing early/midgame instances
  • Newbie friendly, easy to start with if you are new to the game
  • Strong GX build for basic dungeon and midgame instance farming.

Notable Skills
Skill Description
Rolling Cutter
  • This is our main DPS skill.
  • Deals up to 450% ATK in a 7x7 area of effect around you.
  • Damage scales with your Base Level. (900% ATK at level 200)
  • Has 0.2s Cast Delay. (Requires 38% After Cast Delay Reduction for max spam)
  • Can be casted up to 8 times per second. (This skill is an exception, since most skills can only cast 7.14 times per second at most)
Back Slide
  • Allows you to instantly move 5 cells backwards. Great for escaping area of effect skills and moving through maps very quickly.
  • Spam increases with higher ASPD.
Enchant Deadly Poison
  • Allows you to use  Poison Bottles, increasing your total damage by a large amount.
  • Multiplies your Weapon ATK by 5, and your Equipment ATK by 4.
  • This is what makes Guillotine Cross do high damage, unless you naturally deal high enough damage for what you need, always use this.
  • THIS DOES NOT ENDOW YOUR WEAPON WITH THE POISON PROPERTY!!
Enchant Poison + Venom Impress
  • Enchant Poison: Endow your weapon with the poison property. Good for dealing with ghost monsters that take reduced damage from neutral.
  • Venom Impress: Causes target enemy to take 50% more damage from ALL poison property attacks.
  • This combo is great against ghost and neutral enemies where its difficult to get elemental advantages.
Dark Claw
  • Causes a single target to take 150% more damage from all MELEE attacks for 20 seconds. (75% on Boss monsters).
  • This also removes and prevents the target from having any reflect skills. (You still take damage from this skill damaging reflect).
  • Does not remove Max Pain. (This is custom to NovaRO).
Cloaking Exceed
  • Allows you to hide from all enemies except for Boss monsters.
  • Increases your movement speed to 140%. (Stacks with Increase Agility)
  • If used at the same time as a Halter Lead, you can reach max movement speed.
Poisoning Weapon
  • Allows you to use a variety of potions, and increases their duration. (Also increases  Poison Bottle duration).
  • For this build these are the most used:
    •  Magic Mushroom gives -10% After Cast Delay.
    •  Toxin recovers 1% Max SP every second.
    •  Death Hurt recovers 1% Max HP every second.
    •  Oblivion Curse makes you immune to Curse and Silence.
Hallucination Walk
  • Gives you +250 Flee at max level, and a 50% chance to dodge magical attacks for 50 seconds.
  • Useful against bosses that don't use Dispel.
Phantom Menace
  • Reveals any hidden enemies in a 7x7 area around you.
