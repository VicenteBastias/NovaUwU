Ancilla Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
  • SP % Cost: 10% of max
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Consumes: 1  Blue Gemstone
 Creates a single  Ancilla.

You can carry up to 3  Ancilla in your inventory at a time, and it is character bound.


Ancilla can be consumed to increase Healing effectiveness by 15%, and SP recovery by 30% for 60 seconds.

Canto Candidus Support
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 180 + (20 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 3.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 Bestows the effects of Increase Agility on the caster and party members in a large area centered on the caster.

Targets will receive the Increase Agility bonus as well as extra AGI based on the casters Job Level.


  • The casters Job Level increases the AGI bonus given. (AGI bonus = Job Level / 10).
  • Canto Candidus uses the highest level of Increase Agility the caster has learned.
  • Does not affect Mechanics inside Madogear.


  • [Lv 1] : 7x7 Area of effect, Duration 120 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : 15x15 Area of effect, Duration 180 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : 31x31 Area of effect, Duration 240 seconds.
Clearance Support

  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 48 + (6 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 4.0s

  • [Lv 1] : 68% Chance.
  • [Lv 2] : 76% Chance.
  • [Lv 3] : 84% Chance.
  • [Lv 4] : 92% Chance.
  • [Lv 5] : 100% Chance.
Clementia Support
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 180 + (20 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 3.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 Bestows the effects of Blessing on the caster and party members in a large area centered on the caster.

  • [Lv 3] : 31x31 Area of effect, Duration 240 seconds.
Coluceo Heal Recovery
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 180 + (20 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 3.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.5 - (0.5 x Skill Level)
  • Cool Down: 2.0s
 Restores HP to the caster and party members in a large area centered around the caster.

Restores more HP if there are more members in the party.


  • Coluceo Heal uses the highest level of Heal the caster has learned.


  • [Lv 1] : 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 2] : 15x15 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 31x31 Area of effect.


Bonus HP = [(HP Restored ÷ 100) × (Party_Members × 5) ÷ 2]

Praefatio Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 80 + (10 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 2.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cool Down: 10 + (3 x Skill Level)
 Applies a barrier that blocks melee and ranged physical damage on the caster and all party members.

The barrier will block a certain number of hits, and its durability is equal to a portion of Max HP of the target.


  • The barriers durability is increased depending on the amount of party members there are.
  • The highest level Praefatio will overwrite the duration of lower level Praefatio, and lower level Praefatio cannot refresh the duration of higher level Praefatio.
  • Praefatio and Kyrie Eleison interact in this way as well. Using Praefatio and Kyrie Eleison at the same level will refresh the barrier's duration.
