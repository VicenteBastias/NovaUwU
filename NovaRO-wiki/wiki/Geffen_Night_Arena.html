An illegal Geffen Magic Tournament happens at midnight. Unlike the previous instance, you can come with a party, and you probably needed it because the midnight figthers are way stronger than the normal Geffen Tournament version. Now some details about the instance:

  • There are 10 rounds, each round the enemy will have have more HP.
  • You will fight a random enemy each round, except for the last round which is always against Fenrir.
  • You have one minute to complete each round.
  • Fenrir takes 90% reduced damage.
  • Only the center of the arena is safe. If you walk or get knocked back into the borders you will take heavy damage that can potentially kill.
  • If any party member dies during a fight, the enemy will take no damage until all members are alive again.
  • Unlike Geffen Magic Tournament, you can only collect rewards if you complete the round and choose to quit, so don't forget to talk to the NPC if you wanna leave the instance.

Cards

All enemies have a chance to drop card from the respective Geffen Magic Tournament instance. You can also craft the new Arena version of those cards at the Abandoned Machine (dali02 84, 68)Copied!.

You have 2 ways to craft a new Arena card:

  • 10x  Geffen Arena Championship and 7x of the monster respective Geffen Magic Tournament card.
OR
  • 600x  Geffen Arena Championship and 3x of the monster respective Geffen Magic Tournament card.
