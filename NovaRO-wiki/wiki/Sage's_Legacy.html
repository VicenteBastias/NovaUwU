Sage's Legacy

Gaining access to Varmundt's Mansion

Requirements
Base Level: 130
Starting Point: (sp_cor 255, 285)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Capturing Elyumina
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: 5  Varmeal Tickets
  1. Speak with Lucky in Cor at the north eastern area, at the sewage (sp_cor 255, 285)Copied!. The entrance has been unlocked.
  2. Speak with Lucky again to enter the Sewage Treatment Plant. He tells you a colleague might have fell in the sewage water.
  3. Click on ??????????????? near (ba_pw02 93, 89)Copied!. The location is marked on your minimap.
  4. Bring 1  Yggdrasil Leaf and talk to Dew again to save him.
  5. Go meet Magie on the other map, by entering the portal on the middle left (ba_pw02 8, 149)Copied!.
  6. Upon entering the map, Magie will talk to you. You meet with the old crew.
  7. Meet with Alp at the noth western portal (ba_pw01 87, 290)Copied! and talk to him. He will warp you to the next area.
  8. Once inside, speak with Lacis (ba_in01 20, 319)Copied!.
  9. Speak with Tamarin near the entrance (ba_maison 70, 145)Copied!. You need to register as a guest before being able to navigate through the Varmundt's Mansion. You will receive 5  Varmeal Tickets and some experience.
  10. Speak with Mansion Manager Alpha right above Tamarin (ba_maison 74, 150)Copied!, to complete your registration as a guest of Mansion.
    You have unlocked the Playing with Pitayas quest.
    You have unlocked the Lost in Time instance quest.
  11. Speak with Mansion Manager Alpha again. She tells you there is a problem with the relay network.
    You have unlocked the Potato Chips (Eorpa's Request) daily quest.
    You have unlocked the Sewage Treatment Plant Cleanup daily quest.
    You have unlocked the First Power Plant Cleanup daily quest.
    You have unlocked the Checking the Underground Condition quest.
    You have unlocked the Silence in the Library! quest.
    You have unlocked the Bathroom Monster quest.
    You have unlocked the Water Garden quest.
    You have unlocked the Dew quest.
    You have unlocked the Hidden Flower Garden instance quest.
  12. You can continue the main quest with Relay Network Repairs.


Note: At this point, you have unlocked access to Varmundt's Mansion Garden. If you leave the map and want to go back, you have two options:

  • Go ask Nillem in Cat on Bullet pub (Einbroch > walk to (einbroch 302, 326)Copied! > NPC is at (pub_cat 86, 105)Copied!) to warp you back. He will charge 500 zeny each time. The first time you ask him to warp you, he will also give you the Magician's Bell.
  • Use the  Magician's Bell (given by Nillem or bought in Varmundt's Mansion Garden for 10 000 zeny): Double clicking on it allows you to call Nillem anywhere to ask him to warp you to Varmundt's Mansion Garden, with a 500 zeny fee each time. Please note that the Bell has a 20 minutes cooldown.

Relay Network Repairs

Requirements
Base Level: 130
Starting Point: (ba_maison 146, 109)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Gaining access to Varmundt's Mansion
Rewards

Items: 30  Varmeal Tickets
  1. Talk with Tasty at the restaurant (ba_maison 146, 109)Copied!.
  2. You will need to look for a Broken Beta (on the same map) and get 1  Automatic Doll Communication Chip.
  3. Return to Tasty and talk to her. You will be rewarded with 5  Varmeal Tickets.
  4. Go to the next Repeater at (ba_maison 334, 143)Copied! and talk to it. You will be rewarded with 5  Varmeal Tickets.

    You have unlocked the Supply and Demand for Reserve Parts daily quest.
  7. You can continue the main quest with The Hidden Story.


The Hidden Story

Requirements
Base Level: 130
Starting Point: (ba_in01 206, 106)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Relay Network Repairs
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: 35  Varmeal Tickets
  1. Talk with Philofontes (ba_in01 206, 106)Copied! at the Teahouse. The Teahouse is located at (ba_maison 105, 65)Copied!. They do not trust Philofontes's documents.
  2. Speak with Dien next. To get a neutral point of view, you'll have to talk with the automatic dolls of the Mansion yourself.
    • Talk to Yunas at (ba_maison 189, 56)Copied!.
    • Talk to Adoren at (ba_maison 249, 161)Copied!.
    • Talk to Skippy at (ba_maison 318, 169)Copied!.
    • Talk to Cleaning Robot at (ba_maison 357, 291)Copied!.
    • Talk to High High at (ba_maison 232, 310)Copied!.
  3. Return to the Teahouse and speak with Lucky. Select Evidence of communication with enterprises.
  4. Speak with Ridsh next.
  5. Speak with Kaya Toss.
  6. Speak with Lucky again.
  7. Meet with Kaya Toss outside (ba_maison 103, 92)Copied! and talk to him.
  8. Speak with Dien next to him (if Dien doesn't show up, talk to Kaya Toss again).
  9. Speak with Kaya Toss again. You will be rewarded with 15  Varmeal Tickets.
  10. Talk with Tatio (ba_in01 205, 109)Copied! at the Teahouse. You will be rewarded with 20  Varmeal Tickets.
  11. You can continue the main quest with Tracking Illusion.


Tracking Illusion

Requirements
Base Level: 130
Starting Point: (ba_in01 205, 109)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Relay Network Repairs
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: 35  Varmeal Tickets
  1. Meet with Lucky right outside (ba_maison 116, 67)Copied! outside the Teahouse.
  2. Look for 5 Manager Betas around the Mansion. The locations can be found on the minimap below:
    • (ba_maison 150, 47)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 221, 193)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 208, 87)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 314, 146)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 207, 276)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 70, 256)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 64, 187)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 275, 313)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 338, 267)Copied!
    • (ba_maison 131, 197)Copied!
  3. Meet with Elena Bolkova at (ba_maison 303, 141)Copied!. You will receive 10  Varmeal Tickets.
  4. Go to the guests' rooms at the top left of the Mansion area, near (ba_maison 100, 320)Copied!.
  5. Once inside, speak with Rebellion to enter the guarded room.
  6. Speak with Elena Bolkova. You will need to get the following items:
    • 10  Fluorescent Liquids
    • 10  Broken Swords
  7. Return to Elyumina inside the room (ba_in01 15, 147)Copied!. You will be rewarded with 10  Varmeal Tickets.
  8. Speak with Erst outside of the room (ba_in01 51, 157)Copied!.
  9. Talk to her again to start the Twilight Garden instance.
    1. Once inside, talk to Erst at the entrance.
    2. Move to the right and a cutscene will trigger. Kill the Heart Hunters and then talk to Erst.
    3. Move to the next location ([email protected] 208, 281)Copied!. Kill the Heart Hunters.
    4. Talk to Erst.
    5. Talk to Amanda and follow her. Talk to her again once she stops walking.
    6. Defeat the Heart Hunters and then talk to the Intruder.
    7. Defeat the Heart Hunter Commanders.
    8. Talk to Amanda and follow her. Talk to her again once she stops walking and watch the cutscene.
    9. Talk to the Intruder again, next to the Repeater.
    10. Go back up a little and talk to Amanda, follow her.
    11. Talk to Erst.
    12. Move to the Hangar to the middle left side area ([email protected] 33, 221)Copied!.
    13. Once inside, approach Faulty and a cutscene will trigger.
    14. Defeat him.
    15. You can leave the area with the left portal.
  10. Speak with Erst outside of the room again (ba_in01 51, 157)Copied!.
  11. You can now enter the real Hangar area (ba_maison 33, 222)Copied!.
  12. Once inside, speak with Smart Ellie (ba_in01 26, 266)Copied!. You'll be rewarded with 15  Varmeal Tickets.
    You have unlocked the Hey! Sweety! daily instance along with 2 of its daily quests.
  13. When you leave the Hangar area, Philofontes and Crux will appear.
  14. Talk to Crux.
    Note: If you chose to depart later, Crux will appear in the guests' room, at (ba_in01 48, 146)Copied!.
  15. Once you get warped to the Prontera Castle, a dialogue will trigger for Nyhill's coronation.
  16. Move to the room below and speak with Nyhill.
    You have now finished Sage's Legacy (17.2) main story quest.
    You can now use  Ep. 17 Storybook on another character.

Checking the Underground Condition

Requirements
Base Level: 160
Starting Point: (ba_2whs01 150, 51)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Gaining access to Varmundt's Mansion
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: 15  Varmeal Tickets
  1. Talk with Chirp inside the Tartaros' Warehouse (ba_2whs01 150, 51)Copied!.
  2. Hunt down 5 Broken Cleaning Robots (they are found on the same map).
  3. Return to Chirp and talk with her. You'll get 3  Varmeal Tickets.
  4. Investigate the Lock at the top right corner of the map, near (ba_2whs01 274, 290)Copied!.
  5. Return to Chirp one more time, you'll get 3  Varmeal Tickets.
  6. Investigate these three locations (marked on your minimap) and you'll get 1  Emergency Key:
    • The first Emergency Key is at (ba_2whs01 110, 218)Copied!.
    • The second Emergency Key is at (ba_2whs01 14, 282)Copied!.
    • The third Emergency Key is at (ba_2whs01 222, 257)Copied!.
  7. Return to the Lock at the top right, you will be warped one floor below.
    You have now gained access to the basement of the Tartaros' Warehouse.
  8. Talk to the Head on the left side of the room, near (ba_2whs02 37, 312)Copied!.
  9. Go through the pile of Junk at (ba_2whs02 31, 50)Copied! for a body and you'll get  Automatic Doll Parts.
  10. Go through the pile of Junk at (ba_2whs02 129, 82)Copied! for an arm and you'll get  Automatic Doll Parts.
  11. Go through the pile of Junk at (ba_2whs02 129, 82)Copied! for legs and you'll get  Automatic Doll Parts.
  12. Go back to the Head at the top left corner and assemble the body. You will get 3  Varmeal Tickets as a reward.
  13. Talk to Chirp back on the first storage floor (ba_2whs01 150, 51)Copied!, you'll get 6  Varmeal Tickets and some experience.
    You have unlocked the First Warehouse Floor Cleanup daily quest.
    You have unlocked the Underground Warehouse Cleanup daily quest.
