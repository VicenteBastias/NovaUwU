Ahoy, ahoy, crew!

Welcome to my guide on hoarding items. This is (or will be over time) a list of items that you should save or keep because they are used in various crafting recipes, class skills, or other important things.
You can keep these items in mind when you randomly drop them, or try and farm them to get some ezpz zeny.
NOTE: You can use Ctrl + F to search specific items names on this page.
