Runestone Skill Type Properties Description
 Turisus Runestone Giant Growth Status Increase
  • Target: Self
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 For 15 minutes, gain the following effects:
  • Increase STR by 30.
  • Add a 30% chance to hit an enemy for 2.5 times the damage while melee attacking.
  • Increase ATK-based physical damage by 250% (125% during PVP). This effect can not stack with Crush Strike and is additive rather than multiplicative.
  • Increases melee physical damage by 15%.
  • The damage bonus now also applies to skill attacks.
  • Endows Dragon Breath with the Holy Element.
 Isia Runestone Vitality Activation Status Increase
  • Target: Self
 For 15 minutes, gain the following effects:
  • Healing received is increased by 50%. (Disabled inside Monster Hunter)
  • Reduces melee physical reflection damage by 50%.

  • Target: Self
  • Var. Cast Time: 2.0s
  • Cooldown: 1.0s
  • Cast can not be interrupted
  • Can damage traps
 Deal damage to all enemies in a 7x7 area around you.

 Urj Runestone Abundance Recovery
  • Target: Self
 For 15 minutes, restore 60 SP every 10 seconds.
 Rhydo Runestone Crush Strike Melee Physical Attack
  • Target: Self
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 Your next physical melee attack will deal massive damage.


  • Once you attack, there is a 20% chance of your weapon breaking. This effect runs out if not used in 30 seconds, or if you change equipment.
  • This rune has a 30 second cooldown.


Damage is calculated using the following formula;

 Nosiege Runestone Refresh Recovery
  • Target: Self
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 Recover 25% of your MaxHP and restore and prevent the following status ailments for the next 60 seconds:
  • Stun / Sleep / Curse / Stone Curse / Poison / Blind / Silence / Bleeding / Confusion / Freeze / Deep Sleep / Burning / Freezing / Crystallize
  • Guillotine Cross Poisons
  • Marsh of Abyss
  • Howling of Mandragora


  • 120 second item reuse delay.
 Verkana Runestone Millenium Shield Protective
  • Target: Self
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 Create shields that absorb any type of damage and lasts for 15 minutes.

There is a 20% chance 4 shields are created, 30% chance that 3 shields are created and a 50% chance that 2 shields are created. Each shield has 1000 HP and excess damage does not carry over.


  • 60 second item reuse delay.
 Lux Anima Rune Lux Anima Status Increase
  • Target: Self
 For 15 minutes, gain the following effects:
  • A melee physical attack has a 15% chance to trigger Storm Blast Level 1.
  • Increases damage against all size enemies by 30%.
  • Increases critical damage by 30%.
  • Increases short range physical damage by 30%.
  • Increases long range physical damage by 30%.
  • Increases Max HP and Max SP by 30%.


  • Endows Dragon Breath with the Shadow Element. Takes priority over  Turisus Runestone.
  • Endows Dragon Breath Water with the Neutral Element. Takes priority over  Asir Runestone.
