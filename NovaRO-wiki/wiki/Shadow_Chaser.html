The Shadow Chaser (SC) is the 3rd job class of Rogues. With this evolution Shadow Chasers mastered their copycat skills and thievery and gained access to Auto Shadow Spell and Reproduce, that lets them be a better copy of someone, and numerous annoying debuffs that makes them a very hated class by many in PvP.
