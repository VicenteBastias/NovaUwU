Berserk Pitcher Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Ally
  • SP Cost: 10
  • SP % Cost: 8% of current
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Range: 9
  • Consumes 2  Berserk Potion
 Throw 2  Berserk Potions at a target and give them the effect of the potion, even if their class can not consume Berserk Potions.

Duration 15 minutes.


  • If this skill is used on a character less than level 85, the potions are consumed and no effect is applied.

Demonstration Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.8s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.2s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 9
  • Consumes 1  Bottle Grenade
 Creates a 3x3 area of effect on the ground that causes melee fire damage to any target that walks in it's radius. Also has a chance to destroy the targets weapon.


  • [Lv 1] : 120% ATK, 3% Chance to destroy the targets weapon.
  • [Lv 2] : 140% ATK, 6% Chance to destroy the targets weapon.
  • [Lv 3] : 160% ATK, 9% Chance to destroy the targets weapon.
  • [Lv 4] : 180% ATK, 12% Chance to destroy the targets weapon.
  • [Lv 5] : 200% ATK, 15% Chance to destroy the targets weapon.
Acid Terror Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 15
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.5s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.5s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 9
  • Consumes 1  Acid Bottle
 Throw an acid bottle and deal damage to a single target. Also has a chance to destroy the targets armor, and cause the Bleeding status.


  • This deals additional damage for each level of Potion Research.


  • [Lv 1] : 200% ATK, 5% Chance to destroy targets armor, 3% Chance to cause Bleeding status.
  • [Lv 2] : 400% ATK, 15% Chance to destroy targets armor, 6% Chance to cause Bleeding status.
  • [Lv 3] : 600% ATK, 25% Chance to destroy targets armor, 9% Chance to cause Bleeding status.
  • [Lv 4] : 800% ATK, 35% Chance to destroy targets armor, 12% Chance to cause Bleeding status.
  • [Lv 5] : 1000% ATK, 45% Chance to destroy targets armor, 15% Chance to cause Bleeding status.

Bio Cannibalize Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 20
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.6s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.4s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 4
  • Consumes 1  Plant Bottle
  • Cast can not be interrupted
 Summon a plant monster on the targeted location. Higher levels lets you summon stronger plants but have lesser duration.


  • [Lv 1] : Summons a Mandragora, 5 plant limit, 5 minute duration.
  • [Lv 2] : Summons a Hydra, 4 plant limit, 4 minute duration.
  • [Lv 3] : Summons a Flora, 3 plant limit, 3 minute duration.
  • [Lv 4] : Summons a Parasite, 2 plant limit, 2 minute duration.
  • [Lv 5] : Summons a Geographer, 1 plant limit, 1 minute duration.
