Walkthrough

While walking around Summer Island, you may feel that your pockets are lighter! As you check them, you might notice that a few Summer Festival Coins are missing...

  1. After noticing you're a few coins short, walk up near the Excavation Expert NPC, by the Ferris Wheel, and talk to Newoz.
  2. Newoz will tell you that the team will await your presence by the Voltz Clicker.
  3. Watch the cutscene
  4. The party will ask for a few items before proceeding. You can buy them in shops around Rune-Midgard!
    •  Adventurer's Suit [0]
    •  Muffler [0]
    •  Shoes [0]
    •  Violin [3]
    • The Adventurer's Suit, Muffler and Shoes can be bought from the Prontera Armor Dealer (prt_in 172, 132)Copied!.
    • The Violin [3] can be bought from the Comodo Weapon Dealer (cmd_in01 128, 165)Copied!.
  5. Return to where the party is and deliver the items to proceed the quest.
  6. Enter the cave, watch the cutscene and kill the three wolves.
    File:Hooligan quest3.png
  7. Now you'll need to participate in the Fishing Tournament and fish the following:
    •  Shark x1
    •  Marlin x3
    •  Salmon x5
  8. After coming back inside the cave, the Hooligan will ask for 1000 Summer Festival Coins.
  9. After that, you can choose one of the hats (listed below).
    File:Hooligan quest4.png
  10. If you're lucky, he just might return the coins he stole from you!
