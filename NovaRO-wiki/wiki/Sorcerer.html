The Sorcerer (Sorc) is a 3rd job class and the final upgrade to Sages. Sorcerers use their unique skills to manipulate the elements and the surrounding environment to their advantage. They provide excellent defensive and offensive support in PVM parties, and capable of solo farming in high-end dungeons. Their AoE skills and utility skills can be devastating in PVP, BG, and WoE environment. Additionally, they can be built as a powerful melee magic damage dealer by utilizing skills like Spell Fist, Double Casting, and Auto Spell. To put it briefly, Sorcerer is one of the most versatile magic-oriented job class.
