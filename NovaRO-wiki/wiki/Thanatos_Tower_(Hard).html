Overview

This page is referring to the Hard version of the Thanatos Tower instance and focusses on the MVP mechanics and gears. For a guide on the regular instance, go here instead.

Skill and Mechanics

Mechanic Condition Description
Ground Drive
Thunderstorm 		Every 7 seconds This is the same mechanic from the regular instance. The MVP does a square shaped Ground Drive around itself, excluding the center. This is then quickly followed by a screenwide Thunderstorm. The damage from Thunderstorm can be drastically reduced by using wind resistance.
Hell Judgement Always, frequent There is a small delay with a red plane before the attack will damage you. This mechanic is deadly and will most definitely kill anyone who isn't prepared for it. Hell Judgement can be managed by ways such as:
  • If close range to the MVP, Safety Wall can block its damage.
  • If far (> 5 cells), Pneuma, Neutral Barrier or Wall of Fog can block or reduce its damage.
  • Neutral resistances can lower its damage, such as Ghostring Card, Deviling Card, Raydric Card, etc. However, unless you have a very high resistance, you will not be able to survive this attack this way.
Storm Gust Always, frequent It deals a lot of damage, but can be tanked. This skill will push your tank around, causing many issues if it's not taken care of. A Land Protector on top of the tank, wearing a Strong Shield or using a corner can work well here.
Seal Summons Below 90%
Every 40 seconds 		An announcement from Thanatos will appear mentioning a seal will be appearing. Each seal has its own buff to the MVP and should be treated as quickly as possible. There is a 5 second delay between the announce and the buff being applied to the MVP. The seals can be destroyed by killing the Barricades surrounding the middle part of the arena.
  • Seal of Protection - This buff makes the MVP immune to 99% damage taken.
    Announce (white): Your dull weapons and amateur magic are meaningless to me!
  • Seal of Magic - This buff applies Magic Mirror to the MVP (magic classes should worry of this one).
    Announce (purple): You're as gullible as a younger me. Look in the mirror. We're the same.
  • Seal of Physical - This buff applies Reflect Shield to the MVP (melee classes should worry of this one).
    Announce (yellow): Hah. Your pathetic attempts will hurt you more than they wear me down.
  • Seal of Speed - This buff applies Agility Up to the MVP and increases its damage.
    Announce (blue): Make haste... I'm getting impatient.
  • Seal of Monsters - This buff spawns up 1 monster near the MVP every 5 seconds, up to a maximum of 5.
    Announce (green): Aargh... I cannot stop... these memories...
Wide Petrify Below 80% This skill is usually not a problem. It can be blocked with Medusa Card if needed.
Gravity Field Below 70%
Every 20 seconds 		Two columns of red squares will intersect where on top of the MVP. This is similar to the Floor is Lava event. If you are hit by the red square, you will be taking some damage every time it moves. You will also receive the Gradual Gravity debuff which will reduce your HP by 5% every second for 10 seconds. While avoiding the incoming red cell is possible, it is generally not worth doing so as you risk dying from Hell Judgement. Those red cells will also remove Land Protector.
Attribute Change Below 60% Just like the original MVP, Thanatos will change element depending of its current HP.
  • Below 60%, it can change to Holy Element.
  • Below 30%, it can change to Shadow Element.
  • Below 10%, it can change to Fire Element.

This buff can be dispelled.

Thanatos Fracture At 75%
At 50%
At 25% 		The MVP will teleport to the center of the area and will stop using many of its skills. Script-based mechanics keep happening, but the MVP itself cannot be damaged. The MVP will also start healing up to 15% of its current HP. During this phase, you will get attacked by Lockon Laser near the center of the arena. To end this phase, you must kill the Thanatos Fragments, which has the same sprite as Broken Thanatos. The Thanatos Fragments can only be killed near the center of the arena. If you lure the Thanatos Fragments too far, a yellow warning will be displayed and the monster will become immune to damage.

The most damaging skill during this phase will most likely be the Gravity Field and the Lockon Laser. The Lockon Laser can be easily ignored with a Land Protector or blocked by Safety Wall or Pneuma (depends on the distance from the MVP to the target; SW for 4 or less, Pneuma for 5 or more).

Rewards

Rewards are obtained only by defeating the boss of the instance, monsters don't drop anything. After defeating Thanatos every party member will gain 50  Shard of Wrath, as well 250  Thanatos Sword Fragment split among party members (in a 8 party setup everyone will obtain 31 Fragments).
