Poison React Damage
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 25:30:35:40:45:50:55:60:45:45
 Gives a 50% chance to auto cast Envenom when hit by a physical attack. Higher skill levels allow for more counter attacks.

If the attack you counter was Poison property, it deals increased damage and it has a 50% chance to poison the target.

This skill will end after the maximum number of counter attacks is reached or after 1 Poison property attack is countered.


  • This works against long ranged physical attacks as well.


  • [Lv 1] : 130% ATK, 1 Counter attack limit, 20 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 2] : 160% ATK, 1 Counter attack limit, 25 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 3] : 190% ATK, 2 Counter attack limit, 30 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 4] : 220% ATK, 2 Counter attack limit, 35 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 5] : 250% ATK, 3 Counter attack limit, 40 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 6] : 280% ATK, 3 Counter attack limit, 45 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 7] : 310% ATK, 4 Counter attack limit, 50 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 8] : 340% ATK, 4 Counter attack limit, 55 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv 9] : 370% ATK, 5 Counter attack limit, 60 Seconds duration.
  • [Lv10] : 400% ATK, 5 Counter attack limit, 60 Seconds duration.

Grimtooth Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 3
  • Range: 3:4:5:6:7
  • Required weapon type: Katar
 While under Hiding status, create jagged spikes from the ground to pierce nearby enemies. Damage is dealt in a 3x3 area of effect around the enemies hit.

Normal monsters hit by this skill will get -50% Movement speed for a few seconds.


  • Level 1~2 is a melee physical attack, while levels 3~5 are ranged physical attacks.
  • This skill does not work with cloaking skills, you must be using specifically Hiding.


  • [Lv 1] : 120% ATK, 2 Cell range.
  • [Lv 2] : 140% ATK, 3 Cell range.
  • [Lv 3] : 160% ATK, 4 Cell range.
  • [Lv 4] : 180% ATK, 5 Cell range.
  • [Lv 5] : 200% ATK, 6 Cell range.
