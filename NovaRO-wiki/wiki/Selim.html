About Selim

How Selim came into existence is still unknown. What has become apparent over time is that this uncanny cat girl possesses an ability to see the unseen with her one red eye. She was supposed to return to her otherworldly master before the onset of Yuletide, but as destiny would have it, lost her identity card during Halloween. Consequently, due to the denied access to the Dimensional Gate, she must now work part-time at the Main Office to fund the paperwork necessary to file for a new ID Card.

When not fine tuning the color on the rarest of hats, she enjoys dishing out bizarre recommendations and practicing bloodcurdling meows.


You can find her on Third Floor of the Main Office also known as the Style Boutique.

During the Trickster King's Invasion event (Halloween), Selim can be found in the Halloween Hub, near (hallohub 156, 220)Copied!.


Below is the list of services she provides in-game.


For each recolor, you get an achievement point in Splash of Color.

Wing recolors

Selim also provides color unlocks for two Halloween-exclusive garments! What is special about this is that, once unlocked, you may freely swap to that color at no cost.

To unlock one Little Devil Wings color of your choice you need 5  Chromatic Stones.

To unlock one Great Devil Wings color of your choice you need 5  Great Chromatic Stones.

Chromatic Stones Exchange

Selim can also exchange your Chromatic Stones into Halloween Coins by selecting Equipment Recolors > Chromatic Stones Exchange.

She will give you:

  • 5  Halloween Coins for 1  Chromatic Stones
  • 8  Halloween Coins for 1  Great Chromatic Stones


Food and Alcohol

Selim can now combine halloween foods into  Gluttony's Feast. For 1 Gluttony, it will cost you:

  • 1 x  Wrath's Power
  • 1 x  Sloth's Apathy
  • 1 x  Pride's Wit
  • 1 x  Lust's Alacrity
  • 1 x  Greed's Jackpot
  • 1 x  Envy's Skill


She can also merge alcohols boosting stats.
50,000 zenys and 6 alcohols of your choice to obtain 1 big bottle that will last 30 minutes instead of 5 minutes.
Eligible alcohols are:

  •  Powerful Jager
  •  Skillful Whisky
  •  Apathetic Stout
  •  Witty Gin
  •  Speedy Tequila
  •  Lucky Rum
