Distorted Crescent Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ally
  • SP Cost: 60:70:80:90:100
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s:1.5s:2.0s:2.5s:3.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 2.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Cool Down: 30.0s
  • Range: 7
  • Cast can not be interrupted
 Increase or decrease your ATK or MATK depending on your current HP or SP.


  • Even HP = Increase ATK.
  • Odd HP = Decrease ATK.
  • Even SP = Increase MATK.
  • Odd SP = Decrease MATK.


  • [Lv 1] : Increase by (Base Level / 3) + 20, Decrease by (Base Level / 3) + 30.
  • [Lv 2] : Increase by (Base Level / 3) + 40, Decrease by (Base Level / 3) + 60.
  • [Lv 3] : Increase by (Base Level / 3) + 60, Decrease by (Base Level / 3) + 90.
  • [Lv 4] : Increase by (Base Level / 3) + 80, Decrease by (Base Level / 3) + 120.
  • [Lv 5] : Increase by (Base Level / 3) + 100, Decrease by (Base Level / 3) + 150.
Ominous Moonlight Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 20:30:40:50:60
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s:1.5s:2.0s:2.5s:3.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 2.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Cool Down: 30.0s
  • Range: 7
  • Cast cannot be interrupted
 Make all healing skills on a target do damage instead.

All healing the target receives will deal damage equal to half the amount that would be healed.

Example: If a target was healed for 100 HP, they would take 50 damage instead.


  • Can not be used on Boss monsters.


  • [Lv 1] : Duration 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : Duration 15 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : Duration 20 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : Duration 25 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : Duration 30 seconds.
