The Mechanic (Mecha) is the 3rd job class of Blacksmiths. Mechanics are powerful and bulky physical damage dealers that are capable of dealing melee and ranged damage to single targets and destroying mobs of enemies. They also have access to a bunch of support skills such as Repair Weapon and Neutral Barrier that let's them play support both on the front and back lines and even while playing a damage dealer role.

Builds

Axe Boomerang

Axe Boomerang builds are very common with new players. It's single target but seeing that it does ranged damage with no cast time and is only dependent on two cheap gears available on npcs to reduce its heavy cooldown of 3 seconds to 1 second -  STR Supplement Equipment [0]/[1] &  DEX Supplement Equipment [1], it's often the skill and build of election for new players that desire to embrace the end game fast.

Players using this skill should focus on ranged damage and axes with high values of weight since it increases the final damage.

Power Swing

Nicknamed by many the new and improved High Speed Cart Ram/Cart Termination, players focusing on this melee skill should focus on STR, DEX, and gears that reduce their skill delay to be able to use it as a DPS skill.

It's a very simple build to use. You use your buffs and walk close to the monster to use the skill until it dies like in the old times of Whitesmith/Mastersmith.

Axe Tornado

One of the few AoE options, although melee, available to a Mechanic although not the most used skill due to its heavy cooldown. Because of that, it's usually seen being used with Magma Eruption to increase its potential dps.

Players using it should focus on VIT and make a wise decision between less cooldown or more damage depending on the weapon being used since the best options for this build are quite limited at the moment:

  •  Tornado Axe [1]
  •  Illusion War Axe [2]

Knuckle Boost

Great ranged and single target skill with no cooldown and delay only based on ASPD. It's seen by many as a better version of Axe Boomerang that is also not limited to an axe as a weapon although this one has a cast bar.

Players using this skill should use two  Reinforced Parts - Gun Barrel [1] to boost its damage and focus on DEX and ASPD to increase DPS even further.

Arm Cannon

One of the other AoE options, although this one is ranged, available to Mechanics.

While a heavy hitter since it ignores the defense of the enemy, the skill has fixed cast time, a cast bar, and cooldown that goes up with the level of the skill used. Players that focus on this should test and check the levels that the skill provide and adapt gears to their needs. Seeing this, there are usually two types of Arm Cannon builds:

  • -100% variable cast that is less dependent on stats but heavily dependent on gears and does less damage
  • insta-cast, or close to, through DEX and INT stats that does more damage but also harder to achieve

Self Destruct

Another AoE option available to a Mechanic that ignores accuracy, does neutral damage, and has a cast bar that can't be stopped.

It's mostly a last resort option, and more often used on WoE since you lose your Mado after using it and has a huge cooldown.

Players using this are also using any other of the builds above and only swap to gears that maximize its damage when really needed.

If you want to make use of this skill at its max you should maximize your HP, SP, and VIT and be sure that when its used, your HP and SP are full.

Stats

Stat Notes
Strength STR increases your brute power. This is a Mechanic's primary damage stat.
It also oncreases your weight limit, which can be important since a lot of Mechanic gears and consumables are quite heavy.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases weight limit by 30
  • Increases weapon damage by 0.5% of your base weapon attack (Melee Weapons)
Agility AGI makes your character hit faster.
It reduces animation delays and increases your ASPD (Attack Speed).
  • Increases your ASPD.
  • 100 total points (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Sleep and Bleeding status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Flee by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Soft Def by 1
Vitality VIT is a character's constitution. It increases your HP, which is useful for tanking.
For Mechanics, this also increases Axe Tornado's damage.
  • 100 total points (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Stun and Poison status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Max HP by 1% of your base HP

Every 2 points;

  • Increases Soft Def by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Intelligence INT is directly related to magic damage. It also increases your SP, as well as let you use/cast skills or spells faster by a smaller amount compared to DEX.
For Mechanics, it's usually a dump stat unless you're trying to squeeze in some more SP or reduce cast time.
  • 100 points total (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Silence status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Max SP by 1% of your base SP
  • Increases MATK by 1.5
  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Dexterity DEX represents a character's focus or concentration. Higher DEX lets you hit enemies more often. The speed at which you cast spell/skills rely on this stat.
This stat also increases the damage of Knuckle Boost, Vulcan Arm, and Power Swing in varying amounts.
  • Slightly increases ASPD

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Hit by 1
  • Increases weapon damage by 0.5% of your base weapon attack (ranged weapons, including instruments)

Every 5 points;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases MATK by 1
  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Luck LUK represents quite a bit of things in this game. Typically, it improves your chances to hit enemies critically. It also increases your Attack, Magic Attack, Flee and Hit by a bit.
Mechanics don't rely on this stat but it's useful to squeeze in some more Attack damage when getting one more point on STR or DEX gets too expensive.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Critical Rate by 0.3

Every 3 points;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases MATK by 1
  • Increases Hit by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Flee by 1

Every 10 points;

  • Increases Perfect Dodge by 1
