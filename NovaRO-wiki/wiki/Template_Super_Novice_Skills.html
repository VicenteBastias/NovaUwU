Provoke Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 3 + Skill Level
  • Range: 9
 Has a chance to provoke a target up to 9 cells away and make it aggressive toward you.

If successful, it will increase the targets ATK, while decreasing their DEF for 30 seconds.


  • The difference between the user's base level and the target's base level will affect the chance of success.
