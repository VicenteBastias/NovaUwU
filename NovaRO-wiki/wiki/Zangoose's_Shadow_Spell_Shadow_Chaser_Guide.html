Introduction

Welcome to the ultimate guide to the magic sword.

This guide aims to give players a path to success making the most of Shadow Chaser's unique Auto Shadow Spell ability. The road you'll walk is a long and arduous one. For the most part of your career as a spell Chaser you will be far weaker than your peers. However, by the end of this guide you'll have the tools necessary to challenge some of the harder content in NovaRO.

Who is this build for?
This build is good if you:

  • Like not having to press too many buttons
  • Enjoy niche builds
  • Don't mind dealing less damage than others

This build is not good if you:

  • Want to see the highest damage numbers
  • Want to defeat the hardest bosses or challenge MH2
  • Are not ready to invest a LOT of effort
