Lullaby Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 20
  • Required weapon type(s): Instrument:Whip
 Sing a lullaby to put nearby enemies in a 9x9 area to sleep.


This skill is an Ensemble skill;

  • It can only be cast if there is another bard/dancer in the same party within 9x9 cells of each other.
  • After casting the skill both performer's will receive Ensemble Fatigue for 10 seconds. (Can not use skills, reduced movement speed, reduced ASPD).


  • The chance to apply the sleep status is reduced by the targets AGI.
  • Boss monsters are immune to Sleep.
