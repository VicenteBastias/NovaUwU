Hey guys you may know me as Longchamp in the game.
A little info about me.
I started playing NovaRo about 4 years ago, I never had any experience with PreRenewal and everything I experienced from starting was pretty much new to me.
Although I played Ragnarok Online when I was like 10 - 12 years old and I never really experienced the game that much since it was very expensive to play at that time.
I remembered I played as a swordsman and reached level 47 just killing monsters in Bayalan Dungeon and stopped from there.

How did I find NovaRo?
I found NovaRo when I searched google for the best RO private servers. I found it on ratemyserver I think? Then it was history from there.

What is my goal in NovaRo?
I do not have any particular goals but just to have fun I think. With my current situation, I find RO as a stress reliever after work. Something I look forward to after a long tiring day.

Thoughts on the future of NovaRo?
I believe this server would do great still in the future because of the hardwork, effort and love of the staff. I appreciate the staff's drive for innovation and their dedication to improving the game even though the original game is going the weird path.
I hope to still be part of this community in the coming years, since we can never know what the future holds for us. I do pray for more success to this server. Cheers!
