Build Guidelines

  • Temporal Circlet Special enchant goes up to Level 2.
  • Bio5 Special enchant goes up to Level 5.
  • Refinements go as high as needed for their effects, and are not fully minmaxed.
  • All builds are "endgame" unless specified otherwise.

Guillotine Cross

Cross Impact

Cross Impact (Katar) - (Last Updated: January 18, 2022)
Stats Slots Equipment
  • Extremely high single target damage.
  • Deals Critical Damage.
  • One of the most efficient solo builds.
  • Is not good at clearly large groups of monsters at once, (lacks strong AoE). Therefore it's not very good at most endgame field farming, it's best used inside instances, or to quickly defeat boss monsters.


  • Cross Impact
    • 0.5 seconds of Cast Delay
    • 0.35 seconds of Cooldown
    • Scales on your Base Level
  • Level 100: 2150% ATK
  • Level 200: 4300% ATK
  • We need -30% After Cast Delay to spam 2.85 skills per second.

Rolling Cutter

Rolling Cutter - (Last Updated: January 18,2022)
Stats Slots Equipment
  • Decent area of effect damage, and spins up to 7.14 times per second.
  • Effective field farming build.
  • Insanely efficient with leeching effects like Blood Sucker.
  • Lacks high single target damage, making it bad for endgame content like instances and Monster Hunter 2.


  • Rolling Cutter
    • Roughly "0.22" seconds of Cast Delay (Technically it should only be 0.2, but this skill is very wonky on NovaRO and has a history of weird stuff like this).
    • Scales on your Base Level
  • Level 100: 450% ATK
  • Level 200: 900% ATK
  • We need -38% After Cast Delay to spam 7.14 skills per second.
