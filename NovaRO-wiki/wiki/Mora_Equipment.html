Mora Equipment

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Mora equipment refers to the armour and weapons that are available via exchange of Mora Coins. They are generally 4-piece level 100 equipment sets for Rune Knight, Guillotine Cross, Ranger, Arch Bishop and Warlock that provide combo effects when worn together. Mora artifacts are bought from the Master of Artifacts and can subsequently be enchanted by the Master of Enchants.

Enchants

Adding an enchant to a Mora artifact costs  1 Mora Coin per slot. Enchants available are different depending on the item. Additionally, some equipment can access another set of enchants when refined to +9 or higher. Enchanting is an entirely safe process, with no risk of losing refines, cards or existing enchants, and the artifact has no chance of being destroyed.

Enchant Types

The available enchants to specific Mora equipment will normally fall under certain type labels. These labels identify the pool of enchants that is available to each Mora equipment piece. Mora artifacts can only receive enchants in their respective type pool at random, with the more powerful effects generally occurring less frequently.

The categories are:

  • Attack - ATK bonuses
  • Critical - Critical rate bonuses
  • Evasion - FLEE and perfect dodge bonuses
  • Strength - HP and DEF bonuses
  • Physical - General physical combat bonuses
  • Ranged - Ranged damage bonuses
  • Assist - General bonuses
  • Healer - Heal power bonuses
  • Spell Ability 1 - MATK bonuses
  • Spell Ability 2 - MATK bonuses that are on average stronger than Spell Ability 1

Many Mora artifacts gain access to an additional enchant type when refined to +9 or higher. However, they can still choose to get an enchant from the item's original enchant type.

Note that not all Mora artifacts have their enchants fall into the above categories. The majority of Arch Bishop and Warlock equipment select from their own pool of possible enchants.

Resetting Enchants

You can reset the enchants of Mora artifacts. Like enchants, resets can only be performed when the equipment is being worn. The artifact can have any non-zero number of enchants to be eligible for resets.

There are two options for enchant resets.

  1. Normal resets cost  1 Mora Coin. However, there is a 20% chance that the equipment will be destroyed.
  2. Safe resets cost  10 Mora Coins.

Empowering

The Warlock and Arch Bishop weapons can be enhanced into empowered forms. To receive an empowered weapon, you must have the corresponding base weapon's refine level at +7 or higher.

When empowering, the weapon will lose its refine level, cards and enchants, and the level requirement will increase from 100 to 130.

Compared to their base weapons, empowered weapons have only 1 card slot as opposed to 2, but have higher weapon MATK and/or ATK, stronger effects and access to a better set of enchants. Empowered weapons also have an additional enchant category available if refined to +9 or higher.

Recycling

You can exchange unneeded Mora equipment for extra  Mora Coins.

Most Mora equipment can be recycled for  3 Mora Coins each. The exceptions are the  Ur's Plate and  Nab Ring, which can only be exchanged for  1 Mora Coin each.

There is no limit to how many items you can exchange a day, and you can sell Mora artifacts using any character.
