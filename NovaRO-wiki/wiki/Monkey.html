Monkey

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The resident yoyo of the Main Office, descriptively called "Monkey" is a mildly hyperactive but extremely talented cheek, specialized in making various coloring agents. This creature may have stopped hopping around the floor after at long last being hired as a Dye Creator, but that did everything but put an end to his mischievousness.

When not doing his usual monkeying around (such as leaving banana peels across the floor in hopes of Miles slipping on them or causing all manner of trouble to the poor Stylist next-door), he enjoys receiving back scratches from hat-happy passersby and conversations centering on avocado distribution chains.

If you listen carefully, you may occasionally recognize him by the characteristic "Top kek!". Although not (to our knowledge) witnessed in the wild, some onlookers theorize this may be the way yoyos have evolved to laugh, while others remain skeptical of the nature of this sound.


You can find him on Third Floor of the Main Office, also known as the Style Boutique.

He provides services such as regular Dyestuff crafting and also Mysterious Dyestuffs crafting.
