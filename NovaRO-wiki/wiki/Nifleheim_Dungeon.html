Niflheim Dungeon is a leveling map for level 200+ players. It has 2 floors, the first one requires level 200 to be acessed and level 240 for the second floor.

Being in a party for greater exp bonuses and having appropriate equipment is recommended. Also, monsters here take more damage from attacks with Holy Element in general.

Besides being a good leveling place, it's possible to obtain new cards and the new Relapse Weapons. Also, all enemies in the dungeon have a 3.0% chance to drop  Etel Dust

Access

Niflheim F1 Entrance

After obtaining at least level 200, to access the dungeon:

  • Go to Niflheim and enter the portal at (niflheim 149, 118)Copied!.


Daily Quests

To enable the dailies, talk to Old Scholar on Eden Academy (moc_para01 250, 306)Copied! . Talk to Tired Looking Dead (niflheim 164, 102)Copied! again and get 3 daily EXP quests for Floor 1 (Lv 200+):

  • He looks cute: Kill 15 Ghost Cube and 15 Lude Gal
    • Reward: 391,064,250 base EXP and 273,744,750 job EXP
  • That's Not an Instrument: Kill 15 Gan Ceann and 10 Brutal Murderer
    • Reward: 268,903,000 base EXP and 188,547,000 job EXP
  • Plays the Piano: Play the Piano at (nif_dun01 140, 202)Copied!
    • Reward: 250,000,000 base EXP and 187,500,000 job EXP


Talk to Tired Looking Dead (niflheim 164, 102)Copied! again and get 2 daily EXP quests for Floor 2 (Lv 240+):

  • Guys with Messy Hair: Kill 15 Disguiser and 15 Blue Moon Loli Ruri
    • Reward: 1,124,618,125 base EXP and 787,232,675 job EXP
  • The Ones with Dolls: Kill 10 Grote and 10 Pierrotzoist
    • Reward: 748,602,525 base EXP and 524,021,750 job EXP
  • Wake Them Up: Talk to the Dead Portraits at (nif_dun02 249, 221)Copied! and (nif_dun02 37, 221)Copied!
    • Reward: 325,000,000 base EXP and 227,500,000 job EXP
