  • Target: Self

New Moon Kick Damage / Special
  • Max Level: 7
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 20 ~ 50
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0sec
  • Cool Down: 1.0sec
 Moon-line skill only.

Strike all enemies around you and enter a New Moon state.


While under New Moon state, gain 15 seconds of invisibility while still being able to move. You will not be detectable by Demon/Insect race monsters, and you can maintain [New Moon] state for up to 7 hits. However, when a detection skill is used, you will be revealed immediately.


  • [Lv 1] : 700% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 2] : 800% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 900% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 4] : 1100% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 1200% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 6] : 1300% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 7] : 1400% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.

Nova Explosion Damage
  • Max Level: 5

Gravity Control Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 80
  • Var. Cast Time: 2.0 sec
  • Cool Down: 20 sec
  • Cast Delay: 0.5 sec
  • Range: 9
 Universe-line skill only.

Cause a target to float helplessly in the air for 5 seconds. While floating, the target can not move or use skills (but they can use items and change equipment), and they cannot be hit by most attacks.

When the duration ends, deal Neutral property fall damage altered by the casters ATK and the target's current weight. If Gravity Control is used on a floating target, the status ends safely.

  • Can only be used in PVP and Siege areas.

Book of Dimensions Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 40
  • Cool Down: 150 sec ~ 30 sec
 Universe-line skill only.

Grant yourself a buff that lasts for 60 seconds. While the buff is active, if you use either one of the following skills, end the buff and gain the corresponding effect:

  • Nova Explosion: For 5 seconds, remove the skill cool down and the skill usage penalty of Nova Explosion.
  • Advent of the Star Emperor: Gain 2 defensive spheres that last for 30 seconds and have a durability equal to your Max SP. All damage received is blocked by the spheres until their durability reaches 0, and excess damage from one attack will not carry over.


  • Can only be used in PVP and Siege areas.
