The Eden Academy is an extended tutorial, that goes beyond just basic game controls. It aims to better acclimate brand-new and returning players to the pacing of the server, provide them with basic information in-game, and smooth out early progression. The Academy was designed to be a once-per-account experience. Players that complete the main quest from the Academy unlock extra, optional lessons and an exclusive shop containing rewards obtained throughout the main quest and potions.

By registering to the Academy and being at least level 100, you'll be able to undertake a final assignment from Academy Headmaster Emme (moc_para01 146, 117)Copied! in order to obtain, refine and enchant Noblesse, Imperial and Grace class sets. These class sets are early-mid game equipment that provides bonuses to certain skills, with two variants per class. Upon completing this final assignment, you'll also be granted access to special shops, allowing you to freely exchange your set for its other variant, and buy extra sets for other classes.

You can register to the Academy by following the entirety of Mom's Tutorial, or by heading to the Academy Main Building (izlude 143, 352)Copied! and talking to Receptionist Jolene (moc_para01 306,303)Copied! inside.

Walkthrough

  1. Navigate to Eden Academy (izlude 143, 352)Copied! (or type @go academy to teleport there).
  2. Talk to Jolene (moc_para01 306,303)Copied!, the receptionist, to register.
  3. Follow Jolene's (and subsequent NPC's) instructions.
    • The Eden Academy questline is well-designed and completely self-contained.
    • As such, a walkthrough would be of very little to no use, and would instead undermine the purpose of the tutorials.
    • Players are strongly advised to read all NPC conversations thoroughly and attentively, which would provide all vital information needed to complete the tutorial itself, as well as to progress later in the game.
      • This applies to returning, veteran players as well, as over time the game system and the server have evolved drastically.
    • If you are ever stuck in the tutorial, before asking for help, backtrack to the last NPC(s) and read the conversations closely again (some NPCs give more detailed instructions the second time you speak to them).
