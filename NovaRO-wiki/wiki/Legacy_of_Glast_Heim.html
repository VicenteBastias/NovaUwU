Legacy of Glast Heim is a custom instance that aims for being more difficult than regular Ragnarok Online content, while still keeping the feeling of the original game - not restricting gear or skills.

Players playing CRIT based builds:

  • If you're farming only the first zones of the first floor for Corrupted Spells, be aware that you need 149 CRIT (249 with double CRIT rate skills) to have a critical rate of 100% since Demented Monk has 245 LUK and is the monster with the highest value of LUK there.
  • If you're doing the entire instance you need 164 CRIT (264 with double CRIT rate skills) instead since Root of Corruption has 323 LUK and is the monster with the highest value of LUK.

Players playing HIT based builds:

  • If you want to hit everything, you need 835 HIT on you char because of the Demented Archer.
  • If you can ignore the archers because there are more members in the party to deal with them, you just need about 725 because of Root of Corruption.
  • If you're just farming the first initial zones for corrupted spells, 579 should be more than enough.
