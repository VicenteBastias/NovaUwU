Singleplayer Games

If all your friends go home early because of too much fun, worry not! There are some 1 player games around to keep you entertained until the final summer activity is ready!


Poring Patterns


Can you handle these Porings without Unripe Apples?

You will be shown three porings.
In order to win, you must remember their order and type, and repeat them back!
In every round, the number of Porings shown will increase!

Poring Stacker


Stack them up!

The object of this game is to line up the Pouring with the Jejeling.
Each round, you have 15 seconds, and the Pouring will move faster and faster!

Dice Challenger


Are you up for a challenge?

You must roll higher than the Challenger in order to win the game!
Luck may be at your side!

Crystal Chaos


Crystal-fy your eyes with this Chaotic challenge!

Do you think you have good eyes and incredible focus?
Follow the shadows under the Crystals, and when they stop moving,
select which shadow you think it stopped on!

Grand Master


Thought rock, paper, scissors was a game of chance?

Challenge the old man for a good old-fashioned Rock, Paper, Scissors game!
In order to win, you must defeat the Grand Master, and if you do, you will be rewarded with Festival Coins!
Scissors beats Paper, Paper beats Rock, and Rock beats Scissors!

Countdown


Are you good at counting?

In order to win, you must click the NPC after counting down to 0.
At the start of the game, he will begin counting.
Continue counting in your head, and click him again when the timer reaches 0!

Poring Matcher


Test your memory at the Poring Matcher!

You must match all the Porings in pair in order to win the game,
but don't stall, you only have one minute!
