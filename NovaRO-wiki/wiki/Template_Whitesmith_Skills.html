Weapon Refine Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
 Allows you to refine weapons.

After level 50, each Job Level will increase the success rate of refining by 0.5%. (At Job level 70 you will have +10% chance).


  • This lets you refine weapons with a higher success rate than NPC refining, but you can not use special ores such as  Enriched Oridecon with this skill.
  • Any Job level Mechanic will have the same +10% refining bonus as a Job Level 70 Whitesmith.


A different ores is used depending on the weapon level.

  • Weapon Level 1 :  Phracon
  • Weapon Level 2 :  Emveretarcon
  • Weapon Level 3 :  Oridecon
  • Weapon Level 4 :  Oridecon


  • [Lv 1] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +1.
  • [Lv 2] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +2.
  • [Lv 3] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +3.
  • [Lv 4] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +4.
  • [Lv 5] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +5.
  • [Lv 6] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +6.
  • [Lv 7] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +7.
  • [Lv 8] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +8.
  • [Lv 9] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +9.
  • [Lv10] : Allows you to refine weapons up to +10.
