General Report Guidelines

  • If you have any video footage, make sure that the character name is clearly visible in your video. We cannot issue punishments if we cannot confirm the identity of the player.
  • If you only have screenshots, make sure the screenshots include all relevant information such as player name and full conversation. Cropped screenshots showing nothing but a single line of chat message is usually not enough for us to make any decisions.
  • For bug abuse and cheats, include specific details about your suspicion. Best practice is to include a "normal" and "suspicious" comparison to illustrate why you believe this player's activities are suspicious.
  • Wherever possible, include the date of the offense so we may retrieve relevant information from our logs where applicable.
  • For videos longer than 5 minutes, please include timestamps that are relevant to the report.
  • Short .rrfs are accepted. If they are longer than 5 minutes, please convert them into a video.

Player Report on Bug Abuse

  • If the reporter has any inkling of the actual bug being abused, we expect a bug report alongside the player report. If a bug report already exists, please include a link to it in your player report. Failure to submit both at the same time will result in potential punishment for “withholding information”.
  • If you are previously in the same guild/team as the person you are reporting and the action has persisted before and after you are no longer teammates, you will be expected to explain why it was not reported while you were still teammates. "I did not notice" is not a reason we are willing to accept.
    • We heavily discourage "witch-hunting" people who are no longer your teammates out of personal conflict of interest. If you are going to assess the validity of a player's gameplay, we expect it to be done fairly and timely.
  • This means we expect you to review your teammates' gameplay regularly and report anything suspicious at the earliest possible time. Failure to do so will result in potential punishment for "withholding information" as well.

Player Report on Cheats

  • Original video footage must be clear enough to assess the cheat in question. Videos which hide hotkeys, effects, item usages, are sped up or slowed down, or otherwise rendered impossible to properly assess items and skills being used will not be accepted.
  • Analysis videos must include original footage alongside the edited analysis.
  • Similar to bug abuse reports, you will be expected to report cheats as early as you can. You will be expected to explain why you did not report a previous teammate if this player is no longer your teammate.

Player Reports on Bad Behavior

  • Reports on WoE or BG griefing must be accompanied by a video in order for us to properly assess the player’s activities.
  • For harassment and toxic behavior, only submit reports where you truly feel offended. Each player is allowed 3 strikes where their reports are deemed too trivial for any punishments. Any future trivial reports submitted by the same player will result in a warning sent to the reporter.
    • We do not endorse the action of "fishing for punishment" by sending numerous trivial reports against the same player hoping to get them banned. Players found doing so will be punished instead.
    • If context is needed to help us understand why a seemingly trivial report is worth a punishment, please include this context in your report. Do not assume that the staff has the knowledge to understand your culture, background, history, or any other relevant information.
  • Only submit reports if you intend to pursue the case. Once a report is submitted, you may not retract it or otherwise attempt to resolve it privately. If the player you are reporting is found to be breaking any rules, they will be punished accordingly, even if they have apologized to you privately.

General Appeal Guidelines

  • Appeals are expected to be submitted within one week after the punishment was issued. Appeals submitted after a week will not be accepted.
  • Keep the appeal content to your own case. The resolution of any previous case is not to be used as a precedence of how your case should be treated.
  • Rules are not up for your interpretation. Claims of corruption, unfairness, bias, or anything of this nature do not belong to an appeal and will not be entertained.
  • If you have suspicion that a GM has abused their power in issuing your punishment, you may submit an accountability claim.
  • If an honest mistake was made in the assessment and punishment of your case, kindly include any relevant information to help us arrive at this conclusion. Throwing tantrums and using dramatic words usually does not help us understand the mistake and delays the possible resolution of your case.
    • We are only human and our systems are not perfect. Sometimes mistakes can certainly happen. But we try our best to be neutral and fair towards any information that is available to us and we expect the same from our players.
  • Wherever possible, include the date of the offense so we may retrieve relevant information from our logs where applicable.

Bug Abuse Appeals

  • Explain in detail how to replicate your behavior if there was no bug abuse involved.
  • In the case a bug was abused, the staff reserves the right to reject your appeal even if you "did not know it was a bug". What constitutes bug abuse is not up for player interpretation.

Cheat Appeals

  • Include original, unedited gameplay footage for review. This can be either unedited video recording or full .rrf replays. We cannot accept edited footage or footage that does not include your own gameplay.
  • If any ahk scripts are involved, include your ahk scripts for review.
  • Failure to do so will result in a failed appeal.

Bad Behavior Appeals

  • We generally do not accept appeals for bad behavior. However, if you feel the need to include your side of the story, you are welcomed to submit a ticket. Note that this most likely will not result in a reduction in your current punishment. It may or may not result in additional punishment to other parties where we see fit.


All reports and appeals must be submitted on the forums. Reports and inquiries submitted through discord/DMs will be ignored.
