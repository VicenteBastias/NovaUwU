Overview

I'm making this guide as a way to show a different build for wanderer. As you know wanderer is a class that is mostly played as support or better said as Service for You slave or just for the combine songs you can use with a minstrel. Most of the time wanderer is outshine by the dances you can normally use with the minstrel leaving it to just auto-follow and have service up or the mana song up. The reason I'm doing this is so people can besides using those songs make a wanderer that could deal a good damage to help kill faster; of course this is NOT a build where you will be able to deal more DPS than other classes like rebel or ranger for example just to give support in killing faster.


Severe Rainstorm Build

Notable Skills

Archer Skills

  Improve Concentration: A great skill that boost both AGI and DEX which are the most important stats for Severe Rainstorm.

  Arrow Shower: A skill that works on pushing mobs to keep them away from you but still inside the AoE of Severe Rainstorm.

Dancer Skills

  Service for You: Most wanted skill for support as it helps a lot of classes that have high SP Consumption.

  Fortune's Kiss: Good Skill to help increase the Critical Rate for auto-attack builds dependant on crits.


  Drum of Battlefield: Increases ATK and DEF needs to be used in pair with a Minstrel.

  Invulnerable Siegfried: Increases the Elemental Resistance of party members most be used with a Minstrel.

Gypsy Skills

  Arrow Vulcan: Arrow Vulcan with  Floral Mic of Aigu [2] and  Floral bracelet of Aigu [1] help increase the damage of this skill by a lot making it a great single target damage skill to snipe those mobs that survive from Severe Rainstorm.


Wanderer Skills

Solo Skills

  Severe Rainstorm: Primary skill to deal damage, has a high AoE, and with the use of  Bow of Storms [1]. Its damage formula comes from [{(DEX + AGI) × (SkillLv ÷ 5)} × (BaseLv ÷ 100)]%.

  Swing Dance: Increases movement speed and ASPD. Usually a skill used mostly when soloing as the movement speed increase will help you lure mobs and be able to do more easy Hit and Run tactics.

  Voice Lessons: To be able to use different songs and dances together, increases sp and gives a constant sp recovery. If used in combination with night sparrow hat reduces fixed cast time*skill level.

  Frigg's Song:Support skill thatincreases Max HP and gives a HP regen per second. Helps a lot with survival.

  Death Valley: A support skill that helps revive party members.

  Lover Symphony:(Optional) Situational skill to increase hard MDEF.

  Siren's Voice: Makes mobs non-aggresive towards your character.

Chorus Skills

  Dances with Wargs:Increases ATK, ASPD, and reduces fixed cast time. Better used in parties and needs another performer class.

  Song of Mana: Most be used with a performer class and gives mana regen great support skill for parties.

Stats

  • Strength(STR)

Str doesn't really increases much for wanderer classes but its good to have some STR in order to increase weight limit so more arrow quivers of different elements can be carried. If you have Gym Pass on wanderer it's completely possible to leave this stat untouched and use stats somewhere else. Recommended 1-10.

  • Agility(AGI)

Stat that increases ASPD, reduce animation delay of skills and helps both in offensive as defensive since it increases the flee rate of your character;but something that needs to be considered is that its also important in the increase of Severe Rainstorm damage since AGI belongs directly into the formula of the skill itself. Recommended 90-120.

  • Vitality(VIT)

Increases the Max HP of your character by 1 point = 1% Max HP. Its usually recommended to have 100 VIT for Stun immunity. Recommended 88 Base VIT +12 (job level 60).

  • Intelligence(INT)

Improves your sp pool and mostly directly affects the effect of Gypsy Kiss(Service for you) the higher Int you have the more effect of sp boost and sp cost reduce will have. Recommended 90 Base INT +10 (job level 60)

  • Dexterity(DEX)

Your primary stat that is used to increase your damage output. It is recommended to have at least 120 DEX to be able to use  Temporal DEX Boots [1]. This stats also directly affects a lot the damage your Severe Rainstorm does. Recommended 120.

  • Luck(LUK)

Good overall stat that now increases ATK/MATK every 3 points. Also this increases the amount of crit rate that Lady Luck (Fortune Kiss) gives. Recommended 30-50.

Build Reference

In this section I will talk about the stats I suggest in having for the build and pointers on some of the skills most used. Will also cover the equipment that one could use at some points which will work until its possible to get Best in Slot, but all depends on the kind of game play you wish to do on your Wanderer.

Severe Rainstorm Build

Archer & Gypsy

  

Common Skill for Use:

  Improve Concentration: Boost Severe Rainstorm damage with the increase of both AGI and DEX.

  Arrow Shower: The usage of this skill is to push and keep mobs inside the AoE of Severe Rainstorm making it easier for you to stay away from damage and adding some more damage and increasing the chances of HE (HawkEye) proc.

  Double Strafe: This will be your main skill to level up at low levels as its damage and spam ability its enough to snipe mobs.

  Service for You: Great skill since it reduces SP cost highly used in parties as support. have high SP consumption.

  Fortune's Kiss: Increases Crit rate depending on the performer LUK.

Severe Rainstorm Build:

Gameplay Explanation

  • Best way to increase the damage of this build is by building as most DEX as possible that's why  Essence of Evil DEX3 will be mostly used it some slots in your equipment.
  •   Swing Dance as this skill will help you with the mobility of your character. Severe Rainstorm build works mostly by kiting mobs and keeping them in the AoE while keeping yourself far from their hit range.
  •   Siren's Voice A good skill to keep mobs away from you while pulling and kiting as it will make them non-aggresive towards the user. With Voice lessons, job level and skill maxed it might reach a 80% chance of working in a 13x13 area.
  •   Severe Rainstorm Your main DPS skill with a 11x11 AoE and consumes 20 arrows per cast. While its effect is in place you might be able to use other skills like Arrow Shower to keep mobs inside the AoE or to push them away from you. One important point is that HawkEye affects this skill by a lot with those +200 DEX effect. For example a normal damage of 8k-10k can increase to 20k-30k if not with elemental advantage if not the increase is even more; also needs to be max level to be able to do the max damage possible as character level affects its damage.

Stat:

  • STR: 1
  • AGI: 120 - Gives you flee and more important its part of the formula to increase the damage of   Severe Rainstorm.
  • VIT: 91 - Rounds up to 100 with job bonuses for stun resistance and increases your MHP.
  • INT: 90 - Increases your SP pool and also affects the effectivity of your   Service for You.
  • DEX: 120 - Main stat that increases Wanderer ATK, gives  Temporal Dex Boots [1] bonus and its the second main stats that increase the damage of   Severe Rainstorm.
  • LUK: 35 - Increases ATK, MATK but mostly used to not suffer from the - LUK of  Essence of Evil DEX3.

Key Equipment:

  •  Temporal Dex Boots [1] For the bonus of ranged attack 5%, +200 DEX from enchant HawkEye, and enchanted with Expert Archer.
  •  Giant Snake Skin [1] Best in Slot with enchants of DEX.
  •  Bow of Storms [1] Best in Slot for weapon as it reduces the cooldown of your main DPS skill also increases ranged by 30%.
  •  Night Sparrow Hat [1] Best in Slot as long as its refined to +7/+9 increasing the damage of Severe Rainstorm by 25% at +7 and SP cost by 50% at +9.

Build Reference

In this section I will talk about the stats I suggest in having for the build and pointers on some of the skills most used. Will also cover the equipment that one could use at some points which will work until its possible to get Best in Slot, but all depends on the kind of game play you wish to do on your Wanderer.

Severe Rainstorm Build

Archer & Gypsy

  

Common Skill for Use:

  Improve Concentration: Boost Severe Rainstorm damage with the increase of both AGI and DEX.

  Arrow Shower: The usage of this skill is to push and keep mobs inside the AoE of Severe Rainstorm making it easier for you to stay away from damage and adding some more damage and increasing the chances of HE (HawkEye) proc.

  Double Strafe: This will be your main skill to level up at low levels as its damage and spam ability its enough to snipe mobs.

  Service for You: Great skill since it reduces SP cost highly used in parties as support. have high SP consumption.

  Fortune's Kiss: Increases Crit rate depending on the performer LUK.

Severe Rainstorm Build:

Gameplay Explanation

  • Best way to increase the damage of this build is by building as most DEX as possible that's why  Essence of Evil DEX3 will be mostly used it some slots in your equipment.
  •   Swing Dance as this skill will help you with the mobility of your character. Severe Rainstorm build works mostly by kiting mobs and keeping them in the AoE while keeping yourself far from their hit range.
  •   Siren's Voice A good skill to keep mobs away from you while pulling and kiting as it will make them non-aggresive towards the user. With Voice lessons, job level and skill maxed it might reach a 80% chance of working in a 13x13 area.
  •   Severe Rainstorm Your main DPS skill with a 11x11 AoE and consumes 20 arrows per cast. While its effect is in place you might be able to use other skills like Arrow Shower to keep mobs inside the AoE or to push them away from you. One important point is that HawkEye affects this skill by a lot with those +200 DEX effect. For example a normal damage of 8k-10k can increase to 20k-30k if not with elemental advantage if not the increase is even more; also needs to be max level to be able to do the max damage possible as character level affects its damage.

Stat:

  • STR: 1
  • AGI: 120 - Gives you flee and more important its part of the formula to increase the damage of   Severe Rainstorm.
  • VIT: 91 - Rounds up to 100 with job bonuses for stun resistance and increases your MHP.
  • INT: 90 - Increases your SP pool and also affects the effectivity of your   Service for You.
  • DEX: 120 - Main stat that increases Wanderer ATK, gives  Temporal Dex Boots [1] bonus and its the second main stats that increase the damage of   Severe Rainstorm.
  • LUK: 35 - Increases ATK, MATK but mostly used to not suffer from the - LUK of  Essence of Evil DEX3.

Key Equipment:

  •  Temporal Dex Boots [1] For the bonus of ranged attack 5%, +200 DEX from enchant HawkEye, and enchanted with Expert Archer.
  •  Giant Snake Skin [1] Best in Slot with enchants of DEX.
  •  Bow of Storms [1] Best in Slot for weapon as it reduces the cooldown of your main DPS skill also increases ranged by 30%.
  •  Night Sparrow Hat [1] Best in Slot as long as its refined to +7/+9 increasing the damage of Severe Rainstorm by 25% at +7 and SP cost by 50% at +9.
