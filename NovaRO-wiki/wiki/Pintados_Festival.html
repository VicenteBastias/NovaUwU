Pintados Festival

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Pintados Festival is a quest introduced in Port Malaya. At the end of this quest, players will have the option to create powerful accessories, the Tattoos, with the remains of the MvPs in Port Malaya.

Quest Prerequisites

While you don't need these quests to open the Festival, you will need them to open the instance and fight the MvP in question.

Cautious Village Bakonawa Extermination Nurse in Port Malaya Secret in the Wood

Festival quest

To start the Pintados Festival, go and find the Festival Helper Boy found outside Port Malaya (/navi ma_fild01 179/260).

He will need the different materials to start the festival. The materials required are :

5000x Mysterious Flower dropped by: Tiyanak, Manananggal, Mangkukulam & Wakwak, all at 100%. You can also get them from Jejeling at a measly 10%
5000x Jejellopy dropped by Jejeling at 100% chance
300x Dyestuff (of any colour) Obtainable from Old Blue Boxes, Old Purple Boxes, Gift Boxes and from the Dyestuff maker

Festival Tattoos

Once the Festival has started, you can talk to the Tattooist (/navi ma_fild01 172/223) to create the Tattoos.

You will then be able to choose which Tattoo you want to create. Each tattoo has a different set of materials, which you will get while fighting them.

Bakonawa Agimat Tattoo: 10x Ancient Grudges, 1x Piece of Bakonawa Spirit, 1x Bakonawa Doll (All dropped by Bakonawa).

Bangungot Agimat Tattoo: 10x Ancient Grudges, 1x Piece of Bangungot Spirit, 1x Bangungot Doll (All dropped by Bangungot).

Buwaya Agimat Tattoo: 10x Ancient Grudges, 1x Piece of Buwaya Spirit, 1x Buwaya Doll (All dropped by Buwaya).

Creating the Tattoos is a difficult process which takes time and concentration.

ALWAYS select the option “… … …”.

Otherwise you’ll interrupt the process causing the tattoo creation to fail, and lose the crafting materials you gave the Tattooist. After a long process, the Tattooist will complete your tattoo and is now ready for use.
