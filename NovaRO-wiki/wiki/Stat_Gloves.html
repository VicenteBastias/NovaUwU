Walkthrough

The glove merchant.
  • Complete the Encounter in a Strange Land quest until Step 8.
    • NOTE #1 - If you need  Splendide Coins to do step 7, do Troublesome Fairies until Step 2 to get 2 to buy the needed items.
    • NOTE #2 - You need to do the Ring of the Wise King quest and equip the  Ring of the Wise King to buy them.
  • Do the Eclage Daily Quests for  Splendide Coins.
  • Talk to Armor Merchant Naphara /navi ecl_in01 66/95 to obtain your chosen type of glove.


Slotting

The slotting NPC.

You can choose to slot your stat gloves. However by doing this, it has a chance to break your equipment.

  1. Go to Eclage @go Eclage
  2. Talk to Slot Expert. /navi ecl_in01 64/97
  3. The price is 5  Splendide Coins. Choose Activate a Slot. Choose the type of Glove you have. It must be in your inventory.
