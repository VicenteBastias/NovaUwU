Amicitia Dungeon is a leveling map for level 215+ players. It has 2 floors, the first one requires level 215 to be acessed and level 230 for the second floor.

Being in a party for greater exp bonuses and having appropriate equipment is recommended. Also, monsters here take more damage from attacks with Poison Element in general.

Besides being a good leveling place, it's possible to obtain new cards and the new Fortified Weapons. Also, all enemies in the dungeon have a 3.0% chance to drop  Etel Dust
