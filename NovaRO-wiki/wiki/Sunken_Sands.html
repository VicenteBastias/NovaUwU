The Sunken Sands (or Ancient Juperos 4) is a solo-only instanced map, where players will face monsters with incredible toughness. You can enter the map by talking to Josiah (jup_fild03 314, 117)Copied! or Dutch (jup_fild03 318, 113)Copied!. Entering the map will remove some active buffs (so alternate accounts buffs are not really an option).

Inside the map, you will encounter two types of monsters: Pestilent and Primordial. Depending on which NPC you've entered the instance with, you'll be granted a damage bonus and a damage penalty for each type of monster.

  • If you enter the instance by talking to Josiah, you will gain a 300% damage bonus against Pestilent monsters and a 90% damage reduction against Primordial monsters.
  • If you enter the instance by talking to Dutch, you will gain a 300% damage bonus against Primordial monsters and a 90% damage reduction against Pestilent monsters.


Rewards

Monsters inside the instance drop the following items:

  •  Sands of the Lost at 70% drop (all mobs)
  •  Pestilent Cluster at 30% drop (Pestilent mobs)
  •  Primordial Cluster at 30% drop (Primordial mobs)
  •  Radiant Crystalline at 3% drop (all mobs)

Monsters

All monster have a 10% damage modifier, so they take 90% reduced damage.
