Kaite Support
  • Max Level: 7
  • Target: Ally
  • SP Cost: 70
  • Var. Cast Time: 4.8s ~ 2.4s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.2s ~ 0.6s
  • Range: 9
 Grant a buff which will reflect magic spells back at the source, except for ground targeted magic spells. While Kaite is active, physical attacks received will deal 400% more damage.

When Kaite is granted to normally ineligible targets via Spirit of the Soul Linker, the chance of reflects occurring is reduced by 50%.


  • Healing magic reflected will not heal the source.
  • Ka- active skills can only target yourself, family members or other Soul Linker type classes.
  • High level monsters and boss type enemies cannot have their magic reflected, but players can have magic reflected regardless of level.
  • Does not stack with Assumptio.


  • [Lv 1] : Reflect 1 attack, Duration 1 minute.
  • [Lv 2] : Reflect 1 attack, Duration 2 minute.
  • [Lv 3] : Reflect 1 attack, Duration 3 minute.
  • [Lv 4] : Reflect 1 attack, Duration 4 minute.
  • [Lv 5] : Reflect 2 attack, Duration 5 minute.
  • [Lv 6] : Reflect 2 attack, Duration 6 minute.
  • [Lv 7] : Reflect 2 attack, Duration 10 minute.
