Introduction

I embrace the oongaboonga and so should you. PVP information is scarce but screw it, let's make it plentiful. As you can see, I fund a variety of different classes. Some are very solid while some are extremely garbage. Please note that the "What G1 is using" builds are what I am currently using. With higher refines, better enchants, and newly released gear, builds are subject to change and this wiki page might not be 100% accurate/BiS. Enjoy :)

PLEASE NOTE: Some things such as After Cast Delay and Attack Speed may not be the best for you depending on your ping, fps, etc. Please test accordingly to find your ideal setup.
