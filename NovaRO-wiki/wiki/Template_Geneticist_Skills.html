Special Pharmacy Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 12
 Allows you to create more advanced potions and items.



  • [Lv 1] : 600 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 2] : 580 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 3] : 560 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 4] : 540 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 5] : 520 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 6] : 500 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 7] : 480 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 8] : 460 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv 9] : 440 "Specific Value".
  • [Lv10] : 420 "Specific Value".


  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 400, creates the maximum number of potions allowed.
  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 300, creates 3 potions below the maximum allowed.
  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 100, creates 4 potions below the maximum allowed.
  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 1, creates 5 potions below the maximum allowed.
  • If Creation < Difficulty, creates 6 potions below the maximum allowed.


  • "Item Rates" are unknown.
  • Level 10 produces 12, 9, 8, 7 or 6 items depending on the brewer's stats.


Creation = INT + (DEX ÷ 2) + LUK + Job_Level + Random[30, 150] + (Base_Level − 100) + (Potion_Research_Level × 5) + (Full_Chemical_Protection_Level × Random[4, 10])


Difficulty = Specific_Value + Item_Rate

Cart Cannon Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 40:42:46:48:50
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s:1.5s:2.0s:2.5s:3.0s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 7:8:9:10:11
  • Requires a push cart
  • Required ammo type: Cannonballs
  • Required ammo amount: 1
 Shoot a cannonball from your cart at a target to damage them and all nearby enemies.


  • Damage from this skill is forced to the element of the cannonball equipped.
  • Damage is increased based on INT, Base Level and Cart Remodeling.
  • This skill treats Hard DEF as Soft DEF.


  • [Lv 1] : 250 + (20 x Cart Remodeling skill level)% ATK, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 2] : 500 + (40 x Cart Remodeling skill level)% ATK, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 750 + (60 x Cart Remodeling skill level)% ATK, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 4] : 1000 + (80 x Cart Remodeling skill level)% ATK, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 1250 + (100 x Cart Remodeling skill level)% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.


Damage is based on the following formula;
Damage (ATK) = [Base_Damage + {Int * 2 / (6 - Cart Remodeling skill level)}]%

Mixed Cooking Support
  • Max Level: 2
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 5:40
 Allows you to create exclusive foods.


  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 30, 10~12 dishes are prepared.
  • If Creation > Difficulty by at least 10, 10 dishes are prepared.
  • If Creation < Difficulty by exactly 10, 8 dishes are prepared.
  • If Creation < Difficulty by at least 30, 5 dishes are prepared.
  • If Creation < Difficulty by at least 50, the cooking fails.

Item Rates are unknown.


  • [Lv 1] : Let's you create 1 food at a time.
  • [Lv 2] : Let's you create 10 foods at at time.


Creation amount is based on this formula;
Creation = (Job_Lv ÷ 4) + (DEX ÷ 3) + (LUK ÷ 2) Difficulty = Random(30, 150) + Item_Rate

Howling of Mandragora Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
