Combinations refers to a Monster Hunter: The Savage Coast mechanic or behavior. It is only applicable to High Rank Hunts.


By using various items obtained through Foraging and Fishing, adventurers can craft a plethora of consumables that are useful tools during High Rank Hunts.

All the recipes below can be seen in-game, through the  Hunter's Gathering Map Device and crafted with the help of a  Combination Kit.
