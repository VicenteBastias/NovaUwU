Introduction

Hello all my dear Ninja players, first of all, welcome to your way to became a true Ninja. Let me be your Sensei.

This guide was made with my diary journey like Kagerou that i want to share with you. So, that will be based on what i know and use.

We dont have any updated guide on NovaRO about ninja class. So I decided to make this to guide your journey too to became a Sannin.

My nickname on NovaRO is "Churrasquin Sannin", feel free to ask me any of your questions on game

This guide is based on server i’m playing – NovaRO.

  • Important : !This guide is currently in progress!

About guide

  • As i mentioned above, this guide is based on my experience with the class, so i'm going to address only what i know.
  • This guide will focus on Throwing Kagerou (Togatana) and Cross Slash build. This guide can be used to Oboro too, but it's not recommended as you will not be able to use Shadow Warrior
  • In this guide , i will try to share my experience in Farming , Leveling and Equipments obtaining progress.

Class Overview

  • Kagerou/Oboro is the highest upgrade in Ninja class Ninja branch.
    • Ninja -> Kagerou or Ninja -> Oboro
    • Reminder : It is highly recommend to change class and maximum its job level before changing to Kagerou/Oboro class.
  • Most of Kagerou/Oboro skills require items to be able to use them.

Late-game Card Suggestion
Card Card type Notes

 Headgear +10 ATK and a chance to increase +10 Perfect dodge for more defensive option, but this card is kinda rare to obtain.

 +10 ATK, same as  Duneyrr Card but has a chance to drop poison herb that may trouble your inventory, but it's not really a problem.

 Add +2% ATK which is better than  Duneyrr Card and  Dark Pinguicula Card when your ATK > 500.

 Weapon Add +10% Ranged Physical Attack which is a cheap options when saving money for better ones.
  •  Armed Guard Soheon Card
 Gives +10 ATK and if its equipped on a dagger weapon and refine is +10 or more add ASPD + 1 and refine to +14 or more add ASPD + 1. It shines when you combo it with cards of its set:  Sewage Waterfall Card and  Dried Rafflesia Card.
  •  Contaminated Wanderer Card
 Gives 30% Physical Attack against Medium and Large Size monsters. This card is useful in most cases because most of normal monsters and Bosses are of those two size. Actually best card with  Armed Guard Soheon Card to put on weapon.

 Garment The best option for damage at the moment, provide 1% Ranged Physical Attack per 10 base DEX. Since Ninja does have at least 120 base DEX in order to use  Temporal Dex Boots [1].

 If your Ranged Attack Damage bonus is already too high, you will want to boost some %ATK since the formula of damage is multiple of different modifiers.
  •  Raydric Card
 A survival option when you really need it, it's effect even further increases thanks to the combo with  Contaminated Raydric Card.
  •  Brown Rat Card
 Since level 200 is now unlocked, more Status Points can now be obtain, 120 on 3 stats are available. If your Ranged Attack Damage bonus is already too high, you will want to boost some raw ATK since the formula of damage is multiple of different modifiers. This card also provide some ASPD to reduce animation delay.
  •  Rigid Nightmare Terror Card
 Footgears This card itself is useless unless you pair it with  Nightmare Terror Card for +10% ATK. But you should remember that this card may cause you troublesome when you need another option in armor/footgears.

 Survival option , provide fresh +10% MaxHP.
  •  Matyr Card
  •  Blut Hase Card
 Provide 3% ATK, 5% Ranged Physical Attack without any drawback. However, this card is hard to obtain.
  •  Outrageous Cookie Card
 A good card for damage boosting but it requires high refine level and will cost you some MaxHP
  •  White Porcellio Card
 Provide +20 ATK. Simple but good.
  •  Infinite Vagabond Wolf Card
 Provide +10 ATK. Additional +10 ATK at +7 refine level and +15 ATK at +9 refine level. Can combo with  Infinite Chimera Card for more boost.
  •  Dried Rafflesia Card
 This card is useless when standing alone, but shines when combo with  Sewage Waterfall Card and * Armed Guard Soheon Card, which bonus +20% + 5% every 3 refines of footgears Kunai Explosion and Cross Slash damage. Remember this card is only good with combo.

 Accessory Currently the best combo for boosting damage output. They also provide nice AGI and STR.

  •  Sewage Waterfall Card
 Strong card that simply gives 10% Kunai Explosion and Cross Slash damage and 10% MaxHP to improve your survivability.

Generally this is the set you'll want to make before anything else.
