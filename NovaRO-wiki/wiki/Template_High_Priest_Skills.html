Assumptio Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ally
  • SP Cost: 10 + (10 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.4 + (0.4 x Skill Level)
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.1 + (0.1 x Skill Level)
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 9
 Increase a single targets Hard DEF and healing received for a short time.


  • Assumptio can not be cast if the caster is inside a Guild Castle/WoE map.
  • It does not reduce incoming damage from skills that inflict fixed damage, DEF piercing damage, and MDEF piercing damage.
  • Lower level Assumptio can not refresh the duration of higher level Assumptio.


  • [Lv 1] : +50 Hard DEF, +2% Healing received, Duration 20 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : +100 Hard DEF, +4% Healing received, Duration 40 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : +150 Hard DEF, +6% Healing received, Duration 60 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : +200 Hard DEF, +8% Healing received, Duration 80 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : +250 Hard DEF, +10% Healing received, Duration 100 seconds.
Basilica Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 70 + (10 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 3s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Cool Down: 1.0s
 Increase the your Holy property physical and magical damage on Shadow and Undead property enemies.


  • Note that this damage only applies to these specified properties of enemies.


  • [Lv 1] : +5% Physical Holy damage, +3% Magical Holy damage, Duration 60 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : +10% Physical Holy damage, +6% Magical Holy damage, Duration 90 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : +15% Physical Holy damage, +9% Magical Holy damage, Duration 120 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : +20% Physical Holy damage, +12% Magical Holy damage, Duration 150 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : +25% Physical Holy damage, +15% Magical Holy damage, Duration 180 seconds.
Meditation Passive
  • Max Level: 10
 Permanently increase your Max SP, natural SP regeneration, and the Healing amount for some skills.


  • The skills that receive a healing increase is Heal, Coluceo Heal, Highness Heal.


Skill Healing only applies to the specified skills.

  • [Lv 1] : +1% Max SP, +3% Natural SP regeneration, +2% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 2] : +2% Max SP, +6% Natural SP regeneration, +4% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 3] : +3% Max SP, +9% Natural SP regeneration, +6% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 4] : +4% Max SP, +12% Natural SP regeneration, +8% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 5] : +5% Max SP, +15% Natural SP regeneration, +10% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 6] : +6% Max SP, +18% Natural SP regeneration, +12% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 7] : +7% Max SP, +21% Natural SP regeneration, +14% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 8] : +8% Max SP, +24% Natural SP regeneration, +16% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv 9] : +9% Max SP, +27% Natural SP regeneration, +18% Skill Healing.
  • [Lv10] : +10% Max SP, +30% Natural SP regeneration, +20% Skill Healing.
Mana Recharge Passive
  • Max Level: 5
 Permanently reduces the SP cost of your skills.


  • [Lv 1] : -4% SP Cost.
  • [Lv 2] : -8% SP Cost.
  • [Lv 3] : -12% SP Cost.
  • [Lv 4] : -16% SP Cost.
  • [Lv 5] : -20% SP Cost.
