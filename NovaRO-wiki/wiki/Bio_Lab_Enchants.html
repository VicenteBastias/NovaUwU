Guard & Weird Old Man.

Will of Warrior


Secret tunnel & Sorcerer.
  1. Warp to Dungeons > Bio Lab. Go to the second floor.
  2. Go to the south-east dormitories @navi lhz_dun02 206/33. (See map on the right.)
  3. There will be a tunnel in the south-west corner.
  4. Enter the tunnel, go south, east and enter the warp.
  5. Talk to the Sorcerer @navi lhz_cube 233/24 and choose Here you are, sir.


There are a few rules you must keep in mind:

  • You can enchant your items with up to 2 random Enchants (hidden 3rd and 4th slots).
  • Enchants can be reset separately.
  • You must be wearing the equipment to enchant it.
