Monster Hunter Alterations refers to a Monster Hunter: The Savage Coast mechanic or behavior. It is only applicable to High Rank Hunts.

Mechanics

During High Rank Hunts, a set of modifications takes place compared to regular Ragnarok Online content.

Hate

Hate icons
  • Unlike regular monsters, Great Monsters have an Hate system, similar to other MMORPGs. The player with most Hate will be the main target of the Monster.
    • Your current Hate value is indicated by an icon, while you're engaged with said monster. Blue for the player with the least Hate and scaling to Red, for the player with most Hate.
    • Dealing damage and healing generate Hate. The Tank and the Damage Roles have special tools for Hate manipulation.

See more: Hate

Functionality and Damage

  • There is a reduction on the maximum number of skills that can be cast per second.
  • The innate +40% Critical Damage modifier is halved.

Defenses

  • Players on the Savage Coast have -10% to Neutral Resistance, and -15% to All Elemental Resistances.
  • Maximum Resistance to any element has a hard cap of 70%.
  • Maximum Resistance to any race has a hard cap of 90%.
  • Maximum Resistance to any size has a hard cap of 90%.
  • Flee is reduced by 20%.
  • Perfect Dodge is halved.

Healing

  • Healing values inside the island are reduced by 75% and further decreased on 2-Star Hunts. This penalty is partially negated by the Healer Role.
  • Mechanics riding a Madogear are able to be healed by regular means.

Movement

  • All movement skills are disabled on the island.
  • Movement speed has a hard cap, normalizing it between different classes. Using a Dash Juice allows you to reach higher movement speed values.

HP and SP regeneration

  • All regular HP consumables are disabled.
  • All regular SP consumables are disabled.
  • Players have a base SP regeneration of 3% per second. HP regeneration is unchanged.

Shadow Gear

  • Select Shadow Gear have their effect doubled inside the island. For a full list, check: High Rank Shadow Gear.
    • All Shadow Gear with this property include a notice in their description.

Elemental Table Changes

The elemental table in Monster Hunter is flattened overall, but still retains its particularities (such as Fire always dealing more damage to Earth). This change affect monsters and players alike.
