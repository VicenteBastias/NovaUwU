Fishing refers to a Monster Hunter: The Savage Coast mechanic or behavior. It is only applicable to High Rank Hunts.


Fishing is one of the three gathering activities that can be done on the Savage Coast.

Unlike its two Gathering counterparts, you can also fish outside of the island, next to any bed of water.

For Master Fishermen (Fishing Enchant level 3), the types of fish you encounter will change depending on the map type you fish in:

  • Fishing in The Savage Coast will result in a higher chance of finding Large Fish.
  • Fishing in Towns will result in a higher chance of finding Small Fish.
  • Fishing in Fields or Dungeons will result in an even split between Large and Small Fish.

Note: Aqualings will only spawn on the Savage Coast, you can not spawn them in fields, towns, or dungeons.

Fishing Tasks

Tasks for Level 1 Fishing

Oscar, Fishing Instructor
  • To start fishing talk to Oscar, the Fishing Instructor.
  • He gives you a fishing rod, and the ability to start fishing (You now have level 1 fishing).

Tasks for Level 2 Fishing

  • Oscar tells you to fish 10 times, talk to him again to turn in the task. He gives you 1  Azure Sea Star.
  • Fish 15 more times to get the next task.
  • He tells you to catch 15 Slimy Eels. When you turn in the task Oscar gives you 2  Psychoserum.
  • Fish 75 more times to get the next task.
  • He now tells you to mash  Insect Husk and  Slimy Eel together using  Combination Kit
    •  Insect Husk and  Combination Kit can be obtained through Foraging.
  • Mash 5  Insect Husk and 5  Slimy Eel with your  Combination Kit to make 5  Dash Juice.
  • Bring your 5  Dash Juice to Oscar. He gives you "Good Rod" (Level 2 fishing). You can now use Jumping Bait, Juicy Bait, and Lively Bait.

Tasks for Level 3 Fishing

  • Fish 50 more times to get the next task.
  • Oscar tells you to defeat 2 Aqualings. (This step may take some time to complete, as they spawn at a low rate while fishing)
    • Aqualings spawn at low chance after catching a fish.
    • To defeat an Aqualing you must press "Interact" near it 4 times. Note that Aqualings will run away from you.
  • Talk to Oscar after completing the task. He gives you x5 of a 500 point fish of your choosing.
  • Talk to Oscar again, he tells you to catch 30 fish. (If you have 30 fish in your inventory you can talk to him right away {Unsure on specific type of fish})
  • After completing the task, Oscar gives you x3 of a 1000 point fish of your choosing.
  • Oscar asks you to bring him 5 tonics.
  • After completing the last task. Oscar gives you "Super Rod" (Level 3 Fishing). He also gives you x30 Tonics of your choosing.
  • You can now catch 1000 point fish

How to Fish

  • Talk to Oscar, the Fishing Instructor to get the fishing skill.
  • Use Choose Bait in your Misc tab of skills.
  • Stand near an area of water, and use "Interact" to cast a line.
  • When the bobber starts shaking spam the Interact skill, when the bobber stops moving, stop spamming Interact.
  • Continue this until you catch a fish.

Making Tonics

Mack Arell, Tonic Brewer

To make tonics you trade in the fish you catch to Mack Arell. You can find him at the Fishing Camp.

Each fish trades in for points towards a specific stat. The NPC will store your points for you.

You can trade 3000 points of one stat to make 3~10 tonics of that same stat.

Every Tonic has a 10 minute duration, and can only be used during High Rank Hunting missions.
