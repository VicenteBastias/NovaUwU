Banquet for Heroes

Learning About the Families

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prontera 161, 179)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): New World Access Quest
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Honor Token
  1. Speak with the Royal Errand Boy (prontera 161, 179)Copied! in Prontera to start the quest. He will give you a  Banquet Invitation.
  2. Go in the Prontera Castle (@go woe, then walk north). Upon entering the castle, Chamberlain Bell will ask to follow him.
  3. Walk towards the left wing and enter the portal. Keep going forward to enter your room and Chamberlain Bell will be waiting for you inside.
  4. Speak with Chamberlain Bell, he asks you meet with all the members of the other families. You can always come back to him to know which family you haven't talked to yet.
    1. The Heine (left wing, bottom room):
      Walk near the table and a cutscene will start. Speak with Kronecker G. Heine afterwards.
    2. The Nerius (left wing, middle room):
      A cutscene will trigger when you leave the Heine room. Enter the Nerius room and speak with Spica Nerius.
    3. The Walthers (left wing, top room):
      Speak with Ian Walther and then Isenhonor Walther.
      You can now start The Walthers storyline.
    4. The Wigners (right wing, bottom room):
      Speak with Levuiere Wigner and then Jurgen Wigner.
      You can now start The Wigners storyline.
    5. The Richards (right wing, middle room):
      Speak with Butler for the Richards and then Po Richard right next to him.
      You can speak with Po Richard again to unlock the The Royal Richard daily quest.
    6. The Geoborgs (right wing, middle room):
      Enter the Geoborgs's room and a scene will trigger. Speak with the Queen and select Ask about the Geoborg family.
      You can now start The Geoborgs storyline.
    7. The Roegenburg (right wing, top room):
      Speak with Maximilian Roegenburg and then Wolf Roegenburg.
      You can now start The Roegenburgs storyline.
  5. Once you've visited all the families, come back to your room and talk with Chamberlain Bell. He will reward you with 10  Honor Token and some experience.
  6. You can continue the quest with the Ritual of Blessing.


Ritual of Blessing

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: Chamberlain Bell
Prerequisite(s): Learning About the Families
Rewards
Experience: 10,000,000 (Base) and 10,000,000 (Job)
Items: 20  Honor Token
  1. Bell has asked you to participate in the event and meet with Spica in the Banquet Hall. On your way there (prt_cas 174, 163)Copied!, you will trigger a cutscene.
  2. Go find Spica Nerius in the Banquet Hall (prt_cas 106, 240)Copied! and she'll ask you to follow Nyhill since he was acting strange.
  3. Leave the Banquet Hall and on your way back to your room, you will trigger another cutscene.
  4. Go in the room right below the cutscene to meet with Skia Nerius and Nyhill.
  5. Speak with Nyhill in the room. You'll have to go back to your room and speak with Chamberlain Bell afterwards.
  6. Talk with Chamberlain Bell and Nillem will appear next to you. After the cutscene, you'll be warped to the Past Memories of the Royal Family.
  7. While in a party, speak with Mage Nillem and choose Generate instance to attend the Ritual of Blessing. Then talk to the Ritual of Blessing warper.
    1. Speak with Skia at the entrance to start the ritual.
    2. Take your position in the empty space, on the right side.
    3. Enjoy the cutscene...! Walk up a bit to watch the important part.
    4. The Ritual of Blessing is under attack by intruders, fight them off.
    5. Speak with Kronecker G. Heine to finish up the quest and then leave the room by walking down towards the exit.
  8. Back in the Past Memories of the Royal Family, you'll see all the family members waiting for you.
  9. Speak with Ian Walther to know more about what's going on.
  10. After the members leave, talk to Mage Nillem. He wants you to take a look at the royal document Kronecker was looking at.
  11. Speak with Lindt the Librarian and then talk with Mage Nillem again.
  12. Leave the Past Memories of the Royal Family by speaking with the Guardian Knight on the left side. Select Get out now and you'll be warped into the prison.
    You need to exit the map via this Guardian Knight and Overwatch Terketh in order to proceed the quest, otherwise, talking to Chamberlain Bell will make Mage Nillem warp you back to the Past Memories of the Royal Family Room
  13. Talk to Overwatcher Terketh to get you out of the cell.
    You can talk with him again to be warped back inside the Past Memories of the Royal Family at any moment.
  14. Go back to Chamberlain Bell in your room and tell him what happened.
  15. You'll be invited to the Nerius family, go to their room and speak with 'Spica Nerius'.
  16. Go visit the Heine in the room below. Speak to Kronocker G. Heine and listen to what he has to say. You'll be rewarded with 20  Honor Token and some experience.
  17. You can continue the quest with Room of Consciousness.


Room of Consciousness

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: Chamberlain Bell
Prerequisite(s): Ritual of Blessing
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items: 30  Honor Token
  1. Summon the Court Mage Nillem by talking with Chamberlain Bell back in your room.
  2. Speak with the mage and he will warp you in the Past Memories of the Royal Family once more.
  3. Talk to the mage again and this time, he will open a Dimension Warper that you have to use to go in the past.
  4. Talk to Nillem and he will connect you to the Room of Consciousness. Speak to the Swaying Space when you're ready.
  5. You will be warped into the Room of Consciousness. The mechanics of this instance are explained in its own page.
  6. Once you've completed the instance, speak with Nillem again. He'll allow you to move to the Prontera of the past!
    You have gained access to the Invaded Prontera. Simply walk to the portal at the end of the corridor.
  7. Speak with Nillem in the Invaded Prontera and he will warp you back to the current Past Memories of the Royal Family.
  8. Speak with Nillem in the room and Kronecker G. Heine will appear on his left side.
  9. Speak with Kronecker G. Heine and tell him what you've seen. You will be rewarded with 30  Honor Token and some experience.
  10. You now have to wait for Nillem to recover before he can connect to the Room of Consciousness once more.
    You have unlocked the Room of Consciousness daily quest.


Invaded Prontera

  1. To reach the Invaded Prontera from the past, go in the Prontera Castle inside your room.
  2. Speak with Chamberlain Bell and summon Nillem. He will warp you to the Past Memories of the Royal Family.
  3. Speak with Nillem again and he will make a Dimension Warper appear. Use it to go back in time.
  4. Head towards the left corridor and use the portal below and you'll be warped to the Invaded Prontera map.

Alternatively, you may use the Warper > Instances > Sky Fortress

The Royal Families

The Roegenburgs

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 85, 127)Copied!
Items: 12  Soft Silk
Prerequisite(s): Learning About the Families, step 4
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items:  Prontera Badge
10  Honor Token
  1. After speaking with Wolf Roegenburg (prt_cas_q 96, 186)Copied!, go to the Wigner's room.
  2. Speak with Isaac Wigner (prt_cas_q 95, 9)Copied! (he will appear once you walk close to Jurgen). He will give you a  Sample of New Business Item.
  3. Go back to the Roegenburg's room and this time Myer Roegenburg will appear.
  4. Speak with Wolf Roegenburg and give him the password "1618". He will then ask you find more about the rumors in Prontera.
  5. Find a group of kids in Prontera near (prontera 271, 68)Copied! and speak with any of them. They will give you the following request:
    • 12  Soft Silk
    • Hunt 33 Majoruros
    • Bring the  Rune-Midgartz History Book
  6. The  Rune-Midgartz History Book is obtained from the Uptight Librarian (prt_cas 343, 67)Copied! in the Library (Banquet Hall > Bottom right room).
  7. Speak with the Uptight Librarian and answer her questions, the answers are:
    • In memory of defeating Morocc.
    • 7 royal families attended it.
    • Geoborg and Walther.
    • You mean the Geoborgs?
  8. Return to the kids once you've completed their request.
  9. Speak with Wolf Roegenburg in his room and he will give you a  Top Secret Document.
  10. Go back to the Wigner's room and deliver the document to Isaac Wigner (he will appear once you walk close to Jurgen).
  11. Go back to Wolf Roegenburg and he will give you a  Prontera Badge.
  12. Find Helmut Roegenburg at the Border Post (yuno_fild01 242, 175)Copied! and speak with him.
  13. Go back to the Roegenburg's room and speak with Maximilian Roegenburg.
  14. Talk to the kids in Prontera again (bottom right).
  15. Go back to the Roegenburg's room and speak with Maximilian Roegenburg. He will reward you with 10  Honor Token with some experience.
    You have now unlocked the The Dress Dilemma daily quest.


The Wigners

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 85, 127)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Learning About the Families, step 4
Rewards
Experience: 25,000,000 (Base) and 25,000,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Honor Token
  1. After speaking with Jurgen Winger (prt_cas_q 95, 9)Copied!, talk to Levuiere Wigner.
  2. Speak with Katrin Wigner, she's worried and tells you that her jewelry box is missing.
  3. Speak with the Maid (prt_cas 322, 216)Copied! in the Prontera Castle's kitchen (top left from the Banquet Hall). She'll give you 3  Poring Loofah and then ask you to clean up the kitchen in exchange for some information.
  4. Go near the Dark and Damp Wall ((prt_cas 337, 212)Copied! and you will need the following items:
    •  Holy Water
    •  Mould Powder
  5. Go near the Sticky Wall (prt_cas 337, 197)Copied! and you will need the following items:
    •  Orange
    •  Alcohol
  6. Go near the Pitch-black Floor (prt_cas 309, 215)Copied! and you will need the following items:
    •  Lemon
    •  Fine Sand
  7. Return to the Maid and she'll imply it was the Furniture Deliveryman who took the jewelry box.
  8. Speak with the Furniture Deliveryman in Prontera (prontera 68, 68)Copied!. He says he's hungry and will ask you to bring 3  Beef Head before he can tell you more.
  9. Once you've gathered enough, talk to the Furniture Deliveryman again. He tells you he doesn't know anything about it.
  10. Go back to the Wigner room and speak to Jurgen Wigner. There was a misunderstanding and you learn that Isaac was the one who took the jewelry box to hide it.
  11. Katrin is still upset and to make up for it, you'll be asked to bring the following items for her:
    •  Witherless Rose
    • 10  Sea Stone
      The Sea Stones are obtained from killing Medusa in the Comodo dungeon.
  12. Return to Jurgen and he'll reward you with 10  Honor Token and some experience.
    You have unlocked the The Maid and Jurgen quest.

Prerequisite(s): The Wigners
Rewards
Experience: 25,000,000 (Base) and 25,000,000 (Job)
Items: 4  Honor Token
  1. Speak with Jurgen Winger (prt_cas_q 95, 9)Copied! again and help him with the Maid situation.
  2. Go back to the kitchen and speak with the Maid (prt_cas 322, 216)Copied!.

The Royal Head Chef

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas 319, 211)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Learning About the Families, step 4
Rewards
Experience: 35,000,000 (Base) and 17,500,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Honor Token
  1. Speak (or just walk near) to the Royal Chef (prt_cas 213, 177)Copied! right in front of the Banquet Hall. He tells you the Royal Head Chef has been looking for you.
  2. Meet with the Royal Head Chef in the Prontera Castle's kitchen (prt_cas 308, 200)Copied!. She ran out of sauce and needs your help to prepare some more.
  3. Go speak with the Royal Sauce Master (prt_cas 319, 211)Copied! and follow his instructions. You can follow The Perfect Sauce guide for this part.
  4. Return to the Royal Head Chef with the sauce and you can now start cooking the meal!
  5. Get in line next to the other chefs (the empty spot in front of the Royal Head Chef).
  6. She asks you to bring the Grilled Griffon to the dining room, to the south west of the banquet hall (prt_cas 360, 123)Copied!.
  7. After serving the Grilled Griffon, Tamarin will want to speak with you.
  8. Talk to Tamarin (near the table upwards, (prt_cas 361, 142)Copied! and he'll tell you he's currently helping the Royal Head Chef as well.
  9. Go speak with Dew Rianne (prt_cas 111, 235)Copied! in the Banquet Hall and tell him Tamarin won't make it to the Banquet.
  10. Speak with Magi right next to Dew.
  11. Speak with Mark Ishar (prt_cas 82, 268)Copied!, he's upstairs on the left.
  12. Speak with Alice right next to Mark.
  13. Return to Tamarin and he will ask you to collect 15  High-Class Dish. These are obtained from killing the Plates in the dining room.
  14. Once you've collected enough, talk with the Dishwasher in the kitchen, behind the Royal Head Chef.
  15. Speak with the Royal Head Chef and you will be rewarded with 10  Honor Token and some experience.
    You have unlocked the Royal Head Chef dailies.


Cards

The following cards are dropped by mobs that spawn in Prontera Underground Prison and Invaded Prontera. You can access Prontera Underground Prison after completing Restricted Sector quest, and Invaded Prontera can be accessed after completing Room of Consciousness quest.

Card Type Description
Watcher Card Armor Card ATK +30.

Drains 5 SP when using physical attacks.

Taffy Card Accessory Card ATK +1%.
Frozen Wolf Card Accessory Card MATK +1%.
Immortal Legion Card Garment Card HP and SP Recovery -100%.

Restores 50 HP and 5 SP to the user when killing a monster with melee attacks.

Drains 1000 HP and 100 SP when unequipped.

Zombie Guard Card Shield Card SP Recovery -100%.

Restores 1 SP to the user when receiving physical attacks.
