Madogear License Passive
  • Max Level: 5
 Learn the basic skills to be able to ride in a Madogear. Inside the madogear you will have increased damage and decreased movement speed at lower levels.

Inside Madogear;

  • You will have increased HP recovery.
  • You are immune to some movement increase and decrease skills.
  • You are unaffected by some recovery skills and strip gear skills.


  • [Lv 1] : +15 ATK, -40% Movement speed.
  • [Lv 2] : +30 ATK, -30% Movement speed.
  • [Lv 3] : +45 ATK, -20% Movement speed.
  • [Lv 4] : +60 ATK, -10% Movement speed.
  • [Lv 5] : +75 ATK, -0% Movement speed.
