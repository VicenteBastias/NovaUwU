Festival Painter

The Festival Painter is an NPC that is able to recolor a selection of Summer Festival hats.
In order to recolor a hat players must bring the painter 500  Summer Festival Coins and 1  Rainbow Gemstone, which can be found in King's Grotto treasure chests.
Once you bring the required materials to the Painter, he will change a hat of your choice to a random color. There is a low chance that your hat will become a rare color.
Each hat has recolor counter, when this counter reaches 0, the hat can no longer be recolored.
 Golden Pearl can be used to guarantee a hat will become a rare color and also reset the recolor counter. Golden Pearls can also be found rarely in Grotto's treasure chests.
