Earth Grave Damage

  • Cool Down: 5.0s
  • Range: 9
 Raise the ground in a targeted area stabbing enemies with Earth property magic damage. There is also a chance to cause the Bleeding status to targets hit.

  • [Lv 2] : 10% Chance to cause the Bleeding status, 7x7 Area of Effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 15% Chance to cause the Bleeding status, 7x7 Area of Effect.
  • [Lv 4] : 20% Chance to cause the Bleeding status, 9x9 Area of Effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 25% Chance to cause the Bleeding status, 9x9 Area of Effect.


Damage is based on the following formula;
Damage (MATK)% = [{(SkillLevel × INT) + (SeismicWeapon_Level × 200)} × (BaseLevel ÷ 100)]

Damage is based on the following formula;
Damage (MATK) = [{(Skill_Level × INT) + (FrostWeapon_Level × 200)} × (BaseLevel ÷ 100 )]%

Crystallization status duration is based on the following formula;
Duration of Effect = [Base_Duration − (Target_BaseVIT ÷ 10)]
