Rosary in Mouth

Full detailed quest about how to create the  Rosary in Mouth.

  • In the Warper NPC, select Specials, Churches, Hugel and enter.
  • Inside go to the upper left side room and talk to Allyria
  • She seems busy and will only talk to you "after the half-moon". Let's complete Half-Moon in the Daytime.
  • After successfully complete the instance, go back to her and she will talk about an item, a familiar name (Sorin), and that familiar name having it where her spirit resides. Or at a place special to that familiar name.
  • After some research, you will find that what she was talking about this two places (pick one):
    • Where her spirit resides
    • Special place @go 47 > /navi monk_in 23/92
  • There you will find an interesting cursed trinket.
  • Go back to Allyria and she will talk about the trinket and how she can purify it. She will ask you for some items to help her:
    • A regular  Rosary [0]
    • Some  Holy Elemental Converter
    • Way too much  Holy Water
  • Bring 1 Rosary, 1000+ Holy Water and 10+ Holy Elemental Converter. It will take a few tries to get it done.
  • Unfortunately this wasn't enough and she asked us for stronger items to complete the ritual:
    • 100  Level 5 Heal Scroll
    • 50  Shining Holy Water
    • 50  Citrin
    • 5  Gold
    • 1  Rosary [1]


Congratulations, you got a fresh Rosary in Mouth!
