New Basic Skill Passive
  • Max Level: 1
 Adds all the effects of a Novices Basic Skill into a singular point.
  • Enables Trading
    Allows the trading of items with other characters. Right click on a character to initiate.
  • Enables Emotions
    Alt+(0~9) and Ctrl+(1,-,=.)
    Alt+L opens additional Emotion icons.
  • Enables Sitting
    Sitting doubles HP/SP Recovery Speed.
    Press the Insert key or type /sit.
  • Enables Chat Room Creation
    Alt + C Opens a Chat Room or click the Chat button in the Basic Information Window.
  • Join a Party
    Character can join a party.
  • Enables Use of Kafra Storage
    Allows use of Kafra Storage, or the Secondary Storage (Doram storage NPC).
  • Organize Party
    Create a party by typing /organize [Party Name]
    Set party options in the Party Window (Alt+Z).

Hide Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Cool Down: 15.0s
 Hide from enemies inside a bush.
  • You can be discovered by Demon, Insect, and Boss monsters. Similar to thief's Hiding skill.
  • Sight, Ruwach, Detect, and Attention Concentrate will not reveal you.
  • If you are knocked back while hiding, you will be revealed.
  • Cast can be interrupted if you are hit during the activation of the skill.
