Energy Coat Support
  • Platinum Skill
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
  • Fixed Cast Time: 5.0s
 Use your SP as a shield to reduce damage from all incoming sources.

The closer your SP is to maximum, the higher the damage reduction, but more SP will be consumed every hit as well.


  • [Remaining SP 81~100%]: -30% Damage taken, 3% SP Consumed every time you take damage.
  • [Remaining SP 61~ 80%]: -24% Damage taken, 2.5% SP Consumed every time you take damage.
  • [Remaining SP 41~ 60%]: -18% Damage taken, 2% SP Consumed every time you take damage.
  • [Remaining SP 21~ 40%]: -12% Damage taken, 1.5% SP Consumed every time you take damage.
  • [Remaining SP 1 ~ 20%]: -6% Damage taken, 1% SP Consumed every time you take damage.
