Giant Faceworm Snake Skin Chances

WARNING: The algorithm I've written down here is taken from a script that is most assuredly different from Nova implementation. If I were to suspect the changes, it would be that the miniboss worms on Nova use the Queen's drop algorithm, except no chance of double drop, the time is altered to 2 minutes, and the stats are from 1-5. The Queen is also guaranteed to drop 2 worm skins on this server.

As such, there are potentially more differences. Use this only as a rough idea, not be-all-end-all truth. 

   Dark Faceworm:

   If time <= 120 seconds:
       70%:
           No slot, refine +2~+5, two +stat enchants of +1~+5
       30%:
           50/50 slot, refine +4~+6, special enchant, two stat+ enchants of +1~+5
   If you were slower:
       70%:
           No slot, refine +0~+3, one +stat enchant of +1~+5
       30%:
           50/50 slot, refine +2~+4, two +stat enchants of +1~+5

   Queen:

   If kill time <= 270 seconds:
       20% chance of 2 GFS drops, 80% of one
       20%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +9~+11, special enchant, two stat+ enchants of +1~+7
       30%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +5~+9, two stat+ enchants of +1~+7
       50%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +3~+7, one stat+ enchant of +1~+7
   If you were slower:
       5%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +9~+11, special enchant, two stat+ enchants of +1~+7
       20%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +5~+9, two stat+ enchants of +1~+7
       75%:
           50/50 has slot, refine +3~+7, one stat+ enchant of +1~+7

Magic Attack Calc Damage Flow

MATK = (StatMATK + weaponMATK + equipmentMATK) x MATKRate

Contrary to irowiki, MATKRate is only the total sum of bonus bMatkRate on your equipment and cards. It does not include racial, elemental, size or class modifiers, which are instead done after applying the skill multiplier, though since it's all multiplication the result is the same.

Mystical Amplification applies before skill ratio.

  1. Base Damage = minMATK~maxMATK
  2. Multiply in skill ratio.
  3. Add in card fix and equipment modifiers
    1. Sum magical racial modifiers (bonus2 bMagicAddRace), multiply into card/equipment mod
    2. Sum magical element modifiers (bonus2 bMagicAddEle), multiply into card/equipment mod
    3. Sum magical size modifiers (bonus2 bMagicAddSize), multiply into card/equipment mod
    4. Sum magical class modifiers (bonus2 bMagicAddClass), multiply into card/equipment mod
    5. Multiply damage by mod, then add product into damage.
    6. Do the same as the first four card/equipment mod step, but for penalties (bonus2 bMagicSubX).
    7. Multiply damage by mod, then subtract product from damage.
  4. Mutiply damage by sum of skill damage bonus (eg, Crimson Rock damage +X%), then add product into damage
  5. Apply target's skill-specific damage reduction
  6. Reduce target MDEF if PC can ignore some of the MDEF of target
  7. Reduce damage by MDEF:
    Hard MDEF = percentage reduction, Soft MDEF = flat reduction.
    Damage * ( 1000+hMDEF ) / ( 1000+hMDEF*10 ) - sMDEF
    Monster MDEF is Hard.
  8. Apply status effects (Lex Aterna, Stoneskin, etc)
  9. Apply any custom server stuff

Mora Enchant Odds

Reference: https://github.com/rathena/rathena/blob/5f20c0ce0cbe4bc22b8bf1df9c36c0ef9a480ef3/npc/re/merchants/enchan_mora.txt#L466

Staff (roll range 2847)

  • BREAK chance: 855/2847, roughly 30%
  • Strongest enchants set (any duplicate or combination of MATK+2% and INT+4, required to empower): 8/2847 (roughly 0.28%)
  • Decent "common" enchants (INT+1 and mATK+1% or INT+2): 1984/2847 * 1/4 (to get INT+1) * 1/2 (to get mATK+1% OR INT+2) = 248/2847 (roughly 8.7%)
  • Best common enchant (INT+1 and mATK+1%): 124/2847 (roughly 4.3%)
  • Empower-eligible enchant average trials: 356
  • Staves broken: 107
  • Mora Coins lost: 107*40 (staff costs 10, randomly 1 of 4) + 356*2 = 4992
  • +7 trial average (using enriched ori): 1 / 0.344064, roughly 3 trials
  • Total cost for empower eligible staff: 14976 Mora Coins

In short, not worth it. These odds are clearly more meant for places with a safe to +7 cert.

Because Staff not worth it, the other 3 pieces aren't either. Boots have to compete with Temp INT/DEX any way, where it will always fail.
