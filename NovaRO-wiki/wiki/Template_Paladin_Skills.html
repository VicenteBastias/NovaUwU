Sacrifice Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 100
 Sacrifice 9% of your Max HP for each time you are attacked, to reflect damage back to the target attacking you.

The damage dealt to the enemy depends on the amount inflicted on to you.


  • This will work for up to 5 attacks before needing to be recast.
  • This skills damage is forced to the Neutral property.


  • [Lv 1] : 100% Damage reflected.
  • [Lv 2] : 110% Damage reflected.
  • [Lv 3] : 120% Damage reflected.
  • [Lv 4] : 130% Damage reflected.
  • [Lv 5] : 140% Damage reflected.
Gospel Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost:
    • (Lv 1~5) : 80
    • (Lv 6~10) : 100
 Apply random buffs to allies and debuffs to enemies within a cross shaped AoE around you. Continuously drains HP and SP, and has a 55 ~ 100% chance to apply effects throughout the duration depending on the skill level.


  • This will remove all buffs and debuffs from the caster.


Positive Effects

  • Restores between 1 and 9,999 HP.
  • Cures from the majority of status effects.
  • Applies Level 10 Blessing.
  • Applies Level 10 Increase Agility.
  • Endows weapon with the Holy property.
  • Endows armor with the Holy property.
  • Doubles Max HP.
  • Doubles Max SP.
  • Boosts all stats by 20.
  • Boosts hard defense by 25% for 10 seconds.
  • Doubles attack.
  • Boosts flee and hit rate by 50.
  • Grants immunity against status effects.


Negative Effects

  • Inflicts between 1 and 9,999 of Neutral property fixed damage, which ignores the accuracy check and bypasses Kyrie Eleison.
  • Causes the Curse status for an unusually lengthy period of time.
  • Causes the Blind status for an unusually lengthy period of time.
  • Causes the Poison status for an unusually lengthy period of time.
  • Applies Level 10 Provoke.
  • Drops all defense to 0.
  • Drops attack to 0.
  • Drops flee rate to 0.
  • Reduces ASPD and movement speed by 75%.
Shield Chain Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 25 + (3 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.8s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.2s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Range: 11
  • Requires a shield equipped
  • Cast can not be interrupted
 Strike an enemy with your shield 5 times in a row, dealing ranged physical damage.

You also get +20 Hit while using this skill.


  • This skills damage is based on casters Base Level, and the equipped shields weight, and refinement.


  • [Lv 1] : 500% ATK, +20 Hit.
  • [Lv 2] : 700% ATK, +20 Hit.
  • [Lv 3] : 900% ATK, +20 Hit.
  • [Lv 4] : 1100% ATK, +20 Hit.
  • [Lv 5] : 1300% ATK, +20 Hit.
