  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 50
  • Cast Delay: 5.0s
 Allows you to create  Poison Bottle. DEX and LUK increased the chance for successful crafting.

If you fail crafting a Poison Bottle, you will lose 25% Max HP.


Assassin classes can consume a Poison Bottle to gain a small boost in ASPD. If another class consumes a Poison Bottle, they will die instantly.

Enchant Deadly Poison Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 60:70:80:90:100
  • Cooldown: 2.0s
  • Consumes 1  Poison Bottle
 Massively increase your damage dealt, and when you attack an enemy has a chance to afflict Deadly Poison status.


  • This skill increases WeaponATK by 400%, and EquipmentATK by 300%.
  • Grimtooth, Throw Venom Knife, and Meteor Assault, are not affected by Enchant Deadly Poison's damage increase.


  • [Lv 1] : 40 second duration.
  • [Lv 2] : 60 second duration.
  • [Lv 3] : 80 second duration.
  • [Lv 4] : 100 second duration.
  • [Lv 5] : 120 second duration.

Deadly Poison status effects;

  • If target is a player, -10% Max HP at the start of the effect.
  • If target is a monster, -15% Max HP at the start of the effect.
  • Reduces -4% Max HP every second.
  • Has no effect on Boss monsters.
