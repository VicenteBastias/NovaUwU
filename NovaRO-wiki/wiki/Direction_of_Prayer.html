Direction of Prayer

Gaining access to the Grey Wolf Village

Requirements
Base Level: 170
Starting Point: (ba_in01 27, 266)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Tracking Illusion
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: -  Amethyst Fragments
  1. Speak with Smart Ellie inside the Hangar in Varmundt's Mansion at (ba_in01 27, 266)Copied!. The signal location has been identified. You will receive a  Mini Ellie which you must equip at all times to do the quest.
  2. Move to Rachel and speak with the Masked Person at (rachel 182, 176)Copied!. You learn about the ongoing conflict between the current residents and the natives.
  3. Speak with the Anxious Priest right below the Masked Person.
  4. Speak with him again to received 10  Amethyst Fragments. Junok tells you to meet him again in front of the Santuary Temple.
  5. Meet with the Anxious Priest again at (ra_temple 73, 54)Copied!.
  6. Go to the next location (ra_temple 30, 142)Copied! and speak with the priest once again. Follow the guards inside the temple.
  7. Enter Rachel Sanctuary and talk to Guard Dent at (ra_temin 173, 40)Copied!. You'll receive 15  Amethyst Fragments as a reward.
  8. Speak with Guard Neil right next to you.
  9. Speak with the Freya Priest right next to you. Niren will then want to speak to you in private.
  10. Talk to Niren near (ra_temin 213, 87)Copied! regarding the situation with the intruders inside the temple.
  11. Go back to Guard Neil at the entrance (ra_temin 174, 38)Copied! and talk with him. You'll now have to find Niren's son, Maram.
  12. Go back to Rachel and speak with a Villager at (rachel 137, 135)Copied!.
  13. Speak with the next Villager on your left, at (rachel 105, 134)Copied!.
  14. Same as above, next Villager at (rachel 69, 117)Copied!.
  15. Talk to Maram in the alley above the last villager (rachel 70, 147)Copied!.
  16. Speak with Miriam on your left and introduce yourself.
  17. Talk to the Merchant (rachel 87, 122)Copied! to attempt to buy bread and meat for the Grey Wolf village.
  18. Talk to the next Merchant at (rachel 107, 102)Copied! to receive a similar treatment.
  19. Speak with Miriam (rachel 117, 104)Copied! right next to you to acknowlege the situation.
  20. Speak with Maram and he'll ask you to buy goods for them instead.
  21. Talk to another Merchant at (rachel 137, 85)Copied! and pretend to be a merchant buying goods.
  22. Talk to the 'hidden' Miriam at (rachel 120, 79)Copied! to update her on your progress.
  23. Click on the Dumpling while you wait for Maram and Miriam to prepare their merchant disguises. You'll meet Tamarin that likes to cosplay.
  24. Speak with Mark at (rachel 118, 120)Copied! near the fountain.
  25. Return to Maram in the alley, at (rachel 70, 147)Copied! and talk to him. They are done with the preparations and are ready for you to collect the goods.
  26. Speak with the Merchant at (rachel 137, 85)Copied!. The goods have been delivered to the west gate.
  27. Click on the Box (rachel 41, 132)Copied!.
  28. Walk outside the gate, speak with the disguised Maram, as a merchant, (ra_fild11 354, 235)Copied!. He wants you to go without him to the Oz Gorge in the west.
  29. Go to the Oz Gorge entrance near (ra_fild10 179, 176)Copied! and speak with Maram/disguised Merchant again.
  30. Talk with him again to enter the Labyrinth of Oz instance. This story instance is rather short and linear, just follow along Miriam, but there's a part that gets confusing and people often get lost. So follow the steps below if you need help.
    1. Talk to Miriam at the entrance and watch the cutscene.
    2. Move up until you meet her again. Walk near her to trigger the next cutscene.
    3. Continue walking to the left and follow her on the bridge.
    4. Kill the two Ash Toads next to Miriam and watch the cutscene.
    5. Continue on the main path until you meet Maram, walk next to him to trigger the cutscene.
    6. Move to the left a bit and kill the two monsters next to Emile.
    7. Then move north, following the map (that's where people get lost). You can use this link ([email protected] 43, 255)Copied!.
    8. Walk near Maram from the navi link above (you must trigger its cutscene to proceed).
    9. Keep walking south and click on Maram.
    10. Click on the Rope to exit the Labyrinth of Oz and Grey Wolf field.
  31. Once outside, walk near the quest marker next to you. Maram will tell you about the hidden entrance to the Grey Wolf Village.
  32. Speak with Maram at bottom left of the Grey Wolf Forest (gw_fild01 35, 101)Copied!.
  33. Speak with Emile next.
  34. Speak with Maram once more and you'll be warped inside the Grey Wolf Village.
    You've now gained access to the Grey Wolf Village.
  35. Talk to Emile (wolfvill 143, 113)Copied! and you'll received 25  Amethyst Fragments.
    You've now unlocked all daily quests.
  36. In order to progress further in the main quest, you must reach 1000 reputation points in the Grey Wolf Village. You can do so by doing all the story version of the daily quests. There are 10 of them giving you 100 points each. You can also do the daily version of the daily quests to reach 1000 points, though you'll have to wait on cooldowns so it's easier to just do the story versions.

  1. Speak with the Village Boy at (wolfvill 180, 179)Copied!. He tells you the Grey Wolf Contest conference is about to start.
  2. Speak with Suad at the conference (wolfvill 144, 151)Copied!.
  3. Talk to the 4 natives around Suad to gather their opinions.
  4. Talk to Suad again.
  5. Talk to Miriam just above (wolfvill 148, 151)Copied! and you'll receive 20  Amethyst Fragments.
  6. Speak with the Radical Young Man below you at (wolfvill 143, 145)Copied! and inquire about the source of the weapons.
  7. Speak with Jack the Beard at (rachel 75, 44)Copied! and select Smelly Dandelion. He introduces you to the weapons' dealer in Veins.
  8. Go to Veins and speak with Dandelion at (veins 293, 282)Copied!.
  9. Go to Thor Volcano and Maram will be waiting for you at (thor_v01 169, 169)Copied!.
  10. Keep moving in the area and meet with Maram again at (que_thr 133, 53)Copied!. Speak with Maram and generate the Thor Military Base instance.
    1. The instance is very short and straightforward.
    2. Move to the east side until you see Maram again. Get close to him to open the path. You don't have to kill the monsters.
    3. Move to the next area at ([email protected] 226, 109)Copied!.
    4. Keep moving west, to ([email protected] 84, 98)Copied!.
    5. Once you've reached the final room with Maram and Miriam, you'll have to collect 5  Document Files. There are 3 on the left wall, and 2 on the top wall. Just click on them.
    6. Speak with Miriam at ([email protected] 34, 100)Copied!.
    7. Speak with Maram afterwards, you'll receive 25  Amethyst Fragments and then be warped out back to the Grey Wolf Village.
  11. Talk to Suad in the Grey Wolf Village at (wolfvill 140, 112)Copied!.
  12. Click on the Loot box at (wolfvill 131, 257)Copied!.
  13. Speak with Markus right next to you (wolfvill 131, 252)Copied!. You'll receive 15  Amethyst Fragments.
  14. Go back to Rachel and speak with the Ordinary Person at (rachel 169, 245)Copied!. Generate the instance for the Wolves' Gathering. Talk with him again to enter.
  15. Go through the instance, it's mostly about following navi and talking to NPCs, nothing complicated.
    1. Upon entering, follow Miriam to the west to watch the cutscene.
    2. Talk to Suad (he has a red arrow on top of him).
    3. Go into the santuary ([email protected] 180, 165)Copied!.
    4. Speak with Miriam after her cutscene.
    5. Kill all the monsters and then proceed to the next area ([email protected] 59, 104)Copied!.
    6. Talk to Miriam again and kill all the invaders.
    7. Make your way south towards the portal.
    8. Move to the next area at ([email protected] 118, 73)Copied!.
    9. Go to the left and talk to Miriam once more, then kill the invaders. Leave from the same way you came from.
    10. Move to the next area at ([email protected] 182, 104)Copied!.
    11. Talk to Miriam... kill the monsters, leave the room.
    12. Make your way to Miriam on the left at ([email protected] 133, 109)Copied! and watch the cutscene.
    13. Keep moving forward through the portals.
    14. Speak with Miriam, kill the monsters and speak with Miriam again.
    15. Leave the room, then move to the right through the portals until you're out of the sanctuary.
    16. Move to the gathering ([email protected] 120, 171)Copied! and then talk to Miriam.
    17. Talk to Suad.
    18. Kill the Heart Hunters and talk to Miriam.
    19. Exit the area to the bottom left of the map, at ([email protected] 45, 29)Copied!.
  16. Speak with High Priest Niren right outside at (rachel 116, 200)Copied!. You will receive 30  Amethyst Fragments.
  17. Go to the Rally Point in the Labyrinth of Oz at (oz_dun01 223, 116)Copied!.
  18. Talk to Aira to create the High Priest's Villa instance. Talk to her again to enter the instance.
    1. Talk to Miriam to start the cutscene.
    2. Talk to Miriam again.
    3. Talk to the Villa Employee on the bottom left ([email protected] 100, 24)Copied!.
    4. Talk to Miriam again on the right side after killing the two Heart Hunters.
    5. Talk to Miriam on the bottom at the entrance of the instance.
    6. Move up and kill all the monsters. There are some on the left and right wings.
    7. After all the monsters are killed, Miriam will slowly walk up. Wait for her to reach the end of the corridor and then talk to her.
    8. Kill all the monsters on the left corridor and then walk to the end of the path.
    9. Click on the Silver Key to get it.
    10. Click on the Locked Door right below you to enter the next room, at ([email protected] 63, 142)Copied!.
    11. Kill the Heart Hunters inside and then click on the Gold Key at ([email protected] 29, 157)Copied!.
    12. Leave the room and then go meet Miriam on the right side at ([email protected] 182, 144)Copied!.
    13. Talk to the people inside the room, then leave and talk to Miriam again.
    14. Click on the Locked Door on your left and enter the portal.
    15. Kill the monsters in the room and then go up to the Colorful Knight statues. Click on both of them.
    16. Click on the Colorful Door right next to you.
    17. Move to the next floor by clicking on the next door north of you.
    18. Talk to Sulan and then defeat him. Move on to the next room.
    19. Move up and then talk to Priest Youth.
    20. Defeat both of the bosses.
    21. Move south and Miriam. Walk in the portal (may be hard to see) to get out of the room.
  19. Talk to Maram at (oz_dun01 225, 119)Copied! to warp you directly to Rachel Sanctuary.
  20. Go meet with Miriam at (ra_temin 254, 117)Copied! and remove Mini Ellie from your equipment.
  21. Speak with Shape of Ellie next to you. You'll receive 90  Amethyst Fragments.
    You have now finished Direction of Prayer (episode 18) main story quest.
