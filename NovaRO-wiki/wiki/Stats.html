Primary Stats

STR

Strength increases the weight and attack of a character.
It's the main stat, usually, for physical damage dealers (damage reduced by DEF) that do auto-attacks at close range. This means that it's the main damage stat for classes that don't use bows and guns but at the same time it also doesn't apply to instruments and whips.
Each point spent here increases:
Status ATK + 1
Weight Limit + 30
Hidden (doesn't show in the ALT+A menu) Weapon ATK + 0.5%

AGI

Agility increases the attack speed (ASPD; more info about it here) and Flee of a character.
Because it also increases your attack speed, it also reduces your animation delay (this is not the same as ACD/After Cast Delay, it's the time your character takes to finish an animation).
It also increases your SOFT DEF.
Each point spent here increases:
Flee + 1.
Each five points spend here increases:
SOFT DEF + 1 (the X in DEF X+Y).
100 Total AGI Gives Immunity to:
Bleeding
Sleep

VIT

Vitality increases your Health Points (HP).
This means that more points here increase your survival.
This also increases the healing power of potions that recover HP.
Each point spent here increases:
MAX HP +1%.
Healing Items effectiveness +2% (HP Healing Values).
Each five points spend here increases:
SOFT DEF + 1 (the X in DEF X+Y).
Each five points spend here increases:
SOFT DEF + 1 (the X in DEF X+Y).
HP Recovery Rate +1.
100 Total VIT Gives Immunity to:
Stun
Blind
Poison
Deadly Poison

INT

Intelligence increases your Special Points (SP) to cast skills and the magical attack (MATK) of a character.
It's the main stat of any class that deal magical damage (damage reduced by MDEF).
Points spent here, usually (because there are skills with fixed cast time that this doesn't reduce that portion of the cast), also help users to cast skills, with a cast bar, a bit faster.
This also increases the healing power of potions that recover SP.
Each point spent here increases:
Status MATK + 1.5
MAX SP + 1%
Healing Items effectiveness +1% (SP Healing Values).
Decreases variable cast time (Instant Variable Cast Formula is DEX*2 + INT = 530).
Each two points spend here increases:
SOFT MDEF + 1 (the X in MDEF X+Y).
Each six points spend here increases:
SP Recovery Rate + 1
100 Total INT Gives Immunity to:
Silence

DEX

Dexterity increases your HIT and makes you cast skills faster.
It's the main stat, usually, for physical damage dealers (damage reduced by DEF) that do auto-attacks at long range. This means that it's the main damage stat for classes that use bows and guns but at the same time it also applies to instruments and whips.
Points spent here also help a bit to get higher values of attack speed (ASPD; more info about it here), although with a lower influence when compared to Agility, and magical attack (MATK), although with a lower influence when compared to Intelligence.
Because it also increases your attack speed, it also reduces your animation delay (this is not the same as ACD/After Cast Delay, it's the time your character takes to finish an animation), although with a lower influence when compared to Agility.
Each point spent here increases:
Hit + 1
Decreases variable cast time (Instant Variable Cast Formula is DEX*2 + INT = 530)
Each five points spend here increases:
Status ATK + 1
Status MATK + 1
SOFT MDEF + 1 (the X in MDEF X+Y).

LUK

Luck increases your Critical Rate (CRIT).
Points spent here also helps a bit to get higher values of attack (ATK), Magical Attack (MATK), Flee, Perfect Dodge (PD) and Hit.
Each point spent here increases:
CRIT + 0.3%
Each three points spend here increases:
Hit + 1
Status ATK + 1
Status MATK + 1
Each five points spend here increases:
Flee + 1
Each ten points spend here increases:
Perfect Dodge + 1
0 or 100 Total LUK Gives Immunity to:
Curse

4th Job Characteristic Status (Traits)

Players leveling past Base Level 200 (4th jobs) will no longer receive regular Status points. They instead receive T.Status points that are used to upgrade Characteristic Status (a.k.a. Traits or Talent).

Every Base Level past 200 grants 3 T.Status points, with an extra 4 points for every 5 levels (this includes Level 200, immediately granting 7 points upon changing to 4th jobs). This amounts to a total of 197 points at Level 250.

Primary traits

  • POW: (Power) Status ATK + 5 for every point ; P.ATK + 1 for every 3 points.
  • STA: (Stamina) RES + 1 for every point ; RES + 5 every 3 points.
  • WIS: (Wisdom) MRES + 1 for every point ; MRES + 5 every 3 points.
  • SPL: (Spell) Status MATK + 5 for every point ; P.MATK + 1 for every 3 points.
  • CON: (Concentration) HIT + 2 and FLEE + 2 for every point ; P.ATK + 1 and S.MATK + 1 for every 5 points.
  • CRT: (Creativity) 1 H.PLUS + 1 for every point ; C.RATE + 1 for every 3 points.

Sub traits

  • P.ATK: (Trait Physical Attack) Increases final status and equipment ATK by 1% for every point (bonus not shown in regular ATK).
  • S.MATK: (Trait Magical Attack) Increases final status and equipment MATK by 1% for every point (bonus not shown in regular MATK).
  • RES: (Physical Resistance) Reduces physical damage taken by 9*RES / (5000 + 10*RES) x 100%, capped at 50%.
  • MRES: (Magical Resistance) Reduces magical damage taken by 9*MRES / (5000 + 10*MRES) x 100%, capped at 50%.
  • H.PLUS: (Heal Plus) Increases effectiveness of healing skills by 1% for every point.
  • C.RATE: (Critical Damage Rate) Increases critical damage by 1% for every point[1]. (Not to be confused with Critical Rate (CRIT), the chance of landing a critical attack.)
  1. ↑ Note that C.RATE is an additive bonus to the base critical damage modifier (140%) that is applied before other (multiplicative) critical damage bonuses. The formula is therefore
    critical attack damage = normal attack damage * (140 + C.RATE)% * (100% + critical damage modifier) or
    critical skill damage = normal skill damage * (140 + C.RATE)% * (100% + critical damage modifier/2)
