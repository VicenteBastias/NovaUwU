Introduction

Sup, been playing for a couple years this class and wanted to share a build focused on Cannon Spear, this build will cover a lot of min-maxing and talk a little about the future of the build with upcoming updates.

Meta

The class is in a pretty good spot right now with the fourth job, and with all the current items that can help this build it is one of the strongest build for this class.

However, this build won't get support until 19.1 while most of the other builds will get a ton of it, from here until Glacier items, Overlash and RoG will be the staples. And after Glacier items it will come back to being the best Physical Damage Imperial Guard build.

Stats

An general idea on how to level your stats:

Stats
Attribute Value Notes
Str 130 The best stat for this build, Cannon Spear scales heavily on this stat, so every point counts. This stat scales better and better with every skill% you get.
Agi 1 Dump the rest of the point you have in this, you should aim for 190-193 ASPD, however you can easily get it from consumables only.
Vit (100) You are here to do damage, not tank. You only need points here to get a total of 100 stats, not base stats so you don't get stunned. If you want to save some points you can consider stats with the use of Inspiration.
Int (100) The same as before you only need around 100 total stats so you don't get silenced. However I wouldn't use the Inspiration saving strat here because you need Int to increase the chance to dodge skills with Prestige.
Dex 120 You will need 120 Dex here if you are using Thanatos Tower combo, if you aren't you don't really need stats in Dex, around 40 is pretty good.
Luk 120 This stat is important for a consistent crit rate and to increase dodge chance of Prestige. If you are using Galensis card go for 125 instead.

Traits

An general idea on how to level your traits:

Traits
Trait Value Notes
Pow 99 This is the most imporant trait for this class, every 3 points in it it increases you final physical damage by 1% (P. Atk) and every point increases your status atk by 5, so try to keep it divisible by 3 (total).
Sta 0 It helps tank but does no damage so don't level it.
Wis 0 It helps tank but does no damage so don't level it.
Spl 0 Miss me with magical damage.
Con 2 This trait increases your P. Atk by 1 every 5 points. Max job you will get +3 here so in total you get 5, you pretty much gain 1 extra P. Atk with this composition.
Crt 96 Second most important trait and the reason why crit classes will be more and more prevalant from here onwards, every 3 points it increases your C. Rate by 1%. C. Rate increases your base critical damage from 140% this stat scales with critical damage.

I will just talk about the most important skills for each job.

First Job Skills
Skill Notes
Magnum Break Free 20% Fire property damage.
Auto Berserk If you are in red health (less than 25%) you will use Provoke on youself. You can combo this with Ultimate Sacrifice to garantee the burst.
Second/Transcendent Job skills
Skill Notes
Spear Mastery Free 40 mastery Atk. You will need at least 1 here for Banishing Point.
Spear Quicken One of the best skills here, helps a ton to get 140 crit.
Riding Remove size penalty for medium sized mobs.
Cavalier Mastery Get the ASPD lost while riding.
Faith Requirement for Inspiration.
Reflect Shield Requirement for Inspiration.
Shield Charge Requirement for Inspiration.
Third Job skills
Skill Notes
Cannon Spear Best skill in the game, level it to 5.
Banishing Point Need to level it to 5 for Cannon Spear, for early levels it is better to farm with it.
Inspiration A beast of an skill, pretty much our Unlimited. +30 All stats at max level increases the damage of Cannon Spear by a ton. Always have potions so your HP/SP goes low enough to auto deactivate it.
Shield Spell Pretty good for farming (3% Max SP every 5 seconds) and +150 ATK helps to do more damage.
Prestige Free GTB, only weakness is dispell and pretty much gives the same def as Banding when you are 250.
Earth Drive Remove those pesky pneumas.
Fourth Job skills
Skill Notes
Attack Stance Saddly Imperial Guard got the short end of the stick, -200 DEF for 15 P. Atk makes you pretty squishy but does a ton of damage, if you login always remember to activate it.
Grand Judgement Only needed for the buff so just level it to 1. Saddly for now it costs 150 AP, use the healer to guarantee having so much. In the future it will cost 15 AP (thank god).
Shield Mastery OP passive, max it.
Ultimate Sacrifice Extra life and helps set up Auto Berserk, be fast on poting after using it to not deactivate Inspiration.
