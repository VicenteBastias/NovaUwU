The Minstrel (Mins) is the 3rd job class of Bards. Minstrels are primarily a support class, utilising musical skills to buff their teammates and hinder their enemies. Their signature skill, Poem of Bragi, reduces the cast delay between skills. With such a powerful utility, Minstrels are often an essential part of parties, in both PvM and BG/WOE.

The female counterpart of the Minstrel is the Wanderer.

Builds

Severe Rainstorm

This build capitalizes on the damage potential of Severe Rainstorm, while still giving your full supportive potential with Poem of Bragi among other skills.

By stacking a lot of DEX, AGI and Ranged damage bonuses, you can deal a very respectable damage using Severe Rainstorm. This build is particularly useful in helping clear out large mobs, like in Old Glast Heim, Horror Toy Factory, and Bios Island. This can even be used to help chip away at an MVP's HP, when your party is lacking in DPS.

Reverberation

This build, on the other hand, focuses on the skill Reverberation, which can be spammed with the help of Poem of Bragi and After Cast Delay reduction gears to deal consistent sustained damage to a small AoE. Additionally, this skill costs a lot of SP especially when you're spamming it, therefore it's a good idea to only use this when you also have a Sorcerer in your party.

While Severe Rainstorm is best for mobs, Reverberation is ideal for destroying bosses. You can maximize its damage by stacking both ATK and MATK bonuses in your gear. You can even combo Severe Rainstorm with spamming Reverberation to push your DPS even higher.

Pure Support

With a focus on staying alive and keeping buffs up, a full support minstrel should have his full attention turned to his own party instead of the Mobs, knowing who is dying and being ressed in combination with the skill "encore" will maximize "Bragi" up time even when it is on cooldown, and keeping a Iara card with you will make sure your DPS always have maximum up time, even without proper Stone/freeze resistance the tankier build will allow you to stay alive and ress key members of your team on critical moments.

Bio 5 Minstrel

The best way to farm in Bio 5 for beginners is actually by playing as Performers, cause they don't really need high end gears to do it. The most important things are "Keep The Songs Up". Being Bio 5 Minstrel is exactly the same thing as being Pure Support, but we are focusing more on the necessary skills and budget equipment here.

  1. Status Build
    • STR > 38
    • AGI > 100 -> Provide Sleep Immunity
    • VIT > 100 -> Stun Immunity
    • INT > 120 -> at 100 Blind/Silence Immunity
    • DEX > 120 -> to have the fixed cast time reduction from the  Temporal Boots of Dexterity
    • LUK > 1 -> having 0 or -1 LUK for Curse Immunity.  Essence of Evil Dex 3 is a good way to decrease your LUK to 0.
  2. Skill List
    • Into The Abyss -> to allow Sorcerer use the Land Protector endlessly
    • Poem of Bragi -> providing faster cast time, and reducing after cast delay. It's a huge boost for your party
    • Windmill Rush -> increase ATK of party member
    • Song of Mana -> heals SP and provide great SP Regeneration
    • Frigg's Song -> extra Max HP for Royal Guard, and make Rangers less squishy
    • Death Valley -> to revive dead party members
  3. Budget Equipment
    •  Valkyrian Armor with  Marc Card -> Unbreakable armor and Frozen Immunity
    • Any shield works actually as long carded with  Medusa Card -> Stone Curse Immunity
    •  Temporal Boots Of Dexterity for fixed cast time reduction -> don't necessarily need any enchant actually
    •  Giant Snake Skin -> for Max HP and SP Bonus -> don't necessarily need any enchant actually
    • 2x  Sound Amplifier
    • Any instruments -> you might need spares, cause the bio5 mobs usually break your weapon unless you have it carded with  Golem Card
    • Another option would be to buy one of these weapons with "cannot be broken in battle" enchant :
      •  Old Bard's Ukulele
      •  Vicious Mind Violin

Advanced Bio 5 Minstrel

Bio5 Build for those who aim into being as usefull as possible for their party not as easy to play as autofolowing and holding your Bragi button, with a really big focus on the use of Encore to renew Bragi faster to party members, and staiyng alive for resses and Destuning

  1. Status Build
  2. STR: 1< Really just for carry capacity, you dont really need it.
  3. AGI 87< Attention Concentrate will give you 12% of your base AGI as bonus, with 87 you will be able to reach 101 total AGI, so if you can remember to keep it up, you can save some points here, if you cant, just put 95 due to 5 extra AGI from class bonuses.
  4. VIT: 120~130 You want to stay alive, and VIT is your best friend there.
  5. INT: 91< you have a natural bonus of 9 INT, so 91 is all you need for Mute Status immunity, and you should get it, a mute minstrel, is as usefull as a square tire.
  6. DEX: 120 You will need 120 base DEX to take advantage of the -0.5s Fixed Cast Reduction from  Temporal Boots of Dexterity [1], which is very useful but not mandatory, your casts are short enough already, if you feel safe you won't be interrupted you can use a  Temporal Boots of Vitaility [1] for extra tankiness, and put less points here, but is heavily recomended you use a Temporal Dex Boots.
  7. LUK 0> You dont need to be lucky, you need to be curse immune though, which requires either 0 or 100 LUK. You can use an  Essence of Evil Dex 3 to reduce your LUK stat.

Note: You can use the spare points as you prefer, INT will give you Soft Mdef, AGI will give you flee, and STR will give you carry weight, all are valid options.
