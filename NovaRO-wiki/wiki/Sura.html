The Sura (Sura) is the 3rd job class of Monks. Suras are monks that decided to stop being loyal servants of God to embrace isolation to develop new powers to fight evil. Thanks to the extreme train done, they achieved new powers that complete the flaws they had before making them a great class against anything being it single target or a big mob of monsters. Although their physical power increased a lot, they're still great tanks thanks to Steel Body.
