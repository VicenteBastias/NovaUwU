Pushcart Passive
  • Max Level: 10
 Allows you to rent a pushcart, which can store items and unlock additional skills.

Your movement speed is reduced while you have a pushcart with you. Higher levels can remove this reduction.


  • Items inside the pushcart cannot be used or equipped directly, and must be brought outside to be used.
  • You can access the pushcart using Alt+W.


  • [Lv 1] : 55% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 2] : 60% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 3] : 65% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 4] : 70% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 5] : 75% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 6] : 80% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 7] : 85% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 8] : 90% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv 9] : 95% of normal walking speed.
  • [Lv10] : 100% of normal walking speed.
