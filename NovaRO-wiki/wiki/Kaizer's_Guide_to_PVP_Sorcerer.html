Before I begin, This guide was not possible were it not for the lessons and experience I gained to become a knowledgeable sorcerer in PvP. A huge thanks to Sirs Clear Box and Shuuwatsu!

Like all other guides, This is NOT the only way to play sorcerer in PvP/BG/WoE. What I will be discussing in this guide is based entirely from my experience as a sorcerer and if you guys do not entirely agree with the builds, then that is fine.

This guide will be focused entirely on the PvP/BG/Woe side of Sorcerer Class and I will not be talking about any leveling and PvM. If you are looking for a sorcerer PvM guide, please look for Seandh's Sorcerer Guide.

This guide will also not tackle the SpellFist Build as it is a completely different build. This guide will focus more on the magic build/skill tree.

Introduction

My name is Kaizer and my IGN as a sorcerer is SorcererKaizer220. I have been playing support classes for a very long time in RO and ever since pre-renewal was a fan of the professor class. With its upgrade in renewal I would not miss to play its corresponding 3rd job.

Before I go with the guide proper let me list the pros and cons of using the Sorcerer class:

PROS

  • Versatile Class (Can do support and damage)
  • Hard to kill with the right skill/gear setup
  • Skills from the 2nd/Transcendent job tree are still useful for disabling/supporting
  • Relatively large Area of Effect (AoE) Damage per Second (DPS) skills

CONS

  • Difficult to master (Large number of skills to know and use)
  • Catalyst Reliant (Most skills need elemental stones/gemstones to work)
  • Low HP pool due to being a magic class

With that out of the way, let us begin.

Class Overview

Sorcerers enlist the power of the spirits in order to support their allies and disable or damage their opponents. They have been blessed with the ability to endow/enhance their damage dealing skills to either Earth, Water, Wind or Fire depending on the user's desire. In the PvP setting this is useful as only few players have resistance to all 4 elements and even if they do would still be vulnerable to your other disabling skills.

Sorcerers in PvP would rely mostly on their disabling, damaging and land skills in order to turn the tides in their favor. An extremely skilled sorc would be hard to kill and could individually be able to kill a lot of opponents in WoE or Battlegrounds (DPS build). These skills would be explained in detail below. But first, let us discuss stats.

Status (Source: iRO Stats)

STR

  • Boosts ATK and Weight Limit. You would probably need this in order to carry more potions and catalysts.
  • Lowers duration of White Imprison (IMPORTANT!) and indirectly prevents Masquerade skills (influenced by current weight) (IMPORTANT!)
  • This can be lowered if you have used 10 Gym Passes for acquiring Level 10 Increase Weight Limit.

Recommended Value: 30-80

AGI

  • Boosts Flee and Attack Speed (ASPD). Both are not entirely needed for this class because the skills are not heavily reliant on ASPD. As for flee, Wall of Fog fixes this problem (mostly).
  • The main reason to get this status is to reduce the duration of Masquerade skills (IMPORTANT!).

Recommended Value: 50-90

VIT

  • Boosts Soft Defense, Max HP and efficacy of HP potions. Having a relatively high or high amount of VIT will enable you to survive damage for longer periods of time.
  • Reduces Duration of Blind, Crystallization (IMPORTANT!), Curse, Deadly Poison (Enchant Deadly Poison or EDP), Freezing, Howling (IMPORTANT!), Poison, Stasis (IMPORTANT!) and Stun.

Recommended Value - 70-110

INT

  • Boosts MATK, Max SP and Variable Cast Time (VCT). All magic users would not miss this status as this would be what determines the damage they deal. If you are going to go full support (FS) sorcerer however, this could be a little lower since you would be focusing more on disabling and land skills, not damage given that you have gears that reduce your cast time.
  • Increase resistance to Blind, Deep Sleep (IMPORTANT!), Silence and Sleep.

Recommended Value - 90-120

DEX

  • Boosts VCT, Hit and MATK in large quantities. This would no doubt be one of the stats you want 120 on because of the VCT. Hit is not that important on magic classes and most of your gears will not focus on decreasing cast time unless they are DPS gears that do so (especially Temporal Boots of Dexterity).
  • Reduces duration of Freezing and Stasis (IMPORTANT!)

Recommended Value - 120

LUK

  • Boosts MATK and ATK every 3 of this stat, Perfect Dodge, and by a small extension Flee. This stat is usually overlooked by many players but would be useful for the status resistance it gives, especially for howling.
  • Decreases Chance of Coma (Tarot Card of Fate), Deadly Poison, Howling (IMPORTANT!), Poison, Sleep and Stun.

Recommended Value - 1-30 (Could be increased to 60 or 80)

Note that this could vary depending on your the stat build you want to make.

Skill Name Skill Description and Analysis Recommended Level
Health Conversion Converts HP into SP. Due to potions being available on the PvP setting, there is no need to get this skill. N/A
Soul Change Swaps the SP of the target with your own. This is very useful in PvM but in PvP where potions are widely used, this skill is not needed. N/A
Soul Burn Drains all the target's SP if successful and your own if the skill fails. This is useful provided that it succeeds (highest chance is 70%). At lvl 5 this deals damage 2x of the SP burned but it would also break any type of freeze/stone curse the opponent has so better keep this at lvl 4 if you want to use it. N/A or 4
Mind Breaker Doubles MATK of target at the cost of decreasing MDEF. This skill is used mostly in WoE to increase the damage of magic users. N/A or 5
Memorize Reduces the cast time of the next 5 spells by half. A situational spell to get if you have trouble reaching instant or fast cast time (but most of the time you would be). N/A or 1
Wall of Fog Creates a small fog area that reduces ranged damage and inflicts blind on opponents that go inside it. THIS SKILL IS ONE OF THE REASONS WHY I LOVE SORCERER. This skill is a godsend because it reduces ranged damage (Arrow Storm, Gate of Hell) and reduces accuracy of disabling skills particularly Masquerade. Combine this with Safety Wall and you have semi-invincibility against physical skills. Get this skill because you will not regret it. 1
Spider Web Locks the target in place for a short period of time. This is a good immobility skill for battlegrounds especially when chasing opponents. If you want to get this skill do so unless you are opting to get other skills on the sage or sorcerer skill tree. Requires 1 cobweb to use. N/A or 1
Double Casting Adds chance of dual casting bolt spells. This is a damaging kit for professors during pre-renewal but in the renewal era not so much, even less for sorcerers that have AoE skills that deal much more damage. N/A
