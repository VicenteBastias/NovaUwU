Walkthrough

Requirements
Base Level: 50
Starting Point: alberta 214/77
Items: 10,000z
Rewards
Experience: -
Items: -
Others: -
  1. Warp to Towns > Alberta and talk to Captain Janssen /navi alberta 214/77.
  2. Once you are at the Island, talk to the Tower Protection Stone to generate the instance.
  3. Kill every monster on each floor to proceed to the next one.

Important Notes

On NovaRO you don't need to clear it a second time to get access to Floor 101 where Naght Seiger lies. You can enter it as soon as you clear Floor 100 (kill Entweihen Knothen).

Disabled Skills

The skills below cannot be used in the Endless Tower:

  • Extreme Vacuum
  • Fiber Lock
  • Ice Wall
  • Leap
  • Reflect Damage
  • Teleport
  • Snatch
  • Shield Reflect
  • Warg Bite
