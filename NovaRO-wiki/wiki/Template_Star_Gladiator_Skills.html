Solar, Lunar and Stellar Perception Support
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 100
  • Fixed Cast Time: 3.0s
 Designate the current map with one of the cosmic alignments, Solar, Lunar or Stellar, for the use of certain skills.


  • It is impossible to change a map's cosmic alignment once it is designated.
  • Using this skill on a cosmically aligned map will display a list of map designations.

Only 1 map may be designated for each cosmic alignment.


  • [Lv 1] Designate current map with Solar alignment
  • [Lv 2] Designate current map with Lunar alignment
  • [Lv 3] Designate current map with Stellar alignment
Solar, Lunar and Stellar Opposition Support
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 100
  • Fixed Cast Time: 3.0s
  • Range: 9
 Designate the targeted monster with one of the cosmic alignments, Solar, Lunar or Stellar, for the use of certain skills.


  • It is impossible to change a monster's cosmic alignment once it is designated.
  • Using this skill on a cosmically aligned monster will display a list of monster designations.
  • On players, character jobs can be designated without regarding size and Max HP.

Only 1 monster can be designated for each cosmic alignment.


  • [Lv 1] Designate monster with Solar alignment.

Limited to Small sized monsters.

  • [Lv 2] Designate monster with Lunar alignment.

Limited to Medium sized monsters with Max HP of 6,000 or more.

  • [Lv 3] Designate monster with Stellar alignment.

Limited to Large sized monsters with Max HP of 20,000 or more.

Solar Protection Support
  • Max Level: 4
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 70:60:50:40
 This skill is only enabled on Solar (even numbered) days in a Solar aligned map.

Increases your soft DEF for the duration.


  • [Lv 1] Duration 80 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] Duration 160 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] Duration 240 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] Duration 320 seconds.


Soft defense bonus is calculated using this formula;
Soft DEF Boost = (Base_Level + DEX + LUK) ÷ 2

Solar, Lunar and Stellar Union Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 100
 Awaken the caster's hidden potential for 10 minutes while in a spirit status, endowing the caster with attacks that have 100% Accuracy and will ignore enemy's Defense, and increased Movement Speed.

However, 2% of caster's HP will be drained with each attack on monsters and 8% will be drained with each attack on other players.

In this status, attacking when the caster's remaining HP is less that 20% of Max HP will instantly kill the caster.
