Temporal Enchanter NPC outside Legacy of Glast Heim.
Temporal Stat Manteaus are powerful garments that can be obtained with the rewards from Legacy of Glast Heim at the Temporal Enchanter NPC (glast_01 157, 215)Copied!.
To get one you can trade either:
 18 Pure Temporal Crystal
 2 Sealed Temporal Crystal [Bound]
NOTES:
Be aware that the ones traded with  2 Sealed Temporal Crystal [Bound] are account bound which means that trades with other accounts aren't possible.
You can only get  1 Sealed Temporal Crystal [Bound] per week per account.

Enchants

Temporal Manteaus can be enchanted with  1 Temporal Seal Key. Each key gives/rerolls 1 random enchant.
To get a key, you need to trade  200 Corrupted Spell for 1 at the Temporal Enchanter NPC (glast_01 157, 215)Copied!.
