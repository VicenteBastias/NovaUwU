Green Aura

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Field MVPs are found in the wild with a glowing Green Aura. Not only these MVPs have powerful new skills, any damage dealt to them is reduced by 90%.

This only affects Field MVPs. Bosses inside instances, such as Endless Tower, Endless Cellar and Central Lab do not have a Green Aura.

Rewards

Green Aura MVPs keep their usual drop table, as well as having a 100% drop chance for a  Premium Ore Coin.

 Premium Ore Coins can be exchanged with the Coin Exchanger, at the second floor of the Main Office.
He will exchange 5 coins for a  Pick An Ore Pack.
