The Royal Guard (RG) is the 3rd job class of Crusaders. They're an elite order of soldiers whose origins remote to King Schmidt and the need of a special force to fight against evil and chaos. Since they're an order prepared to anything, they don't care about costs to achieve results even if that means to sacrifice their own lives in the process.

Due to their buffs that provide strong defenses and even increase their already decent max value of HP, they're often being seen as mobile tanks, lurers, or devoting their HP to someone who doesn't have enough to survive big hits but has other tools to provide success to the group.

Worry not, they're not only guardians of others, they can also create chaos if needed with their own set of skills based mostly on shields and spears.
