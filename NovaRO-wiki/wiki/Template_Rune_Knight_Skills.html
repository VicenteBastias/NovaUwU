Dragon Training Mount
  • Max Level: 5
 Allows you to ride a Ferus dragon. This skills effects are only active while you are riding a dragon.


  • This skill increases both Dragon Breath, and Dragon Breath Water damage.
  • This skill adds Mastery ATK.
  • While mounted on a dragon, Spear weapons will ignore the Size Penalty.


  • [Lv 1] : +0% Dragon Breath Base ATK%, +10 ATK when using a Spear weapon, +700 Maximum weight limit, 80% of normal ASPD.
  • [Lv 2] : +10% Dragon Breath Base ATK%, +20 ATK when using a Spear weapon, +900 Maximum weight limit, 85% of normal ASPD.
  • [Lv 3] : +20% Dragon Breath Base ATK%, +30 ATK when using a Spear weapon, +1100 Maximum weight limit, 90% of normal ASPD.
  • [Lv 4] : +30% Dragon Breath Base ATK%, +40 ATK when using a Spear weapon, +1300 Maximum weight limit, 95% of normal ASPD.
  • [Lv 5] : +40% Dragon Breath Base ATK%, +50 ATK when using a Spear weapon, +1500 Maximum weight limit, 100% of normal ASPD.

Crafting

  • [Lv 8] : +16% Success rate.
  • [Lv 9] : +18% Success rate.

Phantom Thrust Melee Physical Attack
  • Max Level: 5
  • Property: Based on Weapon
  • Target: Enemy, Ally
  • SP Cost: 12 + (3 × Skill Level)
  • Range: 4 + Skill Level
  • Required weapon type:
    • One-handed Spear
    • Two-handed Spear
 Pull a target closer to yourself, and deal damage only if the target is an enemy.


  • Damage is increased based on your Base Level and Spear Mastery skill level.


Damage is calculated using the following formula;
SkillFormula% = [{Skill_Level x 50 + Spear_Mastery_Skill_Level x 10} x {Base Level ÷ 150}]%


In High Rank Monster Hunting missions:

  • Cooldown: 90 seconds.
Enchant Blade Status Increase
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 35 + (5 × Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s
 Enchant your blade with a magic energy allowing you to deal additional magic damage with your physical attacks for 5 minutes.


  • Bonus magic damage is increased based on your Base, Level, INT, MATK, and skill level.
  • This damage treats Hard MDEF the same as Soft MDEF, reducing damage by a flat amount instead of a %.


Bonus Damage is calculated using the following formula;
Bonus Damage = [{{Skill_Level x 20 + 100} x {Base_Level ÷ 100}} + INT + MATK - {Enemy Soft Mdef + Hard Mdef}]%


In High Rank Monster Hunting missions:

  • Increases Melee Physical damage by 2% for 30 seconds.

Dragon Breath Ranged Physical Attack,
Ground
  • Max Level: 10
  • Property: Based on Skill
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 25 + (5 × Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time:
    • (Lv 1~3) : 0.0s
    • (Lv 4~6) : 1.0s
    • (Lv 7~8) : 1.5s
    • (Lv 9~10) : 2.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.5s
  • Cast Delay: 2.0s
  • Range: 9
  • Area of Effect:
    • (Lv 1~3) : 3x3
    • (Lv 4~6) : 5x5
    • (Lv 7~8) : 7x7
    • (Lv 9~10) : 9x9
  • Requires riding a Ferus dragon mount
  • Cast can not be interrupted
 Command your Dragon to spew out scorching fire at a targeted location on the ground that will damage all enemies in the area.


  • There is a 15% chance of causing the Burning status on enemies for 10 seconds.
  • Damage is calculated based on your Current HP, Max SP, Base Level, Dragon Breath skill level, Dragon Training skill level, ranged damage modifiers, and the property of the attack.


  •  Turisus Runestone endows the skill property to Holy.
  •  Lux Anima Rune endows the skill property to Shadow.
  •  Lux Anima Rune (Shadow) element takes priority if both Runestones are active.


Damage is calculated using the following formula;
SkillFormula% = [{Skill_Level x {Current_HP ÷ 50 + Max_SP ÷ 4} x {Base_Level ÷ 100} x {100 + {(Dragon_Training_Skill_Level - 1) x 10}} ÷ 100}]%
