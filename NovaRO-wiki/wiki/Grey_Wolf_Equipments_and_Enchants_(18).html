Gray Wolf Enchantments

Gray Wolf equipment can be enchanted using the new enchantment UI. An enchantment window will appear as you talk to Emmett (wolfvill 164, 137)Copied!.

Every equipment piece has three enchantable slots. The enchant order is 3rd → 2nd → 1st. The process costs the following (regardless of which piece):

  • For 3rd slot, 15  Amethyst Fragments + 100,000 zeny
  • For 2nd slot, 25  Amethyst Fragments + 150,000 zeny
  • For 1st slot, 50  Amethyst Fragments + 200,000 zeny

Perfect enchant is available to the 1st slot of any piece and costs 2,500  Amethyst Fragments + 10,000,000 zeny.

Some enchantment slots can be rerolled:

  • 1st slot for Armors can be rerolled with 100  Amethyst Fragments + 500,000 zeny
  • 1st slot for Footgears can be rerolled with 625  Amethyst Fragments + 3,000,000 zeny.
  • 2nd and 3rd slots for any piece can be rerolled with 150  Amethyst Fragments + 450,000 zeny.

You can reset all enchants at anytime, which costs 25  Amethyst Fragments + 100,000 zeny.

All of the above processes are safe with 100% success rate. There is no chance of losing or damaging the equipment or the cards in it.

Do note however that a +7 or above refinemenet is required for Armors, Footgears, and Garments to be enchanted.
