  • Target: Ally, Enemy

Taekwon Mission Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast cannot be interrupted
 This skill cannot be used after changing classes.

Displays the current target monster for the Taekwon Mission, or starts a new target.

Defeating 100 of the target monster rewards 1 Taekwon Mission point.


The top 10 Taekwons ranked are able to combo their Tornado Kick, Heel Drop, Roundhouse Kick and Counter Kick skills together endlessly.

Upon reaching Lv90, a top 10 ranked Taekwon will have their MaxHP and MaxSP tripled, and all Taekwon skills are unlocked.


  • If the current mission is at 0% completion, casting this skill again may change the target at a very low chance.
  • The target monster will never be a miniboss or MvP.
  • You can type /Taekwon to find the rankings, rankings will decay slightly after every month.
