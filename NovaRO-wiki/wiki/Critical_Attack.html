Critical Attack

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Critical Attacks are stronger versions of regular attacks that ignore enemies Flee and can be amplified through Critical Damage modifiers.


Critical Attacks;

  • Ignore the target's Flee
  • Deal 40% additional damage. (Called the Base Critical Modifier).
  • Can be amplified further by Critical Damage Modifiers.
  • Use the highest weapon variance possible for the attack.
  • Can be dodged with Perfect Dodge
  • Do Not ignore the target's DEF.
  • But is applied after DEF reduces the attacks damage. (Meaning Critical Modifiers are not reduced by DEF, but the normal attack is multiplies is).


Here's a simplified version of how Critical Damage is applied to the attack formula;
Final Critical Damage = Highest Damage Variance x 140% x (100% + Critical Damage Modifier %)

Base Critical Damage

This is the basic bonus damage you get for landing a Critical Attack. It is always applied regardless of class, stats, gears, etc.


A Critical Attack will deal 40% more damage than a regular attack.


So, if you deal 100 damage normally, a Critical Attack would increase that to 140.
140 Final Damage = 100 Damage x 140%


Critical Damage Modifiers

Critical Damage can be increased through equipment, cards, enchantments, and skills.

  •  Fallen Angel Wings,  Aunoe Card,  Fatal Lv4, Lauda Ramus.


These modifiers are all added together and multiply your critical damage after the Base Critical Damage is applied.


So, let's say we have a single  Aunoe Card, giving us +20% Critical Damage;
Final Critical Damage = 100 x 140% x 120% (100% + 20% increased Critical Damage)


First we multiply in the Base Critical Damage;
Final Critical Damage = 100 x 140% x 120% (100% + 20% increased Critical Damage)


Then we multiply that total by the Critical Damage Modifier;
Final Critical Damage = 140 x 120% (100% + 20% increased Critical Damage)


Giving us a total of 168 Critical Damage in the end;
Final Critical Damage = 168


Critical Skill Damage

Unless specified, skills normally cannot deal critical damage.


But certain skills, such as Sharp Shooting and Cross Impact, are exceptions and can deal critical damage.


Critical damage modifiers are applied with half effectiveness for these skills.


The formula for Critical Skill Damage is
Critical Skill Damage = Highest Skill Damage Variance x 140% x (100% + 0.5 * Critical Damage Modifier %)


Critical Damage Rate (4th job sub trait)

Critical Damage Rate (C.RATE) is a 4th job sub trait that increases the Base Critical Modifier by 1% for every point.


Note that C.RATE is an additive bonus to the 40% Base Critical Modifier, and is applied before other Critical Damage Modifiers.


The damage formula with C.RATE is
Critical Attack Damage = Highest Attack Damage Variance x (140 + C.RATE)% x (100% + Critical Damage Modifier %) or
Critical Skill Damage = Highest Skill Damage Variance x (140 + C.RATE)% x (100% + 0.5 * Critical Damage Modifier %)
