Say you swing your sword and hit me for 1000 physical damage. My soft defense is 200 and is calculated by mostly stats.

Now that 1000 minus the 200 of my soft defense has 800 incoming damage to me.

My hard defense is calculated on this graph (hard defense comes from gear and certain skills like Assumptio) and calculates it into a %



In this case, let's say my hard defense is 500. Elemental and other factors take account somewhere here but just for now. Because my Hard DEF is 500=50% reduction I then take

400 damage to my face.


Again that's:

1000 incoming physical damage - Soft DEF first (200) = 800 800 reduced by 50% (HARD DEF) = 400 to the face.
