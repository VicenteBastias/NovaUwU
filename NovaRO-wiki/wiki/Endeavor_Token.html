 Endeavor Tokens are a special currency exclusive to the server.

They can be obtained through:

  • Weekly Quests: Valerie O'Daley inside the Main Office.
  • Alternative Weekly Quests: Sally O'Weekly inside the Main Office.
  • Daily Quests: Bailey Miller in Battlegrounds (hallofvalor 176, 206)Copied! and Eden (moc_para01 106, 46)Copied!
    • Gramps completion: Finish a Gramps Quest. Reward: 1 (This is directly added to the player's inventory upon the first completion of Gramps each day)
    • Item Delivery: Gather 50 ea of a random item. Reward: 1
    • Monster Subjugation: Kill monsters from various dungeons. Reward: 2


They can be spent on the Master of Coin shop, located on the Second Floor of the Main Office (main_office 91, 301)Copied!.

They are also used for enchanting the Cursed Knight's Shield series.
