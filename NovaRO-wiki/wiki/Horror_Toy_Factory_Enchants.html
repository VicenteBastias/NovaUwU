Enchants

You can enchant the following equipment by talking to Joe Blackbeard (/navi xmas 240/294).

Each enchantment attempt costs 15  Bloody Coins and the item must be equipped to be enchanted. Everything can be enchanted safely, with no chance to fail.

You're also able to reset enchantments on any equipment with no chance to fail, for the cost of 200  Bloody Coins, 1  Bloodstained Letter and 1  Unsent Letter.
Accessories with 3 enchantments cannot be reset.
