Soul Drain Passive
  • Max Level: 10
 Increases Max SP, and whenever you defeat a non-player enemy with a magical attack you recover some SP.


  • [Lv 1] : +2% Max SP.
  • [Lv 2] : +4% Max SP.
  • [Lv 3] : +6% Max SP.
  • [Lv 4] : +8% Max SP.
  • [Lv 5] : +10% Max SP.
  • [Lv 6] : +12% Max SP.
  • [Lv 7] : +14% Max SP.
  • [Lv 8] : +16% Max SP.
  • [Lv 9] : +18% Max SP.
  • [Lv10] : +20% Max SP.


When you defeat a non-player enemy with a magical attack, recover SP equal to:
(Enemy Base Level × ((95 + (15 × Soul Drain Level))) / 100)

Magic Crasher Damage
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 8
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.24s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.06s
  • Cast Delay: 0.3s
  • Range: 9
 Strike an enemy with a weapon property physical attack, but you use your MATK instead of ATK for damage.


  • This attack uses the enemies DEF instead of Mdef.
  • This is considered a Ranged Physical Attack.

Napalm Vulcan Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: (15 x Skill Level) - 5
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.8s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.2s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Cooldown: 1.0s
  • Range: 9
 Strike a target and all enemies in a 3x3 area around them for Ghost property magic damage.

Additionally, there is a chance to inflict the Curse status on targets hit.


  • This skills damage is increased based on the casters Base Level.


  • [Lv 1] : 70% MATK, 1 Strike, 5% Chance to apply Curse status.
  • [Lv 2] : 140% MATK, 2 Strikes, 20% Chance to apply Curse status.
  • [Lv 3] : 210% MATK, 3 Strikes, 15% Chance to apply Curse status.
  • [Lv 4] : 280% MATK, 4 Strikes, 20% Chance to apply Curse status.
  • [Lv 5] : 350% MATK, 5 Strikes, 25% Chance to apply Curse status.
Ganbantein Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 40
  • Fixed Cast Time: 3.0s
  • Cast Delay: 5.0s
  • Range: 18
  • Consumes: 1  Yellow Gemstone
  • Consumes: 1  Blue Gemstone
 Clear a 3x3 area of any ground effects.


  • This skill removes Land Protector.
  • This skill is not affected by effects which remove or lower gemstone costs.
Gravitation Field Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: (20 x Skill Level)
  • Variable Cast Time: 5.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Cooldown: 5.0s
  • Range: 18
 Create a 5x5 area of effect with intensified gravity that deals Neutral property magic damage.


  • This skills damage is increased based on the caster's Base Level.


  • [Lv 1] : 100% MATK, Hits 2 times.
  • [Lv 2] : 200% MATK, Hits 4 times.
  • [Lv 3] : 300% MATK, Hits 6 times.
  • [Lv 4] : 400% MATK, Hits 8 times.
  • [Lv 5] : 500% MATK, Hits 10 times.


Note: The final damage formula is:
Damage = [Skill Lvl × MATK × (# of hits) × (Base Level / 100)]
