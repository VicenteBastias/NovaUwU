Introduction


Hello, my name is Ced, also known as Randomced859. I've been playing this server for a long time. I chose this server because they have Dorams in here and promised to return to RO. I ended up mastering Physical Doram class here.

In this server, I have two Doram builds with a swap each. Each build have some pluses and downs. For this guide, I will be using the two Physical Doram builds, which is Picky Peck build and Lunatic Carrot Beat build.

The difference between these two builds

Here's some pros and cons of each Physical Doram builds.

Picky Peck
Pros Cons
Can make a lot of damage on MVPS. Single target only.
It's very spammable once you have enough Global Cast Delay. The headgear and shield alone for endgame is a super zeny sink because you have to make them on your own.
Double casting chance of the skill is great. They're stuck with two same accessories.
At half HP, the damage is doubled. It's really bad for sweeping since it's only a single target.
Lunatic Carrot Beat
Pros Cons
Best for cleaning mobs. It requires four shadow gears in order to be spammable. One needs a +10 and two sets of combo.
Doesn't require Global Cast Delay. Needs 1 second Fixed Casting Time to make it spammable.
They're free from Chubby Earthworm Talisman lock on accessories. It's not as fast as Picky Peck due to hard cooldown.
They can have 100% ignore defense all mobs from shadow gears and shield alone. Really bad against MVPs unless MVP's minions sweep.

Picky Peck


Picky Peck build has been stable since the release of Dorams. It's also affected by turbo when targeted just for spamming the skills. At the beginning, it has some casting times until level 100, where you get a pair of  Chubby Earthworm Talisman. Also the skill has 1 second variable cast time and global cooldown. If you reached global cooldown of 86% and ASPD of 193, you will be spamming like a machine gun.

Stat Suggestion Notes
STR 120 Picky Peck affects by ATK stat since it's physical. STR also helps to increase carry weight.
AGI 40 - 45 You may go this low once you get  Temporal Jewel (Agi) lv.3 on  +12 Temporal Circlet (Summoner) [1], otherwise go 100 base AGI. Swing Dance from Wanderer is useful as support if you're lacking ASPD.
VIT 90 One of the most stable stat due to this secondary skill of Tuna Party. It also helps for some defenses as well.
INT Leftovers This one is mere a minor stat, unless you're after Silence resistance.
DEX 120 Very important,  Chubby Earthworm Talisman scales with DEX. Picky Peck can't crit. So that means a high HIT is useful against high FLEE mobs.
LUK 90 If you can reduce AGI as mentioned above. You can place points here for more ATK and Hit.
