Instances (also known as Memorial Dungeons) are one of the defining features of Renewal Ragnarok.

They are dungeons which can only be accessed once a party triggers the creation of the specific map.

Once created, the instance will only be accessible to the party which created it. Other parties who wish to enter such a dungeon will create a different instance of the map, which is where the term comes from.

There are all kinds of instances, including solo Instances. To enter said instances, you must create a single-person party.

Some instances have tickets, seconds chances to try. They work as extra lives but on the basis of one ticket per character. This means that in case the party wipes, for example, a member can re-enter the instance, by using a ticket, and try to use Resurrection or an  Yggdrasil Leaf in each member to see if they can now clear the instance.

Instances also have cooldown, a recharge time. Until the cooldown ends, the same character can't generate another one after starting it.

The cooldown of an instance starts to count as soon as the instance is cancelled if there was at least one member from the party that entered it. This means that one can generate an instance but not enter, cancel it to generate another, and the cooldown won't trigger. But the same can't be said of someone that entered, leave, the party leader didn't notice, and cancelled it. This will trigger the cooldown.
