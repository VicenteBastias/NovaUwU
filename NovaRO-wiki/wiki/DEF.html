DEF as seen in the stats menu, usually ALT+A,
SOFT DEF + HARD DEF

DEF reduces the physical damage that you get from any, long or short range, physical damage source being it a skill or an auto-attack.

In Renewal it was completely reworked to be a complement of the character instead of a key stat that nullifies physical damage possible to reach 100% through gear.

It's made of two parts, SOFT DEF and HARD DEF, and both are, respectively, seen on your character stats menu in that exact order.

HARD DEF can also be bypassed, up to 100%, depending on the gear and/or buffs used by the player.


Soft DEF

SOFT DEF, as the name says, it's a soft reduction that comes from stats and base level.

It's a final reduction of damage based on the value you see on your stats menu. This means that 200 SOFT DEF in ALT+A reduces 200 of the damage that you receive.

Both monsters and players have it and the formula for it is based on the following:

Formula for Players:

Soft DEF = Floor[(Total VIT/2)+(Total AGI/5)+(Base Level/2)]

Formula for Monsters:

Soft DEF = Floor[(VIT+Base Level)/2]


Some skills, such as Angelus or Divine Protection, reduce or increase its value.

Hard DEF

HARD DEF, as the name says, is a hard reduction of damage. Any DEF that comes from items is considered HARD DEF even if it only says DEF.

It's a reduction based on the gear you got equipped and, due to the way it works, it's fine to assume that after 500 HARD DEF you already have a good amount of reduction since it's equal to 50% of damage reduction. Some exceptions do exist, such as  Adamantine Lv. 5 from Bio5 Headgears, since they provide a good amount of HARD DEF with a minimal loss of damage in some builds.

Formula for % of Damage Reduced: 

% of Damage Reduced = (1-[(4000+Hard DEF)/(4000+Hard DEF*10)])*100

This means that 500 HARD DEF reduces damage by 50%, 250 HARD DEF will reduce physical damage by ~35%, and so on.

Since its scale is also not linear, the more you add, the more trouble you will have to increase it.

Be aware that it's technically impossible to reach 100% of damage reduction through HARD DEF so it's not a great stat to invest a lot into even if more means less damage received.

Some skills also, such as Echo Song or Concentration, reduce or increase its value.

Bypass % of DEF

Bypass % of DEF (also known as ignore/pierce DEF) is an effect related to HARD DEF even if only says DEF is all cases.

Skills that already bypass it at 100%, such as Wind Cutter, or treat it as SOFT DEF, like Arm Cannon or Cart Cannon, won't benefit from items that provide them.

Any type of it stacks with itself unless stated otherwise. It also caps at 100%.

SOFT DEF still applies at the end outside of very specific cases such as items with the Investigate(also known as Occult Impact) effect like  [MVP] Memory of Thanatos or  Ice Pick because Equip Attack isn't affected by it.

Examples

  • A player with a +10  Abusive Robe [1] (40% vs undead, demon, and demi-human) and Expiatio Lv 5 (25% vs all types of enemies) will provide a max bypass of 65% HARD DEF where applicable.
40 + 25 = 65 % bypass DEF vs Undead
40 + 25 = 65 % bypass DEF vs Demon
40 + 25 = 65 % bypass DEF vs Demi-Human
0 + 25 = 25 % bypass DEF vs other races
  • Inside Monster Hunter, a player with a  Purified Knight's Shield that has the effect of 60% bypass DEF vs Boss (60% vs Great Monsters), +10  Penetration Shadow Ring (20% vs Great Monsters), and +10  Penetration Shadow Necklace (20% vs Great Monsters) will have a total bypass of 100% HARD DEF.
60 + 20 + 20 = 100 % Bypass DEF vs Great Monsters
  • A player with a  Ice Pick (100% vs all type of enemies) and with the buff Expiatio (25% vs all types of enemies) will have a total bypass of 100% HARD DEF.
100 + 25 = 100 % Bypass DEF vs all types of enemies
Be aware that in this case, since the player got Expiatio, the Equip Attack that he will get from  Ice Pick will be inferior because Expiatio, and any other gear with a similar bypass effect, takes priority and removes some HARD DEF from the enemy first and only later the calculation from  Ice Pick enters into play.
If all this seems confusing, you can see  Ice Pick as ignoring 100% HARD DEF instead of bypass and Expiatio as a removal of a percentage of HARD DEF.

Formula to know the remaining HARD DEF left: 

DEF left = HARD DEF*[1-(% DEF BYPASSED/100)]


Other useful info

Skills like Arm Cannon or Cart Cannon that treat HARD DEF as SOFT DEF, even if not mentioned in the skill description, have both DEF types, SOFT and HARD, summed and that value will be reduced at the end of your damage as direct subtraction.

Items with the Investigate(also known as Occult Impact) effect, such as  [MVP] Memory of Thanatos or  Ice Pick, will use HARD DEF as an advantage by increasing your Equip Attack based on that value divided by two (100 HARD DEF means 50 extra Equip Attack, 200 means 100, and so on). This effect works only over the Weapon ATK of your main weapon for auto-attack (means that it doesn't boost the off-hand damage in auto-attack).
