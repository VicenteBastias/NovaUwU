Monster Hunter Rewards

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Venturing through the dangerous lands of the Savage Coast has its benefits. Upon defeating monsters and carving their remains, Monster Parts can be obtained and then traded towards various goods, which not only include Shadow Gears, but also exclusive equipment not found anywhere else.

Below is a listing of every reward obtainable through Monster Hunter hunts. Listings with no Star (☆) rank refer to Low Rank Hunts, while others list their respective ☆ Ranking for Savage Coast hunts.

Shadow Gears

Low Rank Shadow Gear
-

Simple Shadow Gears that provide a low stat boost and are worn over existing gear. A requirement to access High Rank Hunts.

  • Obtained through exchanging parts from monsters from the Flooded Forest.
High Rank Shadow Gear
☆ / ☆☆ / ???

Shadow Gear that provide various bonuses to a multitude of stats, skills and more. Worn over existing gear and can be refined separately.

  • Obtained through exchanging parts from the Savage Coast monsters, as well as assorted items gathered on the island.

Equipment

Fallen Angel Wings
☆

Popular garment that provides boosts based on base stats and can be enchanted.

  • Obtained through exchanging Crystals from Garanth and Nyia, as well as assorted items.
Archangel Wings
☆

A garment that can receive a single enchant. A catalyst for Fallen Angel Wings.

  • Obtained through exchanging Crystals from Garanth and Nyia, as well as assorted items.
Ancient Hero
☆☆

A variety of weapons, each boosting specific class skills and comboing with  Ancient Hero Boots [1] for extra stats. Can be enchanted.

  • Obtained through exchanging Crystals from Garronath and Nysori, as well as assorted items.
Temporal Armors
☆☆

Temporal Armors build upon their boot counterparts, providing Defense Pierce against Brute and Demon races. Can be enchanted.

  • Obtained through exchanging Crystals from Garronath and Nysori, as well as assorted items.
Violet Halo
☆☆

Violet Halo provides an alternative to popular garments, featuring a variety of stat boost through item combos, instead of enchants.

  • Obtained through exchanging Crystals from Garronath and Nysori, as well as assorted items.

Currency

Monster Coupon
-

Monster Coupons can be exchanged at the Retired Hunter shop for some equipment and consumables.

  • Obtained through exchanging parts from monsters from the Flooded Forest.
