Nightmare Pyramid is an extension to the regular Pyramid dungeon. It contains two floors, identical to the two Pyramid Basement floors, but contains stronger enemies, including the MVP Nightmare Amon Ra.

Location

  1. Warp to Pyramid. (Warper, Dungeons, Pyramids)
  2. Go to the center of the map, enter the warp.
  3. Walk to the south and talk with the Suspicious Cat. /navi moc_prydb1 103/54
  4. Inside the dungeon, you can get two hunting quests from the Tomb-Raiding Cat.
