Item Delivery

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Item Delivery is an account-wide daily quest that can be completed by turning in 50 ea of a certain item to Bailey Miller (moc_para01 106, 46)Copied! at Eden Group Headquarter (@go eden).

The requested item is randomly selected from a list every day. These items are usually 100% drops from high natural spawn rate low/medium difficulty monsters.

The reward (1 Endeavor Token) is account-bound. Players with multiple accounts can complete the quest multiple times per day (once for each account).
