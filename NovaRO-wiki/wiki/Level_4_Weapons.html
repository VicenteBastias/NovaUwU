Level 4 Weapons

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Level 4 Weapons is a long, costy but rewarding quest. Besides the ability to get various expensive items, one has to be lucky to obtain their desired weapon (or to obtain anything).

Note that if you start any of these quests, you cannot start another one until you finish that.
