Teleport Mobility
  • Max Level: 2
  • Target: Self

    • (Lv 1) : 10
    • (Lv 2) : 9
 Allows you to teleport randomly, or to your save point.

Warp Portal Mobility
  • Max Level: 4
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 38 - (3 x Skill Level)
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Range: 9
  • Consumes: 1  Blue Gemstone
 Create a portal on the ground that transports any player that steps into it to another map.


  • [Lv 1] Transports players to the caster's save point.
  • [Lv 2~4] Transports players to a specific Memo point.
  • You can use the /memo command to memorize a spot and add it to your Warp Portal destinations. Some maps and dungeons can not be added as Warp Portal destination.
