Item Reform

From NovaRO: Wiki
(Redirected from Equipment Reform)
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Item reform describes the process of converting certain weapons / armors into their corresponding lv5 / lv2 versions (usually superior) respectively (see also: Refinement System). This process only works for a limited list of gear (see recipes). Beware that the list of required materials is customized on NovaRO.

Upon reforming the item, all enchants are lost and the refinement is lowered depending on the used recipe. Any cards need to be removed before reforming.

To start reforming, for most gears, visit Mei in the grading room at (paramk 48, 169)Copied! (item series with specific reforming NPCs will be specified below). If navigation fails, warp to the para market @go 52 and go one map east.

Refining Reformed Items

Reformed items offer additional, guaranteed methods of increasing the refinement level by one. The success-rate is always 100%, there is no chance of breaking, damaging, or not advancing the refinement level.

This alternative way of refining exclusively works for the items listed below, and only if they are within the specified upgrade-levels. Outside that range, refer to the regular refinement process of lv5 weapons and lv2 equipment.
