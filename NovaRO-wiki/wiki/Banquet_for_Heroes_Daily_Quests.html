The Royal Richard

Requirements
Base Level: 140
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 80, 80)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Learning About the Families
Rewards
Experience: 25,000,000 (Base) and 25,000,000 (Job)
Items: 8  Honor Token
  1. Speak with the Butler for the Richards (prt_cas_q 80, 80)Copied! to begin this quest.
  2. He will asks you to hunt down 50 monsters of the race of your choice (undead/dragon/demi-human/etc...). The mobs must be level 140 and above to count towards your hunting mission goal.
  3. Return to him once you've completed your mission and he will reward you with 8  Honor Token.

Chief Guard requests

Speak with the Chief Guard (prt_q 156, 324)Copied! and he will give you one of the following daily quest randomly. There will be a marker on your minimap to know where the rescue takes place. You can talk with the Chief Guard again to show you the marker on the minimap.

Ungrateful (east of the fountain)

Requirements
Base Level: 140
Starting Point: (prt_q 221, 193)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Room of Consciousness
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items: 7  Honor Token
  1. Walk near (prt_q 221, 193)Copied! and the Village Lad will appear. Speak with him.
  2. Bring him to the nearest shelter (the village office, at the bottom left of the map (prt_q 77, 98)Copied!). He'll ask you to find his tool bag.
  3. The tool bag is found in the south-east building near the foutain (prt_q 178, 185)Copied!. You have to walk near the area and then select Pick up.
  4. Return the tool bag to the Village Lad (at the village office).
  5. Return to the Chief Guard for your reward.


Find My Sister (middle east, near the wall)

Requirements
Base Level: 140
Starting Point: (prt_q 276, 242)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Room of Consciousness
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items: 7  Honor Token
  1. Walk near (prt_q 276, 242)Copied! and the Girl will appear. Speak with her.
  2. Bring her to the nearest shelter (the church, at the north east). She'll appear once you get close by and ask you to meet with her sister.
  3. Go to Knights' building (north west) and she'll appear once again over there. She thanks you for bringing her with her sister.
  4. Return to the Chief Guard for your reward.


Blatant Bluff (south of the Chief Guard)

Requirements
Base Level: 140
Starting Point: (prt_q 140, 304)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Room of Consciousness
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items: 7  Honor Token
  1. Walk near (prt_q 140, 304)Copied! and the Soldier will appear. Speak with him.
  2. He wants you to go to the nearby shelter, which is the Knights' building (north west). He'll appear once you get over there.
  3. Speak with him again and this time he says he'd rather help at the village office (south west).
  4. Speak with him again at the village office and he'll take a rest inside.
  5. Return to the Chief Guard for your reward.


Mad Love for Wife (west location)

Requirements
Base Level: 140
Starting Point: (prt_q 38, 261)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Room of Consciousness
Rewards
Experience: 50,000,000 (Base) and 50,000,000 (Job)
Items: 7  Honor Token
  1. Walk near (prt_q 38, 261)Copied! and Eryus will appear. Speak with him.
  2. He'll ask you to meet him at the Church first (north east).
  3. Speak with him again at the Church and he'll ask you to find his wife.
  4. His wife is at (prt_q 108, 164)Copied!, south of the fountain.
  5. Talk to her and then bring her back to the Church.
  6. She now tells you that she's lost her medicine, go back to (prt_q 111, 166)Copied!.
  7. By walking by the area, a dialogue will pop up and you have to select Pick up.
  8. Go back to the Church and speak with Eryus.
  9. Return to the Chief Guard for your reward.


The Dress Dilemma

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 96, 184)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): The Roegenburgs
Rewards
Experience: 10,000,000 (Base) and 10,000,000 (Job)
Items: 6  Honor Token
  1. Speak with Agnes (prt_cas_q 96, 184)Copied! to begin this quest. You can find her in the Roegenburg's room (top right).
  2. She is hesitating on what dress to make for the Banquet. You'll have to bring her the ingredients she needs:
    • The first dress request, it is only requested once:
      • 3  Pearl
      • 50  Feather
    • Pink Petal-like Dress:
      • 30  Red Herb
      • 10  White Herb
      • 15  Soft Silk
    • Beautiful Flower Decoration:
      • 5  Flower
      • 2  Illusion Flower
      • 5  Mysterious Flower
    • Dress of the Night Sky:
      • 20  Piece of Black Cloth
    • Shawl of the Blazing Sun:
      • 20  Burning Hair
    • Step of the Fairy:
      • 5  Fancy Fairy Wing
    • Sparkling Star:
      • 5  Star Dust
  3. Return to Agnes to be rewarded with 6  Honor Token and some experience.


The Maid

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 95, 9)Copied!
Items:  Witherless Rose
Prerequisite(s): The Maid and Jurgen
Rewards
Experience: 15,000,000 (Base) and 15,000,000 (Job)
Items: 8  Honor Token
  1. Speak with Jurgen Wigner (prt_cas_q 95, 9)Copied! to begin this quest. You can find him in the Wigner's room (bottom right).
  2. He will ask you to deliver his love letter to the Maid in the Prontera Castle Kitchen (prt_cas_q 321, 214)Copied!.
  3. Return to Jurgen Wigner to deliver her reply.
  4. He will ask you to bring 15  Sea Stone and a  Witherless Rose to the Maid in the Prontera Castle's kitchen.
    The Sea Stones are obtained by killing Medusa in Comodo's Beach Dungeon.
  5. Return to the Maid and she will reject Jurgen's offer once again.
  6. Return to the Jurgen Wigner to be rewarded with 8  Honor Token and some experience.


Room of Consciousness

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_lib_q 91, 85)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Banquet of Heroes (Room of Consciousness)
Rewards
Experience: 25,000,000 (Base) and 15,000,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Honor Token
  1. After the cooldown has elapsed, go in the Prontera Castle and talk to Friedrich S. Heine (prt_cas_q 22, 82)Copied! in the Hein room. Select Yes.
  2. Go in the Prontera Castle inside your room and speak with Chamberlain Bell. Summon Nillem and he will warp you to the Past Memories of the Royal Family.
  3. Speak with Nillem again and he will make a Dimension Warper appear. Use it to go back in time.
  4. Speak with Nillem again to create the instance and then enter the Swaying Space to start the instance.

More information about the instance can be seen in Room of Consciousness' main page.

Royal Head Chef Daily Quests

The Perfect Sauce

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas 319, 211)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): The Royal Head Chef
Rewards
Experience: -
Items: 4  Honor Token
  1. Speak with Royal Sauce Master (prt_cas 319, 211)Copied! to begin this quest. You can find him in the Prontera Castle's kitchen (north west area).
  2. He will tell you what he needs for the sauce (he only tells you once each time) and you must collect what he requires. These can all be found in the kitchen.
    • The refreshing flavor is the Muka Juice.
    • The chewy texture is the Poring Jelly.
    • The ingredient that shines brightly is the Shining Plant.
    • The soft texture is the Giantaurus Butter.
  3. You must choose the correct ingredient 4 times in a row. Incorrectly choosing the ingredient will reset the count to 1.
  4. Return to the Royal Sauce Master and he will reward you with 4  Honor Token.


Dish Cleaning

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas 302, 205)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): The Royal Head Chef
Rewards
Experience: -
Items: 4  Honor Token
  1. Speak with Dishwasher (prt_cas 302, 205)Copied! to begin this quest. You can find him in the Prontera Castle's kitchen (north west area).
  2. He will ask you to collect 15  High-Class Dish. These are obtained from killing the Plates in the Dining Room (south west area).
  3. Return to the Dishwasher and he will reward you with 4  Honor Token.


Serving the Grilled Griffin

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas 309, 200)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): The Royal Head Chef
Rewards
Experience: -
Items: 4  Honor Token
  1. Speak with the Royal Head Chef (prt_cas 309, 200)Copied! to begin this quest. You can find her in the Prontera Castle's kitchen (north west area).
  2. Go to the empty spot in front of her (prt_cas 313, 198)Copied! and watch the chefs preparing the Grilled Griffin.
  3. Bring the Grilled Griffin to the Dining Room (south west area) to serve the meal and you will be rewarded with 4  Honor Token.
