Enchantment

Contraband Processor (@navi har_in01 17 74) will enchant some gear in exchange for  Rock Ridge Coins.

2  Rock Ridge Coins to enchant.
2  Rock Ridge Coins to reset with a chance of breaking the equipment.
50  Rock Ridge Coins to safely reset enchantments with no chance to break.
