Overview

Large Meditatio Bath is one of the field maps you get access to while doing the main quest of Episode 17.2 - Sage's Legacy.

Access to the dungeon is made through the portal at (ba_in01 385, 40)Copied! from Varmundt's Mansion Map after doing the quests needed to get access to it.

Entrance from Varmundt's Mansion Map
