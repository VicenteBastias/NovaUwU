Rock Ridge

Rock Ridge was founded on the ideals of innovation, opportunity, and industry. Set against the beautiful backdrop of an arid desert landscape bordering a vast ocean, Rock Ridge transformed into a booming village filled with opportunity - all thanks to the industrious efforts of Pink Rose.


Fistful of Zeny

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: alberta 240/103
Rewards
Experience: 62,500,000 (Base) and 30,000,000 (Job)
Items: 200  Rock Ridge Coin

 Sheriff's Left Badge [1]
OR
 Sheriff's Right Badge [1]

  1. Speak with Karam Pucci in Alberta at (alberta 240, 103)Copied! and and accept his mission.
  2. You'll find Costello waiting for you at the port's entrance. He tells you more about Rock Ridge and about a problem with the Buffalo Bandits.
  3. Find the Sheriff Wyatt Warp, he can be found inside the Sheriff's building at (harboro1 241, 215)Copied!.
  4. Talk to the Sheriff. You'll learn that the bandits only want to negotiate with Costello.
  5. Go back to Costello (harboro1 80, 218)Copied! and give him the news. He would rather have you negotiate on his behalf.
  6. Report to the Sheriff and you'll be asked to join the meeting yourself near the unmoving freight train.
  7. Exit the Sheriff's office and walk to the east map. You need to reach (rockrdg1 158, 306)Copied!.
  8. After the cutscene (your choices do not change the outcome of the storyline), go back to the Sheriff's office. (harboro1 241, 215)Copied!
  9. Speak with Ivoka Skudi and then speak with the Sheriff.
  10. Go to the basement (top right portal in the Sheriff's office).
  11. Talk to Johnny James in his cell and make him believe Albert Ford betrayed him.
  12. Report back to the Sheriff, he tells you that McCurdy has a good bargaining chip.
  13. Find Shawn McCurdy in the plaza (harboro1 288, 193)Copied!.
  14. Meet him at the mines. You can get there by going two maps east of Rock Ridge. He is on the far east side of the map (rockrdg2 347, 216)Copied!.
  15. After speaking to him, he'll be able to enter the mine. Speak with him once more inside the cave.
  16. Go further in the mine and destroy a Purple Ore. You will get a  Purple Ore.
  17. Speak with Shawn McCurdy again to leave the cave.
  18. Walk up towards the entrance dungeon (but don't go inside!) and you'll find a Concaved Rock nearby.
  19. Select the Knock, knock, knock signal and Albert Ford will show up! Speak with him and he'll give you his  Buffalo Bandit Mane.
  20. Go back to Johnny James in the Sheriff's prison and show him the mane. He tells you where the ores are hidden.
  21. Report the news to the Sheriff.
  22. Go back to Shawn McCurdy near the mine and he'll let you inside again. Talk with him again once you're inside.
  23. Go further in the mine and you'll see the Broken Elevator where the ores are hidden. Click it.
  24. Three bandits will spawn, kill them all and click the elevator again. You'll get the  Purple Ore Crate.
  25. Exit the cave by speaking with Shawn McCurdy and he'll ask you to collect the following items (these all drop from the bandits):
    • 5x  Brown Mufflers
    • 5x  Bandit Scarves
  26. You will also need to kill 3 of each Buffalo Bandits.
    You have to hunt the Buffalo Bandits from both maps (rockrdg1 and rockrdg2). Once you walk out of the cave, you will also have to go back to the map west to find the third type of Buffalo Bandit.
  27. Once you've collected the items and killed all the mobs, go back to the Concaved Rock and then speak with Albert. (rockrdg2 289, 309)Copied!
  28. Go back to the Sheriff (harboro1 241, 215)Copied! and choose a ring! You've completed the mission and unlocked more dailies.

Spotty and Her Ring

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: harboro1 347/65
Rewards
Experience: Variable
Items: 5  Rock Ridge Coin
  1. Speak with Mrs. McPhy at harboro1 347/65.
  2. Move out to the map east and you'll meet Mr. McPhy at the entrance.
  3. The ring can be found at rockrdg1 43/145. It is alongside the left side fence, at the bottom.
  4. Go back to Mrs. McPhy in Rock Ridge. She will reward you with 5  Rock Ridge Coin and some experience.
    You have unlocked the Spotty stole from her! and the Spotty stole from him! dailies.

Armor Dealer

The Armor Dealer (/navi har_in01 16/84) sells a limited amount of headgears every week. These are random but related to the theme of Rock Ridge (Western).

Affable Lady

The Affable Lady (/navi har_in01 24/69) exchanges zenies for some armors unique to Rock Ridge.

Maestro and Wanderer only.
