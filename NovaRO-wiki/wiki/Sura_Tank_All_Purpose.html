Overview

Sura is the third class job from Monk (Acolyte -> Monk -> Rebirth -> Champion -> Sura). Sura is way better than Monk and Champion because Sura has a variety of Area of Effect abilities, the ability to have up to 15 maximum spheres instead of 5 thanks to Rising Dragon, new powerful buffs like Gentle-Touch-Revitalize, Gentle-Touch-Change and deals lot of damage by using his HP with Tiger Cannon and Gates of Hell. And thanks to Steel Body combine with self buff, AB's buffs, and right gear Sura can tank almost all of the bosses (it might be tricky to tank a boss with Dispel skill, but there's always a trick to solve this, right? :D)

Stats

Here's the list of stats that give benefit to Sura:

  • STR - Increases your Status Attack (the left ATK number on your stats screen) by 1 per point Increases your weight limit by 30 per point. One of Sura's main stats.
  • AGI - Increases your Attack Speed and Flee. Having high AGI increases the damage of Sky Net Blow and ASPD affects how fast you can use combo skills and how fast you can snap around.
  • VIT - Increases your Maximum HP by 1% per point and increases the effectiveness of healing items. Tiger Cannon and Gates of Hell's damage increases based on your Maximum HP and stun immunity is granted at 100 VIT (total vit left+bonus) which makes this important.
  • INT - Increases your Maximum SP by 1% per point and increases the effectiveness of SP healing items. Reduces variable cast time. SP affects Tiger Cannon and Asura Strike and the cast time is very handy. Good to invest points into.
  • DEX - Increases Status Attack by 1 per 5 points, increases your HIT by 1 per point reduces variable cast time with twice the effectiveness as INT and increases attack speed. Very valuable stat even in small numbers.
  • LUK - Increases your total ATK by 1 and your HIT by 1 every 3 points. Increases perfect dodge. Useful in very small numbers when STR becomes expensive to put points into.


Builds

The builds can be different with skills that you want to use, but I have 3 builds for my sura: Levelling Build, Pure Tanking and Maximum Tiger Cannon output.

Levelling Build

I use this build from Acolyte until lv 130-ish Sura, mainly use combo for damage input and agi to increase flee and increase your Sky Net Blow damage.

  • STR: 90+
  • AGI: 90+
  • VIT: 50+
  • INT: 20+
  • DEX: 50+
  • LUK: 1 (Rest of your status)

Pure Tanking

I use this build to purely tanking at instances such as BMD, ET, or EC. The purpose of this build is reach 100 agi, vit, int, and luk to immune with certain stats. This build depends only with your equip combinations and Steel Body.

  • STR: 1-50
  • AGI: 90-100
  • VIT: 120
  • INT: 90-100
  • DEX: 1-50
  • LUK: 90-100
