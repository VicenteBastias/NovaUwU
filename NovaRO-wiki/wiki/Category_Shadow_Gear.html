Example of a character with refined Shadow Gear.

Shadow Gear is gear that provide effects and can be refined that goes into the Costume Tab of Gears Alt+A Menu.

They do not occupy the normal Costume slots that affect the looks of your character but instead all the other ones - Armor, Weapon, Shield, Footgear, and Accessories.

They do. however, have other names to prevent confusion with the normal gear available in the game:

Armor - Shadow Armor
Weapon - Shadow Weapon
Shield - Shadow Shield
Footgear - Shadow Shoes
Right Accessory - Shadow Earring
Left Accessory - Shadow Pendant

Besides their normal effects, refining them also provide other minimal effects per refine:

Shadow Weapon: +1 Weapon Attack
Others: +10 HP
