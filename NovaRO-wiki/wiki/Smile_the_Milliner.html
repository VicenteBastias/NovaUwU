About Smile

Smile is the youngest and self-proclaimed cutest of the Milliners. Little is known about her, aside from her wintery habits.
You can find her hanging around in Prontera, throughout the winter season.

Change hats colors

For the low price of 75  Jolly Socks and 3  Mysterious Dyestuffs, Smile can alter the color of select Christmas Headgears.
