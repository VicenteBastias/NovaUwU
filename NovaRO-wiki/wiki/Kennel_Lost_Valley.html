Overview

Entrance to Kennel Lost Valley

Kennel Lost Valley is the field version of the instance Lost in Time. You can access it as soon as you've access to Varmundt's Mansion.

Contrary to the instance, in this map you can actually drop the cards from those monsters.

The entrance to this dungeon is made through the portal at (ba_maison 313, 203)Copied!.
