Overview

Hello guys! My name is Marynia, I make videos about NovaRO content from time to time and I like to go for a walk around Rune-Midgard. I decided to do this guide due to the lack of content about this wonderful and powerful class, so I hope you like it. If you have any recommendations for the guide or any questions you can ask on my discord: Marynia#6897. My native language is not English, so forgive me if something is wrong!

Warlock is a powerful class for the game's end-game content, but it has great difficulty getting to that point. It is usually a class played in a group as its maximum potential is only extracted in the end-game. His main skills are the neutral element, wind and fire. As you read this guide, remember all that I said here so as not to be frustrated by the class if you are starting to warlock now. I strongly recommend that you do another meta class and then play warlock.

Some descriptions of Skills are from Ara's guide, I recommend going there if you want to make a Sorcerer.

Status

These statuses are recommended when playing in a group. If you are playing solo, I recommend that you invest more points in vit and agi to dodge attacks.

  • STR - 1-60

    It will only be used to increase the load capacity. I usually only carry my consumables, but if for some reason you are going to farm with your Warlock or something, you can invest a few more points here.

  • AGI - 1-100

    Increases your FLEE and attack speed. It's not very important, unless you want to dodge more attacks and get 100 agi to stay immune to sleep.

  • VIT - 100

    Increases the Max HP and DEF and 100 Total VIT can make you immune to Stun. It is a great status and there is no reason not to invest in it.

  • INT - 120-130

    Increases MATK, MDEF and and last but not least increase your MaxSP and 100 total INT will make you immune to Silence and Blind. It is the status that you want to maximize, as it comes raw magic damage. Some people put 130, but if you want to put only 120 to use Temporal Boots it's okay.

  • DEX - 120

    Reduces variable cast time. Warlock has a very annoying fixed cast, which is why we use the Temporal Boots of Dexterity, which is why Dex's 120 is necessary, in addition to helping with the variable cast.

  • LUK - 1-100

    It increases a lot of statuses, but the interesting thing for us is only the MATK (and if you want immunity to Curse with 100 points).

Skills

First Job Skills
First Job Skills are pretty much up to you, the most important is unlocking the skills you'll have after your job changes.
Skill Effect Notes
Increase SP Recovery Increases natural SP recovery and SP recovery from consumables.

Max level: 10

Provides a natural SP Recovery. Good skill to waste some points.
Sight Reveals hidden units around you.

Max Level: 1

I don't have much to say about this skill. It is very useful ... when an enemy is hidden.
Napalm Beat Inflict Ghost Property damage to a target.

Max level: 10

Prereq for Safety Wall, don't waste so many points here plz.
Safety Wall Creates a pink light pillar on a targeted location that blocks every form of close range physical damage until its durability wears off or expires. Each cast consumes a Blue Gemstone.

Max level: 10

Higher levels will block more hits and last longer. Can help a lot in early game when you don't have the fastest cast.
Soul Strike Unleashes light ghosts at a single target, where each ghost will inflict 100% Ghost property magic damage. This skill inflicts additional damage against Undead property entities.

Max level: 10

Fireball is better and is AoE. Don't maximize it.
Cold Bolt Inflict Water Property damage to a target.

Max level: 10

Good against the fire element, but you won't level up in places where you can use this skill, so I don't see any reason to get it right away.
Frost Diver Releases ice at a single target to inflict Water property magic damage. It has a chance of frozen the target.

Max level: 10

It is good if you want to upgrade with Jupitel Thunder and for the PVP.
Stone Curse Attempt to petrify a single target. The cast consumes a Red Gemstone.

Max level: 10

I never used this skill, I think I'm a failure as a Warlock, but I never missed it either.
Fire Ball Shoots a fire ball at a single target to inflict Fire property magic damage to enemies within its area of effect. Enemies at the edge of the area will take 3/4 of the damage.

Max level: 10

Use this skill to level up. Low cast, good ACD, reasonable damage.
Fire Wall Place a Wall of Fire on the ground. Enemies that walk into the cells will be knocked back and take Fire Property damage. Each cell of Fire Wall will break after a certain amount of hits. Undead Enemies will not be knocked back, but will take the max amount of hits per cell and break each cell immediately into walking into it.

Max level: 10

It is good for leveling, but it is on the list of skills that I have never used. It is much faster to just spam Fire Ball.
Fire Bolt Inflict Fire Property damage to a target.

Max level: 10

Use it to kill mobs before have Fire Ball.
Lightning Bolt Inflict Wind Property damage to a target.

Max level: 10

Good if u want to level up in Siromas before level 85. Don't maximize either.
Thunderstorm Inflict Wind Property Damage to an area.

Max level: 10

Who uses it?
Energy Coat Reduce damage taken at the cost of losing SP every time you are hit.

Max level: 1

It is very good to have a survival, since the shield can tank much of the damage without any problem. Keep an eye on your SP, because even Warlock not having a lot of SP problems if you take too many hits can be dangerous.
Second/Trans Job Skills
Again another group of skills that will not make much difference, except while you are in gramps or leveling something, just 3 or 4 skills are really important.
Skill Effect Notes
Fire Pillar Set a 3x3 trap on the ground that deals Fire property magic damage to any enemy that steps on it.

Max level: 10

You can have up to 5 Fire Pillars set at once. I once saw a person uploading it in hardcore mode, but I don't know if it really works.
Sightrasher Blast fire balls outward from you in all directions, reaching up to 7 cells away. Enemies in range are knocked back 2 cells, and take Fire property magic damage.

Max level: 10

This skill requires you to be in Sight status, and ends Sight status after using this skill. Not worth.
Jupitel Thunder Release a ball of lightning at a single target dealing Wind property magic damage and knocking them back.

Max level: 10

BEST skill for leveling with Storm Gust, but the problem is the target to be frozen.
Water Ball Draw water from the ground to summon a Water Ball to throw at a target, using an area around you to strike an enemy many times for Water property magic damage.

Max level: 5

You can use a Sage's Deluge or a Ninja's Watery Evasion for this attack, but the ground effect on each cell is removed for each strike of Water Ball. Not Worth it if u aren't leveling up in places that have water.
Ice Wall Create a wall of ice which prevents anyone from walking through the area.

Max level: 10

Ice Walls can be attacked by yourself, allies and enemies. Use it to teleport MVPs and things like that.
Earth Spike Raise spikes from the ground to deal multiple hits of Earth property magic damage to a single target.

Max level: 5

Heaven's Drive is better, don't waste points here.
Sense Reveal information about a monster. Retrieve their level, current HP, size, race, attribute, DEF, MDEF and property damage table.

Max level: 1

One of Warlock's best skills, incredible as it may seem. Inside the monster hunter it shows the percentage of life for each boss, which is simply AMAZING.
Meteor Storm Drop down meteors in a 13x13 area of effect, dealing Fire property magic damage to all enemies that get hit. Enemies hit have a chance to be stunned.

Max level: 10

It has a very high cast for the beginning of the game, and you will practically stay up in partys, so I don't see the reason to use this skill if Storm Gust exists.
Lord of Vermilion Call forth an explosive thunder bolt to destroy a 13x13 area of effect.

Max level: 10

As I said before, you will level up in Storm Gust basically, but if you want to get some points here, feel free. Some people use this skill when Siromas are close.
Lord of Vermilion Create an ice explosion around you in a 5x5 area of effect, dealing Water property magic damage to all enemies in range.

Max level: 10

Good skill to frozen. Only pick it if you are leveling up with Jupitel Thunder.
Storm Gust Summon an 11x11 area of effect blizzard that lasts for 4.5 seconds and hits all enemies in range for Water property magic damage every 0.45 seconds.

Max level: 10

Can knockback and frozen. Best skill to level up, and in addition it is amazing in partys. Cast at level 1 for the cast to be smaller.
Heaven's Drive Cause jagged spikes to rise up from the ground in a 5x5 area, hitting any enemies inside the area for multiple hits of Earth property magical damage.

Max level: 5

If this skill hits a hidden target it will reveal them. Good earth attack if you need.
Quagmire Create a 5x5 mud bog that cuts the Movement Speed, AGI, and DEX of enemies that enter it by half.

Max level: 5

Also removes the effects of Increase Agility, Two-Handed Quicken, and Adrenaline Rush type skills. Extremely good supportive skill, maximize it.
Sight Blaster Call forth a protective fire ball that lasts for up to 15 minutes. If an enemy walks within a 3x3 area of the caster, it deals 600% Fire property magic damage, and knocks them back 3 cells.

Max level: 1

Platinum Skill, don't use it (unless you want it).
Soul Drain Increases Max SP, and whenever you defeat a non-player enemy with a magical attack you recover some SP.

Max level: 10

Incredible skill, it is still passive meaning that every time you even kill a monster you will receive SP for free. Tell me a reason for not maximizing this skill.
Magic Crasher Strike an enemy with a weapon property physical attack, but you use your MATK instead of ATK for damage.

Max level: 1

Never used.
Mystical Amplification After using this skill the next purely magical skill you use will have its MATK greatly increased.

Max level: 1

It absurdly increases your magic attack for FREE by 100% at level max. The best High Wizard buff by far.
Napalm Vulcan Strike a target and all enemies in a 3x3 area around them for Ghost property magic damage.

Max level: 5

Don't use it (unless you want it).
Ganbantein Clear a 3x3 area of any ground effects. Consume 1 Yellow Gemstone and 1 Blue Gemstone.

Max level: 1

Removes Land Protector.
Gravitation Field Create a 5x5 area of effect with intensified gravity that deals Neutral property magic damage every 0.5 seconds.

Max level: 5

Don't use it (unless you want it).
