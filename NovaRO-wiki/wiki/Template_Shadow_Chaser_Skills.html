Fatal Menace Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 40:45:50:55:60
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Range: 1
 Strike a target and nearby enemies for damage. Higher levels grant extra Hit rate with this skill.


  • If you are using a Dagger class weapon, you will hit 2 times instead.

  • [Lv 2] : 240% ATK, -25 Hit, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 360% ATK, -20 Hit, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 4] : 480% ATK, -15 Hit, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 600% ATK, -10 Hit, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 6] : 720% ATK, -0 Hit, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 7] : 840% ATK, +5 Hit, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 8] : 960% ATK, +10 Hit, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 9] : 1080% ATK, +15 Hit, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv10] : 1200% ATK, +20 Hit, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • Current damage formula: [(SkillLevel * 120 + AGI) * (BaseLevel / 100)]% ATK
Reproduce Support
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 40:45:50:55:60:65:70:75:80:85
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 Allows you to copy a skill equal to this skills level.

To use this skill you need to activate it, take damage from a skill, then deactivate this skill so you can keep the skill you were hit with.


  • Skills copied will consume 30% more than their usual cost.
  • Copied skills still need their weapon or consumable requirements.
  • You can not copy transcendent, or monster skills.
