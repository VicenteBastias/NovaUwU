Pros
  • Pyroclastic can give up to ATK+275 when both Dieter and Pyroclastic are Max Level.
  • Granitic Armor reduces Incoming Damage by 10% at Max Level.
Cons
  • Pyroclastic forces your Damage to Fire Element and you're unable to switch Weapons while active.
  • Dieter must be Max Level to get the most of Pyroclastic.
Pros
  • Overed Boost can fix the Owner's ASPD and Flee Rate by 189 and 500 respectively without adding AGI Stats.
  • Light of Regen acts like a free  Token of Siegfried.
  • Silent Breeze is a spammable healing skill and overrides a lot of Status Effects.
Cons
  • Overed Boost depletes 50% of your MaxSP when re-used while it's still active and after the buff expires.
  • Eira must be Cordial to be able to use Light of Regen.
Pros
  • Pain Killer reduces Incoming Damage by a Flat Amount.
  • Poison Mist inflicts Blind Status Effect.
  • Needle Paralyze immobilizes the target.
Cons
  • Only 1 Poison Mist can be placed at a time and you must wait until the first one expires before placing a new one.
  • Summoned Minions from Summon Legion doesn't give any EXP when they kill Monsters.
Pros
  • Stahl Horn can Stun the target.
  • Stein Wand is basically a free Safety Wall for the Owner and Bayeri.
  • Heilige Stange is an AoE Holy Element Offensive Skill than can be spammed when max level.
Cons
  • Stahl Horn has a Fixed Cast Time.
Pros
