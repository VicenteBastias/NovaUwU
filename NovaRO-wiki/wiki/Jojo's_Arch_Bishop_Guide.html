Introduction

  • So folks, I’m Jojo (in-game name), and I’ve been playing Arch Bishop for a long time, and as the guides here are outdated or have been discontinued, I decided to make my own guide so that I can help beginners and even some veterans who don't play with the job. I've been playing with this job since 2003 ~ 2004, making it my main job since the early days of the game, so in a way I understand a little bit about it. So I will try to bring the Arch Bishop guide as complete as possible to the server.

About Arch Bishop

  • The Arch Bishop (knowed as AB) job is the 3rd evolution of the Priest job and will later become a future Cardinal (4th job). They are commonly built to assist in healing and buffing groups, but are also capable of tanking and even causing damage (whether sacred or even physical), creating a variety of builds for this class. To learn more about the builds, gears and shadow gears, go to the gears section.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • It is the most complete support job in the game.
  • Every party needs an Arch Bishop.
  • You can do a lot with budget equipment.
  • It is a great class for those who are starting to play.
  • There are several builds for this job.
  • The only one with spam cure and effective cure

Cons:

  • Its only source of magical damage is sacred property.
  • You have a lot of skills and sometimes you confuse them (especially being a beginner in the job).
  • Your auto-attack build is poor.
  • It needs a very high sustain te SP because the amount of SP spent with buffs is high.
  • Their endgame equipment is boring to get due to its rarity or little farm of them.


Stats

Stat Notes
Strength When players can not afford Gym Passes or Kafra Cards one may add STR points to hold more catalyst / consumables used for skills.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases weight limit by 30
  • Increases weapon damage by 0.5% of your base weapon attack (Melee Weapons)
Agility Having 190-193 ASPD after you achieve instant cast will assist your animation speed after you cast a skill.
  • Increases your ASPD.
  • 100 total points (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Sleep and Bleeding status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Flee by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Soft Def by 1
Vitality Added after INT and DEX hit 120+ to increase maximum health.
  • 100 total points (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Stun and Poison status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Max HP by 1% of your base HP

Every 2 points;

  • Increases Soft Def by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Intelligence First stat you aim for 120 as a Full Support Arch Bishop due to various skills relying on INT to determine the effectiveness. Every point of INT increases SP healing items effectiveness by +1% (SP) and decreases status variable cast time.
  • 100 points total (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Silence status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Max SP by 1% of your base SP
  • Increases MATK by 1.5
  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Dexterity Used to reduce status variable cast time at twice the effectiveness of INT.
  • Slightly increases ASPD.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Hit by 1
  • Increases weapon damage by 0.5% of your base weapon attack (ranged weapons, including instruments)

Every 5 points;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases MATK by 1
  • Increases Soft Mdef by 1
Luck Used in builds involving one or two Arc Angeling Card(s).
  • 100 points total (including bonuses) grants you immunity to Curse status.

Every 1 point;

  • Increases Critical Rate by 0.3

Every 3 points;

  • Increases status ATK by 1
  • Increases MATK by 1
  • Increases Hit by 1

Every 5 points;

  • Increases Flee by 1

Every 10 points;

  • Increases Perfect Dodge by 1 (PD avoids skilled attacks such as Magnum Break or elemental property basic attacks from monsters)
