Tips and Tricks

A section dedicated to helping you get better at Ninja, even if it is a little bit.

A few things you should know:


- Pure Soul has about a 50% chance to dodge single target magic.


- Use Shadow Leap to move while you are using Pure Soul to move around.


- Shadow Slash does not have an aftercast delay so only ASPD will affect the delay. Use this to close the gap and use the skills you desire, such as Killing Strike.


- Charms will greatly increase your magic damage if you have 10 of the same type of charm then cast the same element magic.

- Even if you do not want to change your weapon's element, use Earth Charm 9 times for 9 charms to increase ATK without endowing the element.

- Killing Strike's damage is increased by HP the most. Ranged damage is the 2nd highest option. ATK is the lowest of the three for damage.

- Dispell will remove ALL buffs you have at your disposal, including Pure Soul.

- The amount of dodges left on Mirror Image will increase your overall damage on Killing Strike. It will make it hit multiple times instead of just 1 and, even though it shows differently, the damage is calculated all at once so people can not pot through it.

- Illusion - Bewitch can switch positions even if your target is in Pneuma.
