Opening Act

Guillotine Cross is a sneaky-style class cannon DPS. That means they are super fragile to any kind of attacks and will most likely die super fast and this sneaky part means you have to play the mindset of a real assassin. Meaning that your positioning gonna be your hardest to earn skill compared to your gears. Cause your positioning means almost everything for Guillotine Cross. Keep this in mind If you are dead, you are useless to your teammate. That is what my Master in PvP used to tell me.

Guillotine Cross is one of the hardest hitting single-target DPS by now (December 2020) thanks to the skill Cross Impact or Soul Destroyer depending on the situation (e.g. Under Pneuma you can't use SBK).

Mainly our task as GX in PvP is Ground Control. The idea of Ground Control itself is basically you are making sure your defense is clean from any enemies. This could be Shadow Chaser, Sura, Genetic, RK, or basically anything that try to stay inside your pack. The way how I imagine this is basically if you are playing Bio5 you are not the main DPS. But you are making sure 100% that there are no other mobs who is currently diving into your small group. Basically those Divers need to be killed by you. After making sure that your pack is clear from enemy divers, another task of Ground Control is making sure that the Emperium Room is clear when you are defending. Basically any space behind your actual defending groups need to be cleaned by you. Those who pass are normally Suras / Shadow Chasers / or anything who passed your defense need to be killed as soon as possible so your teammate can focus on defending and staying at their designated position.

Before continuing to the next section I would like you to read Siege Costumization Page. Since both Cross Impact and Soul Destroyer DO NOT DEAL CRITICAL HIT. That means all of the gears you need to start with PvP-GX will most likely completely different or there are few items that are currently disabled. So properly check there, read the disabled gears part and the Guillotine Cross Alterations too.

Thanks to the 7-cells jump you don't have to move straight next to your target to be able to use this skill and hence why this build is kind of popular. As always this is just guideline. There might be a better build than this and you might also able to find your own theorycraft. So find yourself what actually work the best for your case. And here are my guidelines for the gears:
