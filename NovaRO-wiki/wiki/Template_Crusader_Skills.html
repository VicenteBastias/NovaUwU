Devotion Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ally
  • SP Cost: 25
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.5s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.5s
  • Range: 6 + (1 x Skill Level)
 Adds a protective link to your party members, allowing you to take all of the damage they receive.

This link will cancel if you go out of range of the target, switch maps, teleport, or the duration expires.


  • You can have up to 1 link for every level of this skill learned.
  • Targets must be within 10 base levels of the caster.
  • Crusader, Paladin, and Royal Guards can not be targeted by this skill.
  • Targets linked with this skill will inherit any aura-like buffs the caster has. (eg. Auto Guard, Defender, and Reflect Shield will all block damage for the target).


  • [Lv 1] : 7 Cells range, Duration 30 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : 8 Cells range, Duration 45 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : 9 Cells range, Duration 60 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : 10 Cells range, Duration 75 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : 11 Cells range, Duration 90 seconds.
