Dante's Shop at The Wyvern

Temporal Armors are a prime choice against Brutes or Demons, providing defense pierce against these races, and further increasing damage against Wind or Earth monsters.

All Temporal Armors combo with their respective pairing of Stat Temporal Boots.

They can be crafted at The Wyvern by Dante, at the stern of the Airship.

Enchantment

To enchant any Temporal Armor, a  Temporal Gem is used. They're also obtainable through Dante.

Using a stone will open up an enchanting window that will list every Temporal Armor on your inventory. Confirming the enchant will consume the stone and imbue the armor with 2 random enchants from different lists, based on their enchanting slot.

There is no chance of breaking, damaging, losing refines or cards on your armor. You can overwrite the enchants on your armor by using another stone.
