In NovaRO, you obtain Shadow Gear by running Monster Hunting instances. Shadow Gear is worn over existing gear and provides additional bonuses. High Rank Shadow Gear is crafted from materials obtained in High Rank Hunts on the Savage Coast.

They can be crafted by Harek Ignison, located at The Wyvern (2009rwc_03 245, 194)Copied!, accessible by typing @go MH.


These Shadow Gear come in a wide variety, and must be refined to wield their maximum effects. In addition, some select Shadow Gears have their effect doubled during High Rank Hunts.

You can also check all Shadow Gear available in NovaRO with this player-made tool (forum thread).
