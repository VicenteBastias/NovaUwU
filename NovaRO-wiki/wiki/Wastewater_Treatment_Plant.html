Overview

Wastewater Treatment Plant is the first map that you get access to after starting the Episode 17.2 quests.

Access to the dungeon is made through the portal at (ba_maison 77, 146)Copied! from Varmundt's Mansion Map or (sp_cor 258, 286)Copied! if you're in Cor as shown on the minimap on the right.
