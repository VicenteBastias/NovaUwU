Introduction


Hello, my name is Ced, also known as Randomced859. I've been playing this server for a long time. I chose this server because they have Dorams in here and promised to return to RO. I ended up mastering Physical Doram class here.

In this server as of 4th job released, I became a Spirit Handler. Spirit Handler as a physical build can now hit AGI Up monsters unlike Summoners which requires a Phreeoni pet to hit. Also Hogogong Strike is a crit AoE skill but requires Howling of Chulho status effect to apply first to hit. Also Howling of Chulho hits hiding enemies too and so does applying status ailment on high flee mobs. Howling of Chulho is also an AoE as well. Both are not affected by ACD but requires 1 second fixed casting time reduction to do so. You also need at least 140+ flat crit in order crit all of your skills espcially with consumables like Abrasives, Buche De Noel, and Arunafeltz Sandwich.

For Chulho Sonic Claw, you need 1 second Fixed Cast Time Reduction and 50% Global Cooldown in order to spam.

Status Points Settings

Stat Suggestion Notes
STR 120 Howling of Chulho and Hogogong Strike affects by ATK stat since it's physical. STR also helps to increase carry weight.
AGI Leftovers Howling of Chulho and Hogogong Strike affects ASPD but since you get a lot from AGI Jewel lvl 3 +12 Temp Circlet, you can do minimal on this.
VIT 100 One of the most stable stat due to this secondary skill of Tuna Party. It also helps for some defenses as well.
INT 90 This is more on Silence resistance + more SP due to the skills easily drained
DEX 120 For Ranged bonus boost.
LUK 100 Hogogong Strike and Sonic Chulho Claw can cause critcal damage on mobs.

Trait Points Settings

Trait Suggestion Notes
POW 100 For physical stat boost such as P.ATK.
CRT 97 This is for Crit build traits. It also helps increase the healing of Kisui Water Spray.

Skill Points Settings




Focus of having all three physical offensive skills maxed since you're going to use three of them at the same time for full DPS. You can now take full Spirit of Sea skil tree. Tuna Belly level 4 is recommended because of cooldown. Arclouse Dash lvl 5 is still a must since AGI +40 is really nifty. Power of Life is also crucial due to crit bonus. For ultimate skills, Temporary Communion is self cast that buffs P.ATK, S.MATK, and H.Plus by 15. While Blessing of Mythical Creatures is an instant AP refill on others. Do note however this doesn't work on other Dorams or yourself. Maxed out Marine Festival of Kisui since you're physical and some Sandy Festival of Kisui are trait buffs and it's really useful for party plays. If party members need AP, use Kisul Rampage.

There's a trick on using Howling of Chulho and Hogogong Strike. Use commands "/box and /box_range 5" to tell if you're hitting your AoE.
