Mysterious Sunflower

These dungeons require you to use the Castle Flag system, so you must be a member of a guild that won WoE in the most recent week for you to be able to access them.

Hminn (sch_gld, access to schg_dun01)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Schwaltzvalt Castles > Himinn, enter the castle.
  2. Use the LF-10 flag and select Go to Convenience Facility.
  3. Go to the Mysterious Sunflower (280,391), all the way up.
  4. Select Hold the stem.

Horn (aru_gld, access to arug_dun01)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Arunafeltz Castles > Horn, enter the castle.
  2. Use the LF-10 flag and select Go to Convenience Facility.
  3. Go to the Mysterious Sunflower (312,154), all the way up.
  4. Select Hold the stem.

Viblainn (sch_gld, access to schg_dun01)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Schwaltzvalt Castles > Viblainn, enter the castle.
  2. Use the LF-10 flag and select Go to Convenience Facility.
  3. Go to the Mysterious Sunflower (25,22), all the way left.
  4. Select Hold the stem.

Gate Manager

You need to speak with a Gate Manager to access the First Edition dungeons.

These dungeons open every Tuesday at 12:00 PM (noon) server time, and close when WoE1 begins on Saturday.

gld_dun01

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Arunafeltz Castles > Any (closest is Mardol's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords aru_gld,232,206, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun02

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Aldebaran Castles > Any (closest is Nuenberg's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords alde_gld,191,164, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun03

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Schwaltzvalt Castles > Any (closest is Skidbladnir's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords sch_gld,194,252, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun04

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Geffen Castles > Any (closest is Yesnelph's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords gef_fild13,189,206, in the middle of the map.

Gate Manager (Hall of Abyss)

You need to speak with a Gate Manager to access the Hall of Abyss dungeons.

These dungeons open every Tuesday at 12:00 PM (noon) server time, and close when WoE1 begins on Saturday. You must be a member of one of the current WoE-winning guilds to access these dungeons.

Prontera Hall (gld2_prt)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Schwaltzvalt Castles > Viblainn, enter the castle.
  2. Walk to the coords 290,226, near the flags.
  3. Select Abyss Gate Access.

Payon Hall (gld2_pay)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Arunafeltz Castles > Horn, enter the castle.
  2. Walk to the coords 121,320, near the flags.
  3. Select Abyss Gate Access.

Cat Paw Merchants (investment)

These are the locations of the Cat Paw Merchants. For more information about guild investment, go here.

gld_dun01 or gld2_pay (Arunafeltz - Valkyrja)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Arunafeltz Castles > Any (closest is Mardol's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords aru_gld,235,208, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun02 (Aldebaran - Luina)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Aldebaran Castles > Any (closest is Nuenberg's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords alde_gld,186,157, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun03 or gld2_prt (Schwaltzvalt - Nidhoggur)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Schwaltzvalt Castles > Any (closest is Skidbladnir's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords sch_gld,190,253, in the middle of the map.

gld_dun04 (Geffen - Britoniah)

  1. Warper > Guild Castles > Geffen Castles > Any (closest is Yesnelph's castle).
  2. Walk to the coords gef_fild13,187,208, in the middle of the map.

Namis (investment)

  1. Warper > Town > Malangdo.
  2. Walk to the coords malangdo,218,123.
