Ministrel AKA Maestro
File:File:Character Minstrel.jpg
Job Base: 175
Written By: Cybard
Job Bonuses
STR AGI VIT INT DEX LUK
7 5 7 9 8 1


Playing as a ministrel isn't that hard. you just need to know your positioning, switching your skills, knows what buff you should give, and getting that sweet 150 INT and 150 DEX. In this guide, I'll give out my personal build to barding. You won't be able to survive soloing, but you'll become great asset on instance run or MVP hunting. I also going to give a little bit of tips on how to leveling ministrel on it's early stage
