Daily Rewards

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Each day you log in if you're logged on for an hour or more, you will receive a different reward, and don't worry if you miss a day, you WILL NOT miss out on the reward for that day, you will simply get it the next time you get your daily.

Players have two calendars to choose from: Leveling (Geared towards new players going through the leveling process) and Advanced (Geared towards veteran players).

Daily Rewards NPC

After you get the reward for your 31st login, the Daily Reward Calendar will then reset back to day 1 and the process will begin again, you are also free to switch between the calendars at the start of every new cycle. However, once you start progressing on a particular calendar, you must finish the entire calendar before you can choose a new one.

You can use the @dailies command to bring up your Daily Reward Calendar as well as to check your daily reward timer at any time.
Below are the two available calendars. (Note: You receive Gold Coins every day on top of the daily reward for that day.)

After being logged on for an hour or more, you can redeem your daily reward at main office (@go 50) at Valerie O'Daley (main_office 187, 184)Copied!

Please keep in mind that the daily reward is limited per Person. Claiming multiple rewards on multiple accounts will result in a ban! (See Server Rules)

By choosing the Leveling Calendar over the Advanced counterpart, you get these extra items every month:

  •  30 Adept Hunter Contract
  •  6 Field Manual
  •  6 Job Battle Manual
  •  2 HE Battle Manual
  •  3 Old Card Compendium
  •  15 Token of Siegfried
  •  30 Endeavor Token
  •  20 Tabby Coin
  •  500 Thanks Invest Ticket

By choosing the Advanced Calendar over the Leveling counterpart, you get these extra items every month:

  •  5 Buff Scroll Compendium
  •  5 E Converter Compendium
  •  1 Mystical Card Album
  •  6 Bloody Branch
  •  7 Convex Mirror
  •  1 Battle Coffer
  •  30 Event Ticket
