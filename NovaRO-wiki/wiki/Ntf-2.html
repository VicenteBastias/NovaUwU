Nightmare Toy Factory is a custom instance that aims for being more difficult than regular Ragnarok Online content, while still keeping the feeling of the original game - not restricting gear or skills.

Instance is similar to Horror Toy Factory but harder.

Seeing that is a hard custom instance, there is no restriction on how many times players can enter. This gives everybody more options to learn the fight and find new strategies.

Monsters drop  Bloody Cloth Piece to create some of the rewards.

The instance consists in 4 zones with MVPs in the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th zone. Each player wins 5, 20, and 25  Shard of Nightmare from each respectively.

The MVP in the 2nd zone is similar to Entweihen Knothen. It has slaves that heal and deal a ton of damage.

The MVP in the 3rd zone splits into more MVPs and they've all the same name. Some skill combos from it may end being fatal to many unprepared players.

Last one is a stronger version of Kimi and has some mechanics similar to the final MVP of Glast Heim: Challenge Mode.

NPC that lets you craft gear and orbs with the drops from Nightmare Toy Factory.



With the drops from Nightmare Toy Factory you can craft a bunch of rewards.

You will need  Shard of Nightmare and  Bloody Cloth Piece depending on what you want.

 Shard of Nightmare is a reward that players get after completing one of the MVP stages in this instance.
 Bloody Cloth Piece is a reward from all monsters in the instance (remember to use autoloot to get them!)
