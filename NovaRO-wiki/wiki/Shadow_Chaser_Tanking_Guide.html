Tanking Shadow Chaser
File:SCtank.png
Job Base: Thief
Written By: Munbalanced
Job Bonuses
STR AGI VIT INT DEX LUK
8 5 8 6 4 6


This guide will show you the ropes of the tanking shadow chaser. Shadow chaser is one of the most viable tanks in the game, due to having many resourceful skills to not only effectively tank bosses of all levels but to also provide massive damage buffs to their party.

This guide will assume you know the basics of RO and shadow chaser as this as seen as a very endgame or luxury build. However it can be done eariler on to level as well, it is just a lot harder to make work on mobs.

Overview

Ay yo, I'm Munbalanced, I make videos on youtube primarily about RO! I've talked about a certain build, a tanking build for shadow chaser several times on my channel, on this page I'm going to go into as much detail as I can about the build and hopefully help you guys get your barrings.
