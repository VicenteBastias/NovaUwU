About Miles

Miles the Milliner is the adorable cat girl in charge of a little but thriving store that offers costuming services.
Always fascinated and enthralled (in equal measure) by opportunities to fasten sequins and sprinkle iridescent glitter on everything she comes in contact with, you are sure to delight her by bringing a headgear or two for hat-related science. She herself has shown to be partial to headbands, as she considers them to be the perfect compliment to her fluffy ears. Yet, thus far, thanks to her surprising and occasionally mildly frightening agility that stems from years of needlework, nobody has succeeded in patting Miles on the head and confirming whether or not they are the real deal.

You can find her on Third Floor of the Main Office also known as the Style Boutique, near Monkey.

Below is the list of services she provides in-game.

Make a hat into a costume

Miles spent years training an ancient art that allows the transformation of any regular headgear or garment into pretty, sparkly costumes.

Note that she won't be able to undo this process.

It costs 500,000 Zeny for a Costume Headgear. Costume Garments are a bit more expensive, at 2,500,000 Zeny. Still, a low price to pay for such great skills.

Change a hat's color

Miles is also exceptional at changing the color of various hats. She never has the materials on hand, but if you bring her 3  Mysterious Dyestuffs and 2,750,000 Zeny, she can do it in no time! Below is a list of all the hats this service is available for:

Change a hat's position

An incredible feat to learn at a such early age (even if she doesn't tell it to anyone), the ancient skill passed down generations of Milliners: Being able to swap the position of a number of headgears between middle and lower.

This service goes for a harsher price of 15,000,000 Zeny, due to how rare it is to find someone capable enough to do it.

Costume enchants update : If your M/L costume headgear has a Costume Enchant slotted inside, the NPC won't let you change its position. Remove your stone before changing the position.

Compress a Hat

Miles can compress a Hat in order to reduce the number of slots some hats occupy.
Any compressed hat will no longer grant any stats or effects it originally had and will turn into a costume.
It will cost you 15,000,000 Zeny.
Costume enchants update : If your costume headgear has a Costume Enchant slotted inside, it will be deleted ! Remove it before compressing.

Recycle package hats

Being a hat collector at heart, Miles basks in an uncountable amount of costumes. Nevertheless, she's always looking to expand her collection even more.

She will exchange 5 hats from the same Nova Shop hat box for an unopened box of the same type.
