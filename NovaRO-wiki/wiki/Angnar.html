Angnar is an earth elephant found during High Rank Hunts at the Savage Coast.

It is seen in habitating either the Highland or Forest biomes on the island (zones 4, 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9).

Angnar can spawn on the Red or Green areas

Skills

Germination

Angnar will choose a random player and give him a germination effect that lasts for 10 seconds.
While a player has a germination effect, they will take damage every second. This effect can be reduced by half using Slow Poison and it can be removed by using Sanus.
Germination is a physical earth attack that can only be reduced by earth resistance, earth armor and DEF.

Plague

Randomly a fig will appear on the ground somewhere close to Angnar. It will grow over the course of a few seconds. Once fully grown it can be eaten by interacting with it. The fig has only 4 pcs so only 4 players can eat it (eating the fig will make you immune to Plague). Then after few seconds Angnar will cast Plague and anyone who did not eat the fig will take considerable damage.
Plague scales up with player's HP and can only be reduced by poison resistance and poison armor.

Seismic Quake

Randomly Angnar will cause Seismic Quake on the ground. This quake only causes small damage to players.
Seismic Quake is a physical earth attack that can only reduce by earth resistance, earth armor and DEF.

Charge

Angnar will mark a random player except the tank (or whoever has highest hate). Then after a few seconds he will charge toward the target and deal some damage to them.
Charge is a physical earth attack that can only be reduced by earth resistance, earth armor and DEF.

Rot

A stacking debuff that increases all damage taken by 0.5%. Upon reaching 10 stacks, it becomes Necrosis.
Two ways to get this stack:
  1. Randomly there is purple square spawn on the ground, any player on top of it will get 3 stacks.
  2. A Lotus Flower will appear randomly and spread Rot stacks to all players simultaneously. if a player uses Interact on the flower, they will go inside of the flower. During this time they will not be able to move or cast any skills and will be the only one to get Rot stacks. The flower can be targeted and killed by other players but it is no necessarily the best strategy.

Necrosis

A stacking debuff that increases damage taken by 5%. Every 10 stacks of Rot will increase Necrosis by 1. When a player reached 10 stacks, they will get high damage until death.
To reduce the Necrosis stacks, there are some Pome Stalks around the zone and after a certain time they will become Wild Pome. Eating one of these is the only way to reduce stacks.

Stone Rain

Earth damage only applies to the tank and it does five hits to the target.

Tips

  1. Utility or whoever has the lowest DPS should use Interact on the Lotus Flower as soon as the flower appears to prevent other players from getting Rot stacks. Other DPS players don't have to hit the flower just focus on hitting Angnar. Eventually the player will be released from the lotus and can rejoin the party.
  2. Always watch your Necrosis stacks especially for utility who may get a very high stack count due to interacting with Lotus Flower. Whenever you have more than 5 stacks try to eat Wild Pome to reduce it. Or just kill Angnar quickly :)
  3. Use Slow Poison or Sanus whenever someone gets the germination debuff. Be careful as this debuff can easily kill players or at least make them use a Max Potion or two.
  4. Figs only have 4 pieces, so usually utility/Tank or whoever has high resistance should not eat the fig. Also, eating fig makes you immune to Plague for just a few seconds so don't eat it right away, it is better to wait a few moments and then eat it to make sure you have Plague immunity when Angnar casts it.
  5. It is recommended for everyone to have some earth resistance to reduce the damage coming from Germination, Seismic Quake and Charge.
