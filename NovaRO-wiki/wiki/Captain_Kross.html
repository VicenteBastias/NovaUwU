Crafting

Pharmacy Crafting

Brewing Rate = Crafting Success Chance
(Pharmacy Skill Level x 3%) + (Learning Potion Skill Level x 1%) + (Instruction Change Skill Level x 1%) + (Job Level x 0.2%) + (Dex x 0.1%) + (Luk x 0.1%) + (Int x 0.05%) + Item Chance

  • Every 1 level of Pharmacy, Brewing Rate +3% (Max +30%)
  • Every 1 level of Learning Potion, Brewing Rate +1% (Max +10%)
  • Every 1 level of Instruction Change, Brewing Rate +1% (Max +5%) (Vanilmirth Homunculus Skill)
  • Every 1 Job Level, Brewing Rate +0.2% (Max +14% at Job Level 70)
  • Every 1 Dex, Brewing Rate +0.1%
  • Every 1 Luk, Brewing Rate +0.1%
  • Every 1 Int, Brewing Rate +0.05%
  • Each potion increases or decreases the Brewing Rate


While the bonus to Brewing Rate is random, you should always calculate for the lowest bonus, this is to make you can get 100% crafting chance. (Use the Green value for each potion).
