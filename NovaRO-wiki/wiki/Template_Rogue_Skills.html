  • Max Level: 5

  • SP Cost: 12:14:16:18:20

Raid Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 15
  • Area of Effect:
    • 5x5
 Deal damage to an enemy and the targets around it. All enemies hit by this will take additional damage from all sources.

Additionally this skill has a chance to Stun and Blind the targets.

The damage increase is reduced against Boss monsters.


  • [Lv 1] : 200% ATK, +30% Damage to the target, (+15% to Boss), 13% Chance to Blind and/or Stun the target.
  • [Lv 2] : 350% ATK, +30% Damage to the target, (+15% to Boss), 16% Chance to Blind and/or Stun the target.
  • [Lv 3] : 500% ATK, +30% Damage to the target, (+15% to Boss), 19% Chance to Blind and/or Stun the target.
  • [Lv 4] : 650% ATK, +30% Damage to the target, (+15% to Boss), 22% Chance to Blind and/or Stun the target.
  • [Lv 5] : 800% ATK, +30% Damage to the target, (+15% to Boss), 25% Chance to Blind and/or Stun the target.

Plagiarism Passive
  • Max Level: 10
 Allows you to automatically copy the last skill used against you. You can find the skill in the "Misc" tab of your skills.

Higher skill levels lets you copy stronger skills.


  • This can only copy 2nd class and lower skills that deal damage to you.
  • This will not copy monster exclusive skills.
  • Requirements to cast the skill are still needed, like consumables or specific weapon types.


  • [Lv 1] : +1% ASPD, Able to copy a level 1 skill.
  • [Lv 2] : +2% ASPD, Able to copy a level 2 skill.
  • [Lv 3] : +3% ASPD, Able to copy a level 3 skill.
  • [Lv 4] : +4% ASPD, Able to copy a level 4 skill.
  • [Lv 5] : +5% ASPD, Able to copy a level 5 skill.
