Hiiii, I'm Lady Narnia! Welcome to my comprehensive High-Rank Monster Hunter (MH2) guide to healing for Dorams. =)


In MH2, Summoners are given many tools that make for a powerful healer that can heal any team!
This guide is commited to helping you reach that potential by breaking down skills, showcasing different gears, and making comparisons to Archbishop to show what you bring to the table.


I will also be highlighting new mechanics and special changes Summoner skills get when entering high-rank MH (and there is a lot).


Dorams are very special, having the only class with skills expanded to fit the healer archetype!

  • Now includes a primary healing skill that heals AND applies a regen packed into one.
  • Reworked skill to instantly shield AND heal for a high amount of HP.
  • Multitude of unique buffs to help your team (Flee+100, AGI+40, Blessing, Increase AGI, ATK/MATK+10%)

Please note that this guide does not apply to Low-Rank Monster Hunter as no mechanics are changed

High-Rank Monster Hunter Mechanics

Monster Hunter vastly changes many things from the Ragnarok you know, especially skills. You can practice these new mechanics and skill changes in Expeditions.
To start an expedition, speak to the High Rank Agent (2009rwc_03 154, 209)Copied!.
Unlike 1-star hunts and 2-star hunts, Monster Hunter Expeditions can be started by yourself.

Main article: Monster Hunter Alterations

Stats

Stat Explanations

STR – 40+

  • Increases carrying capacity and damage done by physical skills.
  • Great for healers who want higher damage Picky Pecks to deal damage between heals.

AGI – 1+

  • Increases Attack Speed and Flee.
  • Doesn’t affect MH debuffs.
  • Not very useful outside of higher Picky Peck speed.

VIT – 110-125

  • Increases your MaxHP and Defense. More HP means less dying. =) Super important!
  • I use 125 VIT, but you can lower your VIT to get a lot of spare points for other stats.

INT – 125

  • The only stat that increases your healing power. MAX THIS STAT!
  • Increases magical attack which isn't important.

DEX – 35+

  • Decreases variable cast time. 125 INT + 35 DEX gives you 30% VCT for Tuna Belly's 0.7 cast time limit.
  • Healing skills get cast time limits, so this only affects buff casting speed. I use 80 DEX for speedy convenience.
  • Raises damage of Picky Pecks when used with  Chubby Earthworm Talisman [1].
  • Does not increase healing power.

LUK – 1

  • Increases perfect dodge and slightly increases MATK (not healing power).
  • Doesn't affect MH debuffs. Not worth any points really. =\

Skills

There isn't much wiggle room for Doram healers in MH2, since healing requires all points to be in the Power of Sea column.
Removing any points from Power of Sea forces you to give up a lot of healing power for very little value anywhere else.
But don't worry! Here is my recommended skill build and the one I use personally! =)

Skill Tree

It's pretty straightforward. Here's a breakdown of how the skills work in Monster Hunter and their priority.
Note: All skills are in the context on Monster Hunter and work differently outside of Monster Hunter.
Another Note: The healing skills were made to mimic Heal and Highness Heal. They do not directly cast them.

Skill Breakdown

Mandatory

  • Fresh Shrimp - Primary heal.
    • Target receives Lv.10 Heal and Fresh Shrimp regen.
    • Lv.5 Heal per regen tick.
    • Instant cast, non-reducable 0.7 second cooldown.
  • Tasty Shrimp Party - Party heal and regen.
    • Party receives Lv.10 Heal and Fresh Shrimp.
    • Party receives a 1.25% HP regen. (Stacks with Fresh Shrimp)
    • Instant cast.
  • Tuna Belly - Burst heal.
    • Target receives Lv.5 Highness Heal.
    • 30 second cooldown at all levels.
  • Tuna Party - Burst heal and burst shield.
    • Target receives Lv.5 Highness Heal.
    • Create a barrier that prevents damage equal to HP healed.
    • 30 second fixed cooldown can not be reduced by items.
  • Hide - Healing steroid.
    • Increases your healing power by 150%.
    • Increases SP consumption by 200%.
    • Reduces aggro generation by 50%.
    • Lasts 20 seconds.

Important

  • Arclouse Dash - Powerful buff.
    • Target receives AGI+40 stat bonus.
    • Target receives Assumptio. (+250 Hard DEF, +10% Healing Received)
    • Target receives increased movement speed.
  • Bunch of Shrimp - Party damage and piercing buff.
    • Party receives +10% ATK/MATK buff.
    • Party receives Expiatio. (+25% DEF/MDEF Piercing)
  • Purring - Party PANIC button.
    • Party receives Flee+100.
    • Party receives Kyrie Eleison.
    • Entire party purrs.


Situational Skills

  • Grooming - Self PANIC button.
    • Gives yourself Flee+100
    • Give yourself Kyrie Eleison.
    • Let's everyone know you're flipping the heck out.
  • Cure  Obeaune Card
    Detoxify  Poporing Card
    • Required for Garronath to remove Fester stacks from the tank or yourself.
    • Removes 1 stack at a time.
  •  Life Powder - Extreme AoE heal item.
    • Heals party for 35% HP.
    • Can only be used 3 times per hunt.
    • Crafted using your  Combination Kit.
  •  Dash Juice - Movement speed increase item.
    • Gives yourself increased movement speed.
    • Raises movement speed limitation.
    • Gives yourself +400% SP regeneration.
    • Crafted using your  Combination Kit.

Disabled

  • Stoop
  • Lope

Monster Hunter Skills

After receiving the Healer Role from the Role Manager (2009rwc_03 162, 196)Copied!, you get access to exclusive Monster Hunter skills that complete your healing kit.
These skills are more important than your main skills!!!

Monster Hunter Skill Breakdown
  • Celeritas - Powerful AoE buff.
    • Party receives Lv.10 Blessing.
    • Party receives Lv.10 Increased Agility. (Stacks with Arclouse Dash)
  • Eucharist - Ultimate healing steroid.
    • Target receives 100% more healing from all healing sources.
    • Instant cast. Last 20 seconds.
  • Covenant - Extreme heal.
    • Pulls target to your location and heals them for an incredible amount.
    • The healing is really strong. Lv.10 Heal x 10 strong.
    • Instant cast.
  • Sanctum - 80% damage reduction field.
    • Creates a 5x5 zone that reduces damage by 80% for everyone inside at target location.
    • Prevents knockback for everyone within the field.
    • Instant cast. Last 15 seconds.
  • Sanus - Cures MH debuffs.
    • Cures Disorientate, Incapacitate, Dizzy, and Snooze debuffs.
  • Divinity - Powerful passive, also a quick heal
    • Passive
      • Healing Power increased by 25%.
      • Reduces After Cast Delay by 20%.
      • All skills consume 100% more SP.
      • Nullifies  Shrimp requirement for all skills.
    • Active
      • Places a 3x3 zone that heals the first player to enter and increases their movement speed
      • Last 6 seconds.

Skill Rotations

With the addition of the Monster Hunter skills, which ability to use when can be a little unclear.
I personally use my skills in this way. I hope you can adopt it to your style!

Skill Rotation
  • Start fight with Sanctum
  • Spam Fresh Shrimp
  • Immediately when off cooldown, Tasty Shrimp Party
  • Using your best judgement, Tuna Party
  • If tank is below 70% HP but is stable, Tuna Belly
  • If tank is below 50% HP and dropping,
    • Cast Eucharist or Hide (Remember, Hide gives you +150% healing power)
    • Then Tuna Party
    • Then Tuna Belly
    • Then keep spamming Fresh Shrimp
  • If tank is unstable and rapidly dying,
    • Quickly cast Covenant
    • Then quickly cast Hide
    • Then quickly cast Tuna Party
    • Then Purring

Equipment

There is plenty of gear for you to choose from. The gear you decide on will depend on your playstyle, confidence, and budget.
This will breakdown different pieces of equipment so you can make smart decisions for your Doram. =)

Mechanics matter just as much as gear in Monster Hunter. However, gear can make your experience a lot better!

A simple guideline you can follow to know when you're ready to take on 1-star hunts is:

  • 320+ Weapon MATK or more when refined (Only Weapon MATK. Not total.)
  • 20%+ Healing Power
  • 20%+ Bonus MaxHP for beginners
  • 125 VIT for beginners
  • Extra: Enter an expedition and heal a Tank Role ally from a Primal Monster for 2 minutes straight.

Try out Muh's Heal Calculator to test out your build!
