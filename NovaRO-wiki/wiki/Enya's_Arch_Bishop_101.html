Class Overview

Arch bishop is the third class job of priest branch. Their main skill set is tailored towards healing and supporting, with a few skills in damage-dealing. As a damage dealer, priests have an advantage over demon and undead race monsters as most of their offensive skills are holy property. Arch bishops are a staple support class in most party-oriented tasks such as running gramps leveling party, instances and battlegrounds or War of Emperium.

Basic Stats

  • Level: Each base level gives +1 hit, +1 flee, an increase to your max HP and SP pool, and some status points you can allocate to any of the following 6 stats. Certain skills also scale off base level, such as Heal. Each job level gives +1 job point, and a bonus stat to one of your 6 stats. Certain skills also scale off job level, such as Clementia and Canto Candidus.
  • STR: STR gives status ATK and weight capacity for carrying equipment and supplies. Each 1 point in STR gives +1 ATK (for arch bishops, who can only equip melee weapons) and +30 weight capacity. As a defensive stat, STR reduces Vacuum Extreme duration by 0.04s per point.
  • AGI: AGI gives flee, ASPD, and resistance to sleep status. Each 1 point in AGI gives +1 flee, 1/6 base ASPD and 1% resistance to sleep.
  • VIT: VIT gives HP, soft Def and resistance to stun and poison status. Each 1 point in VIT gives 1% max HP, 1 soft Def, 1% resistance to stun and poison.
  • INT: INT gives MATK, SP, soft Mdef, and resistance to silence status. Each 1 point in INT gives 1.5 MATK, 1% max SP, 1 soft Mdef, and 1% resistance to sleep. Your total INT also determines your cast time along with your total DEX. INT is an important stat for arch bishop because most of their skills scale off INT or MATK.
  • DEX: DEX gives Hit and and minor ASPD. Each 1 point in DEX gives 1 hit. For arch bishops, DEX is the most important stat in determining your variable cast time. To reduce variable cast time to 0, you need DEX*2 + INT = 530.
  • LUK: LUK gives CRIT, perfect dodge, minor ATK/MATK/HIT/FLEE and minor resistance to many status. Details can be found here.

Leveling Guide

I recommend leveling the "old-fashioned" way if this is your first time playing the priest branch in general, because it would help you learn about many of your skills against different enemies. To level old-fashioned way:

  • Level 1-6: Complete the Academy quest and change to an acolyte.
  • Level 6-30: Grab Heal with some INT and heal bomb Zombies and Skeletons in Payon Cave F1 (pay_dun00). To attack mobs with Heal, press SHIFT while targetting them.
  • Level 30-55: Heal bomb Archer Skeleton and Soldier Skeleton in Payon Cave F2. Learn to pneuma yourself against ranged attack such as those from Archer Skeletons.
  • Level 56-70: Grab Mummy hunting mission from Eden Group board quest (@go eden) and heal bomb mummy in Pyramids F3 (moc_pryd03). Job change to priest when you reach job level 50.
  • Level 71-85: Talk to Instructor Ur in Eden (@go eden) and get the Glast Heim quest. Grab Wraith and Evil Druid quests from Eden Group board quest as well. Warp to Glast Heim St Abby (gl_church) and heal bomb Wraith and Evil Druid. If you find yourself dying a lot, use Kyrie Eleison to negate some damage and add some VIT to increase your HP. Blessing cures curse status and Cure cures blind status.
  • Turn in all your quest should put you at level 85.

If you just want to skip ahead with leveling, complete Cautious Village quest followed by Wraith/Evil Druid quest until you are level 85.

  • Level 85-99: Gramps quest with a party. Do not turn in your Gramps quests! You should be able to reach 99/50 from killing the monsters. Once you complete both Gramps quests and are at 99/50, complete the Siroma and Stapo quests from 91-99 quest board but do not turn them in.
  • Rebirth + Level 1-6: Poke 2 porings from prt_fild08 to hit job level 10, and change into a high acolyte.
  • Turn in Siroma quest to get to job level 50. Change into a high priest.
  • Turn in both Gramps quest and Stapo quest to become a 99/70 high priest. It's very important that you turn in both Gramps quests first or you will not hit job 70! Change into Arch Bishop T when you are 99/70.
  • Level 99-175: Complete Gramps quest with a party.

Skill List

Acolyte Skills

Blessing Your bread-and-butter support skills. Blessing gives +1 STR/INT/DEX per level, for a total of 10 stats at max level. It also gives +2 HIT per level for a max of +20 HIT. This skill can be casted on monsters using shift. When casted on Demon race or Undead property monsters, this skill reduces their DEX and INT by half. Casting this skill on a cursed target removes cursed effect, but does not give them status buffs. Casting this skill on a second-stage stone-cursed target removes the stone curse effect.
Increase Agility Increases your movement speed by 25% and gives (skill level + 2) stats in AGI. Also gives 1% ASPD per level. This skill can be casted on monsters using shift.
Divine Protection Increases DEF against Demon race and Undead property monsters. Passive skill.
Demon Bane Increases ATK against Demon race and Undead property monsters. Passive skill.
Ruwach Reveals hidden enemies and deals Holy damage upon revealing hidden enemies.
Pneuma Creates a 3x3 area that blocks ranged attack, which lasts for 10 seconds. This skill cannot be casted on top of Land Protector, Maelstrom, Safety Wall, or another Pneuma.
Teleportation Level 1 teleports you to a random spot on your current map. Level 2 teleports you back to your save point. This skill cannot be used on War of Emperium maps or inside most instances.
Warp Portal Casts a portal on the ground that can warp other players to a previously saved spot. To save a spot, use /memo while standing on the cell you want to save. In some maps, /memo is disabled.
Heal Your basic single-target healing skill. Heal amount depends in your total int, base level and % heal modifiers. Heal can be used as an offensive holy property magic attack against undead property monsters. Damage dealt equals to half of heal amount. To cast it on a monster, use shift.
Decrease Agility Decreases enemy movement speed and decrease AGI by (skill level + 2). Has a chance to fail based on caster's INT and target's MDEF.
Aqua Benedicta Creates a Holy Water from an Empty Bottle when you stand on a cell that contains water, but is not submerged in water. Most builds contain this skill as a pre-requisite for Aspersio.
Signum Crucis Decreases hard DEF of Demon race and Undead property monsters. Works against Boss monsters.
Angelus Increases soft DEF and max HP of party members.
Cure Single-target skill which removes silence, chaos, and blind status effects. Can be used both within and outside of the party.
Holy Light Platinum skill. Single-target skill that deals Holy property magic. When you are under soul-link effect, this skill deals 500% more damage. Can be used on enemies to instantly remove Kyrie Eleison effect.

Stat Build/Equipment Guide

The most common builds for an arch bishop are Heal, Support and Offense. Heal build focuses on maximizing your healing potential while buffing your party members. Support build focuses on buffing party members, debuffing and tanking monsters or enemy members. Offense build here strictly refers to arch bishops that focus on dealing damage. Since the focus of this guide is on PvP AB, I will only go into details on the most relevant build in PvP, which is PvP support AB.

Heal Build

This build offers high amount of single-cast heal in exchange for tankiness and survivability. Ideal for back-line heal and support, or just for when you do not have enough gears to tank but still want to be helpful in a party. Healer builds are almost exclusively PvM-oriented. In PvP/BG/WoE situations, each member will be expected to bring their own healing supplies.

Stats

  • STR: 1
  • AGI: rest here
  • VIT: ~90
  • INT: 120-130
  • DEX: 120
  • LUK: 77

77 base LUK is required for Arch Angeling Card to make up for the SP you will be using while using either Highness Heal or Coluceo Heal. If you are not using Arch Angeling Card, you may lower your base LUK for AGI or some STR for weight capacity.

Notable Skills

Heal Basic single-target healing skill. Heal amount depends in your total int, base level and % heal modifiers. Heal can be used as an offensive holy property magic attack against undead property monsters. Damage dealt equals to half of heal amount.
Sanctuary Basic AoE healing skill. Heal amount depends on skill level and % heal modifiers. AoE duration depends on skill level. This skill can be used as an offensive ground-target holy' property magic attack against undead property and demon race monsters. Damage dealt equals to half of heal amount. Monsters which take damage within the AoE will be knocked back.
Meditatio Passive skill that increases your max SP, SP regeneration and % heal rate
Coluceo Heal AoE party-wide heal. AoE range depends on skill level, heal amount is the same as your Heal skill.
Highness Heal Third class single target heal. Heal amount depends on your total int, base level, highest level of Heal learned and skill level. Similar to Heal, this skill can be used as an offensive holy property magic attack against undead property monsters. Highness Heal has a fixed skill cooldown of 3 seconds, which can be reduced by Cure lv 1 enchant on  Light of Cure [0]
Offertorium Active skill that increases the heal rate of all 4 heal skills (Heal, Sanctuary, Coluseo Heal and Highness Heal) at the cost of increased SP cost. Casting Magnificant, either by the caster or by a party member, cancels Offertorium.
