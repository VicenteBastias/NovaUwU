Sin Weapons

To start enchanting those items, you must first complete the Gaining access to Varmundt's Mansion quest.

All the NPCs relating to Sin Weapons can be found in the restaurant building of Varmundt's Mansion, at (ba_in01 79, 379)Copied!.

The way of obtaining Sin Weapons was customized in NovaRO. All mobs inside the hard version of the Water Garden still drop the Sin Weapons. As a custom feature, field mobs in Varmundt's Mansion also drop weapons. You can use @whodrops <mob_id> to find out which mob drops the specific item.

Sin Weapons Enchanting

Sin Weapons can receive random enchants, through Improvement Devices. They can be obtained by exchanging 45  Varmeal Tickets or at the price of 1,500,000 zeny, with Yecheng (ba_in01 87, 380)Copied!.

You are able to choose between a Physical or Magical type of Improvement Device. Once you've chosen a category, you will get an enchant tier at random:

  • Normal Improvement Device
  • Advanced Improvement Device
  • Premium Improvement Device

Using an Improvement Device will open up an enchanting window that will list every Sin Weapon on your inventory. Confirming the enchant will consume the item and imbue the weapon with 1 to 3 random enchants from different lists, based on their enchanting slot.

You can re-enchant it as much as you want with another Improvement Device. The new enchants will completely replace the old ones, you can't keep any of previous ones.

Merging Enchants

In case you get too many Normal and Advanced Improvement Devices, they're still useful. You can merge them for their better versions:

  • 3 Normal Improvement Device and 100,000 zeny for 1 Advanced Improvement Device
  • 3 Advanced Improvement Device and 300,000 zeny for 1 Premium Improvement Device
