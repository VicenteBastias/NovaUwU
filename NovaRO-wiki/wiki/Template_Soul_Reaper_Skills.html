Kaute can only target yourself, family members, other Soul Linker type classes, or party members under the effect of Soul Unity. Unlike the Soul Linker Ka- skills, Spirit of the Soul Linker does not allow you to target ineligible targets.

Soul Unity Support
  • Max Level: 7
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 44:46:48:50:52:54:56
  • Fixed Cast Time: 4.0s
  • Cool Down: 0s ~ 180s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 Consume 10 Soul Energy.

Bind together the souls of all party members in an area around you. Allies under Soul Unity will restore HP every 3 seconds, and it will be possible to cast Kaute on them.


  • Soul Unity remains so long as allies stay within a 23x23 area of the caster.
  • If the caster is silenced or cursed, Soul Unity status is cancelled.


  • [Lv 1] : Restore 150 HP every 3 seconds, 3x3 Area of effect, Duration 30 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : Restore 300 HP every 3 seconds, 3x3 Area of effect, Duration 60 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : Restore 450 HP every 3 seconds, 5x5 Area of effect, Duration 90 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : Restore 600 HP every 3 seconds, 5x5 Area of effect, Duration 120 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : Restore 750 HP every 3 seconds, 7x7 Area of effect, Duration 150 seconds.
  • [Lv 6] : Restore 900 HP every 3 seconds, 7x7 Area of effect, Duration 180 seconds.
  • [Lv 7] : Restore 1050 HP every 3 seconds, 9x9 Area of effect, Duration 210 seconds.
