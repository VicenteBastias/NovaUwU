Climactic Fury Passive/Offensive
  • Max Level: 1
  • Active Target: Enemy
  • Max Stacks: 6 stacks
  • Stack Duration: 10 seconds

Skill Form - Passive:

When hit by a Great Monster, you will gain a stack of Fury.
Fury is a cumulatively stacking effect which grants auto-attacks reduction per stack.

  • 1 stack - 3% reduction
  • 2 stacks - 7% reduction
  • 3 stacks - 11% reduction
  • 4 stacks - 16% reduction
  • 5 stacks - 21% reduction
  • 6 stacks - 26% reduction

Fury stacks can only be gained every 3 seconds and when the duration ends, the stacks will decrease one by one.

Skill Form - Offensive:

  • Unleash your Fury, expending all stacks to inflict catastrophic damage to the target, dealing further bonus damage if all 6 stacks were unleashed.

Upon attacking you are left Emotionally Exhausted, unable to generate Fury and unable to re-cast Climactic Fury for 15 seconds.

The severity of the attack is determined by Weapon level, Weapon refine amount current HP, Hard DEF, and Fury stacks.

Retaliate Active
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Enemy
  • Activate Duration: 3 seconds
  • Skill Duration: 10 seconds
  • Skill Cooldown: 6 seconds
  • Exhausted Duration: 6 seconds

Activate-able within 3 seconds of receiving damage from a Monster, Deals damage to all Monster within 3 cells of you with greatly increased Hate and Rage generation,

Damage determined by Weapon level, Weapon refine amount current HP, Hard DEF, and Fury stacks same as Climactic Fury


Hitting an enemy with this ability grants the following statuses;

  • 5% increased damage dealt from all sources,
  • 10% increased damage taken from all sources,
  • Increased Hate generation from all sources, up to a maximum of 30% for 10 seconds.

Afterwards you are left Exhausted leaving you unable to generate Fury stacks and unable to re-cast Climactic Fury for 6 seconds upon taking your stand.

Release Bombring Offensive
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Ground
  • Cooldown: 10 seconds

As a Hunter bored with conventional battle techniques you decided to explore new methods of combat. After much exploration and meditation you developed a technique which allows you to not only produce, but also direct the unstable breed of poring, the Bombring.

  • Release a bombring in the chosen direction, which rolls forward and explodes upon reaching an enemy or traveling too far.

The farther a bombring travels, the faster it will explode and the sooner it reaches an enemy the slower it will explode.

Explosion damage is dramatically affected by the caster's DEX and INT, and AGI and LUK to a lesser degree.

Upon releasing a bombring you gain a Poring Counter, up to a maximum of 6.

Set Sleepring Offensive
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Ground

After much experimentation you have discovered a method to produce and control a new sub-species of bombring, the Sleepring, which thrives on essence left by Bombrings you have previously released.
Sleeprings have mutated explosions that release an anaesthetical gas, which causes enemies around them to immediately fall into a deep, un-interruptable sleep.

  • Consumes 4 Poring Counters to send a Sleepring to a targeted cell.

They explode after 8 seconds, releasing sleeping gas that will cause a monster halt all attacks and movement for 9 seconds.

Upon waking from their unconcious state, the monster affected will be in a state of grogginess.
For 15 seconds the monster has it's defenses weakened, increasing all damage taken by 5%.

This unique gas which the Sleepring releases to trance it's targets quickly losses it's effective duration if a monster is subjected by it too frequently. The only way you've noticed to be able to counter-act this diminishing effectiveness has been to subject the monster to the Dizzyring's disorienting properties.

Damage is based on the potency of Release Bombring's explosion damage.
Grants a Megaton Bomb Counter on use, which you can have up to a maximum of 6.

Set Dizzyring Offensive
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Ground

After much experimentation you have discovered a method to produce and control a new sub-species of bombring, the Dizzyring, which thrives on essence left by Bombrings you have previously released.

  • Consumes 4 Poring Counters to send a Dizzyring to a targeted cell.

They explode after 4 seconds, releasing an electrical zap that will cause a monster stop attacking and move erratically for 12 seconds.

This unique zap which the Dizzyring releases to confuse it's targets quickly losses it's effective duration if a monster is subjected by it too frequently. The only way you've noticed to be able to counter-act this diminishing effectiveness has been to subject the monster to the Sleepring's disorienting properties.

Damage is based on the potency of Release Bombring's explosion damage.
Grants a Megaton Bomb Counter on use, which you can have up to a maximum of 6.

Megaton Bombring Offensive
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self

Wanting to take a more direct approach to your combat style, you began to experiment by consuming the remnants of each poring you have manifested. Extraordinarily the bombring's invasive properties overwhelm your body, reconstructing it into the shape of the king of all porings, the Megaton Bombring.

  • Consume 4 Megaton Bomb Counters to transform into a Megaton Bombring.

Attacking an enemy triggers a countdown, which once completed, detonates yourself into a destructive explosion, severely damaging and stunning any nearby enemies, yet leaving you unharmed.

Damage is based on the potency of Release Bombring's explosion damage.
Movement speed is fixed, and skills are disabled while transformed.
