The Stylist.

Below is a list of Palettes for most of the playable classes in the game. You can change your character's colour palette for free by talking to the Stylist NPC in Prontera.

Note: For the 2nd and trans 2nd classes, the palettes are applicable for 3rd classes who choose to wear the 2nd and trans 2nd outfits. The original 2nd and trans 2nd classes' colour palettes differ slightly from the ones listed below.
