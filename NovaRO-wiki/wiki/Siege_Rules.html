Battleground Rules

  • Do not dual-client in Battlegrounds.
  • Do not AFK in Battlegrounds.
  • Do not grief in Battlegrounds, such as by losing matches on purpose.
  • Do not sell "carry services" for Battlegrounds.

War of Emperium Rules

All server Rules apply to the War of Emperium.

To create a more fun and engaging WoE environment, several rules are set in place. These can be changed by the administration as the situation demands for it.

  • Guilds are only allowed to use 24 characters in total in any way within WoE 1, and 18 within WoE 2.
    • There is a maximum amount of characters within a castle, and any attempts to use more than that amount are forbidden, including through alternating which characters are inside or outside the castle.
    • This does not affect using outside buffers as long as they never go inside the castle.
  • Guild emblem's design must be at least 80% visible to other players.
  • Do not steal another guild's name or guild emblem. Even parodies are not allowed.
  • Alliances between guilds are not allowed.
    • Guilds allied through the in-game system will be able to damage each other within the castles.
  • Do not prevent players from entering a castle during WoE. This includes Bloody Branches at the entrance, Warp Portals, Ice Walls, etc.
  • Your guild must have an unique emblem to participate in WoE.
  • Win-Trading and rigging the outcome of the War of Emperium is not allowed.
  • You need to actively play WoE to be eligible for rewards, if you're not you could lose them.
  • The use of items, skills, commands, clever use of game mechanics or any other workarounds, including file or GRF editing/modifications in order to achieve effects otherwise disabled in WoE or trying to emulate NoDelay-like behavior will result in a ban.
    • If you find an item or other behavior that allows for such, please make a Bug Report and do not abuse or share information on how to reproduce to anyone. Failure to comply will result in a ban.
    • If you are unsure about a behavior being considered an exploit, please ask a member of the Staff team before making use of it.

Breaking WoE Rules

First offense

  • Temporary hardware account ban for 2 weeks (2 WoEs).

Second offense

  • Temporary hardware account ban for 1 month (4 WoEs).

Third offense

This means the player know what he's doing and is doing so intentionally. This person does not care about punishment or the health of WoE on NovaRO at all.

  • Permanent account and hardware ban.

Cheating

  • Cheating is against the rules and will not be tolerated. Players caught cheating and/or using third party tools in NovaRO will have all of their related accounts and hardware permanently banned.
