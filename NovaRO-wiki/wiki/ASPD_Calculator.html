Attack Speed

Jump to navigation Jump to search

Attack Speed or ASPD is the number determining how fast a character attacks. This parameter mainly deals with a character's auto-attacks, but it also acts to determine the Animation Delay of certain skills.

The maximum possible ASPD value is 193 for characters over level 100, and 190 for characters below level 100.

The amount of hits your character makes is calculated as such: 50 / (200-FinalASPD) hits per second

Which means, a character with 193 ASPD will do 50/7 = 7.14 attacks per second.

Formula

NovaRO's ASPD formula is based on KRO, which is very different from iRO's formula. You can expect to find it somewhat more difficult to attain higher ASPD here.

A Character's ASPD is calculated as follows:

ASPD Formula
ASPD1 Base ASPD - Weapon/Shield Penalty + Stat Bonus + (Status Bonus* AGI/200)
ASPD2 (195 - ASPD1) * %ASPD + ASPD1
Final ASPD ASPD2 + Flat Bonus

Which takes these following parameters into account:

  • Base ASPD: A base number determined by the character's class.
  • Weapon/Shield Penalty: A penalty based on the character's class and the weapon/shield being equipped.
  • Stat Bonus: bonus based on a Character's AGI and DEX.
  • Status Bonus: bonuses from status effects bestowed by ASPD potions and certain skills.
  • %ASPD: Percentage bonuses from certain skills, items and equipments such as Two-Hand Quicken, Enriched Celermine Juice and  Cenere Card.
  • Flat Bonus: bonuses in the form of flat additions gained from certain equipment.

The Stat ASPD bonus is calculated differently depending on weapon type:

Bows, Instruments, Whips, and Firearms Other weapons
√(DEX²/7 + AGI²/2) / 4 √(DEX²/5 + AGI²/2) / 4

Examples

Example 1
A Rune Knight with a Two-Handed Sword, using Two-Hand Quicken and Berserk Potion:
  • Class: Rune Knight
  • Base ASPD: 156
  • Weapon/Shield Penalty:
    • Two-Handed Sword: -15
  • Status Bonuses:
    • Two-Hand Quicken: 7
    • ASPD Potion: 9
  • Stat Bonuses:
    • Total AGI: 150
    • Total DEX: 90

Calculation:

  • ASPD1 = 156 - 15 + (√(90²/5 + 150²/2) / 4) + ( (7 + 9) * 150/200 )
    • ASPD1 = 156 - 15 + 28 + 12
    • ASPD1 = 181
  • Final ASPD = 181
Example 2
A Ranger with a Bow, wearing the full White Wing set (ASPD +2), using an Awakening Potion:
  • Class: Ranger
  • Base ASPD: 156
  • Weapon/Shield Penalty:
    • Bow: -9
  • Status Bonuses:
    • ASPD Potion: 6
  • Stat Bonuses:
    • Total AGI: 175
    • Total DEX: 180
  • Flat Bonus: 2

Calculation:

  • ASPD1 = 156 - 9 + (√(180²/7 + 175²/2) / 4) + (6 * 175/200)
    • ASPD1 = 156 - 9 + 35 + 5
    • ASPD1 = 187
  • Final ASPD = 187 + 2
    • Final ASPD = 189
Example 3
A Guillotine Cross with double daggers, stacking various %ASPD and Flat bonuses, using Awakening Potion and Celermine Juice:
  • Class: Guillotine Cross
  • Base ASPD: 156
  • Weapon/Shield Penalty:
    • Dagger: -2
    • Dagger (left hand): -10
  • Status Bonuses:
    • ASPD Potion: 6
  • Stat Bonuses:
    • Total AGI: 170
    • Total DEX: 20
  • %ASPD: 52%
  • Flat Bonus: 4

Calculation:

  • ASPD1 = 156 - 19 + (√(20²/5 + 170²/2) / 4) + (6 * 170/200)
    • ASPD1 = 156 - 12 + 30 + 5
    • ASPD1 = 179
  • ASPD2 = (195 - 179) * 52% + 179
    • ASPD2 = 8 + 179
    • ASPD2 = 187
  • Final ASPD = 187 + 4
    • Final ASPD = 191
Example 4
A Rebel with a Gatling Gun stacking some %ASPD and Flat bonuses, using Berserk Potion, Celermine Juice, and Heat Barrel(ASPD +5):
  • Class: Rebel
  • Base ASPD: 146
  • Weapon/Shield Penalty:
    • Gatling Gun: +3
  • Status Bonuses:
    • Single Action: 5
    • ASPD Potion: 9
  • Stat Bonuses:
    • Total AGI: 120
    • Total DEX: 170
  • %ASPD: 28%
  • Flat Bonus: 6

Calculation:

  • ASPD1 = 146 + 3 + (√(170²/7 + 120²/2) / 4) + ( (5 + 9) * 120/200)
    • ASPD1 = 146 + 3 + 26 + 8
    • ASPD1 = 183
  • ASPD2 = (195 - 183) * 28% + 183
    • ASPD2 = 3 + 183
    • ASPD2 = 186
  • Final ASPD = 186 + 6
    • Final ASPD = 192

Status Bonus

As stated in the formula, these bonuses are further modified by your character's AGI. If your character has 0 AGI, these bonuses will not give any effect.

Skill Modifiers

  • Two-Hand Quicken = 7
  • One-Hand Quicken = 7
  • Spear Quicken = 7
  • Study = 2/level
  • Adrenaline Rush = 7
  • Single Action = 1 every 2 levels
  • Berserk = 15
  • Assassin Cross of Sunset = Depends on caster's stats
  • Fighting Spirit = 0.4/level

ASPD Potion Modifiers

  • Concentration Potion = 4
  • Guarana Candy = 4
  • Awakening Potion = 6
  • Berserk Potion = 9
  • Enriched Celermine Juice* = 10%

*Counted as %ASPD instead of Status Bonus.
