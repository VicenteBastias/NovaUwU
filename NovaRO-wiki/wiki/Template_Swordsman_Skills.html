Provoke Status Increase,
Status Decrease
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 3 + Skill Level
  • Range: 9
 Attempt to enrage an enemy to decrease their defense while also increasing their attack power for 30 seconds.


  • The difference between the caster's base level and the target's base level will affect the chance of success.
  • Players will have their Soft DEF reduced opposed to Hard DEF.
  • This skill can interrupt a skill being cast.
  • This will remove the target's Frozen, Stone, Sleep or Play Dead status.
  • Does not work on Boss or Undead race enemies.


  • [Lv 1] : 73% Chance to apply effect, +5% Damage, -10% DEF.
  • [Lv 2] : 76% Chance to apply effect, +8% Damage, -15% DEF.
  • [Lv 3] : 79% Chance to apply effect, +11% Damage, -20% DEF.
  • [Lv 4] : 82% Chance to apply effect, +14% Damage, -25% DEF.
  • [Lv 5] : 85% Chance to apply effect, +17% Damage, -30% DEF.
  • [Lv 6] : 88% Chance to apply effect, +20% Damage, -35% DEF.
  • [Lv 7] : 91% Chance to apply effect, +23% Damage, -40% DEF.
  • [Lv 8] : 94% Chance to apply effect, +26% Damage, -45% DEF.
  • [Lv 9] : 97% Chance to apply effect, +29% Damage, -50% DEF.
  • [Lv10] : 100% Chance to apply effect, +32% Damage, -55% DEF.

Magnum Break Melee Physical Attack
  • Max Level: 10
  • Property: Fire
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
  • Cool Down: 2s
  • Area of Effect:
    • (Lv 1~4) : 5x5
    • (Lv 5~8) : 7x7
    • (Lv 9~10) : 9x9
 Consume a small amount of HP to inflict damage and knock all enemies around you backwards 2 cells.


  • For 10 seconds after casting Magnum Break, your weapon will receive a 20% Fire property damage enhancement. (This effect gives you extra damage, and does not Endow your weapon to fire).
  • This skill has increased Hit rate.
  • If the targets hit are players outside the 3x3 area, they will take reduced damage from this skill.


  • [Lv 1] : Consumes 20 HP, +10% Hit, 120% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 110% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 2] : Consumes 20 HP, +20% Hit, 140% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 120% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 3] : Consumes 19 HP, +30% Hit, 160% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 130% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 4] : Consumes 19 HP, +40% Hit, 180% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 140% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 5] : Consumes 18 HP, +50% Hit, 200% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 150% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 6] : Consumes 18 HP, +60% Hit, 220% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 160% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 7] : Consumes 17 HP, +70% Hit, 240% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 170% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 8] : Consumes 17 HP, +80% Hit, 260% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 180% ATK instead.
  • [Lv 9] : Consumes 16 HP, +90% Hit, 280% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 190% ATK instead.
  • [Lv10] : Consumes 16 HP, +100% Hit, 300% ATK, Players outside the 3x3 area take 200% ATK instead.

SkillFormula% = [{Base_ATK%}]%

Endure Status Increase
  • Max Level: 10
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Cool Down: 10.0s
 Remove your flinching animation, enabling you to attack and move while receiving damage.

  • This skill has no effect in War of Emperium.

Moving HP-Recovery Passive
  • Platinum Skill
 Enable your natural HP recovery while moving.


  • Only 25% of the HP that is naturally recovered while standing is restored while moving.
  • The Increase HP Recovery skill does not affect Moving HP Recovery.
Fatal Blow Passive
  • Platinum Skill
 Enhance the Bash skill causing the Stun status on the target when you use level 6 Bash or higher.


  • The chance of inflicting Stun increases based on your Base Level and Bash skill level.


  • [Lv 1] : Bash level 6 = {1 x Base Level ÷ 10}%
  • [Lv 2] : Bash level 7 = {2 x Base Level ÷ 10}%
  • [Lv 3] : Bash level 8 = {3 x Base Level ÷ 10}%
  • [Lv 4] : Bash level 9 = {4 x Base Level ÷ 10}%
  • [Lv 5] : Bash level 10 = {5 x Base Level ÷ 10}%
Auto Berserk Status Increase,
Toggle
  • Platinum Skill
  • Target: Self
 Empowered by rage, you enter a condition that is equal to Level 10 Provoke status when your HP is reduced to less than 25%.


  • This effect has no duration, but can be dispelled and will end automatically if HP goes above 25%.
  • This skill costs 0 SP, but cannot be toggled without at least 1 SP.
