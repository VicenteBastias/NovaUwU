Training Chamber

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Training Chamber is a NovaRO exclusive feature where player can test their skills in various WoE-related tasks, as well as their damage against a specific kind size, race, or element of mob.

Reservation and Entry

The Training Chamber NPC can be found near Prontera Fountain (prontera 145/200). You must have a party to reserve the chamber.

The initial cost of renting the Training chamber is 500,000 Zeny. Time extensions cost 200,000 Zeny per 5 minutes.

Once the chamber has been created, all members of the party are able to enter and use the chamber's facilities.

Features

Barricade Master

The Barricade Master is located on the southern part of the chamber. Players can talk to him to summon barricades in one of the 5 possible formations to test their speed and damage in destroying them.

Summoning Barricades is free of charge and can be done an unlimited number of times.

Guardian Stone

The Guardian Stone is located on the western part of the chamber. Players can test their speed and damage in breaking the stone.

Guardian Stone testing is free of charge, and the Stone will resurrect itself after death indefinitely.

Barricade and Guardian Stone Repair

The Repair torches are located on the northern corners of the chamber. Players can test their speed and efficiency in repairing the barricades and guardian stones.

Every rented Training Chamber allows 5 barricade repair and 5 guardian stone repair sessions for free. Additional repair sessions cost 100,000 Zeny each.

Emperium Breaking

The Emperium summoner is located on the western part of the chamber. Players can test their speed in breaking the Emperium.

Every rented Training Chamber allows 1 Emperium breaking test for free. Additional sessions cost 250,000 Zeny each.

Monster Creator

The Monster Creator and his Training Dummy is located on the eastern part of the chamber. The NPC can fully customise the training dummy according to needs.

Properties that can be modified: Size, Race, Element, Element Level, Boss Protocol

Training Dummy resurrects itself after death indefinitely.

Every rented Training Chamber allows 10 property changes for free. Additional changes cost 50,000 Zeny each.

Utility NPCs

Health Manipulator

The Health Manipulator NPC is located on the southwest corner of the chamber. She can modify the player's current HP percentage for free, to test skills which are affected by current HP, such as Gates of Hell and Dragon Breath.
