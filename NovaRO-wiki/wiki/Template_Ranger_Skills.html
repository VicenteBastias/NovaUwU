Electric Shocker Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 35
  • Range: 3
  • Consumes: 1  Special Alloy Trap
 Set a trap on the ground that will immobilize and drain SP from the target and any enemies in a 5x5 area around the target when stepped on.


  • After a set duration the trap will dismantle itself.
  • You can have a maximum of 3 traps set at a time.


  • [Lv 1] : Drains 5% Max SP per second, Duration 20 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : Drains 10% Max SP per second, Duration 22 seconds.

  • [Lv 4] : Drains 20% Max SP per second, Duration 26 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : Drains 25% Max SP per second, Duration 28 seconds.

Camouflage Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 40
 Hide yourself near a wall or obstacle, you will still be detected by Demon race and Boss monsters.


Each second in Camouflage grants +10% Critical Rate, +30 ATK and reduces DEF by -5%, up to a maximum of +100% Critical Rate and +300 ATK.


  • After attacking or leaving the concealed state, you will lose the entire bonus. It is only applied for a single attack.


  • [Lv 1] : Consumes 6 SP per second, Unable to move.
  • [Lv 2] : Consumes 5 SP per second, Unable to move.
  • [Lv 3] : Consumes 4 SP per second, 50% movement speed, Can move away from wall or obstacle.
  • [Lv 4] : Consumes 3 SP per second, 75% movement speed, Can move away from wall or obstacle.
  • [Lv 5] : Consumes 2 SP per second, 100% movement speed, Can move away from wall or obstacle.
