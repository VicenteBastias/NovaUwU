All can drop  Heart Hunter Medal at 10% and  OS Weapon Box at 3%. Both of these drops doesn't affected by Bubble Gum.

UNDER CONSTRUCTION

OSAD Weapons

They can be reformed from OS Weapons from +7 and above with 200  Heart Hunter Medals, 200  Unknown Parts and 40  Cor Cores at Blaze Starfire in (sp_cor, 97, 106)Copied!. Do note that there's no refinement penalty when reforming OS weapons into OSAD weapons however, the enchants from OS base weapons would be removed. For example, if it's a +15 weapon, then the OSAD weapon will be +15 as well.

OSAD Weapon Enchants

OSAD weapons can be enchanted at Blaze Starfire in (sp_cor, 97, 106)Copied!. All of these enchants are perfect enchants which means you can pick which enchants you desired. Both slots can be upgraded till level 5. Resetting cost 500,000 zenies. All of these procedures are 100% safe rate and will not fail.

Enchanting 3rd slot cost: 150  Unknown Parts, 25  Heart Hunter Medals and 100,000 zenies.
Enchanting 2nd slot cost: 100  Unknown Parts, 75  Cor Cores, 25  Heart Hunter Medals, and 100,000 zenies.

Upgrades on both slots cost:

1st upgrade: 50  Heart Hunter Medals and 200,000 zenies.
2nd upgrade: 100  Heart Hunter Medals and 200,000 zenies.
3rd upgrade: 150  Heart Hunter Medals and 200,000 zenies.
4th upgrade: 200  Heart Hunter Medals and 200,000 zenies.
