Overview

To tame a monster, you must have the appropriate Taming Item, and use them on the monster. A slot machine will appear and you may succeed or fail in taming the monster based on random chance. It is unknown if other factors affect the success rate. Another method for taming monsters is by using one of the random spells from the Professor skill "Abracadabra". When you succeed, you'll receive a Pet Egg, which you can hatch using a Pet Incubator.

All pets have Intimacy levels (type @petinfo), which go up or down according to how you take care of them. These intimacy levels can be seen from the pet egg item description. Intimacy levels include:

  • Awkward (1 - 99)
  • Shy (100 - 249)
  • Neutral (250 - 749)
  • Cordial (750 - 909)
  • Loyal (910 - 1000)

Intimacy levels can be raised by feeding the pet when its Hunger level is on Neutral or lower. Overfeeding or starving a pet will lower intimacy levels. When the intimacy goes to 0, the pet will run away forever. Note that on NovaRo, your pet will not lose intimacy if you die while having the pet out.

Pets can also be renamed as many times as you want. A renamed pet will have a "beloved" prefix to their pet egg item when returned to their egg shell. Some pets also have special pet accessories to complement their appearance.

Automatic Feeding

Pets in NovaRO have access to the Automatic Feeding mechanic which will automatically feed them when they reach Neutral hunger as long as you have the required pet food in your inventory. Be aware that this Automatic Feeding mechanic can't be used together with the Auto Trade feature.

To activate the Automatic Feeding mechanic, check the drop menu in the pet menu and click the Automatic Feeding to turn it on.

Automatic Feeding works only when the hunger is 74 at nonloyal pets and 14 for loyal pets.

Pet Groomers

Heidi, the Pet Groomer

Heidi, also known as Pet Groomer, can be found in many locations in-game, one of her favorite places being the Basement Floor of the Main Office, but there is also some well-known spots, such as Prontera. (prontera 218/211)

Heidi will sell Pet Incubators, some pet accessories, Homunculus S foods and most pet foods, excluding Green Herbs, Juice-type Foods, Monster's Feed, White Potions and Shoots.

Pet Stylizing

Heidi not only provides regular Pet Groomer services, but can also Transform the appearance of one of your pets into another. This allows you to keep any pet bonuses without having to deal with their sometimes unbearable appearance (I'm looking at you, Incubus).

To do so, talk to her and select Stylize my pet!. The process costs 2000 Nova Points and consumes the pet egg used for the appearance. The new, stylized pet egg will also become Account Bound.

If you ever change your mind, Heidi can also remove the pet styling, free of charge. Doing so will remove the trade restrictions. You can also choose to pay a  Reversal Voucher to receive both eggs back instead.
