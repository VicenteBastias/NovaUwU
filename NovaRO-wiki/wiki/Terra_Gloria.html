Terra Gloria

Search for Terra Gloria

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (prt_cas_q 29, 28)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Ritual of Blessing
Rewards
Experience: 11,250,000 (Base) and 26,250,000 (Job)
Items: 30  Schwartz's Honor Token
  1. Go in the Prontera Castle and head to your room (prt_cas_q 29, 28)Copied!. Nyhill has been waiting for you and he asks you to wait for Spica.
  2. Once she arrives, speak with Spica. You learn that the chamberlain who gave the drink to Nyhill was from the Schwarzwald Republic.
  3. Speak with Nyhill again, he'll set up a meeting with Crux.
  4. Go see Crux in the Lighthalzen Airport (lhz_airport 189, 39)Copied!. He suspects that the culprit's name is most likely Bruno.
  5. Search the north side of Lightalzen, near (lighthalzen 186, 293)Copied!.
  6. Investigate the slums and speak with Bruno again (lighthalzen 309, 263)Copied!. Crux apprehends Bruno and will interrogate him later.
  7. Head back to your room in the Prontera Castle (prt_cas_q 29, 28)Copied! and talk to Nyhill.
  8. Go to Yuno and enter the Yuno Airport. Crux will be waiting for you there. (y_airport 168, 41)Copied!.
  9. Go to the Lighthalzen Hotel (lhz_in02 238, 150)Copied! and speak with Philofontes (at 2Â° floor, bottom room). You'll have to join the Rebellion Empire and defeat Luke.
  10. Go to Einbroch and head to the Train Station (einbroch 239, 269)Copied!.
  11. Talk to the Unsual Person (Lazy) right next to you, it's the agent of the Secret Wings.
  12. Lazy doesn't want to pay the 200z train fee, so follow him near the sewer instead (einbroch 270, 268)Copied!.
  13. Click the Very Small Hole to be transported to Einbech.
  14. Speak with Lazy in Einbech (einbech 46, 208)Copied!. Follow his traces.
  15. Lazy will be waiting for you near (einbech 76, 131)Copied!, talk to him. You find a body and decide to carry him along to the Rebellion camp.
  16. Speak with Lazy again (einbech 216, 82)Copied! at the entrance of Clana Nemieri. Enter the portal afterwards.
  17. Speak with Lazy inside Clana Nemieri (rebel_in 21, 45)Copied! and a cutscene will trigger.
  18. Walk accross the corridor and speak with Lazy again (rebel_in 85, 61)Copied!. You'll trigger another cutscene and the negotations will begin.
  19. You'll have to start by speaking with Elena Bolkova (rebel_in 69, 78)Copied!. She will challenge you and you must defeat her.
  20. Speak with the Rebellion Leader on the second floor (rebel_in 122, 71)Copied!. He won't accept to cooperate with you, so you'll have to try to convince Dien.
  21. Enter the portal at (rebel_in 123, 41)Copied! and a cutscene will trigger. You'll be warped into Dien's room.
  22. Approach Dien to trigger the next cutscene. They will need some time to think about your proposal.
  23. Speak with Dien again.
  24. Speak with Commander Erst (rebel_in 209, 84)Copied!, she is at the top middle room. She agrees with Dien.
  25. Go back to the Rebellion Leader and speak with him.
  26. He wants Larcis to read the letter, so speak with Larcis right next to the Rebellion Leader.
  27. Speak with the Rebellion Leader again.
  28. Speak with Dien who just showed up.
  29. Speak with the Rebellion Leader and you will get 10  Schwartz's Honor Token.
    You have now unlocked the Dien's Party quest.
  30. Speak with Lazy inside Clana Nemieri (rebel_in 86, 61)Copied! and inform him about your success for the elders' cooperation. You will get another 10  Schwartz's Honor Token along with some experience.
    You have now unlocked the Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base quest.
    You have now unlocked the Rachel's Revolver quest.
    You have now unlocked the Lebedev Twins quest.
    You have now unlocked the Teleportation Prohibition quest.
    You have now unlocked the Leaflets Delivery daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Luke's Requests daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Finding Charity daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Flower Girl daily quest.
    You have now unlocked the Elena's Challenge daily quest.

    You have now unlocked the Weapon Grudges daily quest.


Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 86, 61)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: None
Items: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token
  1. Speak with the Rebellion Soldier (rebel_in 65, 46)Copied!, Tess comes up and wants you to come to the Einbroch Hotel.
  2. To go to the hotel, use @go einbroch and then simply walk to the right. The meeting is at (ein_in01 274, 243)Copied!. Speak with Nyhill inside the hotel room.
  3. The operation base camp is North of Einbroch, at the northeast corner of the map (ein_fild04 281, 333)Copied!. Speak with Julian and he tell him you're ready. You'll need to make a party for the Heart Hunter War Base 1 instance.
  4. Once you're ready, speak with Striker Unit Commander to get warped inside the Heart Hunter base.

Heart Hunter War Base 1

  1. Walk near Julian on your right to trigger a cutscene.
  2. Defeat the first wave of Heart Hunters on your right. Once you're done, Julian will reappear near the barricades (if you walk too close to the barricade you'll lose HP, you can't die, but be careful!).
  3. Walk near Julian to trigger the next cutscene. You'll have to find explosives.
  4. The explosives are north of the entrance (coordinate 58 88). Pick up the gunpowder that drops.
  5. You'll have to defeat another wave of Heart Hunters. Speak with Julian and give him the gunpowder to blow up the first barricade.
  6. Keep walking on the right and you'll find Julian again. Walk near him to trigger another cutscene.
  7. Walk north of the area and you'll find a Suspicious Instrument (coordinate 147 119). Push the button and defeat the new wave of Heart Hunters.
  8. Walk near Julian again and talk to him to trigger a cutscene. Go through the wall.
  9. Keep walking to the right until you find Julian again, walk near him to trigger a cutscene.
  10. Go back near the previous wall and defeat the intruders. A new Pile of Gunpowder will appear (coordinate 166 59), click on it, and pick up the items.
  11. Go back to Julian (on the right side) and give him the gunpowder.
  12. Walk to the right once more and go near Julian. A cutscene will trigger.
  13. Heart Hunter Ebel will appear, you do not have to kill her just run away until she dies. Wait for the animation to finish and then watch the cutscene.
  14. Speak with Julian to get out of the instance and you will receive 10  Schwartz's Honor Token.
    You have unlocked the Heart Hunter War Base daily instance.

Continue with Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

  1. Speak with Luke at the operation base camp to get warped inside the Heart Hunter base.
    You have now unlocked the Chimera Research daily quest.
  2. Go in the portal right up to you, to get inside the Werner Laboratory. Then walk towards Luke near (slabw01 236, 91)Copied! and speak with him.
  3. Talk to him again to create the Werner Laboratory instance. Talk to the portal that appeared to go inside.

Werner Laboratory

  1. Speak with Eisen Werner at the entrance.
  2. Move forward and speak with him again at the end of the corridor.
  3. You'll have to active the Security Devices on each side of the room. If you pick the wrong answer, you'll have to start over with both devices.
    Trick: just spam the first option, red is more likely to be the answer.
  4. Enter the portal to go to the next area.
  5. Move up and speak with Eisen again. You'll have to active the two security devices on the sides once more.
  6. Enter the portal to go to the next area.
  7. Speak with Eisen again and you'll be teleported inside the core of the lab.
  8. Speak with Eisen again and watch the cutscene. On your first time, you will be teleported outside of the instance. You will also get 1  Small Leather Bag with 1  Blessing Star.
    You have now unlocked the Returning the Relics quest.

Continue with Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base

  1. With the Blessing Star in hand, go back to the Hotel Room in Einbroch (ein_in01 274, 243)Copied!.
  2. Speak with Nyhill and listen to the story. You'll be teleported to Prontera. Go back to the Prontera Castle inside your room.
  3. Speak with Skia Nerius. You will be rewarded with 10  Honor Tokens.
    You have now unlocked the Pet Child quest.
    You have now unlocked the YSF01 quest.
  4. Speak with Philofontes at the Heart Hunter base (que_swat 145, 57)Copied!. You will be rewarded with 10  Schwartz's Honor Token.
    You have now completed the Terra Gloria main quest!


Lebedev Twins

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 69, 78)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token
Unlocks the Entertain the Lebedev twins daily quest.
  1. Speak with Elena Bolkova (rebel_in 69, 78)Copied! and select I am not busy today, so I stopped by and select that you can take a secret (first option). Then select I will help and accept to help her with the twins.
  2. Speak with Anya Levedeva (rebel_in 110, 54)Copied! and she doesn't want to speak with you anymore. You'll have to gather information from other members:
    • Speak with Milo River near (rebel_in 108, 27)Copied!.
    • Speak with Mason Bender near (rebel_in 19, 48)Copied!.
  3. Return to Anya and tell her what you've learned. When prompt a choice, choose to ask directly. She will get more upset and you'll have to ask Elena for help.
  4. Speak with Elena Bolkova and tell her about the Lebedev twins. She suggests to speak with Ericsson Adler (rebel_in 91, 67)Copied! instead.
  5. Speak with Ericsson Adler and he will give you  Delicious Handmade Cookie to eat with the twins.
  6. Go back to Anya and ask her if she Want some cookie? (second option).
  7. Report what they've told you back to Elena Bolkova. She now wants you to bring them to her.
  8. Go back to Anya and tell her Elena wants to see them.
  9. Go back to Elena and tell her I have brought the Lebedev twins. You'll get a longer cutscene along with 10  Schwartz's Honor Token and some experience.
    You have now unlocked the Entertain the Lebedev twins daily quest.


Teleportation Prohibition

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 82, 68)Copied!
Items: 4  Flame Hearts
4  Mystic Frozens
3  Rough Winds
5  Great Natures
1  Star Crumb
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 3,250,000 (Base) and 3,250,000 (Job)
Items: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token
  1. Speak with Arinoa Yurik (rebel_in 82, 68)Copied! and her brother will show up next to her.
  2. Speak with Arivero Yurik, he will need some to prepare.
    Come back tomorrow after 4 am, server time.
  3. Speak with Ariona and then Arivero. He will get a headache from thinking too hard and needs to rest.
  4. Speak with Arivero again. He will ask you to solve a 'mystery'. You'll have to select (one after the other):
    • A strong wind.
    • The turned-off torch.
  5. You will need 1  Flame Heart to break the Teleportation Prohibition magic device. Once you have the item, go to Einbech near the train at (einbech 211, 212)Copied!, Arivero will appear and you'll have to speak with him.
  6. Arivero now needs 3  Mystic Frozens for the Teleportation Prohibition magic device. Once you have the item, go back to him near the train in Einbech.
  7. Go back to Arinoa and then speak with Arivero inside Clana Nemieri. When asked, select Rough Wind.
  8. This time, Arivero needs 3  Rough Winds and 1  Mystic Frozen. Once you have the items, go to Lighthalzen and go in the garden near (lighthalzen 134, 233)Copied!.
  9. Go back to Arinoa and then speak with Arivero inside Clana Nemieri.
  10. You will need 1  Star Crumb. Once you have the item, speak with Arinoa and then Arivero again. Select Ask him option.
  11. You will need 4  Great Natures. Once you have the items, go to Yuno and walk near these coordinates (yuno 206, 224)Copied!.
  12. You will need 1  Great Nature and 3  Flame Hearts. Once you have the items, go to Einbroch near the Wind Power Generator at (einbroch 262, 42)Copied!.
  13. Go back to Arinoa and then speak with Rebellion Leader who appeared next to her. You will receive 10  Schwartz's Honor Token along with some experience.


Rachel's Revolver

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 90, 51)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: None
Items: 7  Schwartz's Honor Token
Unlocks Rachel's Revolver Repairs daily quest.
  1. Speak with Rachel Smith (rebel_in 90, 51)Copied! and she'll ask you to consult Einbroch's Blacksmith for her revolver.
  2. Go meet Blacksmith Jack in Einbroch (ein_in01 38, 21)Copied! and show him the weapon.
  3. Go back to Rachel and tell her she needs better materials. You will get 7  Schwartz's Honor Token.
    You have now unlocked the Rachel's Revolver Repairs daily quest.



Dien's Party

Requirements

Starting Point: (rebel_in 123, 41)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: None
Items: Unlocks Elders Party Preparations daily quest.
  1. Go back to Dien's room (rebel_in 123, 41)Copied! and a cutscene will trigger. You'll overhear a conversation.

Returning the Relics

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: none
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Infiltrate Heart Hunter Base
Rewards
Experience: 15,000,000 (Base) and 15,000,000 (Job) total
  1. Upon completing the Werner Laboratory for the firt time, you will get a  Small Leather Bag. Open it to get the relics. You will get experience as a reward per item delivered.
  2. Speak with Blacksmith Cleaver at (yuno_in01 163, 31)Copied! and give him the  Decorated Archer's Thimble.
  3. Speak with Knight Windsor at (prt_in 177, 26)Copied! and give him both the  Old Rings and the  Locket Pendant.
    Don't forget to speak with him twice!
  4. Speak with Sister Margaret at (prt_monk 93, 254)Copied! and give her the  Wood Rosary Note: dont use the navi-link inside a building, it might misdirect you.
  5. Speak with Hunter Sherin at (payon_in03 194, 39)Copied! and give her the  Portable Sewingbox.
  6. Speak with Guile Marovitz in Morroc at (que_job01 89, 91)Copied! and give her the  Assassin's Mark Dagger.
