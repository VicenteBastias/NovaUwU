Auto Counter Reflection
  • Max Level: 5
  • Property: Based on Weapon
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 3
  • Required weapon type:
    • Any except Bows
 Enter a defensive stance for a short time, and after receiving a melee attack from an enemy, negate the damage and retaliate back with a mighty critical attack.


  • The stance ends after countering one attack.
  • This skill can be combined with Crush Strike.


  • [Lv 1] : Stance lasts for 0.4 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : Stance lasts for 0.8 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : Stance lasts for 1.2 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : Stance lasts for 1.6 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : Stance lasts for 2.0 seconds.

 Allows riding of a Pecopeco mount. While mounted on a Pecopeco you will have +1000 weight limit.

Pierce Melee Physical Attack
  • Max Level: 10
  • Property: Based on Weapon
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 7
  • Range: 2
  • Required weapon types:
    • One-handed Spear
    • Two-handed Spear
 Attack an enemy using a thrusting stab, larger enemies will take additional hits. Increased Hit rate while using Pierce.


This skill hits multiple times depending on the targets size;

  • Small enemies are hit 1 time.
  • Medium enemies are hit 2 times.
  • Large enemies are hit 3 times.


  • [Lv 1] : 110% ATK, +5% Hit.
  • [Lv 2] : 120% ATK, +10% Hit.
  • [Lv 3] : 130% ATK, +15% Hit.
  • [Lv 4] : 140% ATK, +20% Hit.
  • [Lv 5] : 150% ATK, +25% Hit.
  • [Lv 6] : 160% ATK, +30% Hit.
  • [Lv 7] : 170% ATK, +35% Hit.
  • [Lv 8] : 180% ATK, +40% Hit.
  • [Lv 9] : 190% ATK, +45% Hit.
  • [Lv10] : 200% ATK, +50% Hit.
