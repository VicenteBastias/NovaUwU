Overview

The Library is one of the first field maps you get access to while doing the main quest of Episode 17.2 - Sage's Legacy.

Access to the dungeon is made through the portal at (ba_mansion 197, 104)Copied! from Varmundt's Mansion Map after doing the quests needed to get access to it.
