Entering the Tournament

  1. Use the Warper and choose Heroes' Trails > Geffen Magic Tournament. Talk to Emmitt White to create the instance. Use the Dimensional Device.
  2. Talk to the Receptionist. @navi [email protected] 122 58 Choose How do I register?
  3. Kill the Desert Wolf under 60 seconds and talk to the Receptionist again.
  4. Enter the portal below the fountain. @navi [email protected] 120 34

Caution!

  • You have 2 hours to complete this instance.
  • If you die during a duel, you cannot re-enter it. You should bring a few  Tokens of Siegfried with you.

Information

  1. Each round has a time limit of 3 minutes except for Fenrir whose time frame is 5 minutes.
  2. You will be rewarded with Geffen Magic Contest Coins for winning each round.
  3. Talk with the Healer after each round to fully restore your HP/SP.
  4. Every opponent is a Demi-Human. They are usually Neutral but prepare for Earth and Ghost too.
  5. The skills below cannot be used during the duels:
    • Abracadabra
    • Dimension Door
    • Expulsion
    • Ice Wall
    • Ignorance
    • Intimidate
    • Spider Web
    • Teleport
    • Thorn Trap
    • Vacuum Extreme

Enchants

Using 65  Geffen Magic Tournament Coins, you can enchant the Armors and Accessories you can buy from this instance at the NPC outside the instance at the Geffen Enchanter @navi dali 93 146.
Using another 65 coins, you can reroll both of the enchants. There is no chance of breaking or damaging your armor, accessory, or the cards in them.
