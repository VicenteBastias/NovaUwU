The Guillotine Cross (GX) is the 3rd job class of Assassins. Guillotine Crosses are powerful physical damage dealers, capable of dealing heavy melee damage to a single target with skills such as Cross Impact or destroying mobs of enemies using Rolling Cutter. They also have access to special new poisons, which perform a variety of unique buffs and debuffs.

Builds

Rolling Cutter

Beginner/levelling AoE build that also works in the mid/end-game as a pack killer.

Since it's an AoE skill that has an animation based on delay and APSD to be spammed, you want gears that help with delay and ASPD so that you can spin more to do more hits per second.

To achieve the max number of spins with the skill you will want between 187 to 190 aspd and 35 to 40 less delay from gears/skills. There isn't a sure value because it depends on the ping of the player so players are advised to test in the Training Chamber before fully spending into it.

It's also nice to mix it with items that help you to recover HP and SP such as  Rideword Hat [1] or  Thanatos Katar [1] because you do a lot of hits per second to many mobs at the same time.

Rolling Cutter Build

STR : 100-130
AGI : 1-120
VIT : 100 TOTAL at least
INT : 1-30
DEX : 80-120
LUK : 1-100 TOTAL

STR is you main source of damage.

AGI will depend A LOT on the gears of the player. It's possible to achieve high values of ASPD with 1 base AGI nowadays no matter what some players think although it's not advisable as it also helps a bit with flee and to resist some status. The main reason to increase AGI is to reach higher values of ASPD and make use of the delay gears you've equipped since both are connected and you can't fully benefit from one without the other.

INT doesn't matter much unless you've trouble with SP.

DEX is mostly for the HIT values since your damage per second means zero if you don't hit but it also increase ASPD on a minor level.

LUK depends on the needs. It increases HIT and ATK but 100 total also makes you immune to chaos and curse.

Cross Ripper Slash

Cross Ripper Slasher is ranged physical damage build that depends on HIT and Rolling Cutter's spin counters to do damage.

It's a cheaper version of the Soul Destroyer build below and a complement to a Rolling Cutter.

Be aware that it still depends a lot on owning a  Old Bone Circlet [1] with a high refine to achieve the high numbers so while it's a bit cheaper, it's not exactly that cheap.

Since it's a cheaper version of Soul Destroyer, don't expect it to be viable at 100% in the same places as it since to use this skill you need to have at least one Rolling Cutter spin counter and that takes a bit of time to setup. Due to that, sometimes you can't setup the counter fast enough before getting hit by a monster making it impossible to use the skill and feel inferior. Don't be scared about it, you can still hit very high numbers with it.

Soul Destroyer

Soul Destroyer, also known as Soul Breaker, is one of the most expensive builds of Guillotine Cross. With it you can deal ranged damage that can also crit although is very high risk, high reward due to the limited range of 4 cells and 0.25s of cooldown per use.

It's the expensive version of Cross Ripper Slasher since it needs 75% of delay reduced, 200+ CRIT to deal critical damage, and also 188 ASPD plus a decent amount of DEX and INT to cast it fast. Because that's not enough as a cost, it also has 0.25s of fixed cast so you've to find a way to remove it or the skill will be a bit slow to be used and even interrupted all the time.

@spam also doesn't work with it so players are advised to check Training Chamber to find if investing into more ASPD or into gear that reduces the delay actually does something for them since ping and fps heavily affects the number of skill per second that one can deal with the skill. To some, even having instant cast with the skill will help spamming more. Due to the reason mentioned above, it's very normal to find players that can't do more than 3 hits per second.

If you've trouble to reach high values of CRIT, you can give a look here for a list of gear that can help to solve that.

Soul Destroyer Build

STR : 100-130
AGI : 1-120
VIT : 100 TOTAL at least
INT : 1-100
DEX : 80-120
LUK : 90-130 TOTAL

STR is you main source of damage. INT also increases the damage of Soul Destroyer but increases a lot less compared to STR.

AGI will depend A LOT on the gears of the player. It's possible to achieve high values of ASPD with 1 base AGI nowadays no matter what some players think although it's not advisable as it also helps a bit with flee and to resist some status. The main reason to increase AGI is to reach higher values of ASPD and make use of the delay gears you've equipped since both are connected and you can't fully benefit from one without the other.

INT doesn't matter much unless you've trouble with SP or need an extra boost of dmg to one-shot something since it also boosts the damage of Soul Destroyer. It's mostly a complement or a place where you want to use your leftover points.

DEX is mostly used to remove the fixed cast with  Temporal Boots Of Dexterity [1] (asks to have 120 base DEX) but it also increases ASPD on a minor level. If you use anything else to remove the fixed cast time, you simply care about it for the cast time.

LUK is used mostly because of the CRIT since the skill asks for 200+ to do critical damage and any CRIT helps to reach that. Depending on the needs, more or less can be adjusted.

Cross Impact

Cross Impact is, in terms of numbers, the strongest build of a Guillotine Cross.

It makes you jump to a target to deal single target damage so, depending on the situation, sometimes using it is the same as asking to die.

Since it also has some cd and cast delay, it's a high risk high reward burst damage skill that is often used with Back Slide as a way to evade death.

At the same time it's also very newbie friendly since, thanks to katars innate bonus to double your CRIT, makes it very accessible to a new player reach the needed 200+ CRIT to deal critical damage with it early on and slowly progress to a double dagger build later to maximize its damage.

If you've trouble to reach high values of CRIT, you can give a look here for a list of gear that can help to solve that.

Also remember that if you want to see massive improvements with it, after the initial stage, you will need to have access to the following:

  •  Old Bone Circlet [1]
  •  [Bio 5] Eremes Guile Card

Since this is a single target build, you may want to mix it with Rolling Cutter or Meteor Assault, for example, to deal faster with pack of monsters.

Cross Impact Build

STR : 100-130
AGI : 1-100
VIT : 100 TOTAL at least
INT : 1-30
DEX : 1-100
LUK : 90-130 TOTAL

Since it has 0.35s of cooldown and 0.5s of cast delay, it forces you to be with -30% delay on gears/buffs to spam it at its max. Knowing that in advance...

STR is you main source of damage.

AGI doesn't matter much here outside of specific occasions. You still need some to make use of -delay gears at 100% but outside of that it won't make a huge deal to get more for more ASPD unless you want to make it register faster, for example. Some of those special occasions where high AGI helps it's mostly because it adds flee and helps to deal with ASPD locks or to dodge some big hits that otherwise could kill you.

INT doesn't matter much unless you've trouble with SP.

DEX doesn't matter much unless you need it for a hybrid build since it also increases HIT, cast times, and ASPD on a minor level.

LUK depends on the boots you use and your CRIT needs. Since you need 200+ CRIT, any CRIT helps and it's normal to go for 120 or more base LUK. At the same time it's possible to use  Illusion Leg A-type [1] with 90 base LUK to get 130+ CRIT, and, thanks to katars, get it doubled to 260+ CRIT, more than enough CRIT to deal critical damage in most content. Just remember that stuff like the bonus of  Temporal Boots Of Luck [1] asks for 120 base LUK.
