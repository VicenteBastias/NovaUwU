Overview

The way a Rune Knight differs from the Pre-Renewal Lord Knight is more than just one, this meaning that you can actually go for three different build paths, all viable and efficient at certain areas. You enjoyed going full on attack speed and bashing on monsters or other players?, then AGI RK is for you. Do you think a Rune Knight and his Dragon pal sound more like a magical duo? Then Dragon Breath is the way to go! Do you want to surprise players with a heavy bash on their head which will most likely just kill everyone? Then Crush Strike is for you! My name is Tachi and I'd like to welcome you to the exhausting but marvelous world of playing with your dragon.

Stats

First of all, the stats for every build are ridiculously different from each other and I'd like to start up with the more common, WoE and PvE Oriented one: The Dragon Breath or Magical Burping Fire or Ice Dragon. As the name of the skill suggests you need to have your Dragon with you at all times, which means there is a certain class that is your worst enemy: the Shadow Chasers. Hence, you will need to bulk up in some Masquerade resistances.

  • STR - More weight limit! Meaning that you can free more weight up to have a better chance to resist Masquerades and also higher attack.
  • AGI - Masquerade Resistance and Attack Speed for less animation delay between casts.
  • VIT - This can vary depending on the gears you have available and if you'd like to stack on HP or resistances.
  • INT - Same as above. My advice is go as close to 120 as you can as you get a good increase in SP and therefore more damage (explained later!)
  • DEX - For Temporal Boots of Dexterity Fixed Casting decrease and percentage damage, also for some hit.
  • LUK - If you plan on going critical, you need at least 120 to combo with your Luk Temporal Boots.

Skills

There are some variables depending on what will be your final build, as which skills to level up and which cards to put on certain situations. The truth is the LK Skills don't have much of a heavy impact in a WoE/BG/PvP Setting as a DB RK, as much as what you do with your RK Skills, for example: Dragon Breath is a must have for any and all WoE/BG RK, even for PvE. You will find in the following section a list of the skills that are a must have and those that will make your PvE life easier.

First Job Skills

  Sword Mastery Just level 1 to unlock Two-Handed Sword Mastery.

  Two-Handed Sword Mastery Level this up to 10 so you can get the LK skills Two-Handed Quicken and Parry.

  Magnum Break I usually always carry this skill around at level 10, just because it helps level-up in lower levels and it increases your Weapon Damage by a whooping 20%, fire property, which translates into DB (and by extension, most of your skills) into doing more damage.

  Provoke You can leave this at level 5 or you can get more points into it if you are really into interrupting people's castings and lowering the defense on your enemies.

  Endure A must have. Simply because it can get you out of many tricky situations when leveling up, doing gramps or while doing some instances.
