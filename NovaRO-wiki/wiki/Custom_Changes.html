Custom Changes

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Nova Ragnarok mainly follows the Korean release of Ragnarok Online (commonly referred to as kRO). However, there are a select few alterations specific to Nova to accommodate the player economy or to improve player quality of life. This page attempts to list all the exceptions where Nova differs from kRO for content common to both.

Class Related

Mechanic

When using the skill Weapon Refine, a Mechanic's success rate is equivalent to a Job Level 70 Whitesmith, regardless of what Job Level the Whitesmith changed jobs.

The skill Weapon Refine can be used while riding a Madogear.


Drop Changes

NovaRO does NOT follow kRO's level range drop table. This means a character whose level is vastly outside of a monster's level range receives the same drop rate chance as a character who matches or is close to the same monster. This allows players to use max level characters to farm lower levelled monsters without needing to constantly remake new characters or force characters to stay within a certain level range to farm specific items.

Crimson Weapons

NovaRO does NOT multiply Crimson weapon drop chance by 10. Due to the Crimson weapons being extremely powerful at high refine levels and how easy they are to find, multiplying their drop rate would severely harm the pricing of comparable weapon options that take more effort to acquire. As such, they are kept at the official drop rate of 0.5% to give players more choices when deciding what weapons they wish to work toward.

White Wing Suits

The White Wing Suits drop chance from Anubis was changed to 1% instead of 5% on NovaRO.

Item Price Changes

Witherless Rose & Crystal Mirror

The NPC sell price of the Witherless Rose and Crystal Mirror are reduced. This is due to the ease of farming Mavkas, which drop these items frequently and would otherwise cause the zeny to inflate very quickly.


Old Glast Heim Rewards

The item cost of the rewards here are multiplied by 10 to reflect the amount of effort required to attain them and preserve their value as endgame equipment.
