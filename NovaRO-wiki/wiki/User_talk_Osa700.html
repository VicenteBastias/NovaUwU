Overview

Bio Weapon Helms EDDA hats is a Headgear that can obtain through MH Hat Quest and it combos with EDDA biolab weapon

Quest

Mist (Edda Hats)
NPC Purpose: EDDA hat quest
Map: valkyrie
Coordinates: (valkyrie 41, 86)Copied!
1. Start the quest by talking to Mist (valkyrie 41, 86)Copied!.
2. She will ask you to bring  Silver Gem and  Zaedronath Shadow Orb Silver Gem can be obtained from killing Zaedronath at 1% drop rate or can be crafted using 15 Shattered Silver Gem by talking to Dante who is located on the far right side of the Wyvern. ( Shattered Silver Gem also dropped from Zaedronath at 100% drop rate).  Zaedronath Shadow Orb can be obtained by talking to Lisbeth who is located on the middle right side of the Wyvern see Shadow Gear Refinement (Shadow Orb). Both items are tradable meaning you can get them from other players.
3. Then, she will ask you to bring 600  Faux Gold (dropped from mobs in Bioresearch Laboratory - Gauntlet Mode)
4. After that, She will ask you to bring 7  Carbalite Alloy (crafted from Ingots through Vulcan at The Wyvern)
5. Finally, she will ask you to speak to thy class to obtain the reward:
