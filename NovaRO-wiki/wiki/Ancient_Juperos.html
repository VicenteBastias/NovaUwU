Great Expedition

You can choose to meet with the Rekenber members or the Academy members in whichever order you prefer.

Meeting the Rekenber Members

Requirements
Base Level: 180
Starting Point: (lhz_in01 157, 37)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Envoy Request
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
Items: 30  Rekenber Tickets
  1. Speak with Immanuel Rittenburg inside the Rekenber Headquarters at (lhz_in01 157, 37)Copied!. Select Make your presence known to get his attention.
  2. To prove yourself, you will need to find 1  Green Heavy Tablet and 1  Red Heavy Tablet. They are dropped from Green Venatu and Red Venatu respectively if you have the quest active.
  3. Return to Immanuel and give him the tablets. You will be given 3  Expedition Letters to deliver to his team members.
  4. Speak with Elsa at (lhz_in01 22, 248)Copied! within the Rekenber Headquarters. You'll be given a  Misplaced Book.
  5. Go to the Yuno Library at (yuno_in04 34, 67)Copied! and speak with the Librarian. Resist her shameless attempts to seduce you.
  6. Return to Elsa to complete her request.
  7. The next team member can be found in the Lighthalzen slums, at (lighthalzen 312, 304)Copied!. Speak with the Guard.
  8. He refuses to speak to you without wearing  Geek Glasses and  Grampa Beard. Both itens can be bought with the Toy Supplier at (lhz_in02 85, 210)Copied!.
  9. Wear those items and speak with him again.
  10. Juliet can be found in the Einbroch Workshop. To get there, go to Einbroch > then use (ein_in01 30, 88)Copied!. Speak with her and accept her request.
  11. You need to collect 20  Rusty Screws and 10  Used Iron Plates.
  12. Return to Juliet to complete her request.
  13. Speak with Immanuel Rekenber at (lhz_in01 157, 37)Copied!, in the Rekenber Headquarters and tell him you've delivered all the letters.
    You'll be rewarded with 30  Rekenber Tickets and some experience.
    You have now completed the Rekenber team.

Deciphering the Ancient Tablets

Requirements
Base Level: 180
Starting Point: (yuno_fild07 221, 202)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Expedition Letters Delivery
Rewards
Experience: - (Base) and - (Job)
  1. The new Juperos exploration camp entrance can be found near the normal Juperos entrance. The portal is at (yuno_fild07 216, 202)Copied!.
  2. Upon entering, a cutscene will trigger.
  3. Click on the 'Device and select I have an idea!
  4. For the next step, you have to decipher the tablets in the proper order given by the note Isabel left on the Device. If you do it in the wrong order, you'll have to start over.
    1. Speak with Richard and select Follow the steps on Yellow Plate.
    2. Speak with Immanuel and select Follow the steps on Red Plate.
    3. Speak with Immanuel and select Follow the steps on Green Plate.
    4. Speak with Richard and select Follow the steps on Blue Plate.
  5. Click on the Device to finish the activation.
    You have gained access to the jup_fild01 and jup_fild02.
    You have unlocked the Rekenber repeatable quests.
    You have unlocked the Academy repeatable quests.
    Note: In order to use  Ancient Juperos Storybook on another character, the player must also complete the Repeatable Quests once.


Ancient Juperos Repeatable Quests

If you're doing the quests for the first time, you can pick all of them at once. The second time however, you will only be able to choose the quests from either the Rekenber or the Academy. The quests must be fully completed before they can be reset. There are no cooldowns on them, so you can repeat them as many times as you want. You simply cannot do both groups at the same time.

  • Completing all Rekenber Repeatable Quests gives 30  Rekenber Tickets (for a total of 55) and 1  Research Papers.
  • Completing all Academy Repeatable Quests gives 30  Academy Tickets (for a total of 55) and 1  Research Papers.

Enchants

All the Racing Caps can be enchanted once acquired and at the same NPC - Republic Quatermaster.

You can reset the enchants at any moment for 10  Juperium Energy and 500,000 zeny. The whole enchantment process has no risk of destroying your item.

On the final enchant, Republic Quatermaster will offer you an option to buy the Special Enchant corresponding to your headgear for a higher price.

It costs 950  Juperium Energy and 500,000 zeny to pick the matching enchant. You can use the regular price for a chance at a random enchant.

Once you have obtained a Special Enchant, it can be upgraded in a similar fashion to Bio5 Headgears. In this case the max level is 3 and it has the following costs:

  • From level 1 to level 2: 25  Juperium Energy and 100,000 zeny. (20% success chance)
  • From level 2 to level 3: 50  Juperium Energy and 300,000 zeny. (5% success chance)

Be aware that this upgrade has a chance to fail and downgrade to the previous level. But it won't go below level 1:

  • Safe Enchant Upgrade cost from 2 to 3 (No downgrade upon failure): 250  Juperium Energy and 1,500,000 zeny

You can also re-roll the stats at any time. Each attempt costs:

  • Slot #1: 30  Juperium Energy, 10  Rekenber Ticket, and 300,000 zeny
  • Slot #2: 60  Juperium Energy, 10  Academy Ticket, and 350,000 zeny
