Multiplayer Games

Face off against fellow Festival Attendee's in these 5 Multiplayer games!
Multiplayer games require 4 players to start.
You can join a random multiplayer game from anywhere on the main island map using the @joinqueue command and leave the queue with @leavequeue command.
You can also join the multiplayer game on frenzy using the @joinqueue bonus command.

Shellfish Shuck


Are you Shellfish enough?

Gather as many Shellfish as you can by running around the map!
The two players who caught the the most will be rewarded with Summer Festival Coins!

Voltz Clicker


A game to test your multitasking abilites!

When the game begins, a special effect will be displayed on a Rafflesia
You must click on that Rafflesia as fast as possible!
Incorrect clicks will temporarily stun you.
The player with the most points at the end of the round wins!

Dodgebomb


Are you the Survivor?

You and three other players will be warped inside a walled area.
Once inside, Bombrings will appear each round! If they explode and you get hit,
you are out of the game! The last player standing wins the game.
If all remaining players die in the same round, there will be no winner.

Poolside Panic


Got Oxygen?

You and three other players will be warped inside of a pool.
Once inside, players will be seated and the game will begin.
When the game begins the Timekeeper will start counting down to 0.
Your goal is to be the last person to stand before the clock hits 0.
The person who stood up first will be eliminated each round.

Seaside Shuffle


Just keep running, just keep running!

Race against other players around the Summer Festival Island.
Simple as that.
