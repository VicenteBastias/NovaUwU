Reading Spellbook Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Passive, Self
  • SP Cost: 40
  • Var. Cast Time: 5.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 0.5s
 Allows you to read Spell Books to store spells in your mind, letting you later use them with Release to cast the spell instantly.


To store a spell you must put the Spell Book item in your skill hotkey bar and use it as if it is a consumable. (It will not be consumed, and there is a different book for each skill).
Each spell stored will take a different amount of Mind Slots, which determines the amount of spells you can hold.


  • The number of spells you can store is determined by your INT, Base Level, Freezing Spell level and the spells you are trying to store.


8 Mind Slots

  •  Spell Book (Drain Life)


10 Mind Slots

  •  Spell Book (Lord of Vermilion)
  •  Spell Book (Meteor Storm)
  •  Spell Book (Storm Gust)


12 Mind Slots

  •  Spell Book (Jack Frost)
  •  Spell Book (Earth Strain)
  •  Spell Book (Crimson Rock)
  •  Spell Book (Chain Lightning)


22 Mind Slots

  •  Spell Book (Comet)
  •  Spell Book (Tetra Vortex)


The formula to calculate mind slots is:
(Freezing Spell Level × 4) + Floor(Base Level ÷ 10) + Floor(Total INT ÷ 10)

  • Var. Cast Time: 1.5s + (0.5 x Skill Level)
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.5s

 Create a shower of Icy Mist around an area, dealing Water property magic damage to all enemies in range, also has a chance to inflict 10 seconds of the Frost status.

Targets with the Frost status will take additional damage from Jack Frost.

  • [Lv 4] : 600% MATK, 45% Chance to apply Frost status, 9x9 Area of Effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 700% MATK, 50% Chance to apply Frost status, 11x11 Area of Effect.


This skills damage is based on the following formula;
Damage (MATK) = [Base_Damage × (BaseLevel ÷ 100)]%

White Imprison Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 45 + (5 x Skill Level)
  • Cool Down: 4.0s
