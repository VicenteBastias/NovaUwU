Template:NarniaGearDivider

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Used in a table or Template:NarniaGearTable to add a divider. Used by Lady Narnia.

  • Flexible
  • Easy-to-use
  • Optimized for both desktop and mobile screens
  • Best used with Template:NarniaGearTable for complete functionality.

Syntax

{{NarniaGearDivider |style= |title= }}


Parameters
style (Optional)
Put CSS styles here to customize the row to your liking. Applies to both columns in the row. (background-color,font-size,etc.)
title (Optional)
Label to put into the divider.

Example

Upper + Middle

 Chibi Pope [1] +  Blush [1]

Healing power +15%
Extra +5% when Chibi Pope is +6 or above

One of the best upper & mid headgear combos for pure, raw healing.

Add healing power:  Parus Card per headgear!

Divider
Garment

 Archangel Wings [1]

Enchantable

Powerful healing garment for Monster Hunter when enchanted.

Enchant: Heal Amount 5
Add survivability:  Marse Card or  Essence of Evil VIT3
