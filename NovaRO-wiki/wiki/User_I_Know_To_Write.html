Notes about the following info below

- Is just a way to give an extra hand to players with full list of options to use, they're not guides.
- Yes, the tables have MVP and Mini-Boss cards. They're a list of options, not a what's the best, possible, or reachable to use guide.
- Yes, there are expensive options there (as in money and/or time to get them).
- Note that some values may show more than what you may be thinking. This happens because the piece of gear you're checking may add something extra that influences something else, like a piece of gear adding +1 LUK and that being enough to reach the next CRIT bonus.
- Some items have a min and a max value listed. Please check the description since some may have special conditions to provide higher values.
- If you don't understand why some values are displayed like that, do a quick search about it in the wiki, Divine-Pride, Google, or even DuckDuckGo before pointing that it's wrong.
- Yes, super crappy options aren't displayed here. It's just not worth to mention them.
- I'm not here to teach you math, just to help you with your research.
- Yes, it may miss some good options here and there. I'm not perfect. Feel free to contact me to add it or edit the template.
- The lists below are templates, feel free to use them anywhere.
