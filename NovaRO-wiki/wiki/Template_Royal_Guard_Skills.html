Trample Support
  • Max Level: 3
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 15 + (15 x Skill Level)
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Area of Effect:
    • (Lv 1~3) : 5x5
 Trample the area around you with a chance of removing traps nearby. Explosive traps will still detonate but you have a chance to dodge the damage.


  • Dodge chance is increased based on the casters AGI and DEX.


  • [Lv 1] : 5% Base dodge chance, 50% Chance of removing traps.
  • [Lv 2] : 10% Base dodge chance, 75% Chance of removing traps.
  • [Lv 3] : 15% Base dodge chance, 100% Chance of removing traps.


Formula for calculating chance to dodge explosive traps:
Dodge Chance = [Base_Dodge_Chance + (DEX + AGI) ÷ 4]%

Force of Vanguard Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 Apply a buff to yourself to increase your Max HP, DEF, and allows you to gain Rage counters.

Whenever you receive physical damage you have a chance to gain a Rage counter, increasing this skills level increases the maximum amount of Rage counters allowed.

Continuously drains SP while active.


  • Rage counters can be used for other skills.


  • [Lv 1] : +3% Max HP, +2% DEF, 20% Chance to gain Rage counter, Max 7 Rage counters, Drains 20 SP every 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : +6% Max HP, +4% DEF, 32% Chance to gain Rage counter, Max 9 Rage counters, Drains 16 SP every 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : +9% Max HP, +6% DEF, 44% Chance to gain Rage counter, Max 11 Rage counters, Drains 12 SP every 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : +12% Max HP, +8% DEF, 56% Chance to gain Rage counter, Max 13 Rage counters, Drains 8 SP every 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : +15% Max HP, +10% DEF, 68% Chance to gain Rage counter, Max 15 Rage counters, Drains 4 SP every 10 seconds.

Rage Burst Damage
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 150
  • Cast Delay: 3.0s
  • Range: 1
 Release all the Rage counters accumulated from Force of Vanguard to deal massive damage to a single target. The more Rage counters the higher the damage.


  • This skills damage is increased based on the casters missing HP, Base Level, and the amount of Rage counters.


Damage is calculated using the following formula:
Damage (ATK) = [{(Rage_Counters × 200) + (MaxHP − CurrentHP) ÷ 100} × (BaseLevel × 100)]%

Prestige Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 70 + (5 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 1.0s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 2.0s
  • Cool Down: 60.0s
 Places an aura around you that increases your hard DEF and gives you a base chance to dodge magic attacks.


  • The DEF increase is increased based on the casters Base Level, and Defender's skill level.
  • The dodge chance is increased based on the casters INT, LUK, Base Level, and this skills level.


  • [Lv 1] : +15 DEF, 1% Base Dodge chance, Duration 30 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : +30 DEF, 2% Base Dodge chance, Duration 45 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : +45 DEF, 3% Base Dodge chance, Duration 60 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : +60 DEF, 4% Base Dodge chance, Duration 75 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : +75 DEF, 5% Base Dodge chance, Duration 90 seconds.


Defense is calculated using the following formula:
DEF Increase = {Base_DEF_Increase + (Defender_Level × 10)} × (BaseLevel ÷ 100)

Magic dodge chance is calculated using the following formula:
Dodge Chance = [Base_Dodge_Chance + {(INT + LUK) × (SkillLevel ÷ 20) × (BaseLevel ÷ 200)}]%
