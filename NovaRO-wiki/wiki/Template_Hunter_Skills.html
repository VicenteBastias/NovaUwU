Ankle Snare Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 12
  • Range: 3
  • Consumes 1:  Trap
 Set a trap on the ground that immobilizes anyone that steps in a 3x3 area of the trap.


  • The trap will dismantle itself after a set duration.
  • The targets AGI will reduce the time they are immobilized.


  • [Lv 1] : 4 seconds immobilize, Duration 250 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : 8 seconds immobilize, Duration 200 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : 12 seconds immobilize, Duration 150 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : 16 seconds immobilize, Duration 100 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : 20 seconds immobilize, Duration 50 seconds.


Immobilize duration is based on this formula;
Effect Duration = [Base_Effect_Duration − Target_AGI ÷ 10]s

Freezing Trap Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Range: 3
  • Consumes: 2  Trap
 Set a trap on the ground that freezes anyone that steps in a 3x3 area of the trap. It will also deal Water property physical damage to all enemies in the area.

  • [Lv 2] : 60% Chance to apply Frozen status, 9 seconds Frozen Duration, Duration 120 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : 70% Chance to apply Frozen status, 12 seconds Frozen Duration, Duration 90 seconds.

  • [Lv 5] : 90% Chance to apply Frozen status, 18 seconds Frozen Duration, Duration 30 seconds.


Damage is based on this formula;
Damage = ATK + (Trap_Research_Level × 40)

Blitz Beat Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 7 + (3 x Skill Level)
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.8s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.2s
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Range: 5
  • Requires falcon companion
 Use your Falcon to attack a target and a any enemies in the area of 3x3 cells around it.


While doing normal attacks there is a chance that your Falcon will attack the target on it's own. This chance is increased depending on the casters LUK.


  • Every 3 LUK increases the Falcon auto attack chance by 1%.
  • The damage of Falcon is increased by the casters AGI and DEX.


  • [Lv 1] : Attacks 1 times.
  • [Lv 2] : Attacks 2 times.
  • [Lv 3] : Attacks 3 times.
  • [Lv 4] : Attacks 4 times.
  • [Lv 5] : Attacks 5 times.

Blast Mine Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Ground

Damage = [DEX × (3 + Base_Level ÷ 100) × (1 + INT ÷ 35)] × Skill_Level ± 10% + (Trap_Research_Level × 40)
