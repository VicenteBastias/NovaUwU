Chase Walk Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10
  • Var. Cast Time: 0.5s
  • Fixed Cast Time: 0.5s
  • Cast can not be interrupted
 Hide yourself and become undetectable by all detection skills. You are unable to attack in this mode, and will leave behind a trail of footprints as you walk.

After hiding for 10 seconds you gain a STR bonus for 30 seconds.


  • Getting hit by a AoE spell or attack will reveal you.
  • Insect, Demon, and Boss monsters will be able to see you.
  • SP consumption is 5 times as much in WoE.


  • [Lv 1] : 70% Movement speed, Drains 12 SP per second, +1 STR after 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 2] : 75% Movement speed, Drains 14 SP per second, +2 STR after 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 3] : 80% Movement speed, Drains 16 SP per second, +4 STR after 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 4] : 85% Movement speed, Drains 18 SP per second, +8 STR after 10 seconds.
  • [Lv 5] : 90% Movement speed, Drains 20 SP per second, +16 STR after 10 seconds.
Reject Sword Toggle / Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 10:15:20:25:30
 When an enemy attacks you with a Sword or Dagger class weapon, has a chance to block half of the damage. (Against monsters you will reflect damage back to the monster).

You can block up to 3 times before the skill runs out.


  • [Lv 1] : 15% Chance to block/reflect attacks.
  • [Lv 2] : 30% Chance to block/reflect attacks.
  • [Lv 3] : 45% Chance to block/reflect attacks.
  • [Lv 4] : 60% Chance to block/reflect attacks.
  • [Lv 5] : 75% Chance to block/reflect attacks.
Preserve Toggle / Support
  • Max Level: 1
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 30
  • Fixed Cast Time: 1.0s
 Prevents you from copying skills you get hit by for Plagiarism.


  • This allows you to basically save a specific skill for repeated use, without losing it.
Full Strip Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 22:24:26:28:30
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
  • Range: 1
 Strip the targets Headgear, Armor, Weapon, and Shield at the same time and prevent them from equipping a shield for a certain duration.

Both the chance and duration are increased depending on the casters DEX, and reduced based on the targets DEX.


  • [Lv 1] : 7% Chance to strip, 135 second duration.
  • [Lv 2] : 9% Chance to strip, 135 second duration.
  • [Lv 3] : 11% Chance to strip, 135 second duration.
  • [Lv 4] : 13% Chance to strip, 135 second duration.
  • [Lv 5] : 15% Chance to strip, 135 second duration.


Success rate is based on this formula;
Success Rate = 5 + 2 * Skill Level + {(Your DEX - Enemy DEX) / 5}
