Rolling Cutter Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 5
  • Cast Delay: 0.2s
  • Required weapon type(s): Katar
 Spin in a circle with your katar to hit all nearby enemies.

While spinning you will gain Spin Counters. You can have up to 10 counters at a time, and most of the movement in any way will remove all Spin Counters. There are a few exceptions related to knockback where it doesn't apply.


  • Spin Counter duration: 10 seconds


  • [Lv 1] : 130% ATK, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 2] : 210% ATK, 3x3 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 3] : 290% ATK, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 4] : 370% ATK, 5x5 Area of effect.
  • [Lv 5] : 450% ATK, 7x7 Area of effect.

Weapon Crush Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 20
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 After blocking an attack from an enemy, strike them back with a chance to strip their weapon.

Higher levels increase the duration of the strip.


  • [Lv 1] : 100% ATK, 90 / 75 second duration against monsters and players respectively.
  • [Lv 2] : 100% ATK, 120 / 90 second duration against monsters and players respectively.
  • [Lv 3] : 100% ATK, 150 / 105 second duration against monsters and players respectively.
  • [Lv 4] : 100% ATK, 180 / 120 second duration against monsters and players respectively.
  • [Lv 5] : 100% ATK, 210 / 135 second duration against monsters and players respectively.
Cross Ripper Slasher Damage
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Enemy
  • SP Cost: 20:24:28:32:36
  • Cast Delay: 0.3s
  • Cooldown: 0.5s:0.45s:0.4s:0.35s:0.2s
  • Range: 9:10:11:12:13
  • Required weapon type(s): Katar
 After gaining momentum from spinning with Rolling Cutter, throw a pair of daggers at a target to finish them off.


  • This skill can only be used if you have Spin Counters.
  • The more Spin Counters you have the more damage this skill will do.
  • This skills damage increases depending on the caster's Base Level, AGI and number of Spin Counters.


  • [Lv 1] : 80% ATK.
  • [Lv 2] : 160% ATK.
  • [Lv 3] : 240% ATK.
  • [Lv 4] : 320% ATK.
  • [Lv 5] : 400% ATK.


Damage is based on this formula;
Damage (ATK) = [Base_Damage + {Spin Counters x 200 + 3 x AGI}%]

Poisoning Weapon Support
  • Max Level: 5
  • Target: Self
  • SP Cost: 20:24:28:32:36
  • Cast Delay: 1.0s
 Enchant your weapon with a New Poison. Using a New Poison will give you +5% Melee damage, and an addition buff depending on the poison. Applying a poison to an enemy will give them a negative effect instead.


Poison Negative Effects Positive Effects
 Paralysis
  • -10% ASPD
  • -10% Flee
  • -50% Movement Speed
  • Gives you Increase Agility movement speed, but does not stack with it.
 Pyrexia
  • Causes Blind status
  • Causes Hallucination status
  • Deals 100 damage every second
  • +15% Critical Damage
  • +15% Normal Damage
 Death Hurt
  • -20% Healing Effectiveness (This affects potions, and it stacks with Critical Wounds).
  • Recovers 1% Max HP every second
 Leech End
  • Consumes a small amount of Max HP every second. (Does not work on Boss monsters).
  • Grants immunity to Stun status
  • Grants immunity to Blind status
 Antidote
  • Removes the effects of a New Poison.
  • Not Applicable.
 Venom Bleed
  • -15% Max HP
  • -30% damage taken from melee reflect.
 Magic Mushroom
  • Forces target to spam /heh emote.
  • Forces target to randomly cast skills.
  • Drains 3% of Max HP every 4 seconds.
  • -10% After Cast Delay
 Toxin
  • Inflicts damage causing target to flinch every 10 seconds.
  • Recovers 1% Max SP every second.
 Oblivion Curse
  • Forces target to use /? emote.
  • Blocks targets SP recovery.
  • Has a small chance to apply "Oblivion Curse" (Silence that can't be removed by most Silence cures).
  • Grants immunity to Curse status
  • Grants immunity to Silence status
