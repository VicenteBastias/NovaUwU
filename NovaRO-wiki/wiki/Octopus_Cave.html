Be careful! Once you leave, you cannot re-enter this instance.

  • Go to the Special Vending Machine at (malangdo 218, 165)Copied!. Buy an Octopus Hunting Stick there. You need Silvervine Fruits for them.
  • Open the box and keep that item with you whenever you enter the instance.

Starfish and Weird Entrance.
  1. Use the Warper > Instances > Octopus Cave.
  2. Talk to the Starfish and choose Ask to open the gate.
  3. Click on the Weird Entrance above him.
  4. You'll be warped into a cave with 4 warp portals. Enter one of the portal and walk until the end of the tunnel. There is no need to kill everything and the order of the portals don't matter.
  5. Kill the Octopus Leg.
  6. After killing 4 Legs, you'll be teleported back. Prepare for the boss fight.
  7. Crossing the new warp in the north you'll reach the boss room. There will be the Giant Octopus, several Octopus Legs and Octopuses. Kill the MVP.
