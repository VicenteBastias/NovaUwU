Overview

2nd Power Plant Entrance

2nd Power Plant is the farm map if you want to farm  Varmeal Tickets. They drop from all monsters on this map at a 25% rate.

Access to the dungeon is made through the portal at (ba_maison 88, 271)Copied! as shown on the minimap on the right.
