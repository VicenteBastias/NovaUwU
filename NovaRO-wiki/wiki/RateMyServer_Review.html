RateMyServer Review

From NovaRO: Wiki
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Writing an RMS review greatly helps the server to grow. Here is a guide on how to do it.

Note that you can refresh your review after 30 days. To do so, delete the previous one first.

Walkthrough

  1. Go to ratemyserver.net.
  2. Click on Login on the right sidebar to access your account.
    If you have no account, you have to register first.
    1. Be sure to choose "Server Player Registration".
    2. You have to activate your account, so be sure to enter a valid e-mail.
  3. On the main page, click on NovaRO.
  4. Scroll down and click on the Write Review button.
  5. Fill out the survey and the review section.

Explanations

If you are not sure what they refer to, take a look at these short explanations:

  • Stability: The overall crash and freeze rating that a player would give to the server. The higher the rating, the less it crashes/freezes.
  • Availability: How often is the server accessible? Is the server up 24/7? Is there a lot of down time (because of a crash, system downtime, or manual shutdown)? The higher the rating, the more often the server is up.
  • Community Friendliness: How friendly is the server's player community? Are the players wiling to help other players? Such as answering novice questions or quest questions when asked in public.
  • Community Eventfulness: How often does the server host player events? Special events? Such as PvP tournament, racing or hide and seek. The higher the rating, the more events.
  • Game Master Friendliness: How friendly is the server's game masters? Do they kill players out of pure spite or are they reasonable in actions?
  • Game Master Availability: How often are the game masters are available for support? The higher the rating, the more often they are online and available to help.
  • Game Master Helpfulness: How helpful are the game masters? Are the game masters able to answer player questions and solve problems between players?
  • Economy: How balanced is the server's economy? Are there significant amount of players with too much zeny? Are players willing to hunt items? Is zeny worth anything? The lower the rating, the poorer the economy.
  • Guild Competition: Is it worth investing in a guild? Is there competition between guilds or there is one guild that dominating the game? The higher the rating, the more balanced the guild competitions are.
  • Class Balance: Are all the jobs on the serer balanced by tier? For example, could a player of the swordsman class dominate all the first jobs? The lower the rating, the more unbalance the classes are.
  • Player Population: Just mention on how many players does the server have? Its your option if you want to type the one you see when you type /w or if you can have a estimate amount of people online daily.
  • Donation Level: How well do you find the donations? Do they give a gap between donors and non-donors? Are they obtainable by non-donors?
  • Customization Level: Are the server customs ruining the RO gameplay? Are the customs just for fun? For server cooperation/teamwork?
