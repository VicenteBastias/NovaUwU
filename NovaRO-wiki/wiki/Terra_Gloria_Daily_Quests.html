Clana Nemieri Dailies

Leaflets Delivery

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 19, 48)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 3,750,000 (Base) and 3,750,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token
( First time: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token)
  1. Speak with the Mason Bender (rebel_in 19, 48)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest.
  2. He wants you to deliver  Arms Shop Ad to the people in Einbech to promote the shop.
    • The Tired Looking Old man is at (einbech 79, 100)Copied!
    • The Sleepy Looking Old woman is at (einbech 170, 143)Copied!
    • The Tired Looking Man is at (einbech 71, 168)Copied!
  3. Return to him once you've completed your mission and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Luke's Requests

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 88, 61)Copied!
Items: Denny: 5  Steel and 15  Used Iron Plate
Cyan: 10  Burnt Tree and 3  Coal
Magenta: 10  Crystal Fragment and 10  Dark Crystal Fragment
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token

Speak with Luke (rebel_in 19, 48)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest. You will receive one of following randomly.

Plumbing Repairs for Denny

  1. Denny is complaining about the plumbing, he needs 5  Steel and 15  Used Iron Plate for the repairs.
  2. Hunt down 10 Old Stoves in the Mine Dungeon Floor 2.
  3. Return to Luke once you've gathered all the materials and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Getting Firewood for Cyan

  1. Cyan is cold because the fireplace is running out of wood, he needs 10  Burnt Tree and 3  Coal.
  2. Hunt down 10 Old Stoves in the Mine Dungeon Floor 2.
  3. Return to Luke once you've gathered all the materials and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Gathering Glitters for Magenta's Shooting Star

  1. Magenta needs your help for his Shooting Star by adding glitters, he needs 10  Crystal Fragment and 10  Dark Crystal Fragment.
  2. Hunt down 5 Minerals and 5 Obsidians in the Mine Dungeon Floor 2.
  3. Return to Luke once you've gathered all the materials and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Finding Charity

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 108, 27)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 3,750,000 (Base) and 3,750,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token
( First time: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token)
  1. Speak with Milo River (rebel_in 108, 27)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest.
  2. Milo needs help finding Charity (his cat). You will be given  Crispy Anchovy to get her attention.
  3. Click on the Empty Bench at (einbech 141, 98)Copied! and put down the Anchovy cookies. Keep trying until the quest is completed.
  4. Return to Milo River once you've completed your mission and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Rachel's Revolver Repairs

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 90, 51)Copied!
Items: 2  Oridecon
2  Elunium
5  Iron
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rachel's Revolver
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token
  1. Speak with Rachel Smith (rebel_in 90, 51)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest.
  2. Rachel will ask you to gather materials to repair the revolver. You will be asked to gather one of the following:
    • 2  Oridecon
    • 2  Elunium

  3. Return to her once you've completed your mission and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Entertain the Lebedev twins

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 109, 54)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token

Speak with Anya Levedev (rebel_in 109, 54)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest. You will receive one of following randomly.

Story Telling

  1. Anya will ask you to bring her a book from the Librarian (you get this one randomly).
  2. Speak with the Librarian in the Prontera Library at (prt_in 165, 89)Copied! and select the one requested by the twins.
  3. Return to Anya once you've completed your mission and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Mine Research 1

  1. Anya wants you to explore the Einbech Mine dungeon.
    • Hunt 30 Pitmans
    • Gather 1  Gun Powder
  2. Return to Anya once you've completed your mission and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Mine Research 2

  1. Anya wants you to explore the Einbech Mine dungeon.
    • Hunt 20 Venomous
    • Hunt 20 Noxious
    • Gather 1  Explosive Powder
  2. Return to Anya once you've completed your mission and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Cookies Delivery

  1. Anya wants you to give her homemade cookies to the people of the Rebellion. Speak with:
    • Milo River (rebel_in 108, 27)Copied!
    • Mason Bender (rebel_in 33, 43)Copied!
    • Rachel Smith (rebel_in 90, 51)Copied!
    • Ericsson Adler (rebel_in 91, 67)Copied!
    • Elena Bolkova (rebel_in 70, 78)Copied!
  2. Return to Anya once you've completed your mission and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Flower Girl

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 91, 67)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: None
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token
( First time: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token)
  1. Speak with Ericsson Adler (rebel_in 91, 67)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest. Adler wants you to put down flowers for Irene.
  2. Talk with the Flower Girl at (einbroch 167, 160)Copied! and she will ask you to bring her 3  Slick Paper.
  3. Once you've gathered the items, talk with the Flower Girl again and she will make a bouquet for you.
  4. Click on the signboard in front of the Huge Tree (einbroch 162, 153)Copied! and put down the bouquet.
  5. Return to Ericsson Adler once you've completed your mission and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Elena's Challenge

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 70, 78)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token
( First time: 10  Schwartz's Honor Token)
  1. Speak with Elena Bolkova (rebel_in 70, 78)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest.
  2. Select I've come to fight. Talk to her again and select I'm ready followed by Go to the waiting room.
  3. Enter the chatroom and you'll have to wait your turn before the match can start.
  4. Once it starts, defeat her.
  5. Speak with Elena Bolkova again and she will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.


Elders Party Preparations

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 70, 78)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Dien's Party
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token for each request.

Speak with Rebellion Guard Leader (rebel_in 162, 87)Copied! inside Clana Nemieri to begin this quest.

Dien's High Class Tea

  1. Defeat Heart Hunter Ebel in the Heart Hunter War Base instance and collect  High Class Tea.
  2. Return to the Rebellion Guard Leader and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Ridsh's Old Letter

  1. Defeat Upgraded Heart Hunters in the Heart Hunter War Base instance and collect 1  Old Letter.
  2. Return to the Rebellion Guard Leader and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Cotnes's Very Shining Ring

  1. Defeat Heart Hunter Guards in the Heart Hunter War Base instance and collect 4  Very Shiny Ring.
  2. Return to the Rebellion Guard Leader and he will reward you with 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.

Weapon Grudges

Requirements
Base Level: 100
Starting Point: (rebel_in 104, 36)Copied!
Prerequisite(s): Search for Terra Gloria
Rewards
Experience: 7,500,000 (Base) and 7,500,000 (Job)
Items: 5  Schwartz's Honor Token.
