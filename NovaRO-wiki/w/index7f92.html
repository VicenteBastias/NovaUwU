Ragnarok Online is a game made mostly of episodic content. In this page you can find the list of episodes already available on kRO (this doesn't mean that everything is available on NovaRO).

Important content added between them is added as Others.

Dates added only include the date of the first addition of the Episode.

Links are posted, when available, to the update notices otherwise it's to the patch note page.

Overlook Water Dungeon is an iRO custom update, you won't find it here.
