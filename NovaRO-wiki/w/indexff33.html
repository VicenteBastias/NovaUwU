This templates links to the NovaRO Market page instead of Divine Pride.
Same as [[Template:Item List]], but always pull the item thumbnail from the alternative database (<code><nowiki>https://novaragnarok.com/data/items/icons2/<id>.png</nowiki></code> instead of <code><nowiki>https://novaragnarok.com/data/kRO/inventory/<id>.png</nowiki></code>).


Syntax:
If thumbnail is missing, try using [[Template:Item List]] without <code>alt=yes</code>.
<pre>
{{Item List2 |id=<id> |item=<name>}}
{{Item List2 |id=<id> |item=<name> |num=<num>}}
{{Item List2 |id=<id> |item=<name> |slots=<slots>}}
</pre>
{{Item List2 |id=2285 |item=Apple of Archer |num=2 |slots= 3}}
 
{{Item List2 |id=513 |item=Banana}}
 
For items with a thumbnail with link to DP, use [[Template:Item List]]
 
For cards, use [[Template:Card]]
 
For items without a thumbnail, use [[Template:Item]]
