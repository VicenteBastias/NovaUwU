General Customizations

The following changes are applicable to all Siege environments:

Game Mechanics

  • The entry level for PvP contents has been raised to Base Level 225
  • The Fixed Cast Time capped has been removed in order to make many 4th Job skills viable.
  • All the ultimate skill from 4th job has been disabled
  • Halved effectiveness of subtraits except for H.PLUS, RES & MRES
    • P.ATK: Reduced from 1% per point to 0.5% per point
    • S.MATK: Reduced from 1% per point to 0.5% per point
    • C.RATE: Reduced from 1% per point to 0.5% per point
  • All players receive 92% less damage from skills and 50% less damage from auto attacks.
  • All players receive an innate 20% after cast delay reduction bonus.
  • All Baby characters are considered Medium size, including baby Dorams.
  • Items and skills which ignore reflect have a cap of 30%.
  • Critical Wounds lowers the effectiveness of healing received by 10% per level instead of 20%.
  • Any methods of identifying items, such as  Magnifiers or Identify, are disabled.
  • 100 MDEF no longer grants immunity to Frozen and Stone Curse.
  • Fire Armor granting immunity to Blaze status, Water Armor to Quench, Wind Armor to Torrent, and Earth Armor to Concretion, and Poison Armor to Severe Poison.

MVP Cards

  • All [MVP] cards follow a tiering system. The card can be limited or allowed based on its Tier. All MVP cards have their respective Tier listed on their description.
    • Tier 0
    Cards in this tier are banned from any Siege game mode. This tier is generally reserved as a blacklist for cards that have not yet been obtained and/or implemented at the time they are classified as Tier 0.
    • Tier 1
    Cards in this tier are limited to 3 per Guild in War of Emperium, and disabled in other Siege game modes.
    • Tier 2
    Cards in this tier are limited to 5 per Guild in War of Emperium, and disabled in other Siege game modes.
    • Tier 3
    Cards in this tier are disabled in King of Emperium but have no use limitations in all other Siege game modes.
  • In the draft mode, Tier 1, Tier 2 and Tier 3 are Disable

Partially Disable Items

  •  Evil Gloves does not grant Spider Web nor autocast Kimi's Mind Confusion.
  •  Mad Bunny and its [EVT] variant's Physical Reflect and autocast of Magic Mirror are disabled.
  •  Mettle, Magic Essence, Acute, Master Archer, Adamantine, Affection or  Unyielding higher than level 5 are counted as if being level 5.
  •  Vellum Flail applies Critical Wounds instead of No Recovery at a lower chance.
  •  Purified Knight's Shield's DEF and MDEF bypass enchants are disabled.
  •  Golden Syrup healing has been reduced by 25%.
  •  Illusion Immaterial Sword's SP Drain effect and chance are reduced by 10% each and SP Drain effect can only be applied through Auto Attacks.
  •  Mental Destroyer’s SP drain effect will now only work with auto attacks.
  •  Furious Ice Titan Card and  Furious Snowier Card combo are completely disable.
  •  Dark Priest effect only works with autoattackss.
  •  [MVP] Sarah Card will keep its old effect for Siege environments: If the user receives damage greater than their current maximum HP amount, this card will decrease the damage received by a large amount.
  •  Sropho Card effect only works with autoattacks.
  •  Golden Thief Bug Card’s magic damage immunity no longer grants status or dispel immunity.

Battlegrounds Customizations

The following changes are applicable only in Battlegrounds:

Game Mechanics

  • Every player receives the skill   Freedom.
    • An exclusive Battlegrounds skill that instantly cures the caster from any negative effect and can be use while disabled. Has a 2-minute cooldown.
  • Weapons with refines greater than 15 will be treated as +15 inside Battlegrounds.
  • Negative status effects last a maximum of 20 seconds.
  • All gear break chances are halved.

Items

  • Equipment Repair scroll repairs all damaged items instead of a single one.

Cards

  •  Sropho Card grants -2 ASPD in Battlegrounds.

War of Emperium Customizations

The following changes are applicable in War of Emperium, Draft War of Emperium and King of Emperium:

Game Mechanics

  • Barricades receive only 20% of the damage/healing inflicted.
  • All Barricades and Stones will now be auto built upon a castle break.
  • Increased the HP of castle Stones by times 10.
  • Players take double damage from any source while casting Emergency Recall.
  • Emergency Recall cancels and prevents the effects of skills that prevent taking direct damage or being interrupted.
  • Players that have died more than 100 times in a single War of Emperium will now be unable to re-enter the castle.

Classes

  Genetic

  • Crazy Weed's Cooldown cannot be reduced below 4 seconds.
  • Full Chemical Protection has a 7-cells cast range.
  •  Banana Bomb can be used.
    • This follows the old Item Sling skill numbers. Duration of the sit effect is 3 seconds, with a 1-second cooldown, usable by Genetics only.
  • Pyroclastic does not prevent weapon swapping in War of Emperium
    • Note:Swapping weapon will remove Pyroclastic's effect.


  Kagerou and   Oboro

  • Increased the damage of Freezing Spear by 50%.


  Minstrel /   Wanderer

  Ranger

  • Reduced Aimed Bolt damage by 40% in War of Emperium.


  Rune Knight

  •  Algiz Runestone can be used.
    • Algiz applies the effects of Millennium Shield and 175 MDEF to all party members (excluding the caster) in a 9x9 area and has a 1-minute duration and cooldown.


  Sorcerer

  • Mind Breaker is disabled.


  Soul Reaper

  • Kaite increases physical damage taken by +50% instead of +400%.


  Warlock
