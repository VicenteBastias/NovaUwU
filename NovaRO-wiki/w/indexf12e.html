Overview

This guide is a work in progress.

Stats

Summary of the section

  • STR - Increases ATK and maximum weight capacity.
  • AGI - Increases attack speed and flee. Also adds resistance to bleeding.
  • VIT - Increases HP, soft DEF and resistance to stun and poison.
  • INT - Increases SP, MATK and resistance to blind, sleep and confuse (AKA chaos).
  • DEX - Increases hit rate and reduces casting time.
  • LUK - Increases critical rate and perfect dodge.
