Siege Customizations are changes applied to certain behaviors and skills in Battlegrounds, King of Emperium, War of Emperium and/or Draft War of Emperium environments, which are generally made in an attempt to balance the game and create a more engaging Player vs. Player experience.

General Customizations

The following changes are applicable to all Siege environments:

Game Mechanics

  • All players receive an innate 20% after cast delay reduction bonus.
  • All players receive 65% less damage from skills and 28% less damage from auto attacks.
  • All Baby characters are considered Medium size, including baby Dorams.
  • Critical Wounds lowers the effectiveness of healing received by 10% per level instead of 20%.

Items

The card only affects auto attacks.

 Vellum Flail

The item has a very low chance to apply its effect for 30 seconds (does not apply every hit).

17.2 Related Items and Cards

Every Items and Cards included from Episode 17.2 Sage's Legacy are disabled.

These Items and Cards are disabled till it being discussed between the WoE Commissioners.

  Genetic

  • All Homunculus S are considered Demi-Human.
  • Crazy Weed damage is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Cart Cannon does not take the base level modifier into account.
  • Eraser Cutter and Xeno Slasher have a 0.2 second cooldown. (exception: Ranked/Unranked PvP Room)
  • Pain Killer's duration reduced to 20 sec.

  Guillotine Cross

  • Cross Ripper Slasher has a cooldown of 1 second instead of 0.3 seconds.
  • Soul Breaker cannot apply critical hits and deal 35% of its original damage cause of the following alterations:
    • Enchant Deadly Poison multipliers for Soul Breaker are reduced by 50%.
    • Soul Breaker's Damage is reduced by 30%.
  • Cross Impact cannot apply critical hits.
  • Cross Impact deals only 85.7% of its original damage cause of the following alteration:
    • Cross Impact deals 1500% ATK instead of 1750% ATK (the class rework change was not applied).

  Minstrel /   Wanderer

  • Metallic Sound's Cooldown cannot be reduced to less than .5 seconds.
  • Reverberation's Cooldown cannot be reduced to less than .5 seconds.

  Ranger

  • Unlimit changes:
    • Provides 50% DEF pierce during the effect.
    • Duration reduced from 1 minute to 40 seconds.
    • Cooldown reduced from 5 minutes to 3 minutes.
  • Detect removes Shadow Form at 100% success rate.

  Rebellion

  • Fallen Angel does not consume 'coins' and is usable with any/no weapon equipped.
  • Anti-Material Blast has a fixed 100% chance of applying its debuff.

  Royal Guard

  • Devotion automatically transfers the status effects Defender, Auto Guard and Reflect Shield to the target, without requiring a recast.

  Rune Knight

  • Ignition Break cannot apply critical hits.
  •  Pertz Runestone (Storm Blast) cannot apply critical hits.
  •  Lux Anima Runestone does not apply the 30% all size physical damage modifier or 30% short range modifier.
  •  Isia Runestone does not reduce melee reflect.

  Shadow Chaser

  • Man Hole cannot be used repeatedly to trap targets already under its effect.
  • Shadow Form is removed instantly by Ruwach Sight and Phantom Menace.

  Sorcerer

  • Land Protector removes Escape's Ankle Snare unit.

  Star Emperor

  • Hatred of the Sun, Moon and Stars has its bonus effect halved.
  • The Miracle of the Sun, Moon and Stars's effect is disabled.

  Summoner

  • Doram-race characters are considered Demi-human/Player race instead of Brute.
  • Summoners are allowed to wear  WoE Robe set.
  • Spirit of Life gives its full damage bonus (100%) regardless of current HP.
  • Silvervine Root Twist has a non-reducible 2-second cooldown and does not refresh its duration if used on an already rooted target.

  Sura

  • Cursed Circle can be removed by Dispell.
  • Cursed Circle cannot be cast on top of the Emperium.
  • Gates of Hell damage is altered.
  • Gates of Hell consumes 5 Spirit Spheres instead of 2.
  • Tiger Cannon is calculated individually for each target in the area of effect.
  • Gentle Touch Revitalize does not increase the damage of Gates of Hell and Tiger Cannon.
  • Asura Strike's regen debuff is increased to 5 seconds.
  • Knuckle Arrow will no longer move the caster to the target.

  Warlock

  • Stasis blocks the usage of all skills except Status Recovery, and can be removed by Status Recovery.
  • Mystical Amplification damage increase effect is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Crimson Rock damage is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Soul Expansion damage is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Jack Frost damage is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Comet damage is reduced (the class rework change was not applied).
  • Comet does not apply the Magic Intoxication status effect.

  Soul Reaper

  • Curse of Wicked Souls status effect increases Shadow Property damage for Soul Reapers only.
  • Soul Reapers will no longer lose souls on death.

Battlegrounds Customizations

The following changes are applicable only in Battlegrounds:

Game Mechanics

  • Weapons with refines greater than 15 will be treated as +15 inside Battlegrounds (except the MVP-On BG version).
  • Negative status effects last a maximum of 20 seconds.
  • All gear break chances are halved.
  • Every player receives the skill   Freedom.
    • An exclusive Battlegrounds skill that instantly cures the caster from any negative effect and can be use while disabled. Has a 2-minute cooldown.

Items

  • The SP Drain effect of  Illusion Immaterial Sword is reduced (10% less chance to drain and 10% less drain amount).

  Rune Knight

  •  Turisus Runestone (Giant's Growth) does not apply the 15% increase in melee physical damage modifier.

  Minstrel /   Wanderer

  • Performer Chorus skills can be used without being in the same party.
  • Invulnerable Siegfried no longer requires both performers to be on the same party.
    • Only the caster will be affected by fatigue.
    • Other Ensemble song might still need to be on the same party. (need confirmation)

  Sorcerer

  • Vacuum Extreme is disabled.

  Soul Reaper

  • Receive Soul Linker Spirit status at spawn.

  Sura

  • Asura Strike's 2x damage bonus with 5+ Spheres is removed and reduced its SP recovery lock from 5 to 3 seconds.

War of Emperium Customizations

The following changes are applicable only in War of Emperium and Draft War of Emperium:

Game Mechanics

  • Barricades receive only 20% of the damage / healing inflicted.
  • Players take double damage from any source while casting Emergency Recall.

Items

  • The SP Drain effect of  Illusion Immaterial Sword only applies through auto attacks.

  Genetic

  • Crazy Weed's Cooldown cannot be reduced to less than 4 seconds.
  • Full Chemical Protection have 7 cells cast range.

  Rune Knight

  • A new WoE exclusive Runestone was implemented,  Algiz Runestone, in order to emulate Lux Anima's pre-rework effect.
    • It applies the effects of Millennium Shield, Stonehard Skin, and 50% increase in healing effectiveness to all party members (not including the caster) in a 7x7 area and has a 1-minute duration and cooldown.

  Sorcerer

  • Mind Breaker is disabled.

  Soul Reaper

  • Kaite increases physical damage taken by +50% instead of +400%.

Draft WoE Customizations

The following changes are applicable only in Draft War of Emperium:

  Minstrel

  • Poem of Bragi applies to the caster's entire guild instead of just the party.

  Genetic

  • Full Chemical Protection is not removed by death.
