Siege Customizations are changes applied to certain behaviors and skills in Battlegrounds, War of Emperium and/or Draft War of Emperium environments, which were generally made to balance the game and create a more engaging Player vs. Player experience.

General Customizations

The following customizations are applicable to all Siege environments:

  • The Cursed Circle skill effect can be removed by Dispell

Draft WoE Customizations

The following customizations are applicable only in Draft War of Emperium:

  • The Minstrel skill Poem of Bragi applies to the caster's entire guild instead of just the party.
